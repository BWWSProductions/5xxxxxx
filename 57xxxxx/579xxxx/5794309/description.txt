REVIEW (FOR WHAT ITS WORTH LOL!)

.....and a hooker, a stripper, a Satanist, and a sadistic murderer...oh, and bulimic.

Don\'t expect a plot from this. Don\'t expect continuity from this. But do expect to squirm uncomfortably with your mouth hanging open, that is, if you can even get through the whole thing.

I\'ve seen Cannibal Holocaust. I\'ve seen the films of Andrey Iskanov (Nails, Visions of Suffering, Philosophy of a Knife). I\'ve seen August Underground\'s Mordrum. But this, oh my...kind of makes Mordrum look like \"Snow White\".

The hardest time I had with this is deciding if the stripper chick, the hooker, and the Satanist were the same girl or not. No, forget that, the hardest time I had with this was the gallons and gallons of vomit and the guy that kept drinking his own, only to puke it back up and do it again. No, really, the hardest time I had with this was the girl Princess, who had her arm sawed off and then was handed a guitar (which she declined to play, for some reason).

This is one nasty and f***ed up movie, and did I like it? Uh, well, let\'s just say it got under my skin, so I guess it was effective. Even if it has got to be THE most vile thing I\'ve ever seen, to the best of my recollection. 6 out of 10.
