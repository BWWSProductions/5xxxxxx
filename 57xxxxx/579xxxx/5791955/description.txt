Forbidden Flowers is Nancy Friday's second collection of sexual fantasies -- and it's even
more explicit and outspoken than her original erotic masterpiece, My Secret Garden. The 
constant refrain from the legions of women across America who read My Secret Garden was, 
"Thank God I'm not the only one..." who had those wild, exciting erotic thoughts. With
Forbidden Flowers, these women can yet again experience the exhilarating freedom that
comes with the awareness and acceptance of their sexual selves...................................
