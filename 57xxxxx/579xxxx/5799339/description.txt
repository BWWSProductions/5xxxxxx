Popular indie dance band Klaxons make their return to the music scene, following the making of their much anticipated second album Surfing the Void. Three years in the making and delayed by numerous creative shifts both in direction and production, the London four piece are back to unleash their new sounds on the Reading and Leeds crowd.

Loved for their 2007 Mercury Prize winning album Myths of the Near Future and hit single Golden Skans, Klaxons slap neon sheen on their captivating melodies. New single Echoes shows what they do best. Catch their warm welcome back to the stage as they headline the NME/Radio 1 stage.
