The Dalton brothers escape from jail and head for Canada. On the way they change their names to the Jones brothers: Frank, Louis, Robert and Stupid. The Luckster is continually on their trail, with some help from that stupid dog Rataplan, complaining horse Jolly Jumper and useless character Col Pentegras of the royal Canadian mounties. Soon the Daltons are robbing Canadian banks like there is no tomorrow.
