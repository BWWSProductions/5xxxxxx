Guest stars Albert Salmi, Tim McIntire,
Michael Constantine, and Ken Swofford.

Tim McIntire was the son of John McIntire and
Jeanette Nolan. He previously acted with his parents
in an episode of The Fugitive and also worked
with his dad in a few Wagon Train episodes.

Tim later appeared in two more Virginians
and years later died of heart failure brought 
on by his drug addiction at the age of 41 in 1986.

Veteran announcer / actor Bill Baldwin is the
voice of Trampas' father in a flashback sequence.
