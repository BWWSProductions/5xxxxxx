Beatz Iz My Business

The 2nd Full length compilation from Medicine Man.

One of the HOTTEST underground producers in Northern California.

Producer first, graphic artist second, & everything else after that.

Medicine Man produced, mixed & mastered ALL the tracks on this album & did the graphics.

It features almost 20 artists that appear on this compilation that make up this album.

It took over 2 years to finish this project. The production sound is mostly Old School West Coast Hip Hop.
