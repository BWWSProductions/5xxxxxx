Musical by Japanese theatre Takarazuka, Snow Troupe, 2007. DVDrip, non-translated. Revue included.

Musical follows the story of Okita Souji, captain of the 1st division of the Shinsengumi, a special police force of the late shogunate period. The story is mostly fictional, but based on primary sources.
