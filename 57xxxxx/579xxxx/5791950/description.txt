The purpose of this guide is to show the process involved in tracing an email. The
first step required to tracing an email is finding out the headers of the email. What
are headers? Email headers are lines added at the top of an email message that are used
by servers as the email goes on route to get delivered. Generally email clients only show
the standard To, From, and Subject headers, but there are more.................
