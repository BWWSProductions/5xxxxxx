Great news for all you mafia fans out there!
This mod unlocks the Freeroam Mode in Mafia II.

Install "gui-main.sds" in the /pc/sds_en/gui/ directory.
Remember to back up the original file and your savegames!!
Savegame location: "%UserProfile%\AppData\Local\2K Games\Mafia II"

Run Mafia II, at the Main Menu click on The Story and click Free Ride.
You can choose from six modes (Summer Day, Summer Day Rain, Summer Night, Winter Day, Winter Day Snow, Winter Night).

Don't forget to check out the garage! :)


More info @ http://mafia-empire.com/forum/index.php?topic=25
