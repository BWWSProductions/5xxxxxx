 Lulz: A Corruption of LOL  is a soundtrack containing all the meme songs that have come to be as of this time, January 2008. The project was started independently by an /i/nsurgent who upon realizing just how many songs there are posted to 420chan's /m/usic board, which allows music file uploads. A post was made to /ic/ on 4chan for album art, and the project is ongoing.
