Contains all Pop Group, Rip, Rig & Panic, and Mark Stewart solo records. The Rip, Rig & Panic albums are all extended editions with bonus tracks from their EPs and singles. Float Up CP is basically a fourth Rip, Rig & Panic album with a slight change in the line-up. 

contains: 
(1979) Y (2007 Remaster)
(1980) For How Much Longer Do We Tolerate Mass Murder
(1980) We Are Time (Compilation)
(1981) Rip, Rig & Panic - God
(1982) Rip, Rig & Panic - I Am Cold
(1983) Rip, Rig & Panic - Attitude
(1983) Mark Stewart - Learning to Cope with Cowardice (Director's Cut)
(1985) Mark Stewart - As the Veneer of Democracy Starts to Fade
(1985) Float Up CP - Kill Me in the Morning
(1987) Mark Stewart - Mark Stewart
(1990) Mark Stewart - Metatron
(1996) Mark Stewart - Control Data
(1998) We Are All Prostitutes (Compilation)
