Plot: 


Five years after the events depicted in CLASH OF THE TITANS, Calibus returns to seek revenge against Perseus for stealing the life that should have been his. Perseus and Andromeda are expecting their first child, but when Calibus rises up from the underworld on a mission of vengeance, their peaceful kingdom is once again thrust into darkness. Based on the Bluewater Productions comic-book of the same name.
