Photovoltaic calculator version 7.10. This spreadsheet is designed to model the financial performance of a residential photoelectric system installed in the US. Default assumptions for interest rate, system performance & tax schedules are all indicative of the California market. 

This spreadsheet is a work in progress. Suggestions are welcome. 


New additions in this version include:

--More sources for assumptions
--Financial formulas for Net Present Value, Internal Rate of Return and Simple Payback Period
