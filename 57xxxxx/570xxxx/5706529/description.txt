Abuse is a run and gun video game developed by Crack dot Com and published by Origin Systems. It was released in 1996, and originally ran on DOS and Linux operating systems. An improved port of the game was released for Mac OS.

Requirements :
- at least Mac OS X 10.4
- PPC or intel Mac
