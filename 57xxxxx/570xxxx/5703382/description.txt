All of Gojira's Demos

1996 - Victim (Godzilla)
1997 - Possessed (Godzilla)
1998 - Saturate (Godzilla)
2000 - Wisdom Comes (Gojira)

They were known as Godzilla up to 1999 when they were advised to change their name to avoid copyright infringement. 'Wisdom Comes' was their first release under the monicker 'Gojira'.

They are all in 128 kbps except 'Possessed' which is at 192 kbps
