Anatomy for the Artist
Dorling Kindersley Limited, 2001 / ISBN 0751334413 / 266 pages / PDF 400MB

By: Sarah Simblet
Photographer: John Davis


This is one of the most crucial and compulsory books for any artist who want to learn anatomy. It offers 250 realistic photographs in colour as well as comprehensive sketches to visualize the bodies. There are also a lot of commentaries and a brief history on the subject.
