SUMMARY/DESCRIPTION

Kisaki Kensuke is just an average teenage boy who happens to be in the Love Club at his school. Part of Kisaki's motivation to join the club was due to an incident that happened in the past, in which a classmate confessed their love to Kisaki. He responded by rejecting her and saying some really terrible things. He's lacked the courage to pursue love ever since, which is why he joined the Love Club. Now, he is trying to use the lessons learned in the Love Club to pursue a relationship with the new transfer student named Kasumi (who is seemingly un-interested in him).
