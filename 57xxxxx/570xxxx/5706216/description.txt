Arappatta Kettiya Gramathil [1986]

Written & Directed by: P Padmarajan

Plot: 3 friends Hilal(Ashokan), Zachariah(Mammootty) & Gopi(Nedumudi Venu) setout to
a whore house in a village where there is tension between the local Hindus & Muslims.

Starring: Mammootty, Ashokan, Nedumudi Venu, Jagathy, Sukumari, Unnimary, Philomina, Krishnan Kutty Nair
