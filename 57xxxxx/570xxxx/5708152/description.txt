The "Remains of Tom Lehrer" box set seems to, essentially, include Lehrer's entire discography.
Behold this biting satirist in full FLAC glory.

Disc 1 (Studio) = Songs By Tom Lehrer (1953) + More of Tom Lehrer (1959)
Disc 2 (Live) = Tom Lehrer Revisited (1960) + An Evening Wasted With Tom Lehrer (1959)
Disc 3 (More Live + Assorted Studio) = That Was The Year That Was (1965) + The Richard Hayman Sessions [Orchestra] (1960) +  The Joe Raposo Sessions for The Electric Company (1971-1972) + The Rob Fisher Sessions (New recordings, 1999)
