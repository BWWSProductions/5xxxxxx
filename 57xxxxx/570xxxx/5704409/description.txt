Con Air is a 1997 American action/thriller film
by Touchstone Pictures that stars Nicolas Cage,
John Cusack and John Malkovich. It was produced
by Jerry Bruckheimer, producer of Crimson Tide
and The Rock, and directed by Simon West. The
film borrows its title from the nickname of the
Justice Prisoner and Alien Transportation System.
