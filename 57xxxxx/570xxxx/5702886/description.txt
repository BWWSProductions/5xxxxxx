Elektronikus szotarak (MoBiMouse)

A MorphoLogic 1993 ota foglalkozik professzionalis elektronikus szotarak keszitesevel.

A MoBiMouse szotari rendszer 1999-ben elnyerte az Europai Unio Informaciotechnologiai Dijat, valamint 2004-ben az elmult evtized egyik leginnovativabb IST-dijas technologiaja dijat!

A szotarak a legismertebb hazai kiadok (pl. Akademiai, Adecom, Grimm, Szak) nyomtatott szotarainak digitalis valtozatai, es a sajat fejlesztesu MoBiMouse-technologiaval, XML-formatumban keszulnek.

A MoBiMouse 6 mukodtetesehez szukseges operacios rendszer: Windows XP, Vista, Windows 7 (a program minden operacios rendszer 64 bites verziojat is tamogatja).

Ez a programkiadas a teljes, mar aktivalt verziot tartalmazza. Emellett tartalmaz meg plugint FireFox/ThunderBird es Acrobat Reader programokhoz, amelyek a jobb karakterfelismerest segitik a kepernyorol.

Electronic Dictionaries

MorphoLogic has been dealing with the production of electronic dictionaries since 1994. These are digitized versions of the printed dictionaries published by the best known Hungarian publishing houses (e.g. Akademiai, Adecom, Grimm, Szak).

In 1999 the MoBiMouse dictionary system was awarded the European IST Prize, and in 2004 the Prize for one of the most innovative IST-awarded technology.

Their dictionaries use the self-developed MoBiMouse Plus technology and XML format.

The dictionary programs require Windows 98, Windows NT 4.0, Windows 2000 or Windows XP operating systems.

This release contains the full version (activated) version of the dictionary. Also contains plugins for FireFox/ThunderBird and Adobe Acrobat Reader, which helps the character recognition on the screen.
