State of Shock is a Canadian-based band from Vancouver, British Columbia. They made their debut in June 2004 with their album Guilty by Association.

Life, Love & Lies 2007



Life, Love & Lies is the second studio album by Canadian rock band State of Shock. The album includes their single, "Money Honey". Life, Love & Lies was produced by Canadian Producer Jeff Dawson. The photography on the album was done by Erich Saide. The second single was announced on the band's official website on October 4, 2007 as "Hearts That Bleed". In 2008 State of Shock re-released the album in the U.S. This version included both a brand new song titled "When Did The Love Leave" and a re-recorded version of "Rollin", a song from their previous album Guilty By Association.
