Ivor Agyeman-Duah, author and development policy analyst, takes us through this three part TV series.

Part 1
STRUCTURES AND INSTITUTIONS IN A POST-COLONIAL ECONOMY
Ivor takes us through how the colonial economy changed to post-colonial under Kwame Nkrumah and the consequences of this.

Part 2
A VAMPIRE ECONOMY
Looks at the role of policy in Ghana's post colonial world.

Part 3
CROSSING THE JORDAN: STIMULATION AND INNOVATION IN THE ECONOMY
This part is about the growth of Ghana's financial and private sectors and challenges.
