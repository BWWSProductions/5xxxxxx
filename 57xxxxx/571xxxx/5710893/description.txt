erick kvist and emma sarius - pink taco soundtrack

pink taco soundtrack was composed and recorded by erick kvist and emma sarius using only a casio casiotone CT-660. the music is intended as a soundtrack to an exhibition called pink taco, which took place in gallery hangups, stockholm, sweden 2009. the project was organized by per stenborg and roger von reybekiel. originally released as a limited edition CDR (50 copies).
