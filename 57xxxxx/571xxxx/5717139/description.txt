1. "The Best Policy" features ex-M*A*S*H man Gary Burgoff in 
a mild-manneredrole not much different than all his years 
playing Radar.

Deborah Harmon plays his wife and Logan Ramsey is his boss.

The story is about a man who gets into big trouble on the job
when a co-worker is killed and he is the main suspect.


2. "The Last Bottle in the World" guest stars Anthony Quayle
as an aging wine expert who is challenged by another man.
