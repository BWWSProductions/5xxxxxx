Inspired by a true story, this Lifetime Original Movie is the story of Samantha Horton, a former Texas homecoming queen and physical therapist.

When both she and her husband, Rex, are laid off from their jobs, Samantha accepts an offer to work at a massage studio in a nearby town, not realizing that the other staff members are actually prostitutes who service a clientele of wealthy and powerful businessmen...
