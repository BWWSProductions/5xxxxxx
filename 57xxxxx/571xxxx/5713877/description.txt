For the 25th anniversary of Disney on Ice in Japan, BoA has been commissioned to perform the anniversary song. Woo Weekend, the R&B-dance singer's 31st Japanese single, is a catchy, upbeat dance song that's meant to make listeners stand up and dance. The single also comes with the B-side track No Dance No Life.
