The "War, Famine, Conquest and Death" demo was recorded before the "Blood In The Water" EP. it was a free recording made on a BlackBerry Storm. Though th recordings are rough, it captures the first song ever written or recorded by the band (2012), and it captures the raw energy underneath.

Vocals- Dom Zombie
Guitars- Whitey Quick

Tracklisting:
1. Call Of The Dead
2. Contagium
3. 2012
4. Sweet River
5. I Don't Wanna Die
6. War, Famine, Conquest and Death
7. Killer On The Road
