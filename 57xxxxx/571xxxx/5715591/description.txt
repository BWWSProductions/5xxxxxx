The single for "In The Rain" was released with two extra tracks. The album, recorded at NekroStudios, features Randall Lance of Altered Ending on guitar and backing vocals on track 1. Track 2 is a Beck cover.

Guitar, Harmonica & Vocals- Dom Zombie
Guitar & Backing Vocals- Randall Lance
Guitar- Whitey Quck

Tracklisting:
1. In The Rain
2. Cancelled Check (Beck Cover)
3. Lost Souls
