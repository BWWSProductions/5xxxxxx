"Chess Tips for the Improving Player" provides practical advice on how to improve at chess. It is aimed at an audience above-beginner level. This book is for readers who are familiar with the basic ideas and want to take the next step to chess mastery.
About the Author
Amatzia Avni is an Israeli psychologist who is a FIDE Master of both chess playing and chess puzzle composition. He has written several original and critically acclaimed books. This is his first book for Quality Chess.
