Medication Club is an ambient project from Kristiansund, Norway.
The project consist of Vidar Skaget and Atle Kristoffer Olsen, who has been creating electronic music together in different bands / projects since the early 90's.
This project came to life early 2010 and resulted in this EP which was made available for free download at bandcamp.com the 6th of June 2010.

Tracklist:

01 - Reset
02 - Advances
03 - Interference #1
04 - Fear
05 - Solar Plexus
06 - Freak Science (Bonus Track)
