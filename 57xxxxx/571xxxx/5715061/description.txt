How to Train Your Dragon (2010) 

Directors:Dean DeBlois & Chris Sanders
Writers:Cressida Cowell & William Davies 
Release Date: 31 March 2010 
Genre: Animation/Adventure/Comedy/Family/Fantasy 
Plot
Long ago up North on the Island of Berk, the young Viking, Hiccup, wants to join his town's fight 
against the dragons that continually raid their town. However, his macho father and village leader, 
Stoik the Vast, will not allow his small, clumsy, but inventive son to do so. Regardless, 
Hiccup ventures out into battle and downs a mysterious Night Fury dragon with his invention, 
but can't bring himself to kill it....
