This pack includes:
ykhwong's DOSBox CVS build 0.72 (09/28/2008)
Blake Stone Aliens of Gold - Registered Version 3.0 (pre-installed)
Blake Stone Planet Strike - Registered Version 1.0 (pre-installed)


Use the batch files to play the desired game:


To play "Blake Stone Aliens of Gold" run:
Play Blake Stone Aliens of Gold.bat

To play "Blake Stone Aliens of Gold" with scale2x filter run:
Play Blake Stone Aliens of Gold (scale2x).bat

To play "Blake Stone Aliens of Gold" with hq2x filter run:
Play Blake Stone Aliens of Gold (hq2x).bat


To play "Blake Stone Planet Strike" run:
Play Blake Stone Planet Strike.bat

To play "Blake Stone Planet Strike" with scale2x filter run:
Play Blake Stone Planet Strike (scale2x).bat

To play "Blake Stone Planet Strike" with hq2x filter run:
Play Blake Stone Planet Strike (hq2x).bat
