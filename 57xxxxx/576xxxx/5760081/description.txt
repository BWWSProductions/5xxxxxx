One of the Boys is the second studio album by American singer-songwriter Katy Perry and her major-label  debut. Her change in record label and adoption of a pseudonymous surname accompanies a notable shift from the Christian gospel rock of her self-titled debut album. The album was released in the United States and Canada on June 17 2008 and has sold over 7 million copies worldwide up to June 2010. The album features the Billboard Hot 100 number one single "I Kissed a Girl," top 3 single "Hot n Cold," top 10 single "Waking Up in Vegas", and top 30 single "Thinking of You".
