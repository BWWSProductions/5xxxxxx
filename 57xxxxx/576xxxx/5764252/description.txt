Magnet is the name for musical performer Norwegian born Even Johansen.  His music can be categorized as Alternative/ Folk Rock/ Indie and he's quite popular in Norway and even in Europe, but still relatively unknown in North America.  His songs have been played on TV shows and movies, and On Your Side was his first full length album and received critical acclaim.
