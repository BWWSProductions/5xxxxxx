"Love the Way You Lie" is the second single by American rapper Eminem from his seventh studio album, Recovery. The song features vocals from Barbadian singer Rihanna and is produced by Alex da Kid. Additional vocal production on Rihanna's vocals were provided by Makeba Riddick.[1] The song gave Eminem his fourth US Hot 100 number-one hit and Rihanna  her seventh. It also claimed the top spot on eight other charts. "Love the Way You Lie" has currently been number one on the US Billboard Hot 100 for four consecutive weeks.[2]

The song won its first music award at the Teen Choice Awards 2010, in the Best Rap/Hip-Hop Track category.[3]
