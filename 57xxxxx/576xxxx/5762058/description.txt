At fifty, Pippa Lee positively glows with female serenity, the devoted wife of a 
brilliant publisher thirty years her senior, proud mother of successful twins 
and a lovely and adored friend and neighbor. But, when her husband spontaneously 
decides that they should leave New York for a retirement home as a "pre-emptive 
strike against decrepitude," and has an affair with someone even younger than
