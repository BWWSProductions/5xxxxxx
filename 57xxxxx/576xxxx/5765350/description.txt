Guest stars Edmond O' Brien, Lloyd Bochner, 
Bartlett Robinson, Jill Donohue, John Hoyt,

A little-known Asian actor and singer named 
Aki Aleong plays entreprenuer Ah Sing. 
According to Wikipedia, he had a role on 
"Babylon 5" in the 1990s and he's still active 
in show business at age 75. Ironically his 
real first name is "Assing" !
