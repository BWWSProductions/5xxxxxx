This is an *unedited* video of Roger Hodgson's (former Supertramp member) rock concert, played in Belo Horizonte, Minas Gerais, Brazil, on May 15th 2010.

It was taken by myself at the audience, using an Samsung SMX-C10 camcorder. It's obviously far from perfect, there may be some glitches here and there, but I think it's a quite nice remembrance of the show.

It's just a fan thing that I wanted to share with other Roger's fans. I hope you'll like it. Oh, by the way, when the band was playing the very last song ("It's Raining Again") the camcorder ran out of batteries, so sorry for this one. :-)
