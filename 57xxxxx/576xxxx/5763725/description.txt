Daniela Carpio`s first single TIMBOALIZE, can be found in her debut album SUPER. She mixes her sweet vocals with sounds from the 70`s 80`s made famous by bands like Pink Floyd, Depeche Mode, Erasure, Madonna and others. Influenced by Timbaland, the production was completely recorded @ Citric Music in Guatemala, and was co-produced by Daniela Carpio, Eduardo Santella and Giancarlo Puccini. The lyrics to Timbaolize, were written by Daniela Carpio and Giancarlo Puccini...
