Information:
Ghost Hunters Academy is a paranormal reality television series which
premiered November 11, 2009, on the SyFy network. It is the third spin-off
series based on Ghost Hunters (after Ghost Hunters International and UFO Hunters).
It features TAPS members Steve Gonsalves and Dave Tango who lead a group of
college students on various ghost hunting cases through locations that are allegedly haunted.
