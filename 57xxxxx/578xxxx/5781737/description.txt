\"The Herbaliser is a jazz rap band formed by Jake Wherry and Ollie Teeba in London, England during the early 1990s. Although currently signed to !K7 Records, they were one of the most well-known acts from the Ninja Tune independent record label. They have released 8 LPs, including two DJ mixes: one for Ninja Tune\'s Solid Steel series and the other for Fabric\'s Live Mix series. The Herbaliser\'s album Same As It Never Was was their first release on !K7 Records. Their latest album, Herbal Tonic was release in June 2010.\"
