When her grandmother dies Alice Cohen receives her house as part of her inheritance.  Behind a walled off section of the basement Alice finds a golem created in the 1930's. The creature viciously hunts those who prey on the innocent.

Written by Justin Gray and Jimmy Palmiotti (Jonah Hex, Power Girl). Phil Winslade (Goddess, Nevada) was the penciller/inker for most issues with Tomm Coker (Agents of Atlas) and Peter Snejberg illustrating the other issues.
