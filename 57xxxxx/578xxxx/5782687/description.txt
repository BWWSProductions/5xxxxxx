This is Basically fedora 13 Live installable Dvd, with all the latest updates customized by Dangermouse, aimed at media for backing up cd's,Dvd's and photo management etc..

Please check the laws in your country as this contains third party codecs and may be illegal in your country.

This does not have Adobe flash plugin installed and uses the open source alternative gnash, for legal reasons.
This also does not have libdvdcss installed (codec for playng dvd's that does not require regions to be set).
Both of the above can be easily installed for you using Autoten to do it for you.(Menu>>Applications>>System Tools>>Autoten)
