It\'s 1942 and the seemingly unstoppable Wermarcht is on the march.  As the Nazis swallow more and more of Europe, Hitler\'s scientists perfect a mechanism that could guarantee his ultimate victory!  The U.S. Army has only one place to turn to stop this from happening: the mysterious unit known as the Lost Squad!
