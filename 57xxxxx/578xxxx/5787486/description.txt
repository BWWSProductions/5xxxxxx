Christiane F. (born Vera Christiane Felscherinow on May 20, 1962) is a former heroin addict famous for her contribution to the autobiographical book Wir Kinder vom Bahnhof Zoo, and this film "Christiane F" based on the book, which describes her struggle with various forms of drug addiction during her teens.

A realistic and cautionary tale of teenage heroin addiction. If you are a parent or teacher and want to show your children the reality of heroin, this is the film to show.
