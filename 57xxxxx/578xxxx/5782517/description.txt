A celebrity based panel show for Radio 2, hosted by Claudia Winkleman.

Each week three professionally witty gossipmongers compete. 

There are no teams, it's every man for himself, in rounds designed to unleash a torrent of celebrity-fuelled opinion.

The questions don't require a factual answer: they simply act as springboards for players to unleash a stream of caustic, witty and occasionally unhinged opinion. Claudia decides which gossipmonger she deems to be the funniest.
