A hilarious story of Buster and Butkus, two bums who by a twist of fate become the proud owners of a forty foot Pace Arrow RV. With no money, and fewer brains, they wind their way through a series of adventures involving some of the true characters of the inner city streets.

This movie was unable to find a distributor, so it only aired on Bravo Cable network once, and remains un-seen until here and now!
