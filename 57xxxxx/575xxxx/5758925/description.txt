William Ion Belton Crealock
Vagabonding Under Sail
Hastings House, 1951
304 pp

Bill Crealock, Len Greenwood, Don Hodge, and Ernest Chamberlain sail from Falmouth, England to Spain, Portugal, Morocco, The Canary Islands, British Guiana, Trinidad, Haiti, the Florida Keys, and up the east coast of the United States.
