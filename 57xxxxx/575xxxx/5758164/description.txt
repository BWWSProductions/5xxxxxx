Anthony Gomes (born 1970, Toronto, Ontario) is a Canadian blues and blues-rock guitarist and singer. He was born to a Portuguese father and a French-Canadian mother. After his 1997 debut album release Blues in Technicolor he began touring the United States and Canada and he has since recorded eight more albums.
Gomes resides in Chicago playing a fusion of blues with rock and soul, and with the Anthony Gomes Band has toured North America and Europe.
