A Beautiful Exchange is the nineteenth album in the live praise and worship series of Christian Contemporary music by Hillsong Church. [1]  [2]  It was recorded at the Sydney Entertainment Centre and Hillsong Church in Australia by Darlene Zschech and the Hillsong Worship Team. The album was released in the United States on 29 June 2010 in partnership with EMI. [1]


These worship songs explore themes including sacrifice and redemption ("A Beautiful Exchange"), love ("Our God Is Love") and hope ("Forever Reign", which is also the first radio single). Hillsong Live albums are led by worship leaders including Darlene Zschech, Reuben Morgan and Joel Houston. Other key songs include: "Believe" (sung by Darlene Zschech) and "The One Who Saves" (sung by Ben Fielding).


This album has reached number 4 on the US iTunes albums chart and on July 7, 2010 it reached number 1 on the Australian iTunes albums chart. On July 9, 2010 it debuted at number 1 on the US Billboard Christian Albums Chart and at number 40 on the US Billboard 200 chart. During the initial week on the charts it remained in the top 3.
