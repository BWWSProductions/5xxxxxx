The Amory Wars, originally called the Bag.On.Line Adventures, is a series of comic books written by Coheed and Cambria frontman Claudio Sanchez and published by Evil Ink Comics. The storyline used in The Amory Wars is also the focus of the band's music, each album released by the band representing a corresponding chapter in the four part science fiction concept originally narrated by Sanchez's lyrics.
