HAUNT
ISSUE 8 | JULY 2010
 	 
STORY 	Robert Kirkman
LETTERS 	Richard Starkings
COVER 	Greg Capullo
COLORS 	Fco Plascencia
INKS 	Jonathon Glapion, Todd McFarlane
PENCILS 	Greg Capullo

His brother died, his ghost haunts him, it bonds with him to form the creature known as Haunt - and all this has dragged poor Daniel Kilgore deep into a dark and dangerous world of espionage and intrigue he never even knew existed. Now it appears these events have taken a much greater toll on him than even he realizes.
