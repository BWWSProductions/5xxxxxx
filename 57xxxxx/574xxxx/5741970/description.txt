In our modern day culture, we have used the word "grace" as such a common and familiar term that it's lost the significance and relevance it once held in understanding the true power that grace holds.
 
In this series, Pastor Joseph Price will share with you how grace isn't just a topic or word in Christianity but it IS Christianity! You'll gain insigh and revelation as you understand the amazing power of grace and how it is a foundation truth to enable you to walk in victory and success in every area of your life!
