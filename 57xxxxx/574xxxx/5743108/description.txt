On 26-06-2010 Italy witnessed another step of the hardcore 
evolution. More than 5.000 fanatics gathered and made the Masters 
of Hardcore - The Outdoor Mayhem become a statement of the 
international hardcore scene.

For years there have been a unique connection between Holland and 
Italy, and this year this powerful line of defense strengthen 
even more. Fusing all resources with one unified goal in mind, 
Italy proved its power.
