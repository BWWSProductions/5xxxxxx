            Warhammer: Mark of Chaos puts the Focus squarely on the      
            battlefield, with a Real-time combat system that gives you

            Vampires, and Goblins) clash on a massive battlefield.

            Workshop's renowned fantasy world, where you will be able to 
            control armies that include the all-new Dark Elf and Orcs &  
            Goblins forces - as they battle for supremacy in a world torn
            apart by constant warfare.

            game's tactical foundation to offer new units for each army

            will be able to experience the mysterious  sorcery of the    
            Dark Elves and the savage brutality of the Orcs and Goblins  
            in an all-new chapter of the game's single player campaign   
            as they battle to overcome an invasion by the armies of Chaos
