Language: English


In this third-person action game based on Sam Raimi's blockbuster movie, players assume the role of Peter Parker and his alter-ego Spider-man to defend the city from the clutches of evil. The game starts off with Peter in his wrestling costume, but soon as has players learn and master all-new combat moves, webslinging and dizzying airborne acrobatics. Move through stunning scenes from the movie as you explore enormous city environments with newfound maneuverability. Battle the world's toughest villains, including Shocker, Vulture, and the Green Goblin. The game features unlockable secrets like mini games, new costumes and DVD extra features.
