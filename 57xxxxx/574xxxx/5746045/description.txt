A classic recording in Southern Gospel Music now available on DVD!  Recording combines the Kingsmen Quartet and the Gold City Quartet on stage together!  Directed by Jim Hamill and includes favorites like Ivan Parker, Garry Shepherd, Brian Free, Ray Reese, Tim Riley and many others! 

Songs Include:

Everybody Will Be Happy, Under Control, Winging My Way Back Home, Just A Little Talk With Jesus, Learning To Lean, Someone To Care, What A Day That Will Be, How Beautiful Heaven Must Be, Glory Road, Glory Road Encore, Amazing Grace, How Great Thou Art and How Great Thou Art Encore
