
A real estate developer named Dan Sanders (Brendan Fraser) is given the task to develop a forest into a development by his boss Neal Lyman (Ken Jeong) much to the objections of Dans son Tyler (Matt Prokop) and his wife Tammy (Brooke Shields). Unfortunately, the animals who live in the forest dont intend to take the loss of their home lying down, and instead decide to turn the tables on Dan. Tammy is planing an eco friendly fair and Dans comapny decides to sponsor it.The Sanders family later finds out that Dans company is not eco friendly and wants to cut down the forest to build houses.
