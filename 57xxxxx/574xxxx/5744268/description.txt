A self-proclaimed anarchist is one of many young people caught up in the moral conflict of personal freedom versus their responsibility to society and its needs. As Japan emerges from centuries under a feudal system, youthful rebellion and free thinking are the main concerns of many students. Past and present tense are combined and the main character is stabbed by his mistress. He begins to feel lost in the changing political and social climate of the times.
