Starcast : Vinu Mohan, Vishnu Priya, Kalabhavan Mani
Description : Keralotsavam 2009 movie tells Padmanabhan Embranthiri was running an institition 'Kalanikethan' . He was invited to perform Kerala art forms on the stage of Keralotsavam 2009. His daughter Ganga (Vishnu Priya) was doing research in Mohiniyaattam. So she was entrusted in doing all the arrangements of the programe.Sandeep Subramanyam (Vinu Mohan) arrives there to perform Kalaripayattu. There he meets Ganga and fell in love.
