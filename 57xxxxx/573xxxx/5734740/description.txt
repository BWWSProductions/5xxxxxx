This amusing program creates at a touch of a finger very realistic fake cracks, holes and the burnt tracks on the screen your favourite Phone. With its help you can play friends and acquaintances imaginary breakage of the device added with realistic sounds of damages. And that draw has not gone too far, you easily can calm again the friends, simply having shake your device. After that all images of fake damages will instantly disappear - and you Phone again will be brand new, shining and beautiful!
Also by means of this application you can always decorate with images of imaginary cracks, holes and burnt traces any picture at your choice and as it is easy to clear it then. Have fun!
Modes of drawing of imaginary damages:
- The mode "Cracks": creates images of cracks by pressing by a finger the screen. Images are cleaned if to shake the device.
- The mode "Big Bang": creates the image of the big punched hole in the device screen by pressing by a finger the screen. Images are cleaned if to shake the device.
- The mode "Flame Strike": creates the image of the burnt trail of fire remaining behind your finger at carrying out it on the screen. Images are cleaned if to shake the device.
