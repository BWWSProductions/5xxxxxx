Seven different planets
================================================
World ROSE consists of seven vast planets. Each planet has its own set of particular characteristics and environments.
Interplanetary explorers will enjoy a variety of adventures, which this world can offer.
================================================
Interplanetary Battle
================================================
Players can form teams and compete for control of the seven planets. Unlike a small scale war, such as war guild where players fight for possession of the castle, players can take part in the great war in which at stake the fate of an entire planet. Once you become the king of the planet, you will have the political and economic leadership. Anyone will be able to take control of all the seven planets will receive the greatest power in the universe.

Players can create their own story of the legend.
================================================
Quests in the world of ROSE are all connected with the social life of players. Each change in the game, such as the price of things, the rate of spawn monsters, monster figure (MVP) or the weather - all this under the influence of the players.
Each main evet, which takes place on 7 planets is recorded on the official site. Charov names who have played key roles in these events are displayed on the official website that their legendary cases were recorded for posterity.
================================================
Controlling functions of NPC.
================================================
Factions differ from Clans because a society ruled by faction NPC and not the players.
Combining fractions, consumers can participate in various quests. Each faction has a certain length of leadership in economics, politics, public order, magic, art and religion. Each faction has its own alliances and hostilities.
================================================
Economy of supply and demand.
================================================
Materials for creating items are distributed in different areas. Consequently, cities in different areas have different prices for materials. As a result, when the city has too much of one material, the price of this material goes down automatically, and when the amount of a specific material is lower than expected, immediately activated the quests of the city, which contributes to, the collection of material to neutralize the city's economy. Consumers may have an advantage over this system of economy by buying things at a low price in one town and selling it at a higher price in another city.
