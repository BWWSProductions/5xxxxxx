Illicit lays down his debut release for USA with an effortless blend of uplifting Drum & Bass and Dubstep.

Lisa Fitzgibbon steps up to deliver a beautiful vocal for the title track Alchemy, adding mainstream appeal whilst maintaining dance floor credibility.


With DJ support from:

Vegas (Bad Company & Blokhe4d) / Xample / Simon Bassline Smith 
Nicky Black Market / Brockie / Q-BiK / Drunken Masters (Dope Ammo)
