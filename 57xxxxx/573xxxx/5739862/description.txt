Artist: Radical Project
Title: Astro Zombies / Storm


Fast becoming one of the UK's hottest Electronic acts, Radical Project deliver their second single for USA in the shape of Dance Floor Drum & Bass smashers Astro Zombies and Storm.

Fusing their trademark style of mind bending synth melodies and heavy bass lines, the Radical Project boys continue to assault the airwaves with maximum sonic chaos.

With recent DJ support from the likes of Dr P, Vulgatron (Foreign Beggars) and Chasing Shadows the future looks bright for Radical Project!
