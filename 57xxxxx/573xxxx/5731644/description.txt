The Only Easy Day Was Yesterday is an EP from the American rock band 12 Stones. It was released on July 20, 2010 by Wind-up Records and debuted at #103 on the Billboard 200.

Originally the band was going to release a full album but due to their record label, they were forced to release an EP. It's a 5 track EP which contains the single "We Are One".

Track listing

   1. "Welcome To The End" 
   2. "We Are One" 
   3. "Disappear" 
   4. "Tomorrow Comes Today" 
   5. "Enemy"

 Singles

   1. "We Are One"
