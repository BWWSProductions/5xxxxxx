Disks: 2 x 2,88mb Date: August 01, 2010

Release Description:

With Power Screen Capture, you can capture screen from your desktop with
high quality. Besides, it offers you the function of capturing video and
image from other devices, DV and TV tuner. A list displays all the files
captured by Power Screen Capture, and then you can view, copy, delete
and rename these files freely and easily! Various settings let you set
video compression codec, audio compression codec, output frame size and
frame rate. You can also set properties of each capture device, preview
and record real-time video, watch TV (TV Tuner is installed), add water
mark or texts on video or image, etc

Installation:

Just unpack&install. Register with the serial in serial.txt
