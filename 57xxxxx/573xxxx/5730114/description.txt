                                      Iron Sight


                                                            
Iron Sight offers a unique blend of action, strategy and simulation in a real 3D. The basic premise is deceivingly simple: destroy the enemy before he destroys you! Attack with a large and varied range of weapons and power-ups: homing missiles, 
artillery, satellite support, cluster missiles and much more.
