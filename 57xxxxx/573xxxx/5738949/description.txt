iMedia Converter for Mac is an all-in-one converter that supports DVD ripping and video conversion. It lets you rip DVDs and convert videos directly for use with iPad, iPod, iPhone 4, Apple TV, or other devices. Easily convert DVDs and videos to almost any format of your choice, including WMV, AVI, MP4, MOV, FLV, etc. Fully support camcorder video files like AVCHD (MTS/M2TS) and JVC (MOD/TOD). iMedia Converter 2.0 is specifically designed for Mac OS X 10.5 Leopard and 10.6 Snow Leopard.
