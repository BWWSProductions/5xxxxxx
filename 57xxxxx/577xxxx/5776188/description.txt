Lotus Juice is a rapper hailing from Japan. Those of you who know him at all probably recognize him from his work in the Persona 3 & Persona Trinity Soul soundtracks. Here's one of his albums for those of you who want to hear more of him, or who just want some J-rap. The songs on Only A Test are mostly in English with some Japanese mixed in. All songs are encoded in 256kbps mp3. Enjoy!
