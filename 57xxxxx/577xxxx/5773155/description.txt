Neozoic explores the possibility that the dinosaurs did not become extinct and that humans are no longer the dominant speicies.  Forced to evolve beside these mighty predators, humanity created huge walled cities in order to survive.  But not all humans decide the live behind the walls.  

Written by Paul Ens (Afterburn) and illustrated by J. Korim and Jessie Lam.
