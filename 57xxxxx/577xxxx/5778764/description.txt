
Heimerinoi Kolymvites is a musical group from the north of Greece. 
Led by Aryiris Bakirtzis, (a professional architect, when not recording and performing) the band released an eponymous first album in 1981 that became a cult hit by virtue of its wild blend of rembetika and gypsy styles and bizarre lyrics.
