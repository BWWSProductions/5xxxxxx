Teach Me How to Dougie is the debut single by American hip-hop group Cali Swag District. The song was written by Corey Fowler, Chanti Glee, Cahron Childs and Ebony West, and produced by RunWay Star. It serves as the lead single from their debut album, The Kickback, scheduled to be released September 14, 2010. The song revolves around the Dougie dance, similar to the jerkin movement. The songs official remix features Red Cafe, Jermaine Dupri, B.o.B. and Bow Wow.
