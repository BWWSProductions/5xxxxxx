With the finanical and human costs of combat in the middle east growing, the United States military has adopted a new weapon... Zombies.  When one of these zombies somehow escapes, a team of elite soldiers must enter hostile territory to stop a growing zombie army.

Created by Kevin Grevioux who wrote the original screenplay for the movie Underworld, and co-created the Underworld franchise.  Art by Geraldo Borges (Nova, Justice League: The Rise of Arsenal) and David Youkovibch (The Broccoli Agenda, Death by Chocolate).
