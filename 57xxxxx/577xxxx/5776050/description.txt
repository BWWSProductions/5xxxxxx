Lights is the debut studio album by English singer-songwriter Ellie Goulding, released in the United Kingdom on 1 March 2010 by Polydor Records. Produced by Starsmith, Frankmusik, Fraser T. Smith and Richard Stannard, the album debuted at number one on the UK Albums Chart selling almost 40,000 copies in its first week.
