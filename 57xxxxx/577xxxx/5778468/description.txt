I have compiled the Afghan War Diary into files compatibal with the UNIX program "fortune". Once you have decompressed the files you can use them like this assuming you have fortune installed and are in the directory that you extraced the folder into:

foo@bar:~$ fortune warfortune
2006-10-03 00:00:00

VBIED (motorcycle) was discovered by ANP in an abandoned house in Baghlan City.  (CJ2 INTSUM, 5 Oct 06)
foo@bar:~$
