From the 9th Season DVD set recently released,
here is the 2 hour season premiere (actually 
97 minutes without the commercials)!

Featuring Khigh Dhiegh, who returns as
Wo Fat!  After this episode he wouldn't be
back again until 4 years later in 1980 in
the series finale "Woe to Wo Fat".

With Richard Denning as the Governor and
guest stars Dina Merrill, David Tomlinson,
and Michael Anderson, Jr.
