Pretty Boy Swag is the first official single released by Soulja Boy Tell Em from his upcoming third studio album The DeAndre Way. The song was produced by G5 Kids. Pretty Boy Swag has peaked at number #1 on Atlanta radio stations Atlanta Radio Hot v103 & Atlanta Radio Hot 107.9, this was confirmed by Soulja Boy Tell Em in a video released on hischannel. It was officially sent to radio on July 20, 2010. It debuted at #90 on the Billboard Hot 100 and has reached since then reached #34.
