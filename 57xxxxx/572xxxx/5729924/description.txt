                          Scan2PDF Mobile 2.0 Version. 2.0.4


Document scanning using your phones camera to convert one or more pages into a PDF. Like having a documentscanner/photocopier/fax machine in your pocket!

PDFs are compressed and enhanced, saved to SD card and can be emailed directly.
