UberStudent (1.0) is a free Linux learning platform for learning, doing, and teaching academic computing at the higher education and advanced secondary levels.

The UberStudent learning platform features several categories of Core Applications:

# Research and Writing - To collect, organize, comprehend, and analyze sources, write and edit properly formatted and cited documents, and professionally present research.
# Study Aids - To organize and elucidate materials for maximum understanding and retention, make connections between ideas, concepts and sources, and draw creative inferences from them.
# Self-Management - To evaluate and be steward over one's time, tasks, personal finances and social networks.

Using its on-board tools, UberStudent can be easily extended for specific academic disciplines.

For full info see http://www.uberstudent.org
