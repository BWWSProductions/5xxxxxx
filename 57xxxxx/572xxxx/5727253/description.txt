For english-only speaking:
This book is in german language. English title would be: Worldoctober - Who plans the socialist world government? (2007). Torsten Mann documents how the events that supposedly brought down the Soviet Untion and communist ideology were all part of a highly sophisticated and long-range plan to merge East with West and bring about a new October revolution - a Worldoctober. Was it all really a cleverly made deception to satisfy the masses who were hungry for freedom? According to the years of research of the author this is exactly what transpired in front of the eyes of millions while all along the architects behind the scenes wanted to establish a socialist-like new world order. 280 pages, some pictures.
