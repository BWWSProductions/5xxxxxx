                          N.O.V.A. (Near Orbit Vanguard Alliance)





Join the fight for mankinds survival against the aggressive aliens known as the judges, in this revolutionary sci-fi first person shooter, Take on 13 levels in single player campaign or play against people with both local and online multiplayer, with global leaderboards. You have 6 weapons to choose from as well as grenades and machine gun turrets and the entire game is full rendered high detailed 3D graphics to boost your gaming experience to a whole new level.
