The Entertainer is the third studio album by English recording artist Alesha Dixon. It is to be released on 29 November 2010 by Asylum Records. The album's lead single is "Drummer Boy", which was released in September. Additionally, the album's second single, "Radio", is scheduled to precede its release by one day. The album was originally planned to be named Unleashed, however the plans were scrapped and The Entertainer was decided upon instead.
