Perumazhakkalam is a 2004 Malayalam film directed by Kamal. 
The film stars Meera Jasmine, Kavya Madhavan, Dileep and Vineeth. The film was adapted to Hindi by Nagesh Kukunoor as Dor.

Kavya Madhavan got the 2004 Kerala State Film Award for Best Actress for her performance the movie. 
The film won the National Film Award for Best Film on Other Social Issues in 2005.
