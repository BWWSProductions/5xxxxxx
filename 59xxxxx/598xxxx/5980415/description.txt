Dead Babies (2000) aka Mood Swingers

Residents prime themselves for both a visit from three Americans and a weekend of copious decadence.

Dead Babies, (also known as Mood Swingers for US release), is a 2000 film directed by William Marsh. It is based on the novel by Martin Amis. Actors include Paul Bettany, Katy Carmichael, Hayley Carr, Charlie Condou, Alexandra Gilbreath.
