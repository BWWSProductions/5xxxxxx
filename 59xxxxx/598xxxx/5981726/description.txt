Metallica - Kill 'Em All [Megaforce Records MRI CD 069]

This is the very first CD pressing of Metallica's debut album "Kill 'Em All" by Megaforce Records. It sounds better than later pressings (and remasters) in my book.

6.  Whiplash
7.  Phantom Lord

9.  Seek And Destroy
10. Metal Militia
