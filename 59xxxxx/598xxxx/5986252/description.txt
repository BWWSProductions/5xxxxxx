Jordi Savall - Les Voix Humaines

Jordi Savall - viola da gamba

An amazing range of music by high Baroque composers including J.S. Bach, Marin Marais, G.P. Telemann, Jean-Baptiste Forqueray, and others, selected by Savall to demonstrate the expressive powers of the gamba. Except for the Bach, all of these pieces are extremely rare--it's great that Savall has taken the time to record them.

Ripped with cdparanoia 10.2 and encoded with FLAC 1.2.1. Enjoy and please seed!
