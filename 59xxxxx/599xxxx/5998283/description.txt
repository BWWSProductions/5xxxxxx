Talk Talk - Life's What You Make It [TopPop 1985].VOB  



HQ very rare tv show promo. AVRO's "TopPop" was the first weekly pop program broadcast on Dutch television. It ran from September 22, 1970 to June 27, 1988. 

"TopPop" was mainly filled with pop artists in the top pop studio, playing to their latest hit. During the years of 1970-1974, 1978-1981 and 1986-1988, the artists were selected by the choice of the Viewer. In the years 1974-1978 and 1981-1986, the National Charts were used instead.

"Life's What You Make It" is a song by the English band Talk Talk. The song was released in 1985 and appears on the album The Colour of Spring; it became a big hit in the UK, peaking at #16, and internationally, reaching the Top 20 in several countries. The track also appeared in the game Grand Theft Auto: Vice City, where it plays on the pop radio station, Flash FM.
