Jonah Hex is a 2010 post-Civil War antihero Western film based on the DC Comics character of the same name.[4] Distributed by Warner Bros., the film is directed by Jimmy Hayward and stars Josh Brolin as the title character, Jonah Hex, and also stars John Malkovich, Michael Fassbender, and Megan Fox. The film was released on June 18, 2010. It was a critical and commercial failure.
