Johann Sebastian Bach - Cello Suites, BWV 1007-1012

Lillian Fuchs - viola

Fuchs was the first to perform these suites on the modern viola. These mid-30s recordings have been re-released on CD by Doremi with minimal noise reduction, so they're a bit rough but also unmarred by over-zealous "correction". Whatever: the performances are so spectacular it hardly matters. Fuchs is really unbelievable.

Ripped with cdparanoia III 10.2 and encoded with FLAC 1.2.1. Enjoy and please seed.
