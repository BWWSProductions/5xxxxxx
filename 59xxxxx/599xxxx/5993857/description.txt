Katalyst Presents: Dusted - Essential Mix Of Rare Groove & Hip Hop Joints.


Sydney based award winning Deejay, Katalyst's double compilation album, 'Dusted'. This album is an essential mix of rare groove and hip hop. A fantastic collection of tunes from Public Enemy, Run DMC, A Tribe Call Quest, through to Nina Simone, Portishead and Sergio Mendes. Universal. 2005.

DUSTED is a mix CD by Australian DJ talent Katalyst.
