Weekly Playboy (Shukan Pureiboi) also known as Shupure or WPB 
is a Japanese weekly magazine published by Shueisha since 1966. 
This magazine is not a regional edition of the American Playboy 
magazine. The Japanese edition of Playboy is published as 
Monthly Playboy (MPB) by Shueisha.
