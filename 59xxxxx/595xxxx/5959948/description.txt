Highlights from Episode 1

Karan is excited to have Abhishek and Aishwarya back on the show. Abhishek reveals to Aishwarya that Karan had played cupid between them. Karan is amazed to hear Aishwarya's bold answer when he quizzes her about her USP. Aishwarya is in splits to know the story behind Karan's "I'm willing to do anything". Abhishek is amazed to hear Aishwarya's witty answer for the question whom she would choose as the Khan of all seasons.
