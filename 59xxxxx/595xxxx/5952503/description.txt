Las Vegas Weekly Magazine. 11-17 November 2010.

Las Vegas Weekly is a weekly newspaper with an emphasis on 
arts, entertainment and popular culture. The Weekly is the 
city's source for an utterly unique, combustible mix of 
embedded nightlife coverage, thoughtful arts and cultural 
criticism, sassy lifestyle features, provocative essays and 
edgy journalism. From the bottle-serviced tables of the 
latest ultra lounge to the front rows of the hottest 
concerts; wherever the movers are shaking, the Weekly 
is there, helping readers make sense of this crazy town.
