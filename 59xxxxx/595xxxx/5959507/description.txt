Glee: The Music, The Christmas Album is the fourth soundtrack album, and first Christmas album, by the cast of the American musical television series Glee. The album was released on November 9, 2010 and accompanies the Christmas episode "Sue the Grinch",planned to air December 7, 2010.Dante DiLoreto and Brad Falchuk serve as the album's executive producers.
