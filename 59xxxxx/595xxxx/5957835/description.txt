We Will Remember Them
Thursday November 11 at 9 pm ET on CBC-TV

Synopsis
As of today, 152 Canadian soldiers have died since Canada entered the war in Afghanistan in 2002. In this documentary, we go beyond the static photographs and news headlines and get to know the men and women who paid the ultimate sacrifice. Meet Canadian fallen soldiers as civilians and see them in the uniforms they were proud to wear.
