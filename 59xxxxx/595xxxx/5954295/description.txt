Bilib Ako Sa'yo (1999)

After losing his job for nearly losing a cargo of gold bars to hold uppers. Gatdula finds himself working as a bouncer in a night club. There, he meets and is smitten by Varga, a college coed forced by poverty to work as a ramp model. But other men also captivated by Varga's charm, and two of them are a cruel general and his equally cruel right hand man, But the two men turn out to be more than just Gatdula's rivals. They happen to be the brains behind the attempted robbery that earlier caused Gatdula his job...

Cast:

Robin Padilla
Joyce Jimenez
