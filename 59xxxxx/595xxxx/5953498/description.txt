Twinkblood 

Eighteen-Year-Old Miles Pride has decided to move to Phoenix to start his first year of college. He settles in, adjusts to the burning heat and the frigid nights; and finds that more than just the cold penetrates the city of the sun when that sun goes down. Follow Miles as he risks everything to discover the truth behind the vampire attacks and the twist that turns his life upside down.
