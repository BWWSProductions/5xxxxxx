A master swordsman is taken captive and his body and soul is incorporated in a fortress for the arrival of the Dark Overlord. What remains is a cyborg ninja: Shiranui. Bent on getting his humanity back and rescuing her princess, he storms the fortress with members of his previous clan.

Futuristic samurais and ninjas with antique Star Wars-like beam weaponry. 


Directed by: Keita Amemiya

Spoken Language: Japanese
Subtitles: English
Running Time: 1:12:14
