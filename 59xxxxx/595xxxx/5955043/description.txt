O Z R I C   T E N T A C L E S
- The Cassette/Tape Era -

These are the self-produced cassette releases (later re-issued on CD) O.T. managed between 1984 and 1989. During that time they weren't signed to any label, hence production quality is inferior compared to their subsequent albums.


1985 - Erpsongs
1985 - Tantric Obstacles
1986 - Live Ethereal Cereal
1986 - There is Nothing
1988 - Sliding Gliding Worlds
1989 - The Bits Between The Bits

original cover art included
