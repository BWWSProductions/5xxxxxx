Major update from last torrent of soundtracks:

I converted every song from 22.5 kHz to 44.1 kHz, as well as did a little EQ job in FL Studio 9 so they sound much, much cleaner. For NFS2SE, I deleted any songs that were repeats of longer songs (hence the shorter playlist), and I made the tracks more linear by taking away unnecessary parts of some songs (you'll know what I mean if you listen), as well as add missing parts from the official versions (like the beginning of Pavlova). For NFS3HP, Aquila and Cetus had missing parts in the album, so I combined my version with those version; all the other songs are complete ("Win" was actually part of the MCO soundtrack and by Matt Ragan, not Saki Kaskas, so I removed it). For NFS4HS, I replaced any shortened game versions of songs with complete songs from various albums, and the NFS4 album for in-house music (like Bring That Beat Back). Enjoy!
