Our World - Europe's Secret Prisons (2010)BBCWorld

An investigative report by BBCWorld's Our World Program on the secret prisons throughout Europe- in places like Poland and Lithuania- that were used int the US War on Terror for the extraordinary rendition of prisoners deemed "enemy combatants", whom were subsequently tortured on European soil in order to "extract information". This program provides an in depth look into th conspiracy to cover up the existence of such prisons and allegations of torture by CIA contractors working in them.
