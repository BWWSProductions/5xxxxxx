Our World - Nigeria - The Legacy Of Empire (2010)BBCWorld

A Britsh Colonialist given the responsibility of cultivating 1000\'s of acres of land  for the British Empire. In this Our  World documentary the team brings John Smith back to what is now Nigeria, a journey in which Smith brings us through his recollections and reflections on his Colonialist past, as well as some observations on the state today.
