PureOS Gnome 1.0 2010 is available.

Features: Kernel 2.6.34 with linux-headers, EXT4 FS now supported in live mode, improved installer (grub + passwords)
Office: Freeplane 1.1, OpenOffice.org 3.2 (Base, Calc, Draw, Impress, Math and Writer) with help
Internet: Iceweasel 3.5, Icedove 3.0 + Lightning, Empathy, Transmission, FileZilla, Kompozer
Multi-media: Gnome MPlayer, Banshee, Brasero
Graphics: Gimp 2.6 with help, Simple Scan, Eye of Gnome, Evince
System tools: Gparted, GUFW, Nautilus actions for modules management, smxi/sgfxi scripts...
Choose the 'locale' for NTFS partitions mounting and choose the keyboard from the boot menu or with the bootcodes 'keyb' (keyboard) and 'kbvar' (keyboard variant).

Thanks to tiheum for the Equinox GTK theme and the Faenza icon theme, to john007 for the Faenza-Cupertino icons, to applesactually for the Influx wallpaper and to the beta-testers and contributors.
