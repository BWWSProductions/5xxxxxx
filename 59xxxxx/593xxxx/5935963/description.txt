- English 

Interview with world renown cult director Shinya Tsukamoto. This interview covers how and when he started creating his own world of cinema, exactly the way he thought it should look. He is also an accomplished actor appearing in many films directed by other directors, such as Takashi Miike (Ichi the Killer). The interview includes very rare footage of never released films , his TV commercials and rare footage of live shows, like "The Adventure of Denchu Kozo" ,at the Kaijyu Theater in Tokyo.
