This charming collection features 7 classic Disney cartoons from the 30's and 40's. Two of which however are not even Christmassy. Unless you count a parade of walking biscuits. "The Cookie Carnival" The other unrelated christmas short is "Ferdinand the Bull"

The delights are "Santas Workshop" (1932) where its Christmas Eve and Santa and his elves are preparing the toys for Santa to deliver.
It also contains its sequel "The Night Before Christmas" (1933) where we see Santa deliver the presents to a house and the toys come alive in this enchanting animation.

Another delight is "Pluto's Christmas Tree" where Mickey's newly decorated christmas tree is infested with those mischevious chipmunks Chip 'n' Dale.

Also included is "On Ice" (1935) where Mickey shows off for Minnie during some ice skating, but must rescue Donald when he's nearly blown over a frozen waterfall. Meanwhile, Goofy tries a new form of ice fishing.

And the final Christmas themed cartoon is "Donalds Snow Fight" (1942) in which Donald's ends up in a sledding "accident", which results in an all out snow-war between Donald and his nephews.
