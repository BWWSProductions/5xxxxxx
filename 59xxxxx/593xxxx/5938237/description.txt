Some Kind of Trouble is the upcoming third studio album from English singer-songwriter and soft rock musician James Blunt. The album will be released on November 8, 2010, in the United Kingdom.The album's lead single, "Stay the Night", was released on October 25, 2010.The album was recorded in Blunt's private recording studio in Los Angeles. The album will be available in two editions - a standard, general edition, and a deluxe edition, exclusive to Play.com, which features an exclusive slipcase and Blunt's autograph on the cover.Blunt will begin promoting the album with a new tour, due to start in 2011.
