"Rock This Town" is a song by the Stray Cats from their UK debut album Stray Cats. Its first US Release was on the 1982 album Built for Speed. It peaked at #9 on the Billboard Hot 100 in 1982, becoming the first of the band's three top ten hits in the U.S. It had also previously reached this position on the UK Singles Chart in 1981.
