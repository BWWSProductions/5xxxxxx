Some say three is the magic number. You've got the past, the present and the future. The heart, the brain and the body. And now with the help of legendary filmmaker Taylor Steele, Billabong brings you Trilogy. The Andy, Parko and Taj motion picture. All three of a kind, raised in completely different backgrounds, with totally unique styles. And yet they all the share the recognition of having redefined the parameters of modern day surfing.
