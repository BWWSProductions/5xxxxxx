Kings of Leon join Jools in the studio for the eighth live show of this series.They
perform tunes from their new album, Come Around Sundown, the follow-up to their
multi-platinum award winning breakthrough Only By the Night.

Eric Clapton plays tracks from his current album Clapton, which sees him paying
tribute to the songs that inspired him early in his career.

MIA also joins Jools, with a couple of songs from her recent long player. Brand
new UK band The Vaccines perform, with their blend of Beach Boys meets The
Ramones pop tunes. There's also English folk band Bellowhead, the eleven piece
band bought together by John Spiers and Jon Boden. Plus a debut performance
from Jonathan Jeremiah, the London born singer songwriter who has been gaining
comparisons with Cat Stevens.
