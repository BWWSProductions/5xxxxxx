Vijf pratende puppy's trotseren de vrieskou om mee te doen aan een hondensleerace door Alaska. Ze, moeten alles op alles zetten om de race te winnen.

The movie follows the story of Buddy and Molly's offspring in Washington. After saying goodbye to their owners one morning, the puppies decide to play hide-and-seek when Budderball finds an ice-cream truck. Rosebud spots the hapless canine and follows him in an attempt to help. Hilarity ensues when Buddha finds them both in the truck and B-Dawg and Mud-Bud end up in the truck which is shipping to outside the town of Ferntiuktuk, Alaska.
