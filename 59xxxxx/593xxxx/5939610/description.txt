Shutterbug Magazine. Decembar 2010.

Perfect for the amateur photographer, Shutterbug provides 
tons of advice on how to use the best techniques and 
equipment to get brilliant and exciting photos both 
indoors and out! Each issue includes reports of hands-on 
camera, lens, and film tests, news on the latest products, 
including digital imaging equipment, profiles of renowned 
photographers, and insider tips from the pros on creating 
special visual effects that change good photos into 
stunning images.

Enjoy.
