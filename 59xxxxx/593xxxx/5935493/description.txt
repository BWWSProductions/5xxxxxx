Those of You with or Without Children, You'll Understand is a 1986 album by Bill Cosby. It was his first of two for Geffen Records and the first album to be produced by his wife.

Recorded at The Syria Mosque, Pittsburgh, PA, and The Riverfront Coliseum, Cincinnati, OH.

Won 1986 Grammy Award for Best Comedy Recording

1. Genesis (18:02) 
2. The Great Quote (4:10) 
3. Window of Life (26:56)
