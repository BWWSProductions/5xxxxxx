
                          Whip My Hair

                        Single by Willow

"Whip My Hair" is a song by American recording artist Willow Smith. Smith wrote the lyrics while rookie producer Jukebox handled the music. The song serves as her debut single from her yet-to-be-titled debut release. While attending events, Smith received media attention for her extravagant outfits compared to Rihanna, whom she later credited as a musical influence. Coincidentally leaked on the same day as Rihanna's "Only Girl (In the World)", the song set media outlets abuzz, being covered by Time, Billboard, and CNN the day of its release.
