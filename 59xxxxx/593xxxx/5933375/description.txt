Golden Earring - Back Home [alternate promo].VOB



HQ very rare alternate promo music video.  "Back Home" is a song from the 
album Golden Earring, a self-titled album by Dutch hard rock band Golden 
Earring, released in 1970. It is also known under the title Wall of Dolls. 
This is the first Golden Earring album with drummer Cesar Zuiderwijk 
as a member. This lineup would be kept intact for 40 years.
