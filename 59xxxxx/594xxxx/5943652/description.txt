Australia's national wine buying guide and magazine. 
Published bi-monthly. Includes reviews, tasting 
notes and informational articles.

Launched in 1978, Winestate is one of the worlds longest 
running wine magazines, featuring over 140 pages on all 
things vinous from around the globe. Winestate tastes and 
rates over 10,000 wines a year, making Winestate the 
leading authority on Australian and New Zealand wines.
