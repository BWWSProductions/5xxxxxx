An addon for Rock Band 2 DLC PC Tools 1.3 that gives you access to download the entire 6th gen of DLC (For Rock Band 3) to your PC for easy management

Also has an option to create an unlock WAD for the 6th Gen (sZJE) which works on both NTSC and PAL versions of Rock Band 3 and marks all DLC including the Rock Band 2 export pack and Green Day export pack as Purchased.

To access the Green Day export pack, go to Manage Songs
To access the Rock Band 2 export pack go to Redeem Code > Previously Redeemed Codes

*Please read the instructions included in the readme*


Tested using Uloader 5.1E with Hermes cIOS 223 v5
