Package of languages WORKING on final version of Football Manager 2011.

All language files were tested.

Also should be noticed that are some words still not translated.

Languages available:

- Czech
- Danish
- Dutch
- English
- English US
- French
- Italian
- Norwegian
- Portuguese
- Russian
- Spanish
- Swedish

INSTRUCTIONS:

- Extract and copy the content to the folder:
~Football Manager 2011/data/languages/

- Open the game, go to "Preferences", choose your preferred language and then hit "Confirm"

- If this doesn't work go to "Preferences", tab "Display and Sound" and deactivate "Use Skin Cache" while activate "Always reload skin on confirm". "Confirm"

- Enjoy
