Music Performer Willie Nelson
This DVD presents Willie's 70th birthday party live at New York City's famed Beacon Theatre on April 9, 2003. Willie is joined by some of the top names in rock, pop, and country including Eric Clapton, Shania Twain, Paul Simon, Sheryl Crow, Kris Kristofferson, Toby Keith, Diana Krall, Elvis Costello, Wyclef Jean, Norah Jones, Lyle Lovett, Ray Price, ZZ Top, Shelby Lynne, Leon Russell, Ray CHarles, John Mellencamp, Ray Price, Scotty Emerick, Kenny Chesney, and Steven Tyler. Bonus features 5 great new songs not appearing in the original broadcast, Q&A session, and extensive artists biography.

Country legend Willie Nelson celebrates his 70th birthday in style with this concert in New York City, which took place on April 9, 2003. Some big names joined Nelson for the festivities, including Elvis Costello, Eric Clapton, and Sheryl Crow, and LIVE & KICKIN' captures it all on camera.
