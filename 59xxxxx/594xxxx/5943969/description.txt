
Manos Eleftheriou was born in Ermoupoli on the island of Syros. 
He has published two novels, nine collections of poetry, three volumes of prose, three albums, four volumes on the theatre in Ermoupoli (1901-1921) and an anthology, Ermoupoli, a City in Literature (Metaichmio 2004). 
He was awarded the 2005 State Prize for Best Novel for The Time of Chrysanthemums (Metaichmio 2004). 
He has written the lyrics for over 400 popular songs and has worked with virtually all the major Greek composers.
