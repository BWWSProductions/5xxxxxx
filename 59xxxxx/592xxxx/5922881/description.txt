Durham County S03E01: Homelands

Mike Sweeney's wife Audrey is expecting a baby in a few weeks and his daughter Sadie is secretly expecting a child of her own. Now the Superintendent of the Durham police precinct, Mike is on the task force investigating the gruesome murders of two young brothers, who were drug runners along the 401 highway corridor. Mike's team is also joined by ex military Ivan Sujic who, unbeknownst to Mike, has just killed his wife Katya in a jealous rage.
