
Woodworkers Journal Magazine. Fall 2010.


Woodworker's Journal is designed for all woodworkers 
(from hobbyists to professionals) looking for new project ideas, 
woodworking techniques and shop jigs and tips that will enhance 
their time in the shop. Projects are presented in a detailed 
step-by-step format with photos and illustrations. A full size 
pattern is inserted in the middle of each issue. Departments 
cover joinery, finishing, products, techniques and hardware. 

Enjoy.
