The band grew up in 1994: the first intention was to make popular music revive expecially in the live performances through dancing and raw lirycs: the musicians were all convinced that traditional music was becaming too much intellectual and was loosing the contact with people. They began playing all around (over 500 concerts in 12 years) beginning from pubs and small villages feasts and concluding with big festivals in Italy, Europe and South America (Switzerland, Austria, Slovenia, Sweden, Croatia, Serbia, Bosnia, Spain) as Folkest, Onde Mediterranee, Etnica, Ariano Folk Festival, Mediterran Festival, Liet Lavlut and reaching a great popularity in their region and a close contact with the people. Almost all the songs are written by the band, the lirycs are both in Friulan, Italian and Slovenian (the three languages spoken in the region). The music is a mix of traditional (violin, mandolin, accordion, flutes, horns) and modern (electric guitar, drums) and is very suitable both for dancing than for listening: the influences come out from every corner of the world but the most characterizing aspect of the band is the mixing of a punk attitude with popular roots and lyrics.
