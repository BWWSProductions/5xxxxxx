                                    No Hands

             Single by Waka Flocka Flame featuring Wale & Roscoe Dash
             
                           from the album Flockaveli

 "No Hands" is a song performed by American rapper Waka Flocka Flame featuring Roscoe Dash and Wale, from his debut album Flockaveli. The song has Roscoe Dash on the chorus and a verse from Wale. It is produced by Drumma Boy. It originally leaked in May and was officially released in August.
