Battle of the Blades S02E09: Performance Show - Second Chance Week - Halloween

Battle of the Blades is a Canadian figure skating reality show and competition that airs on CBC. The series resembles Dancing with the Stars, but in this case the women are famous figure skaters and the men are well-known hockey players.

If you are a fan of figure skating, ice hockey or Dancing With The Stars, this show is worth a try.

In this episode, the 4 previously eliminated teams return to compete for the one available spot to rejoin the competition. The theme this week is Halloween.

The 4 pairs this week are:
- Russ Courtnall and Christine Hough-Sweeney
- Georges Laraque and Anabelle Langlois
- P.J. Stock and Violetta Afanasieva
- Patrice Brisebois and Shae-Lynn Bourne

Two of the judges are the same each week: Sandra Bezik and Jeremy Roenick. The guest judge this week is Curtis "Cujo" Joseph.

The hosts are Kurt Browning and Ron MacLean.
