                              My Wicked Heart

                          Single by Diana Vickers 

              from the album Songs from the Tainted Cherry Tree 

\"My Wicked Heart\" is a song co-written and co-produced by British indie pop star Diana Vickers. It will be released as the first single from the re-release of her debut album Songs From The Tainted Cherry Tree, but the third single overall. It is due to be digitally released on 17 October 2010 and physically released on 1 November 2010 by RCA Records in the United Kingdom. The track features Vickers playing the trumpet.
