Responsible for the terrible car accident that left her older sister disfigured, Christy (Nora Zehetner) is wracked with guilt for years after the tragedy. Returning to her hometown, she's haunted by frightening premonitions as well as nightmares of the crash. Are her visions merely the product of remorse, or is something more sinister at work? Matthew Settle also stars in this imaginative thriller.
