                             Heart Vacancy

                         Single by The Wanted 

                       from the album The Wanted 


  "Heart Vacancy" is a pop song by British boy band The Wanted from their self-titled debut album. It was released as their second single in the United Kingdom on 17 October 2010 through Geffen Records. The song was written by Jonas Jeberg, Cutfather, Lucas Secon, and Wayne Hector.
