This is a keygen for Navicat for OSX, it supports v8 and v9

Installation:

1. Go to www.navicat.com and download the 30 day trial
2. unpack the zip and double click on the DMG file.
3. Run the keygen and use the serial to activate the product.

Worked great with v9.0.12
Thank you for CORE for making this kegen.

Enjoy!
