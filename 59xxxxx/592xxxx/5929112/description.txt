This is an absolute Have-To-Listen-To (no need to have heard of paniq ever before) - it's amazing! :)

It is electronic but contains elements of many different genres - citing the artists website:
"style is very diverse, but it has traditionally been dance music that fascinated me. The idea of music that you can move to is fantastic."
 - http://www.paniq.cc/pages/about.html

The torrent includes all albums by paniq released between 2003 and 2009. They are also available online at http://www.paniq.cc/pages/archive.html and creative commons licensed, but torrents are smarter so download, and seed!
All tracks are at the highest available quality, this means flac for the newer ones and ogg for olders where the artist lost the masters.

Have Fun Listening and please again, don't forget to seed, it's CreativeCommons Licensed so you're even legally allowed to share.
