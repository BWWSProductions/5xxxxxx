For english-only speaking:
This book is in german language. English title would be: Top secret Third reich - Thuringen and the german atom bomb (2004). Edgar Mayer and Thomas Mehner continue their hunt for what seems as one of the biggest secrets of World war 2, namely that Nazi Germany was not only developing an atomic bomb but that it had it already successfully tested before anyone else could. It seems that the area of Thuringen in Germany played a special role as the major centre of high technology research. Authors unearth long ignored and suppressed evidence that one of the projects undertaken there must of been an atomic bomb together with corresponding deployment system. This fascinating book presents for the first time original research, documents and eyewitness records for one of the german miracle weapons that surprisingly completely vanished after the war. Find out what were the plans of the Nazi elite for its use. 255 pages.
