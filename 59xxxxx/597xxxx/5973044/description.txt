For english-only speaking:
This book is in german language. English title would be: Secret acts Krausinger - A report overdue (1998). Theodor Kaiser's documentary novel features a dramatic story about top secret flying saucer research in Nazi Germany and the enormous weight it supposedly has on underground politics to this very day. The main character, a journalist from Kassel in Germany, unearths evidence that puts him in lethal danger. It connects military personnel on both sides of the iron curtain, containing possible links to extraterrestrial presence that has been supressed for decades. Or are there simply secret projects that use the supposed E.T. presence as a clever camouflage? Are there flying saucers still hidden away in underground hangars in Germany? 300 pages.
