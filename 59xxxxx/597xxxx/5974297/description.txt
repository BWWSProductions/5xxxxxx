The family that plays together stays together. Or something like that. That's the theme of this heated 
tribute to interfamilial eroticism. Sweet little Nikki Charm stars as a hot-to-trot teenage vixen who 
can't help but turn on every man who comes into contact with her -- including members of her own 
family! And while most sisters would do their best to look the other way, Nikki decides to find out
just how far her ribald relations are willing to go! 
Whether watching her girlfriend Sheri St. Clair treat her beau to some back door boffing, or learning 
the ropes about oral fun with a guy of her own, Nikki exudes sensual allure from the moment she steps 
on the screen. Gorgeous Elaine Southern also shines as Nikki's sassy sister, trysting with a couple of 
well-built studs out by the pool. 
Things get even wilder when Nikki decides to let her closer relatives in on the fun! It's a naughty yet 
nice look at the ins and outs of youthful sexual expression
