sHoTV released the premiere of SpikeTV network's new reality series "Auction Hunters", which follows two of the best prospectors in the business, Clinton 'Ton' Jones & Allen Haff, in their quest to win auctions, acquire treasure and sell it for profit.

A bidding war erupts with Allen's old rivals; a slot machine from the Great Depression, a custom mini-bike and a Colt Peacemaker from the 1800s are featured.
