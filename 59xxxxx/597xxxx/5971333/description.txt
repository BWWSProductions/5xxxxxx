Ashampoo HDD Control is a complete utility to monitor, optimize and clean your hard drives.

With a friendly interface and fully translated into French, Ashampoo HDD Control pulls out of the game by integrating all the functionality required for maintenance and supervision of your hard drives.

Each feature has its tab with a tape system which allows a quick start and a perfect readability.

The first tab is dedicated to monitoring and displays the overall health of your disks, temperature and a summary of SMART information A button allows access to accurate data on the hard drive with a contextual help very well designed to decipher the readings.

Ashampoo HDD Control also offers a full disk defragmenter with 3 modes and manual mode defragmentation proactive resident. The latter runs automatically during periods of inactivity to preserve a system continuously optimized.

Then there is a benchmarking tool for measuring disk performance and compare it to a database online. Always useful for diagnosing a problem. Alas, the tests only measure reading performance.

Ashampoo HDD Control also offers two cleaners to clean up files in your browsers cache and temporary files from the system. An operation to be performed regularly to maintain good performance.

Finally, Ashampoo HDD Control Guard is the name given to the program icon in the systray. While flying, a popup displays essential information for your records, right-click menu starts with quick access to the program.

Comprehensive and well designed, Ashampoo HDD Control fulfills its role of all-in-One for your hard drives.
