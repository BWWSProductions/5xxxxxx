Boat Mart aims to make boating affordable for everyone with a broad 
range of advertisers alongside informative editorial giving advice 
about how to get the best deals, getting into boating and maintaining 
a boat without spending a fortune. Boat Mart covers a broad range of 
boating areas from RIBs and fishing boats to sports boats and cruisers, 
so has something for everybody. The comprehensive editorial, from 
well-known marine writers, features new and second hand boat tests, 
boating news, readers stories and letters, diary dates, plus equipment, 
engine, travel, angling and DIY features. Readers can buy and sell boats 
and equipment with the free classified photo section.
