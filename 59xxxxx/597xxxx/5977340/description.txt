Masters of the Universe - The Secret Birth of the Federal Reserve

Was there a takeover of the United States by international bankers? You will visit the scene of a crime so perfect that, for thirty years, no one knew it had even taken place. Join us as we investigate the birth of a criminal conspiracy to rob each and every bank vault in America, all at the same time. This is the true behind-the-scenes story of the birth of the Federal Reserve.

Featuring: Eustace Mullins, Jim Tucker, Michael Collins Piper and William Gill. 

Running Time: 43 minutes
Released Date: 1999
