Jerry Cotton is a fictional G-Man working in and around NYC with his sidekick Phil Decker in a series of trivial crime novels, always showing some comedy, but hardly any sex. The series was written over the years by different authors, the first novel already back in 1954. In 2005 the series reached the 2500th edition, total sales of the series are well over 850 million to date.

In the Sixties, US actor George Nader starred as Jerry Cotton in a series of eight German movies, with German actor Heinz Weiss as Phil Decker. The new movie of 2010, released in March 2010, has more comedy than the orginal novels or the original movies of the 60s.

The series Jerry Cotton has cult status in Germany and other German speaking countries. This kind of comedy will, however, be difficult to understand in other parts of the World.
