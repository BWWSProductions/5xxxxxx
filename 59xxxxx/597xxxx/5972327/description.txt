Naadodigal is a 2009 Indian Tamil drama film written and directed by Samuthirakani. The film stars Sasikumar along with Chennai 600028 fame Vijay Vasanth and Kallori fame Bharani in lead roles, with newcomers Ananya, Abhinaya and Malaysia-based Shanthini Deva.  Music by Sundar C. Babu. The film has the famous numbers "Aadungada Macha aadungada" and "Sambo Siva Sambo"
