Episode 4: Wet Behind the Gears
Airdate: 2010-11-15

The remaining drivers begin this episode in reverse. It all begins with our head to head reversing challenge. This Crazy Eight challenge is just that - crazy.

Later, our drivers put the pedal to the medal and tackle the longest slalom course we've ever created. Finally, its smooth sailing as our abysmal drivers try to stay dry as the maneuver themselves through the smooth driving challenge. It's the annual Water Tank Challenge. As the driver's towel off the experts make a decision.

One driver graduates and heads home after showing significant advancement and our experts continue to study closely to determine who will be Canada's Worst Driver.
