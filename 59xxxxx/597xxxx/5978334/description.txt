|                                 Description                                 |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------+
Darker has the player assume the role of Tolly, a civilian shuttle pilot in the 
city of Delphi, the only city situated on the dark side of a planet in 
synchronous rotation around the local star. The player must defend Delphi from 
the Halons, inhabitants of the planet's light side, who are invading Delphi...
