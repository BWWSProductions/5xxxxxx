The 8th release of my Best Of House series. This release is somewhat special as it focuses on Deep House only, so no Tech-House, Progressive, etc as in previous editions, and focuses on tracks mostly done with real instruments as opposed to the fully synth-based ones. Of course, this doesn't imply there aren't any of them in it ;)
It also has quite a few classics in it and the release is dedicated to the recently deceased Mr. Aaron-Carl, a true Detroit House hero who'll be missed for sure!

As usual, most tracks in 320kbps or V2, with some older ones in 192kbps. Enjoy and don't forget to support the artists by buying some! ;)
