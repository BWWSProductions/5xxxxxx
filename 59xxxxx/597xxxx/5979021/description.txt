Israel Vibration - Live & Jammin'


Top reggae group Israel Vibration, live in concert at the EXO 7 venue in Rouen, France from February 2003. The DVD also includes an intimate look at the band during rehearsal, working out songs from the "Fighting Soldiers" CD, at home in New York and in Paris, also a video clip of "Fighting Soldiers".
Israel Vibration perform live at EXO 7 in France, February 2003. With a hectic yearly touring schedule, and a commitment to producing some of the finest reggae sounds around, the band have garnered an incredible reputation on the live circuit. Tracks included on this release are "Rockford Rock," "Vultures," "Ball of Fire," "Cool and Calm," and many more.
