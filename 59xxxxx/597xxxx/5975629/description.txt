This is ripped from a promotion cd, that was released from Renault and Champion in 1994. 

Recorded in august and september 1994 at Spa, Silverstone, Monza and Estoril,
with Damon Hill as driver.

6 tracks:

1. The big day
2. Last minute warm up
3. The start
4. A lap with Williams Renault
5. A close-up on the V10
6. A chorus of engines

Lots of noice and screaming engines!
