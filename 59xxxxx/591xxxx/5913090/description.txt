Monster Trucks Nitro features stunning 3D-graphics, realistic physics and extreme stunts that put your racing skills to the test. Tracks are filled with traps, loops, obstacles, speed boosting nitro tanks, and a number of other special features. Drive through re-playable tracks to develop your skills, then earn medals, unlock new tracks, and unlock special, surprising vehicles. Race, jump, and crush the competition in Monster Trucks Nitro!
