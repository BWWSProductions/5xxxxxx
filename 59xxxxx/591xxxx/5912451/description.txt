HQ rare "Ed Sullivan" show performance music video. "Good Vibrations" is 
a song by American rock band The Beach Boys. Composed and produced by 
Brian Wilson, the song's lyrics were written by Wilson and Mike Love. 
Released as a single on October 10, 1966  it was The Beach Boys' third 
U.S. number-one hit, reaching the top of the Billboard Hot 100 chart in 
December 1966, as well as being their first British chart-topper.
