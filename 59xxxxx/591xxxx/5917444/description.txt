Cheetah DVD Maker - Burn Video DVDs... Fast - New

Burn Video DVDs from JPG, BMP, AVI, DivX, WMV, and MP4 files. The
Cheetah DVD Maker can burn NTSC or PAL compatible DVDs to DVD-R and
DVDR discs. It has options to create different menu types or a DVD with
no menu just chapters. The Cheetah DVD Maker can also make DVD Slide
Shows with pictures, video and audio. It is a fast and easy to use
program for creating home DVDs
