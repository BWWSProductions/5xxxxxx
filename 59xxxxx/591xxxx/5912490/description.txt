God of War: Ghost of Sparta is a game set in Greek mythology for a single player that allows players to become the mighty warrior Kratos. This new adventure takes the story right after God of War, telling the rise of Kratos as the God of War. In his quest to end the nightmares that haunt him, Kratos must begin a journey that will show us the origins of lost worlds and finally give an answer to questions about his dark past. Armed with the deadly Blades of Chaos, will face armies of mythological monsters, legions of undead soldiers and brutal landscape full of incredible dangers along their merciless mission.
