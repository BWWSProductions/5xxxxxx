Beach Boys - Do It Again [Ed Sullivan 1968].VOB



HQ rare "Ed Sullivan" show performance music video. "Do It Again" is a song 
written by Brian Wilson and Mike Love for the American rock band The Beach Boys. 
It was first released as a single by The Beach Boys in 1968 on Capitol Records. 
The song was recorded in May and June 1968 and was included on the band's 1969 
album 20/20. The song was produced by Brian and Carl Wilson. It features Mike 
Love on lead vocals. The band performed this song on the Ed Sullivan Show on 
October 13, 1968. The band sang live vocals over a pre-recorded track. 
The footage was shot in color in a special blue matte video montage.
(video does not have any logos)
