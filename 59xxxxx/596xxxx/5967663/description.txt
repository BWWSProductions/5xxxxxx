X is the tenth studio album by Australian recording artist Kylie Minogue. It is her first release since 2004's greatest hits compilation Ultimate Kylie, and her first studio album since 2003's Body Language. X was preceded by lead single "2 Hearts" and released worldwide in November 2007. The American release of X came in April 2008, preceded by the single "All I See".

Work on the album began following Minogue's gradual recovery from breast cancer and subsequent radiotherapy treatment.
