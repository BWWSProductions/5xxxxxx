                             23rd March 1931: Shaheed 


23rd March 1931: Shaheed is a 2002 Hindi film directed by Guddu Dhanoa which depicts 

the events leading up to the hanging of Indian freedom fighter Bhagat Singh and his

companions Rajguru and Sukhdev on 23 March 1931. The film stars Bobby Deol as Bhagat 

Singh, his elder brother Sunny Deol as another revolutionary Chandrashekhar Azad and 

Amrita Singh in her comeback role as Bhagat Singh's mother. Aishwarya Rai has a 

special appearance in a song.
