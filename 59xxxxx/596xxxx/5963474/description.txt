Occult Forces & Political Witchcraft

Date: 10-30-10
Host: Ian Punnett
Guests: Peter Levenda, Zak Bagans, Nick Groff

Investigative journalist Peter Levenda joined Ian for a discussion about the Satanic undercurrent in American government, the dark forces that have overtaken the political process, and geography's influence on the nation's history.

In the first hour, Zak Bagans and Nick Groff talked about their TV show Ghost Adventures and work with the paranormal.
