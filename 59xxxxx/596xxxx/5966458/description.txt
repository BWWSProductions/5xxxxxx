Bio-
From the rainy city of Seattle emerges what could possibly be the freshest new Roots Rock Reggae to hit the airwaves. The Wet City Rockers have been compared to the likes of Bob Marley, Shinehead, Police, and steel Pulse. With a humorous viewpoint The Wet City Rockers maintain the foundation of roots Reggae while adding an appalling new dark flavor. Releasing their debut self titled album in 2005 critics have given the Wet City Rockers rave reviews and high regards. The band is deeply influenced by the sounds of King Tubby, Scientist, and Bob Marley. The main musicians, Micah Averiett Vocals, Bill Patton Guitar. Sinci lovers sit back and indulge in the Rootsy, Dubby sound of the Wet City Rockers.
