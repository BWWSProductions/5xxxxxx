Cannibal is the upcoming first extended play (EP) by American recording artist Kesha, set to be released on November 19, 2010. Group CaHeSo brings you this release one day before official release date, enjoy!

The EP is a follow up companion to her previous record, Animal. Originally the record was set to only be released as a deluxe edition of Animal, but was instead sold and released as both an EP and a deluxe edition of Animal. Kesha worked with a variety of producers and writers such as Dr. Luke as the executive producer, Benny Blanco, Ammo, Max Martin, Bangladesh and others.
