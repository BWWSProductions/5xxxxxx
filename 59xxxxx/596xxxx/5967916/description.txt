This is the story of Lena and Mahad. The year is 2251. The earth is divided into one million pieces aimlessly follow a path around the core of the earth. These chunks are controlled by the atmosphere, under the leadership of the mad ruler Oslo.


Lena and Mahad have joined pirates after their mother was captured by Oslo. Together with the pirates they fight against the oppression of the atmosphere. With only one goal: to save their mother!
