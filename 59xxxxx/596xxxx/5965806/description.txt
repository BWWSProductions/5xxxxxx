Here we have Ernest Ansermet with the Suisse Romande Orchestra playing all of Stravinsky's best works over 8 CDs.
Some of the works include The Rite of Spring, The Firebird, Petrushka, Pulcinella, The Soldier's Tale, The Song of the Nightingale, Symphony in C, Mavra, Concerto for piano and Capriccio for piano, Apollon Musagete and more.
Format is 256k VBR AAC. Enjoy!
