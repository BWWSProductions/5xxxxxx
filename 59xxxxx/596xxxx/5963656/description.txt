Alien Implants

Date: 11-05-10
Host: George Noory
Guests: Dr. Roger Leir, Bob Koontz, Steve Colbern

In the first half of the show Dr. Roger Leir discussed alien implants. He was joined by scientists Bob Koontz and Steve Colbern, who have analyzed alleged alien implants.

The third hour was devoted to Open Lines, with a special line for truckers to share their scary stories from the road. The final hour featured a replay of 'Laugh Line' calls from 1/18/08.
