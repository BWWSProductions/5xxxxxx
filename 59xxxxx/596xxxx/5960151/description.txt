This collection contains ancient scriptures, medieval poems and some modern works. There are books by A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami, Narayana Maharaja, Sridhar Goswami, Bhaktivinode Thakur, Krishnadas Kaviraj, and the 6 Gosvamis. You will find the rare Jouney to Unknown India by Walter Eidlitz, an Austrian who met his guru in a concentration camp in WWII. Mind expansion at it's best.
