WWF One Night Only was a professional wrestling pay-per-view event produced by the World Wrestling Federation (WWF). It was broadcast on September 20, 1997 on Sky Box Office from the NEC Arena in Birmingham, England.

The show was shown on pay-per-view in Canada and Europe and later released on VHS in the United States. (The U.S. release did not include the WWF title match.)
