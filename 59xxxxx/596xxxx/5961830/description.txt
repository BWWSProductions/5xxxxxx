Pink Friday is the upcoming debut studio album by American rapper Nicki Minaj, set to be released on November 19, 2010 through Young Money Entertainment, Cash Money Records and Universal Motown. The album was preceded by the singles "Your Love" and "Right Thru Me" which were released in June and September 2010, respectively. In addition a previously released joint single between Nicki Minaj & will.i.am titled, "Check It Out" will appear on the album.
