PwnageTool 4.1.2 is a Mac based iPhone firmware creation tool for creating custom ipsw/firmware for various Apple devices running the iOS operating system. 

This point version supports firmware from Apple for various devices and fixes a bug in PwnageTool 4.1.1 that caused problems for hacktivated users. 

Please visit our blog at http://blog.iphone-dev.org for further and more specific details
