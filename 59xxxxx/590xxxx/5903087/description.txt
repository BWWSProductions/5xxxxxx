B.J. Thomas - Raindrops Keep Fallin' On My Head [Ed Sullivan 1970].VOB



HQ rare "Ed Sullivan" show performance. "Raindrops Keep Fallin' on My Head" 
is a song written by Hal David and Burt Bacharach for the 1969 film Butch 
Cassidy and the Sundance Kid, winning an Academy Award for Best Original Song. 
David and Bacharach also won Best Original Score. This version by B. J. Thomas 
was number one on the Billboard Hot 100 chart in the United States in January 
1970 for four weeks and the first number-one single of the 1970s. It also spent 
seven weeks atop the U.S. adult contemporary chart and was listed at #85 on The 
Billboard Hot 100 All-Time Top Songs. (video does not have any logos)
