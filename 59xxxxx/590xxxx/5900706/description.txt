


Pancharangi is a movie which is about 5 aspects of life. Pancharangi which mean that  5 colors of life. Topics of life that are dicussed in the movie are parents, friends, goals, success, failure, love, marriage and kids. The movie is about a lazy boy his love story, his parents, his brothers, his life goals.  Pancharangi movie is directed by Yograj Bhat and produced by Yograj Bhat and Subramanya. Main cast of movie include Diganth, Nidhi Subbaiah and Ananth Nag. Lyrics of movie are composed by Jayanth Kaikini and Yogaraj Bhat. Song list of movie include Lifeu Ishtene, Udisuve Belakina Seereya, Hudugaru Beku and Pancharangi Haadugalu.
