Pixar Shorts
------------
I have carefully collected together as many Pixar and Disney Pixar short films as I can.  This collection is not for full movies, but those little extras that appear before (or after) the movie in the cinema, as an extra on a DVD, or is just shown on TV.  Below is a list of all the files in this collection.

File Formats
------------
The collection has been compiled from a large number of sources.  Every effort has been made to standardise them to AVI files, and I've converted all non-AVI files to DivX standard.  This does not mean that all the videos in this compilation are fully DivX format, but it does mean that they are all AVI files.  The quality is also variable, but where possible, I have chosen the best quality version I could find.

Incompleteness
--------------
The following shorts are known to be missing from this collection.  Without these titles, this cannot be considered a complete collection.  Unfortunately, I was not able to find any of these, although "Heavy Metal Mater" is available on YouTube, it's all really bad quality, so I refused to include it in this collection.

Mater's Tall Tales: Heavy Metal Mater (2008)
Mater's Tall Tales: Moon Mater (2008)
Finding Nemo: Submarine Voyage (2007)
The Incredibles: Mr. Incredible and Pals (2005)
The Incredibles: The Incredible Socks (2005)

Pixar have also created several commercials for various companies.  I have not included these, as you can find many of them by searching YouTube for 'Pixar Commercial'.
