Hugh is going back to basics with simple recipes for everything from breakfast to baking to bring a little bit of River Cottage to our lives in River Cottage Every Day

In River Cottage Every Day, Hugh takes his passion for cooking into other peoples houses, as he helps everyone from mums and dads to kids bring real, simple and tasty food to the table.

Hugh believes good food should be at the heart of every kitchen and the kitchen should be at the heart of every home, so each week he will tackling a different food favourite from breakfasts to bread making, meat to treats, to show us all how to eat well, without blowing our budget.

He'll be joined by River Cottage favorites like Jon the Forager and Pam the Jam who'll be giving their tips for making top treats. While River Cottage chefs, Tim and Gil will be on hand to add some recipes to the foodie feast. 

To get us all cooking, each show will tell you how to set up yor own foodie community, giving you a taste of River Cottage living, wherever you call home.
