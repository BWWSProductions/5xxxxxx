Dr. Andrew Burt ( www.aburt.com ), professional science fiction writer and former Vice President of Science Fiction and Fantasy Writers of America, Inc., has published dozens of short stories and one novel. You can find more of his fiction at http://aburt.com/fiction . He also herds Critters ( www.critters.org ), the first writers' workshop on the web, specializing in science fiction, fantasy, and horror, and home to other fun tools for writers and readers.  Outside of science fiction, he's been a computer science professor (research in networking, security, privacy, and free-speech/social issues), founder of Nyx.net, the world's first public Internet service provider, purveyor of the NewNewForum.com - The Innovation Forum, the "Wishes for the Future" Forum ("If you could have anything in the future what would it be?" - http://NewNewForum/wishes ), and a technology consultant/author/speaker. He's currently CEO of TechSoft, and President of GreenAroundYou.org. For a hobby, he constructs solutions to all the world's problems. Fortunately -- nobody listens. He lives in the Rockies with his wife and their two parrots.
