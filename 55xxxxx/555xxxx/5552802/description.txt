The Courage of Others is the third studio album by American Folk rock band 

Midlake. It was released on February 1, 2010, on Bella Union Records. The album 

sleeve pays homage to Andrei Rublev, a 1966 film by Russian writer and director 

Andrei Tarkovsky.
