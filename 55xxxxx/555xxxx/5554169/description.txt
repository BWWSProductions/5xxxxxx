TM 9-726 (Stuart)
TECHNICAL MANUAL
LIGHT TANK M3
Operation and Maintenance
July 15, 1942
Manufactured by: American Car & Foundry Co.
These instructions are published for the information and guidance of the personnel of the using arm charged with the operation, maintenance, and repair of this materiel. They contain descriptions of the major units and their function in relation to the other components of the tank, as well as instructions for operation, inspection, minor repair, and unit replacement.
