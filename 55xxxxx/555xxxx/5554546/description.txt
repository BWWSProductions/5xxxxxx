Enjoy the computer version of NBC's popular Texas Hold 'Em poker tournament where players compete head-to-head to win! Select and customize your player as you compete against smart and cunning opponents in the traditional 64 player bracket! You have full control over how you play. Will you be reckless and aggressive or will you play it tight and consider your every bet? It's all up to you! There are many ways to win the tournament - but only the best can be a champion!
