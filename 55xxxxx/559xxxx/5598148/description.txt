An epic debate between professors Richard Dawkins and John Lennox, both of Oxford University.
Richard Dawkins is a professor of public understanding of science and is an atheist.
John Lennox is a professor of mathematics and believes in intelligent design.

The debate is held in Oxfords museum of natural history.
This is the second debate they've had.
The first one is also available as video and it's called "The God Delusion".
