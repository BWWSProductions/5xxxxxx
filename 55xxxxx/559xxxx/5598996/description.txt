All the tools and tactics of the U.S. Marines Corps are at your disposal as you lead your four-man squad through the dangerous streets of Beirut. Take aim and take cover all while taking charge of the battlefield through a single-player campaign. Bark out orders and watch your squad follow every command. Your squad will respond and fire with intelligence and accuracy just like real soldiers. Challenge a friend to a gun battle in split-screen multiplayer for additional training. This is no arcade shooter, this is the real thing!
