BBC R4 Documentary - 'The Ted Hughes' Letters'
Read by Richard Armitage
Presented by Jane Feaver
Produced by Susan Roberts
Broadcast October 29, 2007
Coded from tape at 128/44.1

Ted Hughes was Sylvia Plath's husband, and latterly
Poet Laureate, though he seems to have liked fly
fishing best of all.

A selection of his previously unavailable letters to
family, friends, academics, children and fellow writers.
