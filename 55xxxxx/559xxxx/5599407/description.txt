Giannis Poulopoulos (John Poulopoulos, b. 1944 in Mani, Greece) is a Greek singer-songwriter who had many prominent hits in the Greek music world during the 1960s, 1970s and 1980s . 
He has sold the fourth-highest number of albums of all Greek singers.

Poulopoulos resides in Greece; many fans consider him a legend; he still sings but refuses to give concerts due to a fear of flying.
