Romeo's Heart is a studio album by Australian singer John Farnham. The album was released in Australia on 3 June 1996, and is Farnham's first studio album since the release of Then Again... in 1993.

This album peaked in the ARIA charts at #2, and was 4x platinum. It won the 'Best Adult Contemporary Album' at the ARIA Awards in 1996. This album produced five singles in total, with "Have A Little Faith (In Us)" gaining the most commercial success, reaching #3 in March.
