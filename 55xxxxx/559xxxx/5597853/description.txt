A man with no home and no job Paul Erdos was the most prolific mathematician who ever lived. Erdos who died in 1996 at the age of 83 inspired generations of mathematicians throughout the world with his insightful approach and wry humor. This whimsical documentary follows him for four years through four countries presenting his mathematical quest and its personal and philosophical dimensions.
