An Oscar winner for best foreign film in 1980. This is a drama about the life stories of three girls. The three friends, Antonina, Liudmila and Yekaterina, come to Moscow in search of their dream. Sharing a hostel room, they work and play and grow up together.

Director: Vladimir Menshov

Cast: Vera Alentova, Irina Muravyova, Aleksey Batalov, Raisa Ryazanova, Aleksandr Fatyushin

Year	Result	Award	Category/Recipient(s)
