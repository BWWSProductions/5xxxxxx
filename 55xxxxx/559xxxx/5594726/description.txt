Sex Change Hospital (2007) - ReSeed

IMDb:
Sex Change Hospital is a six part series based in Trinidad, Colorado, 
the sex change capital of the world. Each episode is a self-contained 
journey following 2 people as they take the final step in their 
transition from one gender to another. Dr. Marci Bowers performs the 
surgeries and calls upon her own experiences as a transgender woman to 
guide her patients as they go through the ultimate life changing procedure.
