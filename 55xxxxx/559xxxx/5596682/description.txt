A Bridge At The Edge Of The World
Documentary / 2007

In 1969, while the impassable Zap River running in Hakkari, "the most remote and poor city in Turkey", claimed lives, preparations were under way to build the Bosphorus Bridge in Istanbul. The youth '68 opposed this huge building. They were defending equal investments both of the east and west of the country. With a national campaign that they initiated, "Revolutionary Youth Bridge" was built on Zap river and it became a legend. The story of this bridge that was blown up by still unidentified persons is told through the testimonies of the generation of '68.
