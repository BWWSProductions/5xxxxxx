Linus Torvalds and David Diamond - Just for Fun (Audiobook, MP3 64Kbps)

Just for Fun: The Story of an Accidental Revolutionary
is a humorous autobiography of Linus Torvalds, the creator
of the Linux kernel, co-written with David Diamond.
The book explains Linus' view of himself, the free software
movement and the development of Linux.

Linux was made with the help of Minix, from Andrew Tanenbaum.
