HER VENGEANCE aka I PISS ON YOUR URN aka HYUT MUI GWAI
Hong Kong 1988, Lam Ngai Kai (STORY OF RICKY, THE SEVENTH CURSE, etc.)
86 Mins, Adults only
Cantonese with hardcoded English subs

A savage attack leaves a nightclub waitress devastated and aching for revenge against her assailants!

HK babe Pauline Wong gets gang-raped by a bunch of drunken thugs and takes cruel revenge in the good ol' exploitation way.

A dark and brutal rape n' revenge flick, Hong Kong style, by the creator of STORY OF RICKY.

If you are a fan of extreme cinema give this HK gem a look.
