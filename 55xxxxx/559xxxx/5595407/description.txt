This DVD companion to Tesla's most popular live album "Five Man Acoustical Jam," shows how good ol' fashioned Rock 'N' Roll combined with raw, young talent and acoustic instruments will rock your socks off. Shot and recorded at Philadelphia's Trocadero club in 1990, experience the remastered visuals and audio of a once in a lifetime video performance of their only top 10 hit covering the Five Man Electrical Band's "Signs." Includes "Comin' Atcha Live/Truckin'," "Heaven's Trail," "We Can Work it Out," "Before My Eyes," and more!

California's hard rock quintet Tesla mix decidedly classic rock influences with a modern metal edge. Their love of classic rock 'n roll and blues can be glimpsed on this filmed concert performance, at which they perform the Five Man Electrical Band's "Signs" (one of their biggest radio hits) and The Beatles "We Can Work It Out." In addition, they play several of their original songs at this 1990 concert at Philadelphia's famous Trocadero venue.
