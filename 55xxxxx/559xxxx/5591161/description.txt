BBC R4 - Ariel Dorfman's 'Konfidenz'
Adapted by Ariel Dorfman and his son Rodrigo 
Music by Simon Morecroft
Directed by Rosalynd Ward
Broadcast January 5, 2001
Coded from tape at 128/44.1

Paris. 1939. War has not yet been declared.

Truth and illusion blur as a young German receives
a call warning her that, because of his political
activities, someone she's due to meet is in danger
from the German authorities.
