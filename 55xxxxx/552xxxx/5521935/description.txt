Starring David Janssen; Ralph Meeker

Harry Walker, Former World WWII Ace (Flying Tigers), works for a radio station in Salt Lake City as a traffic helicopter pilot. 
While on his rounds he observes a bank robbery taking place and the abduction of a female teller. 
He follows, 1st the criminals in their car, then they transfer to a helicopter.

This is one of the better helicopter movies pre-dating Airwolf and Firebirds.  

Also contains some black and white scenes as part of Harry's flashbacks.
