HOUSE FULL:-

Movie Details 
Genre: Comedy 
Language: Hindi 


House Full Story :-

Arush (Akshay Kumar) has a deep dark secret; he has three wives: Sandy (Deepika Padukone), Hetal (Lara Dutta) and Devika (Jiah Khan). Obviously, each doesn't know of the others existence. But all that is about to change. And Arush will need all the help he can from his friend Bob (Ritesh Deshmukh) to keep his secret a secret. And now that Sandy's brother Krishna (Arjun Rampal) is also involved, it will be so much more difficult for Arush.
