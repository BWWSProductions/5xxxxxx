PureLoad is a load testing tool that dynamically
simulates thousands of users executing requests
against server based applications.

Use PureLoad to verify performance and stability
of your server applications and telecom services.
 
http://www.pureload.com/

Unpack, Install.
Use our keygen-patch in /iNViSiBLE dir:
java -jar minqkeygen.jar
or use keygen.bat/sh to launch keygen.
Choose the product from the list, press
'Patch', locate installdirlibpureload.jar
Then enter name and organization and generate
your key.
