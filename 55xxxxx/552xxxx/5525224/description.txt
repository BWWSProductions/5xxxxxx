The satirical feminist underground short film written, produced, and directed by the famous/infamous (take your pick) Sarah Jacobson (1971-2004, RIP).

Starring Kristin Calabrese, Corey, and Scott Garvey.

Music by Charles Manson (Eyes of a Dreamer), Gas Huffer (Body Buzz), Steve "Garth" Hermann (Love Theme Gem), and Heavens to Betsy (My Secret).

Produced with the assistance of underground filmmaker George Kuchar ("Hold Me While I'm Naked"). Made with 16mm film on 1,600 USD in San Francisco, California.

Unrated, black & white, 25 minutes. MKV, H.264 video/MP3 audio.
