numerous revisions to the 1992 Director's Cut. 

II.  Ridley Scott's Final Cut (2007, 117 minutes), or the "25th Anniversary 
Edition", released by Warner Bros. theatrically on October 5, 2007, and 
subsequently released on DVD, HD DVD, and Blu-ray in December 2007.
This is the only version over which Ridley Scott had complete artistic 
control as the Director's Cut was rushed and he was not directly in charge.
