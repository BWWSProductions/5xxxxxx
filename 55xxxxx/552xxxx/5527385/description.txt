Now you can get all the thrills and adventure in your own home theater.  Pilot Jeff Ethell checks out the special cockpit features before you climb in and start-up your Rolls Royce Merlin engine.  The roar and crackle of the mighty 1500 horsepower engine surrounds you as you shove the throttle forward for takeoff.
