"Your Love Is My Drug" is an electropop  song by American recording artist and songwriter Kesha, from her debut album, Animal. It was released as the album's third single. Prior to the release of Animal, the song charted in the United States, the United Kingdom, Canada and New Zealand. It was officially available for U.S. airplay on April 20, 2010

The song was written by Kesha, her mother Pebe Sebert and Joshua Coleman. It tells about one of Kesha's previous romances, which she compares to a drug. The song was featured in the film The Bounty Hunter
