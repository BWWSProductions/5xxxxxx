In Tenebris is a unique collaboration of guitar, bass, synth and percussion with haunting female vocals. These elements give rise to original music that is both heavy and ethereal. Track listings on this self-titled 2007 release are: 1. Chrysalis 2. At Sea 3. Sunrise 4. Torch Song 5. Havoc 6. Intervention 7. Transcendence 8. Holy Ghost 9. Fear To Breathe 10. Wounded Bird
