Texas Calculatem is an online poker software that offers you real time
information about the poker game that you are playing. It provides a
complete analysis of the strength of your hand based a variety of factors.
Texas Calculatem will also give you some hints on what you should do in
order to have the highest odds of winning. Texas Calculatem will be
attached to your online poker room, and unlike other odds calculators out
there, it is compatible with more then 250 major poker rooms including
PokerStars, Titan Poker and Full Tilt Poker.
