SILENT CIVILIAN stormed onto the metal scene with their stunning debut offering,
Rebirth Of The Temple, in 2006 led by Spineshank vocalist Jonny Santos, who used
this platform to unleash all his inner demons. SILENT CIVILIAN are now back and
have come of age. No longer a side-project of Santos, but now his full-time
band. Brutal, intense, uncompromising and relenteless are just a few words that
one will use to describe the vicious new SILENT CIVILIAN album, Ghost Stories.
With Ghost Stories the band are back and their is definitely no shortage of
demons to be exorcised. This is the new wave of thrash metal.
