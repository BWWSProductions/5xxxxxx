BBC R4 - John Milton's 'Paradise Regained' [1992 dramatisation]
Adapted by Adrian Mitchell
Music by Elizabeth Parker & The BBC Radiophonic Workshop
Production by Catriona Wane
Directed by John Theocharis
Broadcast in nine parts December 1992
Coded from tape at 128/44.1

John Milton's revolutionary poem, and sequel to 'Paradise Lost'.

This is a dramatisation with different voices for each part.
The voices follow Milton's text.
