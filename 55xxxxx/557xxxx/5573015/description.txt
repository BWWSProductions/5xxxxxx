BBC R3 - John Spurling's 'The Heresy'
Directed by Richard Wortley
Broadcast June 3, 2001

The story of Hypatia, female Alexandrine mathematician
and philosopher, killed by a mob of christians, 415CE

The play explores the conflict between Christian orthodoxy
and neo-Platonism, and the consequences of mixing politics
and faith.
