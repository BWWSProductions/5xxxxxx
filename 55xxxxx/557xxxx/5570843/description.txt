- R E L E A S E   N O T E S -

another djsforfun exclusive!

Hailing from Lake Mary, Florida, the members of It's Alive are all still in
their early 20s, but they sound like nothing short of rock veterans on their
debut album Human Resources. Human Resources boasts the camaraderie of four
like-minded musicians on a mission. The first single, "Pieces," offers a taste
of their vision, a heavy rock and roll bottom provided by the rhythm section of
bassist Juice Dean and drummer Eddie Matta that gives way to lush melodies,
characterized by Zach Webb's full-throated, emotional vocals and Clay Haywood's
anthemic riffs. Opening track "The Bottom" is a thudding, intense number that
sets the tone for the rest of the album, an intense and driven twelve tracks
with prog-rock leanings on tracks like "Selfless" and "Back to the Rain".
