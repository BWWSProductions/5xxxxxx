Robinson Crusoe and the Cursed Pirates 

Robinson Crusoe and his friend Friday are back in their greatest adventure yet in Robinson Crusoe and the Cursed Pirates! After escaping the inhabited island, Robinson Crusoe sets sail for 
Brazil. Four weeks later, however, he and Friday encounter an uncharted land mass in the middle 
 the ocean. Take on the undead pirates and find a cure for a mysterious voodoo curse in this 
Hidden Object Adventure game!

    * Fantastic puzzles
