BBC WS Documentary - 'Bombs, Stamps, and Throat Singers'
Presented by Ilona Vinogradova
Broadcast August 8, 2009
Coded from tape at 128/44.1

Richard Feynman and dreams of Tuva.

Tuva is a remote region of Russia close to the Mongolian border.

Richard Feynman fell in love with Tuva through its stamps.

He died just before his Tuva visa arrived.
