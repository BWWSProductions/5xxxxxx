 	
Original Wing Chun, by Ip Chun

This DVD is a re-release of one of the earliest Wing Chun videos, Original Wing Chun.  Watch as Grandmaster Yip Chun does all three empty hand forms and then explains the applications, chi sau, and lap sau drills.  A must have for any Wing Chun collection!

Contents:

# Sil Lum Tao and Chum Kiu by Yip Man
# Sil Lum Tao demonstrated from multiple angles by Yip Chun.
# Chum Kiu demonstrated from multiple angles by Yip Chun.
# Biu Jee demonstrated from multiple angles by Yip Chun.
# Applications by Yip Chun and trained by Samuel Kwok and his student Trevor Jefferson.
# Chi Sao between Yip Chun and Samuel Kwok, and Yip Chun and Trevor Jefferson.
# Lap Sau Drill between Samual Kwok and Trevor Jefferson with Yip Chun watching.
# More Chi Sao
