Bhool Bhulaiyaa (2007)

Director:Priyadarshan
Writers:Neeraj Vora & Manisha Korde  
Release Date: 12 October 2007  
Genre:Comedy/Drama/Mystery/Thriller 
Plot:
When U.S.-based Siddharth visits his Indian home town with his new wife, 
he insists they stay at the ancestral home, laughing off family members' 
warnings of ghostly goings-on in the mansion. But events soon make him 
reconsider his beliefs. As unexplained and terrifying occurrences arise, 
Siddharth calls on his doctor friend to help solve the mystery
