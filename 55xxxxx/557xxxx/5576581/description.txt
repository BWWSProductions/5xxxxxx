A last hurrah from the vastly under-appreciated band Harvey Danger. Sold only at concerts during their final tour, undistributed through sales on internet or in stores. Even if you've never listened to them, give this album a try.

Contains:
1. Big Wide Empty
2. Cold Snap*
3. Defrocked
4. Diminishing Returns*
5. Humility On Parade*
6. Louisiana, 1927
7. Mainland
8. Maneater
9. My Human Interaction
10. Pity & Fear
11. Plague Of Locusts
12. Save It For Later
13. Sometimes You Have To Work On Christmas (Sometimes)
14. The World's Greatest Living Dancer
15. You Look So Happy

and album artwork. Share and enjoy!

*Remixed versions of previous studio releases.
