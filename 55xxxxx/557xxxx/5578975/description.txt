Synopsis:

A triad boss, Andy (Cheung Siu Fai) is arrested for smuggling counterfeit Renminbi into Hong Kong. In this period the organization is leaderless and a new leader had to be appointed fast. His fortune is then left to his two children, Audrey (Bernice Liu) and Jason (Lai Lok Yi).

One of the triad brothers, Funky (Simon Yam) insists the money belong to the whole gang and wants some share of the money. Later some lives are lost in a mysterious circumstances and someone is seeking revenge to become a new triad leader for the group.

The suspects for the incident include Calf (Andy On), Hung (Ken Lo), Zen (Chan Wai Man), Kong (Xiong Xin Xin) and a mute girl, Dumby (Jiang Lu Xia). More lives are lost after the non-stop fighting continues. Who is the mastermind behind all of this and who will become the new leader for the gang?
