Steve Hackett, always know as "former Genesis guitarist", is much more than that. A guitar pioneer, adept at both rock & classical, his output since quitting Genesis has exceeded that of all his ex band mates combined.

Out Of The Tunnel's Mouth was first released in autumn 2009, and is relaunched in May 2010 in a special edition, featuring a bonus disc of 5 live tracks & 1 studio track, in a new digipak, with an expanded 16 page booklet.
