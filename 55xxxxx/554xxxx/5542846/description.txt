These three episodes are from a DVD purchased at Dollar Tree, in the last sad days of the Golden Age of the Dollar DVD.  No apologies for quality here - they seem to have been taken from a VHS copy of some pretty burned out TV distribution films - and any tweaking I tried to do only degraded the quality further.  It is what it is, good to fair, but it retains its visual flair and power in spite of everything. Note that there are no written titles or credits on any episode.

The episodes are:
#14 - The Racing Rockets (faded, with muffled sound)
#34 - Battle of the Brothers (best quality of the three)
#38 - The Atomic Ghost

They were originally broadcast in 1965.
