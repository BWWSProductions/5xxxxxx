Urban Sickness Audio: USA1002

Artist: Tim Besamusca featuring Chrysa T
Title: Wasteland


Renowned Trance producer Tim Besamusca delivers his debut release for USA adding an unusual layer of darkness to the typical Trance sound.

Chrysa T adds mainstream appeal to the track with her haunting vocals while Radical Project and Red Math step in to inject some Dubstep and Drum & Bass madness to the package.


With DJ support from: 

Xample / Vulgatron (Foreign Beggars) / Dr P / Swan-e / Simon Bassline Smith
Sappo / Soul Intent / Tony Future Prophecies / Ed Solo / Isaac Maya / Ben XO
