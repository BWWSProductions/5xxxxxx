Based on the life of recently deceased victim's rights activist Jane Alexander.

When she learns that her closest friend and lover, Tom O'Donnell , is the chief suspect in the brutal slaying of a dear relative, ordinary citizen Jane Alexander will stop at nothing to track him down and discover the truth. But as she closes in on her quarry, she realizes that she might be next on the murderer's hit list...
