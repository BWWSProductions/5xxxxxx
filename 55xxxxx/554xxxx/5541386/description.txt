"Total Life Forever" is the second studio album from Oxford band Foals. It was produced by Luke Smith, formerly of post-punk/electropop band Clor, and was recorded at Svenska Gramofon Studios in Gothenburg, Sweden.

It is set for release on 10 May 2010.
