English | 60 Pages | 6MB | True PDF

G3 is a term used by NATO and its partners to refer to that part of an armed force responsible for operations, including staff duties, exercise planning, training, operational requirements, combat development & tactical doctrine. G3 Defence is a magazine that aims to keep servicemen and women informed about equipment entering service, available or being developed for training and operations. Produced bi-monthly and is distributed to the three services, the decision-makers within industry and government. The magazine is produced and written by acknowledged experts in their fields. G3 Defence is essentially a window to showcase new equipment and solutions, some produced to order and others developed independently.[/b]
