Round 2 of the 2010 FIM Speedway Grand Prix Series from the Ullevi Stadium, Gothenburg. Riders on the night were Jason Crump, Tomasz Gollob, Emil Sayfutdinov, Greg Hancock, Andreas Jonsson, Nicki Pedersen, Rune Holta, Kenneth Bjerre, Fredrik Lindgren, Hans Andersen, Magnus Zetterstrom, Chris Holder, Jaroslaw Hampel, Chris Harris, Tai Woffinden and Antonio Lindback.
