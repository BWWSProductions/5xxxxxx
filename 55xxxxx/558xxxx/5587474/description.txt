BBC R4 - Terry Johnson's 'Insignificance'
Directed by Roxana Silbert
Broadcast June 9, 2007
Coded from tape at 128/44.1

1953. New York Hotel bedroom. Marilyn Monroe tries to seduce
Albert Einstein (who has no idea who she is) and successfully
acts the specific thoery of relativity.

Meantime Joe Dimaggio and Joseph McCarthy make life difficult
for the pair of them.

A good adaption of Terry Johnson's successful West End play.

Cast
The Professor - Allan Corduner
The Senator - John Guerassio
The Actress - Megan Dodds
The Ballplayer - Damian Lewis
