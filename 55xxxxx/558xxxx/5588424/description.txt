Better Off Ted is an American single-camera situation comedy, which premiered March 18, 2009 on ABC. The show deals with the absurd day-to-day at a heartless technology company, Veridian Dynamics. ABC has aired 11 out of the 13 episodes produced for the second season. On May 13, 2010, the series was officially canceled.
