The Lord of the Rings Extended DVD Box Set is a 12 disc collection. (4 DVDs for each of the 3 movies)

The first 6 discs are the extended version of the movie, and the last 6 discs are the Extras Features and Bonus Material.

The following are the EXTRA FEATURES and BONUS MATERIAL ONLY. They are found on the last 6 discs of this collection.

There are 78 individual videos explaining the making of, and other behind the scenes footage.

Hours of enjoyment for the true fan.
