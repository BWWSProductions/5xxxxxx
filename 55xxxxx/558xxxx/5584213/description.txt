Capsules is the second album from Model Warships (Leeds UK-based noise-pop musician Michael Waters). Implosive indie in the vein of Xiu Xiu, Parenthetical Girls, Frog Eyes, Former Ghosts, Casiotone for the Painfully Alone etc.
