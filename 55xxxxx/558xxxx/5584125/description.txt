BBC R3 Documentary - 'Shelley's Invocation'
Presented by Paul Farley
Produced by Emma Harding
Broadcast July 23, 2009
Coded from a digital source at 128/44.1

Paul Farley explores the inspiration behind Edward
Elgar's Second Symphony, Shelley's 'Invocation', which
begins: 'Rarely, rarely comest thou, spirit of delight'. 

He also examines 'Ode To The West Wind' and 'To A Skylark'.
