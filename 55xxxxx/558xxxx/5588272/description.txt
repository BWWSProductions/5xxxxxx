320kbps MP3 recording from Led Zeppelin's June 2nd 1973 concert in San Francisco's Kezar Stadium.
It's only the last portion of the show, but the sound quality is great: an excellent soundboard recording.
The bootleg is called "Vibes Are Real", presumably based said phrase being said by Plant towards the end.
