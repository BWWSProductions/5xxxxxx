Soul eater episode 1-9
------------------------------
Students of the Shinigami Technical School are called Meisters. Each of them possesses an intelligent weapon which can change into a human form. The goal of every Meister is to collect 99 evil human souls and 1 soul of a witch so that their weapon could become the death scythe to be used by Shinigami-sama himself.

Genres: Action, Adventure, Comedy, Supernatural

Studio: Bones
