Two and a Half Men is an American television Comedy, which premiered on CBS on Monday, September 22, 2003. The sitcom stars Charlie Sheen, Jon Cryer and Angus T. Jones. The show is about a hedonistic jingle writer, Charlie; his uptight brother, Alan; and Charlie's (at the start of the series) 10-year-old nephew, Jake. Charlie's free-wheeling life is complicated and altered when his brother gets divorced and moves into his beach-front house. There have been six seasons of Two and a Half Men. On March 18, 2009, CBS renewed the show for an additional three seasons.
