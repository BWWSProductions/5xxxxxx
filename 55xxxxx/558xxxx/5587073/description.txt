Star Trek: Fan Collective - Klingon is a DVD collection released by Paramount Pictures. This four disc set contains episodes from Star Trek: Enterprise, Star Trek: The Original Series, Star Trek: The Next Generation, Star Trek: Deep Space Nine and Star Trek: Voyager that deal in large part with the Klingons. It was the first Fan Collective to feature episodes from all five Star Trek series.

  Episodes
1
  ENT: "Broken Bow" (feature length)
  TOS: "Errand of Mercy"
  TOS: "The Trouble with Tribbles"

2
  TNG: "A Matter Of Honor"
  TNG: "Sins of the Father"
  TNG: "Redemption"
  TNG: "Redemption II"

3
  DS9: "The Way of the Warrior" (feature length)
  DS9: "The Sword of Kahless"

4
  DS9: "Trials and Tribble-ations"
  VOY: "Barge of the Dead"

Special features
   * Disc 1 contains an audio commentary by Rick Berman and Brannon Braga on "Broken Bow" and text               commentaries for "Broken Bow" and "The Trouble with Tribbles" by Michael and Denise Okuda.
   * Disc 2 contains a text commentary for "Sins of the Father" by Michael and Denise Okuda.
   * Disc 3 contains a text commentary for "The Sword of Kahless" by Michael and Denise Okuda.
   * Disc 4 contains a text commentary for "Trials and Tribble-ations" by Michael and Denise Okuda.
