Greyson Michael Chance (born 1997) is a 12-year-old singer/songwriter/pianist from Edmond, Oklahoma whose April 2010 performance of Lady Gaga's "Paparazzi" at Edmond's sixth-grade music festival became a hit onin May, 2010 following media reports that he would appear on The Ellen DeGeneres Show.[1] As of May 22nd, 2010 the video had a total viewership of over 19.5 million.

"Broken Hearts" and "Stars" are original compositions by Greyson.
