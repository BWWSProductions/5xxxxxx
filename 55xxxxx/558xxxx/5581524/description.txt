BBC R4 Documentary - 'The New York City Blackout of 1977'
Presented by Sidney Schanberg
Produced by Simon Hollis
Broadcast July 12, 2008
Coded from tape at 128/44.1

BBC Blurb
An exploration of the blackout on 13 July 1977 that
plunged a sweltering and near-bankrupt New York City
into chaos as the lights went out at 9.27pm.

Music stations switched to rolling news and the sound
of store alarms was the prelude to a night of fear and
unprecedented lawlessness.
