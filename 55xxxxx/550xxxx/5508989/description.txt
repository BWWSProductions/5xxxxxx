Genre: Horror

Country:Sweden

Languages:English

When they stop at a country gas station on the way to a cheerleading competition, Jenna (Victoria Bloom) and Tara (Yohanna Idha) pick up Chad (Andreas Vaehi) and Oliver (Max Wallmo), whose car has conked out. But their cheerful mood turns to terror as a group of sadistic killers stalks and captures them. Now the four teens must fight for their lives as their captors start slaughtering them one by one in this gory spine-chiller.

Starring: Max Wallmo, Andreas Vaehi

Director: Sonny Laguna
