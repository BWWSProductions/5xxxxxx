If a family that plays together stays together, this game is the glue! The second edition of the award-winning Hasbro Family Game Night features five popular board games in one virtual package. Play Jenga, Operation, Bop-It, Pictureka, and Connect 4x4 in their original versions, or make it a party on the Hasbro Family Game Show. You'll be taken to the set of a live TV game show where you'll compete for prizes before a studio audience. Your emcee is none other than Mr. Potato Head! Each classic has been re-energized for the Wii with new and exciting ways to play.
