S U Z A N N E   V E G A
 - The Studio Albums -

Suzanne Vega, born in 1959, California, is an American songwriter and known for her eclectic, folk inspired music. Her restrained voice and unostentatious singing are familiar like a sonic fingerprint. Always open-minded towards modern production techniques she refined her frugal compositions increasingly with electronic-influenced, experimental sounds.
