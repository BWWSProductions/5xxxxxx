Every relationship is unique. Take the journey between Christina (Kaylani Lei) and Curtis (Rocco Reed) for example. Each of them relives the incredible adventure of their crazy relationship, but even the happy couple each sees things just a little differently. As we follow along, we're reminded that when you blend true chemistry with hot sex you're bound to get some crazy, mad love...

CAST : Kaylani Lei, Anthony Rosano, Asa Akira, Brad Armstrong, Dylan Ryder, Jayden Jaymes, Raquel DeVine, Rocco Reed, Tee Reel, Tommy Gunn
