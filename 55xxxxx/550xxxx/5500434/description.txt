Wiki:
Now That's What I Call Music! 37 or Now 37 was released on 14 July 1997 on audio cassette and Compact Disc. The album is the 37th edition of the (UK) Now! series. Four tracks on Now 37, "MMMBop", "I Wanna Be the Only One", "Who Do You Think You Are" and "I Believe I Can Fly", were number-one songs on the UK Singles Chart.
