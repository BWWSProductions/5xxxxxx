Genres: Horror

Dr. Lorca (Michael Citriniti) travels the globe in search of rare oddities, assisted by fellow enthusiasts Caitlin (Alli Kinzel) and her boyfriend, David (Lane Compton). But they make a deadly mistake when they unwittingly collect the remains of the original Demonic Toys. The trio's next stop is an Italian castle, where they face a demon (Selene Luna) with the power to resurrect the evil toys. Elizabeth Bell and Billy Marquart co-star.

Director: William Butler
