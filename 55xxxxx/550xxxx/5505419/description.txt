Bionic (stylized as {Bi-on-ic}) will be the fourth full-length English studio album by American singer-songwriter Christina Aguilera set to be released worldwide in June 2010. Bionic will be her first studio album in four years since 2006's Back to Basics.

"Not Myself Tonight" was announced as the album's lead single on Aguilera's official website on March 23, 2010. The song was produced by Polow da Don and written by Ester Dean. Lyrics to the song were revealed on the site on March 24, 2010. The song premiered on Aguilera's official website on March 30, 2010 and its radio premiere was on the same day. It officially impacted U.S. radio on April 5, 2010 and was made available on iTunes on April 13, 2010.
