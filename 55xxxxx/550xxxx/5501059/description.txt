A selection of manga.  There is essentially no theme to it, simply manga that I have downloaded recent from various web sites and torrents, collected together to save others the work of finding everything individually.  For some, I've collected individual chapters into volumes, for others the naming schemes for the images were screwed up enough that I threw up my hands and left them as chapters.  This is the first of several groups that I intend to bundle together into roughly DVD-sized batches.
