Panchavadi Palam

It is the best "political comedy" ever told on Indian Screen

Director : KG George

Starring : Bharat Gopi, Thilakan, Nedumudi Venu, Srinivasan, Sreevidya, Sukumari...

Plot : A henpecked politician wants his name attached to a new bridge, even if that 

means destroying another, perfectly serviceable bridge.
