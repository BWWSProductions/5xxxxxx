An Encyclopedia of Battles: Accounts of Over 1,560 Battles from 1479 B.C. to the Present

Dover Publications | ISBN: 0486249131 | edition 1985 | PDF | 543 pages | 15,4 mb










Essential details of every major battle on land and sea in recorded history, from the first battle of Megiddo in 1479 B.C. to the Grenada conflict in 1984. 


Included: strategic situations, roles played by political/military leaders, troop numbers and training, tactics and objectives, casualties and losses and military/political consequences. Index. 99 illustrations.
