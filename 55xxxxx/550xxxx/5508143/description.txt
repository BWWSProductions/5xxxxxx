Title: Beat Hazard
Developer: Cold Beam Games
Publisher: Cold Beam Games
Genre: Arcade shooter
Released: 2010
Description: Experience your music collection like never before with this intense music driven arcade shooter. Each of your songs will have its own unique ebb and flow based on the music.

Installation:
1) Run setup.exe;
2) Enjoy the game;
3) Support the producer.

Important!
Leaderboard does not work, stats, ranks and achievements do not save. If you want these features, buy the original.
