- R E L E A S E   N O T E S -

djsforfun exclusive!

Formed in early 2007, La Strada has quickly made a name for itself with raucous,
foot-stomping shows. The quintet sports a grand yet playful sound, and the live
lineup follows suit, expanding to include additional strings and horns. Recorded
at Miner Street Studios in Philadelphia and The Buddy Project in Queens, the
band s debut LP, New Home, features 13 tracks of raw emotion channeled into well
crafted pop songs. With influences ranging from ancient music to modern indie
rock, the band has garnered comparisons to Neutral Milk Hotel, Beirut, and
Arcade Fire. The group has already toured much of North America with the likes
of the Bowerbirds and Canada s Hey Rosetta! And Cuff the Duke.
