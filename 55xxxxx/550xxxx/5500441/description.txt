Description:


Paul Weller

Paul Weller has just released his 10th album Wake up the Nation and we welcomed the mod-father back to Later. Having just hit 50, he was awarded the NME Godlike Genius award this year.

Hot Chip

Hot Chip made their third appearance in the Later studio to play tracks from their recent fourth album, One Life Stand ahead of summer festival slots.

Marina And The Diamonds

Marina And The Diamonds made her debut in the Later studio. Marina was runner-up in the BBC's Sound of 2010 poll. Don't be fooled, the Diamonds are not her band but you, her fans!

Gogol Bordello

Gogol Bordello are one of the world's hardest working bands. The gypsy-punk outfit performed tunes from their new album, Trans-Continental Hustle.

Villagers

Villagers is Dublin-born singer-songrwiter Conor J. O'Brien's recording alias. He's already supported Neil Young and Wild Beasts.

Paul Rodgers

Paul Rodgers is one of the best-known vocalists of all time, having fronted Free, Bad Company and, more recently, Queen.

Polar Bear

Polar Bear feature percussionist Seb Rochford, who has played with everyone from Brian Eno to Pete Doherty. The Mercury-nominated outfit made their first appearance on Later.
