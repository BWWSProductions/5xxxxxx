The end is here, but that's only the beginning in this disaster thriller set in a not-too-distant future. When an enormous meteor breaks through the Earth's atmosphere, the whole world shares a similarly terrifying fate. With billions of lives hanging in the balance -- and the whereabouts of one man's family up in the air -- is there any hope for humankind's survival? Joe Lando and Claudia Christian co-star.
