This is the complete history of Marvel Comics contained in an interactive scrapbook. With sixty years under its belt, the Marvel Vault archive spans three generations of readers who grew up with its instantly recognizable characters including "The Incredible Hulk", "X-Men", "Spider Man", "The Fantastic Four", "Captain America" and "Daredevil". The Marvel Vault is a treasure trove of comic book history, which delves into the archives to tell the story of Marvel across the decades.

Written by Roy Thomas (Conan the Barbarian) and Peter Sanderson (Marvel Saga).
