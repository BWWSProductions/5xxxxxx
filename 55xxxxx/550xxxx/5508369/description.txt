A group of misfit Gillingham FC fans embark on a very British road trip in Steve Kelly's football comedy THE SHOUTING MEN. Making use of their new disabled 'friend' Terry's mobility bus, the diehard fans head to Wembley to watch their beloved team face Newcastle United in the quarter finals of the FA Cup.

Along the way they learn more about the volatile Terry, do battle with SatNavs and discover why roadtrips and dodgy bowels don't mix...
