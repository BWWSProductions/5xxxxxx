The Scottish quintet return home to Glasgow and record their ultimate and definitive live show at a usually intimate venue. All of their classics are revisited, including Razamanaz, Bad Bad Boy, Hair Of The Dog, This Flight Tonight, My White Bicycle, Holiday, and Love Hurts.

Recorded live in their hometown of Glasgow, this is Nazareth at their very best. Tracks include "Holiday," "Dream On," "Simple Solution" and "My White Bicycle."
