Star Trek: Fan Collective - Time Travel is a DVD collection released by Paramount Pictures. This 4 disc set contains episodes from Star Trek: The Original Series, Star Trek: The Next Generation, Star Trek: Deep Space Nine, and Star Trek: Voyager dealing with time travel.

  Episodes
1 TOS: "Tomorrow is Yesterday"
  TOS: "The City on the Edge of Forever"
  TNG: "Yesterday's Enterprise"
  TNG: "Cause and Effect"

2 TNG: "Time's Arrow"
  TNG: "Time's Arrow, Part II"
  TNG: "All Good Things..." (feature length)

3 DS9: "Little Green Men"
  DS9: "Trials and Tribble-ations"
  VOY: "Year of Hell"
  VOY: "Year of Hell, Part II"

4 VOY: "Endgame" (feature length)

Special features
  * Disc 1 contains text commentaries for "Tomorrow is Yesterday" and "Yesterday's Enterprise" by Michael and Denise Okuda.
  * Disc 3 contains text commentary for "Little Green Men" by Michael and Denise Okuda.
