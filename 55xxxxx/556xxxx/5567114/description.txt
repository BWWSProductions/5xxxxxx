Akale [2004]

Director : Shyamaprasad

Starring : Prithviraj, Sheela, Geethu Mohandas, Tom George

Plot : Neil, a middle-aged writer, struggles with the story of his life, which he has promised to give to a publisher. As he starts writing, we are transported to his past, occupied by his cranky mother Margaret and sister Rose.
