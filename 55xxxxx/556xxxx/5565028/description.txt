Game Title: The Sims 2 - Glamour Life Stuff
Year: 2006
Languages: 16 languages, including Spanish, English, Chinese & Korean.

The Sims 2: Glamour Life Stuff is the second "stuff pack" for The Sims 2. It was officially announced on July 13, 2006 and was released on August 31, 2006. As the title suggests, this pack focuses on "the glamorous life", and adds a series of new objects, clothing, and wall/floor textures relating to the glamorous lifestyle. The pack also includes two pre-made houses. It includes 33 items.
