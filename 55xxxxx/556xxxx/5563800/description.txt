Greyson Michael Chance (born 1997) is a 12-year-old singer/songwriter/pianist from Edmond, Oklahoma whose cover of the Lady Gaga song "Paparazzi" became a hit on May 11, 2010.

Career

Greyson performed "Paparazzi" at Edmond's Sixth-grade Festival, regendering the lyrics to "I won't stop until that girl is mine."

On May 13, 2010, Greyson appeared on The Ellen DeGeneres Show[1]. He has two original compositions, "Stars" and "Broken Hearts," both posted on hischannel, greyson97. Greyson stated that he has three years of piano lessons but no vocal coaching.[2] He claimed his inspiration comes from Lady Gaga and Augustana.
