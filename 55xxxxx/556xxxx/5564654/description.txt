Project Thomaz Farkas is a collection of 39 documentaries produced by Thomas Farkas  in the 1960s and 1970s, featuring various cultural events of Brazil linked to the specific reality of each region. The documentaries were organized in a box containing seven discs, and include a short interview with the participation of renowned photographer, producer and documentary filmmaker, exposing a broad universe of Brazilian culture.
