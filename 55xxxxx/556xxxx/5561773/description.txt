Product Description
Music s finest come together for a concert in honour of rock n roll legend Chuck Berry on his 60th birthday. Among the star-studded line-up are Eric Clapton, Etta James, and BruceSpringsteen, who each offer their own interpretations of his songs. Directed by Taylor Hackford (RAY, AN OFFICER AND A GENTLEMAN), this feature-length documentary paints an often fascinating portrait of Berry and his pioneering work in thegenre. Further insight can be gleaned from interviews with a host of rock n roll icons including Jerry Lee Lewis, Little Richard, Roy Orbison, and The Everly Bros. Tracks include 'No Particular Place To Go', 'Sweet Little Sixteen', and 'Johnny B. Goode'.
