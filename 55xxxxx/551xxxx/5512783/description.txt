Basic Plot Summary of Pucciniâ€™s opera Suor Angelica (Sister Angelica) 

The nuns are all happily doing their chores with the exception of Suor Angelica. Her family has ignored Sister Angelica for several years.
Unexpectedly, her aunt, a princess, arrives at the convent. Angelica is eager to see a member of her family and rushes to her.
