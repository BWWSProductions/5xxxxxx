A off-world look at the superstitious repercussions of tattooing an Ouija Board on your body.

Hagen, who has a dead wife believes that he can revive her from the dead.

Travis, a man who lost his brother and wants to join him in the after life.

Morbius, a bartender who is betrayed by those he loves comes back from the dead to take revenge.

And a strange man only known as Mr. Skinny protects the secrets of the Ouija Board and how the stories weave and affect each other.
