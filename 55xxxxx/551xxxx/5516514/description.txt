Shree DURGA Saptashati ------- SAPTA means seven ;  SHATI  means  hundred. Thus these are seven hundred slokas in prayer of  Godess Durga .  In Hindu religion these are the highest level of worship to Godess Durga.  The origin of this scripture is traced back to  MARKANDEYA  PURAN.   This collection presents some of the rarest , beautiful and complete  slokas .
