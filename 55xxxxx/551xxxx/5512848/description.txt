Dr. Spoon's English-French Baby Talk DVD Volume 1 is a fun and easy way to introduce your child to speaking and understanding two languages. This DVD includes colorful pictures, animations, video and songs designed to hold your child's attention. On this DVD children's voices speak over 250 common words combined in English and French that every baby or child needs to know for communication in both languages.  Playful background music plays as the objects and word appear on the television screen.  Recommended for kids 3 months and above.
