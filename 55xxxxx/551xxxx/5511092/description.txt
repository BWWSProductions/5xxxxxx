With his newly acquired karate skills, Kenichi Shirahama is no longer a victim of bullies at his high school, but he faces new challenges -- chief among them his adversary Hermit, who's one of the top fighters for the Ragnarok gang. Although their first battle is interrupted, Kenichi can't avoid another confrontation with his nemesis after his friend Honoka gets kidnapped. Part 1 includes episodes 27 to 38.
