Mini-series that spotlights the growth of Natasha Romanoff as a secret agent and super hero and her interactions with other leading ladies of the Marvel Universe.  The series looks at her early KGB years, as an agenf of S.H.I.E.L.D., her life as an Avenger, and finally her freelance years.

Written by Paul Tobin, with pencils by Salvador Espin.
