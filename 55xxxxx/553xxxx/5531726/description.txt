SYNOPSIS

Following the failed march back to Tibet from India led by Tibetan exiles in 2008, 
a second mission to Tibet was launched, with prolific activist and writer Tenzin 
Tsundue at the forefront. This film charts this secret journey as it follows this 
second group in their unrelenting efforts to reach the border before the opening 
of the Beijing Olympics.
