Wiki Info:

My Heart Will Always Be the B-Side to My Tongue is the first EP by American rock band Fall Out Boy, released May 18, 2004 through Fueled by Ramen. It features a total of five tracks; "Grand Theft Autumn/Where Is Your Boy" was redone from the previously released version from Take This to Your Grave. "Nobody Puts Baby in the Corner" appears on their third album From Under the Cork Tree, and "My Heart is the Worst Kind of Weapon" is featured on the From Under the Cork Tree (Limited "Black Clouds and Underdogs" Edition). A cover of the Joy Division song "Love Will Tear Us Apart" is also included. The CD is packaged with a bonus DVD, featuring the background of the band, music videos for "Dead on Arrival" and "Grand Theft Autumn / Where Is Your Boy", an acoustic performance and more extras. There is also hidden footage of the band on the eye of the angel in the main menu.
