SPB Wireless Monitor is a complete solution for measuring data traffic via all types of connections and calculating network usage costs according to your data plan.

The application allows you to analyze your spending through time-based and per-application reports, and includes table and chart views. You can monitor traffic from any period and SPB Wireless Monitor lets you export this information to HTML and CSV.

The program helps you stay within your budget by warning you when your data usage limit has been exceeded. You can apply cost and usage limits through an easy and intuitive configuration, with any number of alarms.
