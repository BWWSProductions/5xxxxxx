Plot
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The Boys Are Back is a confessional tale of fatherhood. It follows a witty, 
wisecracking, action-oriented sportswriter who, in the wake of his wife's death, 
finds himself in a sudden, stultifying state of single parenthood. Joe Warr 
throws himself into the only child-rearing philosophy he thinks has a shot at 
bringing joy back into their lives: "just says yes." Raising two boys - a 
curious six year-old and a rebel teen from a previous marriage -- in a household 
devoid of feminine influence, and with a lack of rules, life becomes exuberant, 
instinctual, reckless... and on the constant verge of disaster. The three 
multi-generational boys of the Warr household, father and sons alike, must each 
find their own way, however tenuous, to grow up.
