Jtag Tool v1.42

Coolshrimp of gueux-forum.net released Jtag Tool v1.42 - an all-in-one GUI installer to create and put freeBOOT 0.032 on your jtag'able 360.

- Abillity To Remove FreeBOOT
- Generation FreeBoot in a click.
- Nice interface.
