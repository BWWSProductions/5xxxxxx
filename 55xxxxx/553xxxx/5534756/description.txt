This is a collection of 500 oldskool hardcore, jungle and techno tracks from the early rave scene in 1990-1994 , it took me years to collect and compile this collection - I have tidied up the ID3 tags , reformatted the audio and normalized the volume as best as I could as all tracks are from various sources - what results is a brilliant catalogue of rave tracks....overground and underground (mostly underground) with awesome sound quality (compared to the C60 cassette tapes of the day..lol)
