The sheet music of the theme song of The Boondock Saints, The Blood of Cu Chulainn by Jeff & Mychael Danna.
Contains scores for the following instruments:
 - Uilleann Pipes
 - Fiddle
 - Whistle
 - Flute
 - Trumpet in Bb
 - 2 Vielles
 - Tubular Bells
 - Bodhran
 - Bones
 - Shakers
 - Tambourine
 - Snare Drum
 - Low Concert Tom
 - Bass Drum
 - Strings

21 page PDF file.
