Plot
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Norma and Arthur Lewis, a suburban couple with a young child, receive a simple 
wooden box as a gift, which bears fatal and irrevocable consequences. A 
mysterious stranger, delivers the message that the box promises to bestow upon 
its owner $1 million with the press of a button. But, pressing this button will 
simultaneously cause the death of another human being somewhere in the world; 
someone they don't know. With just 24 hours to have the box in their possession, 
Norma and Arthur find themselves in the cross-hairs of a startling moral dilemma 
and must face the true nature of their humanity.
