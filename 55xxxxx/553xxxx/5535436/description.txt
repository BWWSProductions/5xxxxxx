Classic sludge rockers Melvins are releasing their first album in nearly two years on June 1 with The Bride Screamed Murder on Ipecac Records. It also represents their third record as a quartet. Founder Buzz Osborne and Dale Crover have been joined by Big Business' Coady Willis and Jared Warren since 2006's excellent (A) Senile Animal.
In conjunction with the release of The Bride Screamed Murder, Melvins are setting out on a tour of the United States and Canada. And they will be joined by post-metal rockers Isis for most of the shows.
