                           SPB News Version 2.0.0 (Build 3175)


Spb News is a news reading Pocket PC program that gives a rich offline experience along with an easy interface and full RSS/Atom support. With Spb News you'll always have all the latest information, right in your pocket, with images and whole articles, yet fully optimized for small screen viewing


                                                             


                                       Channels:




* Full Website News

* Full RSS/Atom support (0.9x, 1.0, 2.0)

* Categorized multilingual online channel catalog

* Easy custom channel adding

* Channels search


                                                           
                                       Articles:




* Full Text Articles

* Offline reading with images, text formatting and links

* Article thumbnails

* Automatic cleanup

* Adjustable fonts
