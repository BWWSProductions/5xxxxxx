Ancient Wars: Sparta recreates the epic history of the Spartans,
Persians and Egyptians in three vast campaigns. Fight for supremacy
in the Eastern Mediterranean, using each nation's specific powers
in large scale battles. Equip your soldiers with weapons of your
choice and with the weapons captured from slain enemies.
Use environmental effects like wind and fire to your strategic
advantage. Let your workers gather resources for a thriving economy,
while you upgrade your defences with manned city walls and strengthen
your army with battleships, siege weapons and cavalry.
