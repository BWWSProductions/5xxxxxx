The Bright Lights And What I Should Have Learned is an album by Duels released July 2006. It received a positive review in the New Musical Express, garnering an 8/10 review score, 4/5 in Clash magazine, 4 stars in Time Out and was named Album of the Week in The Sunday Times, The Guardian and on XFM.
