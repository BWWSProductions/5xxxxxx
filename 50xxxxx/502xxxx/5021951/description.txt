Serenity - Culture Beat [1993]


Serenity was the second album by Culture Beat. The line up changes with Tania Evans becoming the new singer, Jay Supreme remains the rapper. Culture Beat scores a massive worldwide hit with, "Mr. Vain" which topped the charts across Europe and Australia. A total of four singles were released from the album: "Mr. Vain", "Got To Get It", "Anything" and "World In Your Hands."
The album went on to sell over two million copies worldwide.
