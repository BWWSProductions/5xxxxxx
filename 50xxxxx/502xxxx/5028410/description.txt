Robert Llewellyn hosts this 'new' game show called "Scrapheap" (Later changed to "Scrapheap Challenge").

The two teams are simply 'Yellow' and 'Orange'.

The Yellow team consists of Major Dick Strawbridge, Kali Monroe, Dave Price and guest specialist Don Mansfield. 

The Orange team consists of Anne Saunders, Richard 'Bowser' Munson, Shen Chiu and guest specialist Paul Harrington.

The challenge : build medieval turnip-throwing machines from materials scrounged in the junk yard and launch an assault on two 'castles'.
