Category	Comedies, Switching Roles
Starring	George Burns, Ron Silver, Ted Wass, Ian Giatti

In his third "Oh, God!" movie, George Burns not only plays his witty, widely praised role as God, but also the menacing, trick-playing demon himself...the Devil. Directed by Emmy Award-winner Paul Bogart, co-starring Ron Silver, Ted Wass and Roxanne Hart. Director: Paul Bogart. Actors: George Burns, Ron Silver.
George Burns plays both title roles in this amusing comedy about a singer who sells his soul to the devil in exchange for fame and fortune.
