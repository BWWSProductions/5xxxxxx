The complete third series of the popular television series, The Tudors, which looks at the life and times of Henry VIII.

Now that Henry has rid himself of the ambitious and manipulative Anne Boleyn he can turn his attention to gaining a male heir with his third wife, Jane Seymour.

However, his equally ambitious and ruthless Chancellor, Thomas Cromwell, is causing trouble for Henry with his religious reformation which bring about a rising in the north which is to be known to posterity as The Pilgrimage of Grace.

Can Henry survive this rebellion and will Cromwell go the same way as Anne Boleyn before him?
