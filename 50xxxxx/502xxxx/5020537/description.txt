Jannat is a Bollywood movie directed by Kunal Deshmukh starring Emraan Hashmi, Rizwan Ahmed, Sonal Chauhan, Sameer Kochhar and Javed Shaikh.

The story revolves around a man who is caught in a quagmire of crime and consumerism as he struggles to find heaven on earth. It borrows certain elements of the plot line from the Hollywood movie Lord of War.
