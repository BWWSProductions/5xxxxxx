Digital Versatile Doom: Live at the Orpheum Theatre 37 A.S.

Director: Meiert Avis
Starring: Ville Valo, Linde, Mige, Burton, Gas

Digital Versatile Doom (a.k.a Digital Versatile Doom: Live at the Orpheum Theatre XXXVII A.S.) is HIM's first commercially released live album and live DVD, as opposed to the bootlegged Live and Undercover which was not official. The DVD was directed by Meiert Avis in Los Angeles at the Orpheum Theater between 14 and 15 November 2007.
