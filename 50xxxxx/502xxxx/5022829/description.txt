This is George Nothere's informative booklet Privacy in the Information Age which describes several useful ways to do your business in a more discrete way as it is done by the power elite. How can we stop this decrease in our lives and retain our privacy? This book provides you with some very important techniques for achieving and retaining your privacy in the "information age" - techniques that may well prove vital to your survival. Find out how to: Choosing Remote Property; Purchasing Property Privately; Geting Set Up on the Land; Buy Foreign Real Estate; Communicate Privately; Avoid Electronic Surveillance & Countermeasures; Travel in Privacy; Defeat caller ID; Protect Yor Vehicle from EMP; Purchase and Register a Vehicle Privately; Learn About and Use Pure Trusts; Receive Your Mail Privately; Manage Your Money Privately and much more. 60 pages. A must read for everyone.
