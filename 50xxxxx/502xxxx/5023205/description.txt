Maulawi - Maulawi (1975)
MP3 320 kbps

This is the only recording of Maulawi Nurudin and his group. Originally recorded in Chicago in Sep 1973, and released by a small independat label in Detroit the following year. The music blends Deep Jazz, Heavyweight Street Funk, and Latin in a unique combination.
Soul Jazz Records are releasing this long lost classic recordings, originally released over 30 years ago with only 1000 ever pressed in the USA.
