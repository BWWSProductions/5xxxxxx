Sheherezade with Suhalia Salimpour

DVD Rip

Bellydance Performances

[color=Red]All thanks got to a friend[/color]

Lots of performances by Suhalia and her dance company. Quality is not the best, and on some larger screens may get blocky at time, I cannot help this unless the file size was huge.

I would not call this typical style bellydance or typical costuming...Suhalia uses lots of modern fusion in her dancing/choreographies. I just dont seem to "get" her dancing like I do others...I don't know...shes just not to my taste personally, but may very well be other people's.
