How to Fire your Boss - A Workers Guide to Direct Action

A pamphlet by the Industrial Workers of the World (IWW or Wobblies) union that explains methods that organized laborers can use to convince bosses to meet their demands without relying on legislation.

Methods include:
*Slowdown
*Work to Rule
*Good Work Strike
*Sitdowns
*Selective Strike
*Whistle Blowing
*Sick-Ins
*Dual Power
*Monkey-Wrenching

Pamphlet is ready to be printed, assembled, and distributed.

You can visit the IWW online at http://iww.org/
