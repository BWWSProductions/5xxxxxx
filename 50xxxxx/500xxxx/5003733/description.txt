The boys get the adrenaline pumping with a spirited game of paintball in Graffiti 2, and they are going to need all of that energy for the sex that comes after. It is boy versus boy on the field but they are all lovers once the game is over. Fice scenes and tonnes of action!!

Luke Hamil, Sean Berrett, Todd Rosset, Dolph Lambert, Ennio Guardi, Henri Gaudin, Jacques Briere, Florian Nemec, Colin Hewitt, Yann Tiersen
