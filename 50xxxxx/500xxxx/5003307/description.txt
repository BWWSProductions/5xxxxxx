About the band:
--------------

Sounds Like is an independent alternative/experimental rock band from Wicklow, Ireland. Relatively new to the scene, the group prefers to have people hear their music than buy it, providing their first EP (The Critical & The Denial) for free for those who want want.




Please download and share, but do not sell :)
