Join Together is a box set of live material released from The Who's 1989 25th Anniversary Tour. Several of the tracks were recorded at Radio City Music Hall, New York, and at Universal Amphitheater, Los Angeles, with the rest from various other concerts during the tour.

It was released in 1990 by Virgin Records in the United Kingdom and MCA Records in the United States. The album was released on MCA3-19501* USA as a vinyl three LP set in March, 1990, and reached #180 in the US charts. It was also released on cassette tape, and later released on CD-MCA Records MCACD2-19501*, 1990 USA as a two-CD set. The box set included a full color booklet including credits and pictures from the tour, and was dedicated to Keith Moon.

All songs by Pete Townshend unless otherwise indicated
