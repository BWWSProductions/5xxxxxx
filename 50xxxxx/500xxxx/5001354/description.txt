Nine poems by Edgar Allan Poe was the influencing spark to nine dark piano pieces by composer Fredrik Klingwall. A beautiful musical gestalt of the dark, decaying and frail human psyche.



   1. The Sleeper
   2. Alone
   3. Spirits Of The Dead
   4. An Enigma
   5. The Conqueror Worm
   6. The Valley Of Unrest
   7. The City In The Sea
   8. For Annie
   9. A Dream Within A Dream
