Belly Dance Television Volume 1
video news magazine (on DVD)

Spotlight - Ansuya
America's Superstar! Born the daughter of renown dancer, Jenaeni Rathor, Ansuya was always destined for belly dance stardom.

In this in-depth interview, Ansuya shares her thoughts on today's hottest topics, as well as insight into her personal life and dance history.

Rising Stars
Urban Tribal Dance Company, of San Diego, California fuses the past and the future with their innovative style.

Meet the ladies of UTDC and find out how they went from turbans to hip hop while keeping the belly dance theme.

Hip Tips
Anaheed and Paulina show you how to care for your costume when the party's over.

Get some valuable advice from Anaheed on how to prepare your sweaty costume for transportation, and what to do with it when you get home.

Feature Story
Behind the Scenes at Amara's controversial production of "An Evening of Experimental Middle Eastern Dance."

For the past several years, this show has been turning heads and raising eyebrows in the belly dance community. Now BD-TV will introduce you to the players, and show you what it takes to make this unusual event a reality.

News & Events

* Festival of Dancing Colors (and Gala Show highlights), featuring Fahtiem, Yaleil, Jim Boz, and more!

* MECDA's 26th Anniversary Party - Feiruz sheds some light on the history of MECDA; plus performance highlights of Midnight Mirage, Rania, Arturo, and the rest.

* Festival Fantasia - find out why this is the last Festival Fantasia, and get a look at some event performances, including Una, Rachel Brice, Shoshanna, and Kasia the Aging Diva.

* IAMED's Sixth Awards of Belly Dance - meet the winners and get a glimpse of each performance at this outstanding black tie affair!
