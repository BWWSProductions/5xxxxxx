Review	by MusD

From his popular platinum Christmas albums to his Grammy-winning Fresh Aire VII, Chip Davis has won a large, loyal following for his powerful and eclectic musical expressions of emotions and experiences. But not until Impressions, his first release entirely independent from The Mannheim Steamroller ensemble, has Chip revealed to his fans such an intimate insight into his creative impulse and his lyrical vision of the world. Impressions is a musical diary of compositions written for very special friends to commemorate some very special events.
