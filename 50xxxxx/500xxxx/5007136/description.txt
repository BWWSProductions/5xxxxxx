Jeremy Enigk - The End Sessions

Recorded in the same Bobcat studio where Enigk made his Return of the Frog Queen LP, only this time Enigk and his nine-piece orchestra are recorded live, August 29, 1996, in front of an audience that included radio contest winners. Broadcast on KNDD's show The End, hosted by Marco Collins.
