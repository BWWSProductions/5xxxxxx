A book about philosophy by Francois Tremblay. In .pdf format. If you enjoy it, check out more of Tremblay's work at http://www.strongatheism.net/ and his Check Your Premises blog.


Book Description:

An examination of the presuppositional current in Christian apologetics, why it fails, the counterpoint of Materialist apologetics, and an atheistic worldview (Emergentism) proposed as alternative.
