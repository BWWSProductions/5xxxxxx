Dave Matthews Band - Off The Record: A Compilation Of All Non-Album Tracks

While this compilation focuses on Dave Matthews Band, Dave has also played with other line-ups.  The following abbreviations will be used for artists on this compilation:
DMB - Dave Matthews Band
DM - Dave Matthews
D&T - Dave Matthews & Tim Reynolds
D&F - Dave Matthews & Friends

This compilation was assembled in June 2009.  I went through all the official live releases (Live Trax Volumes 1-15, DMBLive releases, widely distributed live albums like Listener Supported and The Central Park Concert, Warehouse 8 and Warehouse 5 CD's) and audience recordings and leaked material that I found on the internet.

Disc 6: Odds And Ends, Official Live Recordings
Track #. Track Name (Artist) [Release Name - Performance Date]
01. Heartbeat (DMB) [Listener Supported - 09/11/1999]
02. So Much To Say -> Anyone Seen The Bridge? -> Too Much (DMB) [Live Trax Volume 1 - 12/08/1998]
03. What You Are (DMB) [Warehouse 8 Volume 1 - 06/30/2000]
04. Everyday (DMB) [Live Trax Volume 9 - 04/23/2007]
05. Richmond Jam (DMB) [Live Trax Volume 4 - 04/30/1996]
06. American Baby Intro (DMB) [Live Trax Volume 9 - 04/24/2007]
07. The Last Stop (DMB) [Live Trax Volume 6 - 07/07/2006]
08. Kit Kat Jam (DMB) [The Gorge - 09/07/2002]

Notes:
* Heartbeat is labeled Intro on the Listener Supported release.
* Anyone Seen The Bridge is usually played to bridge the songs So Much To Say and Too Much.  It is sometimes played into Ants Marching, Tripping Billies, Halloween, PNP/Rapunzel, and others.
* What You Are has a long introduction that is performed live but was not included on the album version.
* Everyday includes an interpolation of the song #36, which is the original basis for the song.
* Richmond Jam is labeled Untitled (Jam) on the Live Trax Volume 4 release.
* American Baby Intro has grown into a much larger song that the album version.
* The Last Stop includes the reprise which is the hidden song on Before These Crowded Streets.
* Kit Kat Jam has vocals while the album version is instrumental.
