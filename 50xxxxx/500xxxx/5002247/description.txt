FCBD Tattooed One

VHS Rip

Tribal Bellydance Performances

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This first performance video by FCBD, produced in 1992, is a now a classic. A kaleidoscope of inspiration featuring an elaborate set, skilled musicians (including Susu Pampanin and Nancy Hall), adorned audience members and six of the original members of FatChanceBellyDance.
