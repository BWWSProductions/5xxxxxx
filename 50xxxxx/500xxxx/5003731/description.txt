Summer brings lemonade stands, blue skies, and a break from school. Under the scorching sun things can get hot and sweaty, too. Seek out some shade, freshen up, and relax with ten of the Bel Ami boys as you watch them take advantage of the best things that the season has to offer in Lemonade.

Troy Allen, Justin Boyd, Jean-Claude Duvall, Kevin Elola, Tobias Hayek, Fabien Lally, Dolph Lambert, Brandon Manilow, Ollie Safranov, Paul Valery
