CNBC and Fortune Magazine Present: Fastest Growing Companies of 2009 
Hosts Tyler Mathisen and Andy Serwer profile the five businesses that topped Fortune magazine's annual list of fastest-growing companies

Each September Fortune Magazine publishes its Fastest Growing Companies list. This year CNBC and Fortune collaborated on a one-hour primetime special profiling five companies that made it onto the list. The companies are ranked on revenue, earnings growth, and their stock returns over the past three years. 

See which sectors of the economy are still booming and go inside the hot companies that are taking full advantage of their position by posting sizzling growth in a slow-growth world. 

CNBC Managing Editor, Tyler Mathisen and Fortune Magazine Managing Editor, Andy Serwer will co-host the primetime special.
