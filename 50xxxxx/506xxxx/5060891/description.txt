The much-loved comic book hero Tony Stark came to the small screen on April 24th, 2009. The series stars a 16-year old version of Tony and his friends, Jim Rhodes and Pepper Potts, who are also portrayed as much younger than in the comic books. With the help of Jim and Pepper, Tony saves the day numerous times as the invincible Iron Man!

Episodes are in AVi format for easy watching.

Episodes 1-8,11, and 13 have the 2 minute prequel to the show that were not shown in Australia
