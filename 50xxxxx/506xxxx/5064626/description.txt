Strap in for hours of frantic matching fun playing Hedgehogs in Space! It is your task in this wacky arcade puzzler to collect the elements your greedy boss needs to amass untold riches and cleanse the cosmos of pesky hedgehogs. So soar off in your rocket ship and visit five planets, where you'll gather matching elements by drawing lines between them and battle angry hedgehogs by capturing bonus items within your outlines.

* Five planets
* 25 levels
* 25 awards
* Original music
* Vibrant graphics
