Color Your World with Natural Church Development
"What is the church? The church is people. What determines the health of a church? The health of the heads, hands, and hearts of those people. How can we enhance the health of a church? By enhancing the health in our heads, hands, and hearts." With Natural Church Development, Christian A. Schwarz has introduced a fascinating approach to church growth that really works and has been accepted all over the world. Now he shows how the biblically-based principles of Natural Church Development can also be a blessing for the spiritual development of the individual believer
