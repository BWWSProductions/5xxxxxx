Homeworld 2 is a real-time strategy computer game sequel to Homeworld,
developed by Relic Entertainment. It takes place after the events in
Homeworld and concerns Hiigara's response to a new enemy called the Vaygr.
It features enhanced graphics and audio and its gameplay takes a new
direction. Vaygr and Hiigaran ships differ significantly in design and
application, in contrast to the closely equivalent Kushan and Taiidan
forces of the original game.
