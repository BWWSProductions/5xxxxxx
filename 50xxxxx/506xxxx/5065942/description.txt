Auto Drive Hellas v1.80 (07/31/09)

DESCRIPTION

Cities updates: In total, 4087 navigable cities / towns / villages (see the detailed list for the larger cities included in this version).
Road network updates: Important improvements and additions in areas of Attica (street numbers and restrictions in areas of West Attica, Mesogeia, and many central municipalities), Thessaloniki, Ioannina, Igoumenitsa, Arta, Preveza, Crete, Serres, Kilkis and Chalkidiki.
As with every update, this version includes all recent changes in Egnatia Motorway ("Via Egnatia"), the new tunnels in Keratsini, and the ring roads of Agrinion and Edessa.
Points of interest: Updates to reflect changes in non-static POIs that have been modified, lodging and event halls updates based on new data collection procedure and new additions (mainly police stations, public services, post offices, medical centers, hairdressers & beauty salons). Also many updates regarding franchising chains such as Starbucks, Fridays, CostaCafe, Vodafone, Germanos, ElectroWorld, MediaMarkt etc. Also many new additions in Thessaloniki, Komotini, Arta, Igoumenitsa, Preveza, Rhodes, Mykonos and Zakynthos.
New version of road network: All country's road network is being redone at scale 1:5000. Following the example of Lefkada (v1.30), areas of West Greece (v1.40), many areas in Epirus, Peloponnese, Macedonia and Trache, Ionian Islands and Central Greece (v1.50-1.70), this version includes the rest of Thessalia and Central Greece (along with Evia and a large part of Attica) as well as NW Thessaloniki and Rhodes. The following peripheries-prefectures have been updated:

Periphery of Peloponnese: 100%
Periphery of Epirus: 100%
Periphery of West Greece: 100%
Periphery of West Macedonia: 100%
Periphery of Thessaly: 100%
Periphery of Central Greece: Nearly 100% (a small area of N Evia remains)
Periphery of East Macedonia and Thrace: Rodopi (100%), Evros (100%), Xanthi (75%), Drama (90%), Kavala (70%)
Periphery of Central Macedonia: Imathia (100%), Thessaloniki (50%), Pella (55%), Pieria (40%), Chalkidiki (40%), Serres (35%), Kilkis (30%)
Periphery of Ionian Islands: Kerkyra ("Corfu") (100%), Kefallinia (100%), Lefkada (100%)
Periphery of North Aegean: Chios (85% - island of Chios)
Periphery of South Aegean: Dodekanisa (50% - island of Rhodes)
Periphery of Attica: 70% Piraeus (45% - area of "Argosaronikos")


Note: The majority of corrections based upon users' feedback received up to 07/20/2009 have been included. Pending corrections will be included in next release, scheduled for October 2009.
