Egypt: Land of The Gods 


Tells the story of religion in Egypt, which has flourished along the banks of the Nile for over 5,000 years. Examines the four major religions that have prospered in Egypt: ancient Pharaonic polytheism, Judaism, Coptic Christianity and Islam. 

Looks at the monuments, sacred places, and symbols of the faiths of the Egyptian Pharaohs, Jews, Christians, and Muslims. Examines their shared belief in a better life after death and looks at magic's role in Egyptian life in matters ranging from health and wealth to love and war.
