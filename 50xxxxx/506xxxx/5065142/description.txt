This is a .iso of a free CD distributed by blueletterbible.org containing the textual and graphical contents of their site. CD is fully functional offline, and includes:

Fully functional search
Greek and Hebrew lexicons
Strong's concordance
Commentaries by several authors such as Martin Luther and Sir Isaac Newton
Interlinear Greek and Hebrew for every verse
Maps and images
Audio Commentaries
Devotionals
Charts and Study Tools
The International Standard Bible Encyclopedia
Several Bible translations including the KJV, RSV, ASV, Webster's, Young's, Darby's, and the Latin Vulgate

Does NOT contain the NIV. Search operates in King James only; other versions must be viewed side-by-side for individual selected verses. Requires an emulator such as Daemon tools to mount.
