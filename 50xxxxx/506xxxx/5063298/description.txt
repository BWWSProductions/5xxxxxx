Ooooooohhh.... On the TLC Tip is the debut studio album by American singer group TLC. After signing a contract with LaFace Records on August 16, 1991[1],the album was released by LaFace Records on February 25, 1992 (see 1992 in music) in North America.
It peaked at number fourteen on the Billboard 200 and number three on the U.S. Billboard Top R&B/Hip-Hop albums chart becoming certified quadruple platinum by the RIAA with sales of 4.3 million copies sold, and shipping over 6 million copies worldwide.[2]. It produced four hit singles with "Baby-Baby-Baby", "What About Your Friends", "Hat 2 Da Back", and "Ain't 2 Proud 2 Beg"
