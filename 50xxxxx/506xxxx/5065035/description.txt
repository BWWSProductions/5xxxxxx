"Finally... Out of Darkness, into His Marvelous Light" is a compilation work consisting of three subsections, including "Your Gift from YAH," "Liberty of Conscience," and "The Modern Reformation." 

Published by Creation Ministries, the book details such concepts as Christian living in practical terms, uninterrupted victory over all known sin in the life of the believer, separation of Church and State, and the three angel's messages of Revelation.
