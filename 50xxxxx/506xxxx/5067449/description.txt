This torrent contains the comic releases from Diamond's "Premier" Publishers, namely Dark Horse; DC; Image; and Marvel, from January 5, 2005. It's the earliest that I have records for. 

You can view the listing on their website, here: http://previewsworld.com/public/default.asp?t=3


At this time, my intention is to continue to put these up, in order, until I can catch up with the currently seeded DCP and/or Minutemen packs. I don't know how long I'll continue with this project, I only just started it. But let's have some fun in the meantime. One thing to note, when it came to trade paperbacks, I relied on sources such as Wikipedia and Amazon for a listing of what the trades contained. I then put those particular comics in a folder with the name of the trade. Of course, it goes without saying that none of this would be possible without the original scanners and uploaders. So a big thank you goes out to them.
