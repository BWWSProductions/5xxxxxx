2002 Winter Special Musical Tanjou! Ankoku no Princess Black Lady Kaiteiban -Wakusei Nemesis no Nazo-

(Come Forth! Dark Princess Black Lady Revision -Enigma of Planet Nemesis-)

The first musical revision of Marina Moon (4th Stage). Inspired by the Romance/2nd season of manga, the Sailor Senshi and Black Moon Ichizoku clash for control of the 21st and 30th centuries. Featuring Four Ayakashi Sisters, full Black Moon Clan (Demand, Esmeraude, Safir, Rubues, Wiseman), Black Lady, and Outer Soldiers.
