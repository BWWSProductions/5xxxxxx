Description:
A side splitting animated action comedy that follows the quest of a lovely yet humble young girl named KG, the Kung-Fu Girl.With the help of her brave yet zany Master Panda, KG must rescue the handsome Prince Po and restore justice to her beautiful kingdom. During her adventure, KG learns that there is no limit to what she can accomplish, if she believes in herself.
