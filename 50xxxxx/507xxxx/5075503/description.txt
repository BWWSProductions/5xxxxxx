Marvel Presents was a comic book anthology series published by Marvel Comics. Twelve issues were published from October 1975 to August 1977.

The first two issues featured Ulysses Bloodstone. The series was most notable for giving the Guardians of the Galaxy their first regular series, from issues #3 to #12, by Steve Gerber, Roger Stern, and Al Milgrom. Issue #7 depicted the first obvious sex act in a Comics Code-approved book, between Vance Astro and Nikki.
