Young Liars centers around a group of 20-somethings in modern day New York City, though the story quickly expands to other parts of America and the world. All of them have disturbing secrets about themselves that they keep from the others, and even the readers are left to decide what is true and what are lies.

You may think you know everything there is to know about Danny Noonan. You may think he's just a normal, gun-toting, angst-ridden kid with a hot girlfriend who has a bullet lodged in her brain. Well, you'd be wrong.Before he was all that, Danny Noonan was a normal, angst-ridden kid without all those other things. Then he met Sadie. And something so wonderful happened to him, it could only end in unimaginable horror.Creator David Lapham ("Stray Bullets"; "Silverfish") embarks on an ultra-cool trip through the American underground, painting a canvas of surreal sex and violence, daring to tread where angels fear.
