Mosby's Confederacy
Strategy|Squad-based Strategy
2008


Features :

- Turn-based Confederacy Level with unit and resource management.
* Units in towns that you can influence to support your goals.
* Availability of units for specific missions is dependent on their
town's support for you.
* Your interactions with towns, as well as casualties you send home
to them, changes the face of the region.
