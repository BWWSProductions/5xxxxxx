Enterprise Architect is a comprehensive UML analysis and design tool,        
 covering all aspects of the software development cycle from requirements      
 gathering, through analysis, model design, testing, change control and        
 maintenance to implementation, with full traceability. Enterprise Architect  
 combines the power of the latest UML 2.1 specification with a high            
 performance, intuitive interface, to bring advanced modeling to the whole    
 development team. It is a multi-user, visual tool with a great feature set    
 (see below), helping analysts, testers, project managers, quality control    
 staff and deployment staff around the world to build and document robust,    
 maintainable systems and processes.
