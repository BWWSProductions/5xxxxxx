The Prowler is a criminal turned superhero by the name of Hobie Brown.  He debuted in Amazing Spider-Man #78 (November 1969).  Hobie Brown was created by Stan Lee, John Buscema and Jim Mooney, following a "suggestion" by a young John Romita, Jr.

This mini-series follows Hobie as he fights an imposter and a hostile takeover of his company.
