DJ Skitz is a British DJ. He was born in Cambridgeshire and started DJing in 1991. He has compiled a number of British Hip Hop compilations and other Hip Hop compilations. The album Countryman is considered by Rodney Smith of Roots Manuva to be "one of the greatest hip-hop records of all time". Along with Rodney P he produced the first track to ever be played on BBC Radio 1Xtra in 2002. They also presented a weekly show on the station for a number of years. In 2008 he competed in the DMC UK DJ Championship. He has also created his own record label, Titan Sounds.
