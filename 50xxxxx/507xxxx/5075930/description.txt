NASA World Wind is a fascinating free program that offers satellite images of the Earth.
NASA World Wind is a freeware which displays high-resolution satellite images of the Earth. Depending on the settings selected the images will show borders, place names and accurate positioning information. You can download and store the images on your hard drive with the click of the mouse. You will need a fast Internet connection (DSL / Broadband), large memory capacity and a 3D graphics card to make the most of this tool.
