Greatest Hits - Jazzy Jeff & Fresh Prince [1998]
	

Greatest Hits is the first compilation album by DJ Jazzy Jeff & The Fresh Prince. The album includes several of their biggest hits including "Girls Ain't Nothing but Trouble", "Parents Just Don't Understand", and "Summertime". This also includes three never-before released tracks and two solo tracks by Will Smith from his major-motion picture film Men In Black.
