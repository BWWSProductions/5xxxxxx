Ruthless by Law is the second album by the RBL Posse. It was released on October 25, 1994 for In-a-Minute Records and was produced by Black C, Mr. Cee, The Hitman, The Enhancer, TC and Cellski. Ruthless by Law was a success, gaining positive reviews as well as peaking at #197 on the Billboard 200, #23 on the Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums and #3 on the Top Heatseekers
