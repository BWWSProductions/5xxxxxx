Nanny 911 is a new time management game inspired by the famous reality TV show. Get ready to help lots of families with their rebellious and unruly children.
In Nanny 911 game you will step into the shoes of one of the three nannies and visit many families who really need your help. Will you cope with your task? Can you show parents how to discipline the naughty kids, how to teach them good manners? 
With loads of challenging levels to play, addictive time management gameplay and bright graphics, Nanny 911 will put your pedagogical abilities to test.
