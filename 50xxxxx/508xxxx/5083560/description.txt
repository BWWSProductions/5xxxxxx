Rugby League-Challenge on the PSP is an official NRL game with all the official clubs and players with the quality of 3D graphics and fast, furious, totally addictive game. 

Rugby League Challenge allows players to play through an entire season of rounds and the final set, leading to the Grand Final. 

Use strategy to manage their clubs and train the key players in key positions to improve their own attack and defense styles. 

* All official teams in all official languages NRL and Super League teams are present and available to play, and play against those that led to the final

* Change of Player Statistics: Players can make strategic decisions about players training in various areas. These statistics are represented in the game with players to physically move faster, take more and more accurately and are more likely to succeed in combat or resist deals 
* Multiple camera angles: Rugby League Challenge has several key camera modes available in the options menu and be able to move through them during the game itself. There are multiple camera angles ranging from very close to the player to zoom and wide angle zoom out too
