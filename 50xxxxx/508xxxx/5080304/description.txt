On September 1 2009, the Internet Channel was again made available for free, users who bought it when it cost 500 Wii Points are to be compensated with a free Nintendo Entertainment System game on the Virtual Console, worth 500 Wii Points, at the end of October. In addition it updated Flash Player to Adobe Flash Lite 3.1 which corresponds to a full implementation of Adobe Flash version 8 with certain features of Flash 9.

This WAD version is for those who don't wish to update their console to use the latest version. To install, download WAD Manager from http://wii.waninkoko.info/ and install the WAD as per the instructions in the readme.
