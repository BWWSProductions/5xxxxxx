Guitar Hero 3 ISO with different songs and stuff.
You need a modded Wii to play this.
 
http://img36.imageshack.us/img36/4752/coverr.png 
http://img401.imageshack.us/img401/390/cover3d.png


ID6 has been partly changed to XKBE52, So it can co-reside of a WBFS drive with other GH3 ISOs. But it will use existing GH3(U) save data if there is any.

This one is a collection of Kamelot and Bullet for My Valentine.  Most of career mode is the former, with the last tier and the bonus being the latter.

PLEASE MAKE NOTE- I take no credit whatsoever for arranging the note charts. The lovely people at the FoF forums did all the heavy lifting. All I did was put their work into a Wii game.

Also, there are a few other things done.  For one, GH3 was made 2 years ago and to account for inflation, the point values have been increased.  And since the song I put for the final battle was not as long as the original battle, it was impossible to beat Lou, so I changed his boss multipliers to equal Slash.  But he is still pretty damn tough considering the chart you play him with.  

Thanks to the FoF forum and teamGhost for making this possible.
