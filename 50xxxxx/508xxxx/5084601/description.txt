BRIT Award-winning pop singer Mika's second album 'The Boy Who Knew Too Much' follows up the enormous success of 2007'sdebut 'Life In Cartoon Motion'. Produced once again by GregWells (Katy Perry, Rufus Wainwright), Mika continues to excell in bringing his own brand of fun and chaos to proceedings. The music on offer is undeniably catchy, although singingalong will prove quite a task as the Beirut-born star has asuperb vocal talent that is hard to match. Includes 'We AreGolden'.
