Episodes Comprise:

1. All Aboard with the Steam Team:

Includes - Thomas to the Rescue, Thomas and the Tuba, Percy's New Whistle, Henry and the Wishing Tree, Squeak, Rattle & Roll and Thomas and the Firework Display

2. It's Great to be an Engine:

Includes - Too Hot for Thomas, Gordon Takes Charge, James Goes Too Far, Emily's Adventure, Edward the Great, Percy and the Magic Carpet and You Can Do It, Toby!

3. Peep, Peep, Hurray! Three Cheers for Thomas:

Includes - Thomas Saves the Day, Halloween, Chickens to School, Don't Tell Thomas, Spic and Span, Thomas and the Circus
and Fish.
