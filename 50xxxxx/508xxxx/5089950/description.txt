This is the second volume in this series which examines in depth the German Blitzkrieg tactic during World War 2. Using contemporary photographs, allied with line drawings and detailed analysis, the author explores how the strategy evolved during the course of the campaign. 

About the Author
Will Fowler has specialised in military history and defence technology since 1972. He was Army editor of Defence magazine and has written a large number of books covering military equipment and conflicts. A former member of the Territorial Army, he served in the Gulf War and was decorated with the Joint Service Commendation Medal by the US Central Command. He is the author of all the volumes in the 'Blitzkrieg' series. He lives in Hampshire.
