In The Joy of Science, award-winning Professor Robert M. Hazen provides you with a clear overview of the key principles underneath all of modern science, from Newton's laws of motion to the latest marvels of contemporary genetic research. These 60 lectures enhance your understanding of all the physical and biological sciences, including physics, biology, chemistry, and thermodynamics. This course is a comprehensive, integrated, and above all, enjoyable introduction to the many marvels of science.
