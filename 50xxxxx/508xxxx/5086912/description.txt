Mashi Hadi (Walking Next To Me)

Bitfakkar Fi Eih is Nancy Ajram's seventh and multi-award winning album. It earned the World Music Award of 2008, which was Nancy's first in her career. This album had a much more mature theme for Nancy, a necessary thing for her career as media and fans have requested this after her previous album dedicated to children came out. Bitfakar Fi Eih came two years and a half after Atabtab, and the long-awaited comeback album held huge change in Nancy's style presenting her matured vocal abilities in a much clearer way than her previous albums. It has a wide mix of several different styles ranging from dance, beat, pop, to drama, romance, Tarab and oldies.
