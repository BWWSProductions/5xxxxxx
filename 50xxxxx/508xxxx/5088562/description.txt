Description:

Easy Time Control Enterprise Edition Software is an advanced Time and Attendance tracking solution that increases efficiency, tracks employee attendance more accurately, and allows businesses to effectively manage their workforce. The business rule based options provided by the system are essential when dealing with numerous employees and multiple schedules. Business rules, such as employee schedules, multiple working shifts, grace periods and overtime rules can be defined at the company, organization, and supervisor level.

Easy Time Control Enterprise Edition Software can be implemented as a stand alone module or integrated to your payroll and/or any existing software or database. When integrated, the products utilize one shared database which eliminates redundant data entry. Integration to the other applications ensures a company's payroll and HR departments are always working with current employee information. Easy Time Control Enterprise Edition will also work with different biometric time clocks.

Easy Time Control Enterprise Edition Software will enforce company rules, regulations, and policies for both time and attendance. Easy Time Control Enterprise Edition Software will make your business EASY!
