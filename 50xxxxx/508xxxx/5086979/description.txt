JONAS tv series

"Karaoke Surprise" aired in September 6, 2009.

For their 15th Friendship Anniversary, Joe suggests to throw a party for Stella and enlists the help of Macy. The more time Joe and Macy spend together makes Stella suspicious and jealous, so she attempts to get it out of Joe and Macy. Stella realizes that they should remain good friends after Joe sings his rendition of "Give Love A Try". Meanwhile Nick and Kevin bet who's better at Hackeysack and Kevin takes a kung-fu twist to the competition.
