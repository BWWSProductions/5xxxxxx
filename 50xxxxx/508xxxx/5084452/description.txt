PC System Requirements

Minimum Requirements

    * CPU: 2.4 GHz (single-core)
    * RAM: 512 MB (XP) or 1 GB (Vista)
    * GPU: GeForce 6600 or greater, ATI Radeon 9800 Pro or greater, Supports shader model 2.0 or above,
    * VRAM:128 MB
    * Video: DirectX 9.0c compatible
    * Sound: DirectX 9.0c compatible
    * Input: Keyboard or dual-analog gamepad
    * Optical Drive: 8x or faster DVD drive
    * Hard Disk: 4.4 GB or more free space
    * Internet: Broadband connection required for Installation and Online Play 

Recommended Requirements

    * CPU: 3.2 GHz single-core or 2.4 GHz dual-core
    * RAM: 1 GB (XP) or 2GB (Vista)
    * GPU: GeForce 7800 or greater, ATI Radeon x1800 or greater, Supports shader model 2.0 or above
    * VRAM: 256 MB
    * Video: DirectX 9.0c compatible
    * Sound: DirectX 9.0c compatible
    * Input: Keyboard or dual-analog gamepad
    * Optical Drive: 8x or faster DVD drive
    * Hard Disk: 4.4 GB or more free space
    * Internet: Broadband connection required for Installation and Online Play
