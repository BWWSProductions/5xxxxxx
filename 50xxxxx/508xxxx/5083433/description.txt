Blueprints Surgery 5th Edition 2009 
Publisher:Lippincott Williams & Wilkins | Pages:320 | 2009-01-01 | ISBN:0781788684| CHM | 48 MB

Part of the Blueprints series, Blueprints Surgery provides a concise review of what students need to know in their surgery rotations or the Boards. Each chapter is brief and includes pedagogical features such as bold key words, tables, figures, and key points boxes. This edition has been thoroughly updated and significantly expanded, with more detail and depth of coverage, additional tables and figures, and case studies. A question-and-answer section at the end of the book includes 100 board-format questions with complete rationales for each answer choice. A companion Website includes a question bank and fully search-able text.
