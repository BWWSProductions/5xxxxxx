New series. In the first of five films following people who push themselves to the physical and mental limits of human capability, Jeb Corliss aims to fly down the jagged face of the Matterhorn Mountain, Switzerland, in a winged Teflon suit. His preparations hit a stumbling block when he is arrested for attempting to base jump off the Empire State Building.
