Test Disc for use by Beta Testers and other NMT Owners.

The files are split between bitrate testing files, Audio Testing Files, Subtitle files and a couple of calibration clips.

The Bitrate files are MKV/M2TS files and start at 20Mb/s and go up to 90Mb/s

The Audio files include most formats including HD audio.

Subtitles files are embedded sub/idx in MKV, external srt with AVI, external sub/idx with AVI, and SSA subtitle files.

Calibration are a couple of files that were in my test folder.

Total size 3.9Gb.
