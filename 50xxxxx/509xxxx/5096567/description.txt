"Chillin" is a song by American rapper Wale. The song features singer Lady Gaga and serves as the lead single from his debut album, Attention: Deficit. It was officially released on June 2, 2009. A remix of the song is present on Wale's Back to the Feature mixtape, under the name "Chillin (Catch vs 9th)". The single has received mixed reviews from critics. The accompanying music video for the single features both Wale and Gaga in various locations in or near Washington D.C..

The video was directed by Chris Robinson. A thirty-second look at the video was later posted on the official MTV Samedi website.

The full video was posted on Interscope'schannel on the June 2, 2009. It features Wale and Gaga on a pier in Chelsea, Massachusetts and Wale in various venues and streets in Washington D.C. This includes one scene in the famous hidden clothing store Bodega in Boston where Wale tries on various clothes. One of Gaga's trademark Great Danes also appears in the video in Beats by Dr. Dre headphones. Several posters of Barack Obama and a banner which depicts a line from the famous Martin Luther King, Jr. speech "I Have a Dream" are also featured.

In August, the song charted at #12 on Billboard's 'Hot Videoclip Tracks'.
