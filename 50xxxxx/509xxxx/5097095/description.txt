"Sugar" is a hip-hop song by American rapper Flo Rida, featuring R&B singer Wynter Gordon. The song was written by Flo Rida and Jackie Boyz and was produced by DJ Montay for the rapper's second album, R.O.O.T.S.. The song was released as the album's second official single in March 2009 as a digital download.

The song's chorus samples the song "Blue (Da Ba Dee)" by Italian electronic music group Eiffel 65. The single became the second top 10 single from R.O.O.T.S., reaching number five on the U.S. Billboard Hot 100, fueled by high sales from digital downloads. The single has also been successful in other countries, reaching the top ten in Canada, and on other Billboard subcharts. Following the song's success in the U.S., the song was released on the May 25, 2009, in the United Kingdom.
