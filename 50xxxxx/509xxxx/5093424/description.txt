Cascada is an award-winning German eurodance group. They are most famous for their hit singles 'Everytime We Touch' which won them a World Music Award in 2007 as well as becoming the groups first No.1 single, 'What Hurts The Most' which stormed the charts in 2008 and 'Evacuate The Dancefloor' which recently became a UK No.1. They have so far sold an estimated 4 million copies of their two albums worldwide. Cascada consists of English-German singer Natalie Horler, and producers DJ Manian and Yanou.

'Evacuate The Dancefloor' is the third studio album released by German dance act Cascada. It was released in the United Kingdom on July 6th 2009, a week after the lead single 'Evacuate The Dancefloor'. The album has a completely different feel to the previous two, different basslines and new instrument sounds contrast with the traditional style of Cascada. Evacuate the Dancefloor made its chart debut on the Irish Album Chart on July 9th 2009 at No.16 and the album debuted on the UK Album Chart on July 12th 2009 at No.8. 'Evacuate The Dancefloor' which was released as the album's first single, debuted at No.1 on the UK Singles Chart.
