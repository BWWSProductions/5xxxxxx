In a move Dan DiDio described as "inspirationally tied to Final Crisis," in early 2009 the villains will take over the main DC Universe titles and some will be featured in "Faces of Evil," a series of one-shots, all designed to examine the question "What happens when evil wins?"[31]

Four Final Crisis Aftermath six-issue limited series were announced at New York Comic Con 2009:[32][33]

    * Final Crisis Aftermath: Run! featuring the Human Flame, written by Matt Sturges,[34][35][36] with art by Freddie Williams
    * Final Crisis Aftermath: Dance featuring the Super Young Team, written by Joe Casey,[37] with art by ChrisCross
    * Final Crisis Aftermath: Escape featuring Nemesis, written by Ivan Brandon, with art by Marco Rudy[38]
    * Final Crisis Aftermath: Ink featuring the Tattooed Man, written by Eric Wallace, with art by Fabrizio Fiorentino[39]

The Flash: Rebirth addresses Barry Allen's return in Final Crisis.

Battle for the Cowl deals with the aftermath of the apparent death of Batman and the selection of his successor.
