A Present For Everyone - Busted [2003]


A Present for Everyone is the second and final studio album by British pop rock band Busted. It was released in November 2003 by Universal Island Records and debuted at number 2 on the UK Albums Chart] It became a triple platinum album with sales of over one million. The album was the 19th best selling of 2003 in the UK and has been certified as 3x Platinum in the UK.
Eight of the fifteen tracks were co-written with Tom Fletcher from McFly, while "She Wants to Be Me" and "3am" were co-written with production team The Matrix. "Fake" and "Better Than This" were collaborations between Matt Willis, Guy Chambers and Steve Power; James Bourne and Neve guitarist Michael Raphael wrote "Can't Break Thru".
Five songs were released as singles: "Crashed the Wedding", "Who's David?", "Air Hostess", "3am" and "She Wants to Be Me". "3am" was issued with the non-album track "Thunderbirds Are Go!" as a double A-side.
