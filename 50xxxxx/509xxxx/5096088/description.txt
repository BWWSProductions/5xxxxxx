Allan Holdsworth Live at the Galaxy Theater
Costa Mesa, CA
April 2000

Allan Holdsworth - Guitar
Dave Carpenter - Bass
Joel Taylor - Drums

Set List:

Zones
Letters of Marque
Gas Lamp Blues
House of Mirrors
Above and Below

A short live show done as part of an educational guitar clinic. A somewhat amatuer level video production but still worth a good look and listen. Stellar, world class musicianship across the board by the band. 

And seemingly a can of Coors beer sitting on Holdsworth's amp.
