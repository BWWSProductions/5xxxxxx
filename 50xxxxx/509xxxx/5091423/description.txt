Four works by one of the foremost video artists working 
today: 

Migration (1976, 7 mins.); 
The Reflecting Pool (1977-79, 7 mins.); 
Ancient of Days (1979-81, 12 mins.); 
Chott-El-Djerid (A Portrait in Light and Heat)(1979, 28 mins.).

"Compounded of many resonances, each level of meaning interwoven with myriad others, these exquisite 'visual songs' give us the elegant music of the poet's 
voice" (G. Youngblood).
