Venture behind the double doors of San Francisco's most notorious gym, The City Athletic Club, and see a world of privileges not usually available to patrons of the larger gyms -- after hours deep-tissue massage, private one-on-one glute work, hands-on stretch sessions and much, much more! Director Steven Scarborough brings you another Plain Wrapped Video classic that's a must for every collector!

Featuring: Paul Dawson, Victor Mariano, Billy Boyd, Devin Cockrum, Matt Willis, Joe Serrano, Joe Stack, Boyd Thomas, Simon Cox and Gavin Press
