This Czech coming-of-age comedy/drama, about a young man who gets a job guarding against shoplifters in a grocery store and enters into a clumsy first romance, was the debut feature from writer/director Milos Forman. Ladislav Jakim, Pavla Martinkova, Jan Vostrcil star. AKA: "Peter and Pavla." 85 min.
