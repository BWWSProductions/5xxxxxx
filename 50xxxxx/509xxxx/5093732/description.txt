At the time he recorded the music heard on this CD he was in the process of forming a new band, as can be seen from the personnel: Tenor-saxophonist George Coleman, Victor Feldman (who turned down the job) and Herbie Hancock on pianos, bassist Ron Carter, and Frank Butler and Tony Williams on drums.
