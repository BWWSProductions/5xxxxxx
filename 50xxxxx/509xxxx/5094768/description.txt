This is Gary Allen's amazing booklet Rockefeller - Campaigning for the New World Order (1974) which covers Nelson Rockefeller's bid for the presidency in 1976 and his campaign for establishing the new world order. The author exposes the Rockefeller's family and their unending lust for money and power. Find out how money can buy pretty much anything in America - including the presidency. 20 pages. A must read for everyone.
