Dub Fx is a Worldwide Street Performer who uses loop & effects pedals to create layers of sounds into music using only his voice. Originally from Melbourne Australia Dub Fx packed his bags 6 years ago and left with a microphone and an idea. Along the way he meet his partner 'Flower Fairy' who now travels and performs along side him. Dub Fx is completely independent using word of mouth to promote himself.
