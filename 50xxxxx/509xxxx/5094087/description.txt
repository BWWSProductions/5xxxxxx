Aloha from Hell was founded in 2006. The band won German magazine Bravo's Newcomer Band Contest in August 2007, and as a result secured a record deal with Sony BMG soon after.

They released their debut single, "Don't Gimme That", on June 6, 2008. It reached number eleven in the Austrian singles chart while it charted at thirty in Germany. Their second single, "Walk Away", was released on 14 November, 2008 and reached twenty six in the German singles chart.

Their debut album, No More Days To Waste, was released on 16 January, 2009. In May 2009 they won the VIVA Comet Award for "Bester Durchstarter" ("Best Newcomer").
