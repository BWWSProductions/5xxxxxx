A great WW2 dogfight game with pure next gen action, physics, graphics
and AI Heroes over Europe is the sequel to the high-selling World War
II flight combat game, Heroes of the Pacific. Focusing on the European
aerial battles of World War II from 1940 to 1945, Heroes over Europe
follows the Allied campaign from the attacks on London to the
destruction of Berlin. Players will once again sit in the cockpits of
the iconic aircraft of the conflict and be forced to survive the most
intense dogfights seen in any next generation flight game. All of this
action will take place over stunning and meticulous environments,
including the faithfully recreated cities of London and Berlin.
