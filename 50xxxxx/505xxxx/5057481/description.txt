2010 Standard Catalog of World Coins - 2001-Date ( Krause publications, 4th edition)
Krause publications, 4 edition (July 21, 2009)| ISBN: 0896898156 | English | PDF | pages 504 

This book takes you deep into the fascinating and revolutionary world of 21st century coinage. This one volume, with search-able electronic pages of the book on the bonus DVD, contains virtually all 21st century coins, including series, singles, bi-metallic and tri-metallic coins, from all corners of the world. Represented in nearly 90,000 listings, all prices are updated to current market values, and presented in five grades of preservation.
