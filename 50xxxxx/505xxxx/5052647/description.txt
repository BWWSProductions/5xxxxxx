Dualistic Nature - Industrial Music (Videos)

Dualistic Nature is an industrial act that includes raw, power-slamming, high energy, in-your-face, angry, harsh, frustrated, seething soundscapes with a nice balance of heavy beats, electronic arrangements, scorching vocals, sampled guitars and great hooks. A nice package for fans of hard industrial music. With influences from modern to old skool, the music range of noise, sound and hardness is all there for industrial music lovers.
