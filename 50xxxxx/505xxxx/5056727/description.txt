Trust Me - Craig David [2007]
	



Trust Me is the fourth album by UK R&B artist Craig David, it was released on the 12th of November 2007 and debuted at #18 in the UK Albums Chart. The album was recorded in Havana, Cuba with Martin Terefe producing.

The album was released on May 6, 2008 in the United States, but failed to make any impact on the American charts, debuting outside the US Billboard 200, but it managed to peak at #58 on the U.S. Billboard Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums. The first single, "Hot Stuff" contains a sample of "Let's Dance" by David Bowie.
