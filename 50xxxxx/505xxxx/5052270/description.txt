Led Zeppelins fourth album released November 1971.It doesnt have a title and is simply reffered to as "The Fourth Album" Each band member instead chose his own symbol with would later appear on the inner sleeve & record label.The Album cover Itself doesnt have any writing on it whatsoever.

1.Black Dog
2.Rock And Roll
3.The Battle Of Evermore
4.Stairway To Heaven
5.Misty Mountain Hop
6.Four Sticks
7.Going To California
8.When The Levee Breaks
