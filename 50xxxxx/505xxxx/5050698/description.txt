A documentary about 'Zaka', a voluntary organisation coordinating almost 1000 volunteers, mostly ultra orthodox Jews, responding to incidents of terrorism, accidents and disasters all over Israel.  The documentary reveals the day to day lives of volunteers and those that oppose their organisation.
 
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ZAKA
