From Hasselby, Sweden, Little Mike is moving up in the world and at a rapid pace. With 2 CD's under his belt in just 1 year, it is obvious that he has ambition and is not about to slow down. His music style is a wild blend with a really funky fresh spin to it. Little Mike was born and bred in Hasselby, Sweden, and started to slowly get into the basic business of music in the beginning of 2009, creating a unique vocal style..

Little Mike discovered his love for music when he was in Stockholm during his Bachelor party.

All in all Little Mike is a very outgoing person who just wants his listeners to enjoy his music and experience what he lives for....
