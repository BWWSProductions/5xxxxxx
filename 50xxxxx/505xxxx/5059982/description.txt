The Summer is here (kind of), and what better soundtrack than the sultry sounds of Latin! This is a collection of the hottest Latin tracks on one album. CD1 is the Latin classics, where as CD2 has more of a party vibe.

40 sizzling tracks including huge Latin hits from Ricky Martin, Santana, Shakira, Gipsy Kings and Harry Belafonte. Perfect for the summer BBQ or party. This is the soundtrack to the summer
