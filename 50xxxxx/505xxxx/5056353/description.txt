-------------------------------------------------------

Dr Monocle's Optical Experiment *Retail* - Inertia Game

-------------------------------------------------------


Info:
`````
Practise observation skills in Dr Monocle's Optical
Experiment: Track down over 600 objects in the 16
rooms of Dr Monocle's spooky mansion. Test your
observation skills against the clock by finding items
as quickly and accurately as you can. Requires
concentration, eye movement and peripheral vision.


System Requirements:
````````````````````
* Windows 2000/XP/Vista
* 200MB free hard disk space
* Pentium II 266Mhz
* 64MB Ram (512MB for Vista)
* 1024x768 screen resolution
* 32 Bit colour
* Direct X9 Compatible Sound Card.
