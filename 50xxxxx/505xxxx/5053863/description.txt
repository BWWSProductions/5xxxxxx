Hummin' Comin' at 'Cha is the platinum-selling first album from Xscape, which included two number-one R&B hit singles: "Just Kickin' It" and "Understanding". Both songs were also top-ten hits on the Billboard Hot 100 at #2 and #8, respectively. Two more singles followed: "Love on My Mind" and "Tonight". The album also received three Grammy nominations for Best New Artist, Best R&B Duo or Group Performance with Vocals, and Best R&B Song.
