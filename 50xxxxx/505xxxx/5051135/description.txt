Documentary which follows three people with autism at pivotal moments on the
rocky road to being accepted as an adult. 23-year-old Oli has high-functioning
autism and is finding it tough to get a job with a condition that makes it hard
to communicate or deal with pressure. Thomas has autism spectrum disorder and
is approaching 16, an age where he demands more independence from his family.

socialising are difficult.
