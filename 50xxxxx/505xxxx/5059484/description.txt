Press Play - Diddy [2006]




Press Play is the fifth solo album by American rapper Diddy. It features a large amount of guest appearances from some of the most successful names in Rap and R&B. The album was available to preview on MTV's The Leak on October 10, one week before being sold in stores. It debuted at #1 in the US with sales of over 173,009. In the UK, the album debuted at #11. "Come to Me" and "Last Night" have both reached the top ten of the Billboard Hot 100.

According to MTV.com there will be an entire remix album of "Press Play" along with a second edition of "We Invented The Remix", Diddy's highly successful 2002 remix album. He co-wrote on all the songs, according to the liner notes.

It is also his first album under the Diddy name and also the first album from Warner Music Group's Atlantic Records, which acquired his label, Bad Boy Records in 2005.
