No Parking on the Dance Floor is Midnight Star's fourth and most successful album, containing the hit singles "Freak-A-Zoid," "Wet My Whistle," "Electricity," and "No Parking (On the Dance Floor)." Also on the album is the classic quiet storm staple, "Slow Jam."
