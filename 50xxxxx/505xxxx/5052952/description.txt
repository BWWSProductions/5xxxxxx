With The Beatles is the groups 2nd album after Please Please Me.Its was released 22nd November 1963 the same day President John F Kennedy was assassinated.The album was only the 2nd album at the time to sell over a million copies in the uk,The 1st being South Pacific soundtrack.It knocked Please Please Me off the top spot meaning that The Beatles held the top spot for 51 consecutive weeks.It was the 1st Beatles album to feature a Harrison composition "Dont Bother Me".
