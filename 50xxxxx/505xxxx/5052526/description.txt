World Of Our Own - Westlife [2001]




World of Our Own is the third studio album by Irish boyband Westlife, released on 12 November 2001 (see 2001 in music).
It reached number one in the UK and includes the singles "Uptown Girl' (UK), "When You're Looking Like That" (UK), "Queen of My Heart", "World of Our Own", and "Bop Bop Baby".
"Angel", a Sarah McLachlan original (music video only), "I Wanna Grow Old With You", and "If You're Heart's Not In It" were also played in airwaves.
