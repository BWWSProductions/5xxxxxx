MTV Unplugged - Jay-Z [2001]
	
Jay-Z: Unplugged is rapper Jay-Z's 2001 music album that contains some of his past songs with live instruments performed by Jay-Z himself with the hip-hop band The Roots. The album was recorded during the taping of a MTV Unplugged 2.0 episode on 18 November 2001. It sold more than 600,000 copies.
