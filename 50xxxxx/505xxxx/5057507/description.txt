Mest kinematograficheskogo operatora aka The Cameraman's Revenge (1912)


Mest kinematograficheskogo operatora aka The Cameraman's Revenge (1912) was the fourth Starewicz film and one of his most amazing puppet animations.

In this short, a jilted husband takes his revenge by filming his wife and her lover and showing the result at the local cinema. This was one of Starewicz's first animated films, and stars very realistic animated beetles.
