Collection of Michael Moorcock Comics featuring various characters from his novels, in particularly Elric, Corum, Dorian Hawkmoon and Jerry Cornelius.

Also included are Moorcock inspired comics such as The Airtight Garage by Moebius, Bryan Talbot's The Adventures of Luther Arkwright, Midnight Kiss by Tony Lee and Grant Morrison's Sebastian O.
