ARTIST : Eva Simons

YEAR : 2009


   Silly boy is the debut single from singer Eva Simons. The song originally had been thought as a demo song for pop singer Rihanna to collaborate with Lady Gaga. However, recently it was revealed that Silly Boy is actually by Eva Simons. It is thought that Eva may have used Rihanna and Lady Gaga to get the song noticed.


The video for Silly Boy debuted on Eva Simon'spage. It features a very Lady Gaga like style but with Rihanna's vocal style. The video is set for international release August 30.
