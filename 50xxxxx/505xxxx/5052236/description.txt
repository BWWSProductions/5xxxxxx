Against All Odds [CDS] - Westlife Featuring Mariah Carey [2000]
	

Against All Odds (Take a Look at Me Now)"  was released as a duet with Irish boyband Westlife and Mariah Carey. This song  was released as the first single from Westlife's second album Coast to Coast. 

The single was successful in the UK and Ireland where it peaked at number 1, giving Westlife their sixth consecutive number 1 entry in the UK charts and making them the first act in the UK to have their first six singles enter at number 1. It also gave Carey her second number 1 single in the UK; however, to date she has only managed one solo UK number 1, "Without You."
