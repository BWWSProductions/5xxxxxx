Trancefusion Radio Broadcast, Vol. 1-4 are live recording by The Disco Biscuits; a jam band from Philadelphia. The band consists of Jon Gutwillig (guitar), Marc Brownstein (bass), Sam Altman (drums), Aron Magner (keyboard). 

They differ from many jam bands in that they infuse electronic elements into their music. This style is referred to generally as trance fusion or livetronica.

Recorded during the band's NYE run in Philadelphia, PA, 2002. This torrent contains only the music for the first three volumes and coverart for al four. If you have access to the Vol.4 please include a torrent link!
