US Army - A Military Guide To Terrorism (2007 edition with supplements), contains the following:

* US Army TRADOC G2 Handbook No. 1 -- Terrorism in the Twenty-First Century
* US Army TRADOC G2 Handbook No. 1.01 -- Terror Operations - Case Studies in Terrorism
* US Army TRADOC G2 Handbook No. 1.02 -- Cyber Operations and Cyber Terrorism
* US Army TRADOC G2 Handbook No. 1.03 -- Suicide Bombing in the COE
* US Army TRADOC G2 Handbook No. 1.04 -- Terrorism and WMD in the Contemporary Operational Environment
