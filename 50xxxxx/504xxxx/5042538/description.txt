FROM WIKI - Replicants was a four-piece band, consisting of Ken Andrews, Paul D'Amour, Greg Edwards, and Chris Pitman, which has apparently been on hiatus since 1996. This is highly unlikely though, as each member has kept busy with other projects and bands. The group has only released one album, a self-titled cover album, in 1995. The band's name comes from the verb 'To replicate', or 'To copy', making fun of the band's status as "a tribute band
