Valhalla Knights 2 features a vast and expansive world to explore, as well as 
customization tools to create the ultimate battle party. This action RPG 
sequel adds new races, job classes, weapons, magic spells, foes, armor and 
more to the Valhalla Knights universe, within an engrossing story arc. 

Controlling and choosing character aspects such as skills, race, and 
appearance, you can engage in real-time 6 on 6 battles, reliant on tactical 
combat strategies to achieve success. Supporting 2 player ad-hoc network play, 
engage in Co-op and Versus modes with friends, or simply trade items and weapons.
