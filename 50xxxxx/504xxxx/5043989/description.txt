Legends is a wonderful CD by Eric Tingstad and Nancy Rumbel. They recorded it before they became legends. Indeed, they are legends in the realm of acoustic ambience. This music is timeless; it transcends levels beyond mere wonderment. The smooth acoustics are gentle and melodic. Tingstad & Rumbel were definitely prognosticating when they titled this CD. It is the predecessor to years of greatness from this duo. ~ Jim Brenholts, All Music Guide
