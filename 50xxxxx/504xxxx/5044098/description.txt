Star of Wonder showcases the exquisite music of Eric Tingstad and Nancy Rumbel. The simple elegance of Eric's guitar and purity of Nancy's oboe, ocarina and English horn create an acoustic celebration of traditional holiday favorites. Featured selections include "Silent Night," "I Saw Three Ships," and "Little Town of Bethlehem..." ~ MusD, All Music Guide
