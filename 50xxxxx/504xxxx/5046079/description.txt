The Boys 33 (2009) (Minutemen-Molotov)
By: Garth Ennis, Carlos Ezquerra, Darick Robertson

With the rest of The Boys out of the picture, the surviving members of Payback find themselves at an advantage of four to one- unfortunately for them, the one is Butcher.

As the carnage unfolds, Vought struggle with ongoing internal divisions, Hughie calls in reinforcements, and Annie finds herself up against the Seven's biggest guns- but an unexpected ally may yet save her honor...
