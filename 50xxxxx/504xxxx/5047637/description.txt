I Fight Dragons is a Chicago-based band that used a mix of modern rock, pop, daily happenings, and classic videogame music. In fact, two of their instruments are actually NES controllers, which can do wonders onstage and in their songs. 

The album on here, "Cool is Just a Number - EP" has five songs, including "Heads Up, Hearts Down" and "The Faster the Treadmill..."

The zip folder has both Windows and MacOSX versions. Go into the zip folder, and copy and paste the songs or whatever else is in there to whereever you want.
