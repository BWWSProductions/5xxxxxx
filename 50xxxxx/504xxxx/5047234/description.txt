Canadian Composer Justin Taylor shocked the classical music world early in 2009 when he began to get noticed by industry insiders as one of the most talented young composers working today, at only 24 years old. In June 2009 he released his cd "Compositions" featuring a few of his compositions played on synthesizer. His popularity has soared throughout Europe as well as his home country and the north eastern U.S.
