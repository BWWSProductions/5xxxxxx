Omnibus: Curiouser & Curiouser: Lewis Carroll 1898-1998

In the centenary year of Lewis Carroll's death, this Omnibus series arts documentary programme delves into the life and works of the author of some of the world's most famous children's books. The film features archive footage from several Alice films; an archive interview with Alice Liddell; and new contributions from Alice's grand-daughter Mary Jean St Clair, Pamela Collingwood and Philip Dodgson Jacques, Carroll's great-niece and great-nephew, painter Graham Ovenden, illustrator Ralph Steadman and biographer Michael Bakewell.
