REVENGE OF TRUE METAL RECORDS is a brand new company dedicated in the obscure underground heavy metal of the 80s. Our goal is to maintain this secret knowledge of the pure heavy metal elitism, furthermore, to help the new generation to re-discover their roots.
The FORGOTTEN METAL COLLECTORS SERIES "Outstanding Metal Gems" is the encyclopedia of the unknown, forgotten and disappeared heavy metal history. Now, we all know that this history was hidden because of the boycott of the mainstream labels and press. Some of those productions perhaps are far away of the extra crystal sound of modern plastic bands... or perhaps they sound quite "garage" and primitive to your ears... but who gives a shit?! This is heavy metal and if you have another opinion, just rot in hell, we don't need you!
By supporting FORGOTTEN METAL series you are helping keep alive the essence of the underground metal cult. Keep in mind that by supporting the "bigger" record labels, you may be supporting trend-following business men who truly do not care about what direction metalheads to. Whether Metal dies or not, they will always have other non Metal music to offer.
Anyway, if you are possessed metalheads vomiting on nowadays trendy scene join us!!!
OLD MIGHTY METAL SPIRITS NEVER DIE! WE HAIL THEM, WE ADORE THEM. JOIN US, JOIN THE CIRCLE OF TRUE METAL WORSHIPPERS!
Note: if you hear some noises, scratches or little errors -> this is not because of ripping failures, it's on the CD! Some sources was not available from the original master tapes, so the label uses other sources (with permission).
