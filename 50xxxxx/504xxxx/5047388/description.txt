A short documentary from the Twin Peaks Season 1 DVD box-set

"An Introduction to David Lynch" is composed of interviews with friends, co-workers and academic types who attempt to nail the director down.

Featurette featuring interviews with Catherine Coulson, Kyle MacLachlan, University of Texas film professor Charles Ramirez Berg, Lesli Linka Glatter, Miguel Ferrer, Michael Anderson, Duwayne Dunham, Sheryl Lee, Peggy Lipton, and Richard Beymer.
