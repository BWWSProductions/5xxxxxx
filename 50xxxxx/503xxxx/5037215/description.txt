The Very Best Of - Fleetwood Mac [2002]



The Very Best Of Fleetwood Mac Is A Compilation Album Released By Fleetwood Mac In 2002 To Promote Their Then-Upcoming Album Say You Will (2003). It Was Released As A Single Disc In The United Kingdom. Fleetwood Mac Is The Original Supergroup With A Career Spanning Over 25 Years And The Album Also Contained A Few Songs From The Peter Green-Led Blues Era Of Fleetwood Mac To The Later Multi-Million Selling Line Up With Stevie Nicks And Lindsay Buckingham.
