This video explains an ineresting and complex pollination process involving insects and milkweed. Pollination in this common plant is very similar to the pollination of Orchids. The video is designed as a resource for teaching plant biology, particularly plant-insect relationships.
Milk weed makes an interesting addition to a wild flower garden. Collecting seeds in the Autumn is easy.
