Songs: I Got a Woman, A Song For You, It Hurts To Be in Love, Georgia on My Mind, The Good Life, Your Cheatin' Heart, They Can't Take That Away From Me, It Had To Be You (with Diane Schuur), You'd Be So Nice To Come Home To (with Diane Schuur), Till There Was You, Say No More, Blues For Big Scotia, If You Go Away, All I Ever Need Is You, Love in Three Quarter Time, America The Beautiful.
Straddling the musical categories of jazz, soul, and rhythm 'n blues, Ray Charles is a nearly undisputed musical genius. The blind singer-songwriter-pianist, who has become branded "The Legendary Genius Of Soul," has earned countless awards and endless accolades. IN CONCERT was filmed live in Miami, Florida as a benefit for the Miami Lighthouse For The Blind. Not only does Charles perform most of his class hits, including "Georgia On My Mind," and "I Got A Woman," he also indulgences in a stunning saxophone solo and some moving duets with jazz singer Diane Schuur.
