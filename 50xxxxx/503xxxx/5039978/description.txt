WaRlOrD presents: Tears for fears: The collection
            -------------------------------------------------

The '80s pop of Tears For Fears shot to the top of the charts in many different countries, and this album collects some of their best songs.

This collection not only provides a bumper selection of the band's UK Top 40 hits, eight featured ...    Full Descriptionhere including the Top 5 hits, 'Shout', 'Mad World', 'Sowing The Seeds Of Love', & 'Change', but also works as a fantastic career overview as it also features B-sides & album tracks from across their four hit albums, 'The Hurting', Songs From The Big Chair', 'Seeds Of Love' & 'Elemental'. 17 tracks. Spectrum. 2003.
