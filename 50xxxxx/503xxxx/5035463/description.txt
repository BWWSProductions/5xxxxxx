BattleTech
: 25 Years of Art & Fiction is the ultimate chronicle of the BattleTech/MechWarrior universe. Everything from a complete history of the computer games, to a timeline through the Dark Ages and more. Full-color, hard-back and coffee-table-sized at over 10" by 12", its 304 pages are brimming with hundreds of color and B&W illustrations, and 18 new stories by fan favorite authors that span BattleTech's 25-year legacy.
