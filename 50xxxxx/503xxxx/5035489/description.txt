An in-depth look at one of history's most dangerous minds.

This documentary probes the unspeakable atrocities of a simultaneously repellent and fascinating madman, Adolf Hitler. His lust for power and extreme capacity for hatred, backed by military might, resulted in a permanent legacy of bloodshed and horror. 

The film examines the twisted thoughts and passions of a fanatic responsible for countless crimes of inconceivable cruelty.
