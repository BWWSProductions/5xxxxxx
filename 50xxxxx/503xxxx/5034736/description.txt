   "One Time" is a single performed by Canadian artist, Justin Bieber. It is the first single released from his upcoming album "My World."  The music video was shot in California by director Va$htie. The single was released July 7, 2009.


The video starts off with Justin playing video games with his friend Ryan. He gets a call from mentor Usher telling him to watch his house while he's gone, instead Justin has a house party. He meets a girl that he likes and they hang out together, she leaves after a while. Usher arrives at the end of the video and catches Justin and the two high five.
