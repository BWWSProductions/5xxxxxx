Few mixtapes downloaded and bundled together for your enjoyment. Jaguar Skills is a sick hip hop DJ and presents on Radio 1 and 1 Extra with Trevor Nelson. 

Each mixtape is continuous and I don't have the tracklistings yet (If I can find them I'll add). With the exception of the hip hop history one they're all about 11 minutes long.

1. 30 years of hip hop in 60 minutes
2. Michael Jackson tribute
3. Jump Up mix
4. Time to get funky mix
5. Workout plan re-up 2009

Quite frankly if you're not up and about to these cracking tunes then there's something wrong with you!
