The Cat from Outer Space (1978)

Mysterious visitor with unknown powers on our planet for supplies.... A six-pack of tuna?

An alien cat has made a forced landing on Earth and must now find a rare element to repair his ship. With the aid of a thought amplifying collar, he is able to project his thoughts and control his environment. Now he must race the clock to find this element, or he will miss his scheduled pickup with the mothership.
