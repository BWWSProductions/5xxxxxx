Isles & Glaciers is an American rock supergroup consisting of members from Chiodos, Emarosa, Cinematic Sunrise, The Receiving End of Sirens, Pierce the Veil, Underminded, and former members of The Sound of Animals Fighting, Dance Gavin Dance, Boys Night Out, and Fordirelifesake. They recently recorded material for their debut EP from December 3rd through the 13th in Seattle, Washington and again in April 2009. The debut record is being produced by Casey Bates, who previously produced Chiodos' sophomore album, Bone Palace Ballet.
