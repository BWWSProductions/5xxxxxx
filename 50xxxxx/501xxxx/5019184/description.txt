God returns to Earth to reunite an advertising man and his real estate saleswoman wife who are separated. He asks their daughter to write a slogan to help sell Him to the public. A sequel to the movie "Oh, God!" Director: Gilbert Cates. Actors: Suzanne Pleshette, George Burns, David Birney.
God makes a return trip to earth and teams up with an 11-year-old girl who promises to make him a household word by writing an advertising slogan. As her efforts rise, she gets kicked out of school and examined by a group of psychiatrists.
