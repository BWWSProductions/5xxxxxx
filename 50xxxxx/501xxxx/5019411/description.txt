Anna Christine Nalick (born March 30, 1984, in Glendora, California), is an American 

singer-songwriter. Her debut album, Wreck of the Day, featuring her first radio hit, 

"Breathe (2 AM)," was released on April 19, 2005. Nalick's second album has been 

recorded but not released date as of early 2009.
