The year is 1984. Bob is a late-night DJ who spends his nights playing old analog hits in a digital synthesized world. Each songs he plays bring back some old memories. In a given night, memories start to get out of control, taking him on a strange trip into his past.

* * *

"Flashback" is the new animated short by brazilian animator and musician Daniel Poeira. It was funded with a government grant and is being released freely over the internet under a Public Domain license. Basically it means that you freely copy and re-distribute this movie, and also make copies of it without being harassed by crazy lawyers and jurassic laws.
