A chronological examination of the central Western philosphers, covering: Pythagoras, Heraclitus, Parmenides, Zeno, Empedocles, Anaxagoras, Socrates, Plato, Aristotle, St. Augustine, Thomas Aquinas, Dun Scotus, Hobbes, Descartes, Spinoza, Leibniz, Locke, Berkeley, Hume, Kant, Hegel, Schopenhauer, Nietzsche, Marx, Kierkegaard, Pragmatism, Wittgenstein and Sartre.

3 parts, about 50 minutes each. British production.
