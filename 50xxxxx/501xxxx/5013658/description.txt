The Visions of War series utilizes archival material and state-of-the-art graphics to examine the various aspects of modern warfare and 20th-century conflicts.  

This volume features the complete mini-series "Blitzkrieg: The Lightning War," which examines the infamous Nazi military tactic as used from the 1939 conquest of Poland to the final onslaught against Berlin in 1945. Includes episodes "Poland Invaded," "France Falls," and "Russia Attacked."
