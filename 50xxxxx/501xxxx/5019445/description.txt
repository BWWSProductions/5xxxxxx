Richard Dragon is a thief who was trained in martial arts and decides to use his abilities for good. Along with Bronze Tiger and Lady Shiva he is considered one of the top three martial artists in the DC Universe.  He has trained a number of the world's best fighters, including Batman, The Question, Oracle and The Huntress.

Dragon was created by Dennis O'Neil and Jim Berry in the novel Dragon's Fists under the pseudonym "Jim Dennis." O'Neil later adapted the character for DC Comics in the comic book Richard Dragon, Kung Fu Fighter.
