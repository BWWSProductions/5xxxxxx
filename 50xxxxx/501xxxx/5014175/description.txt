The 2001-2002 was a momentous season for Arsenal FC as they brought both the Premiership trophy and the FA Cup back to Highbury. Breaking many records along the way, Arsenal turned on the style with outstanding performances from Dennis Bergkamp, Freddie Ljungberg, Thierry Henry and the outstanding player of the season, Robert Pires. This DVD collects all the highlights from this great season plus those from the 1997-1998 season in which they also won the Double.
