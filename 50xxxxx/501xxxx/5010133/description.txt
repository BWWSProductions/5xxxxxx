This is Alan Campbell's shocking booklet Spotlight on Yugoslavia (1995) which exposes the Roman Catholic Ustashi terror in Yugoslavia during World war 2 and its reemergence in civil war in the 1990s. It traces the ancient origins of Serbs, Croatians and Bosnians in order to explain why they are divided and what unites them. Exposes the events in the region during World War II and shows the role of the Papacy, revived Germany, Islam and the New World Order in the Yugoslav tragedy. Find out the amazing origins of the Balkan conflict that always result in horrible genocide. 40 pages, many pictures. A must read for everyone.
