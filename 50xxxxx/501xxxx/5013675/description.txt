This is Offty Grid's informative booklet Off the Grid (1997) which lays open useful ways to become independent in terms of energy, water supplies and other similar things. Learn how to: have your own electrical system; have hot water from your wood stove; supply your house with water; store the electricity you produce; develop a spring; store fuel; maintain your generator; have hot water by just running your wood stove. 30 pages, some pictures. A must read for everyone.
