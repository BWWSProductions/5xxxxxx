Found a good deinterlaced VOB this time.

The only thing I did unusual here was run it through a greyscale
filter to remove mpeg color errors from the video. Converted video
to x264 w/ 320CBR MP3 audio using below settings.

Props to Jody den Broeder for the remix and VJ Dr. D for the video edit.

comments are welcome... enjoy! matt
