The Visions of War series utilizes archival material and state-of-the-art graphics to examine the various aspects of modern warfare and 20th-century conflicts. This volume features the complete mini-series "World in Flames," which dramatically chronicles the major events and battles of World War II, from Hitler's rise to power to the bombing of Hiroshima.
