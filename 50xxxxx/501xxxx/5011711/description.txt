Right Said Fred Are An English Pop Band, Formed In 1989 By Brothers Richard Fairbrass And Fred Fairbrass, Later Joined By Their Friend Rob Manzoli, All From East Grinstead. The Group Is Named After A Song Of The Same Name Which Was A Hit For Bernard Cribbins In 1962.

In 1991, They Released Their First Single, "I'm Too Sexy", Which Was Released Bytug Records. The Song, A Tongue-In-Cheek Dance Song Which Takes A Gentle Swipe At The Fashion Industry, Became A Huge Hit In The United Kingdom, Spending Six Weeks At Number Two Behind Bryan Adams' "(Everything I Do) I Do It For You". The Song Also Went To Number One In The United States On The Billboard Hot 100 Chart And In 30 Other Countries Around The World. Right Said Fred Are The First UK Artist Since The Beatles To Reach The #1 Slot In The U.S. With A Debut Single.
