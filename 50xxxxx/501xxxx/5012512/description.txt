Claire Lawrence - The Claire Lawrence Band (1980)

Clair Lawrence was a founding member of The Collectors, and later, Chilliwack.  After that group's 2nd Eponymous LP, Lawrence left for a solo career.  This album appeared in 1980, and has never been issued on CD.
