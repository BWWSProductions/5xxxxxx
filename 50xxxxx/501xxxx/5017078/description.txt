The Human Target is a unique private detective and bodyguard named Christopher Chance who operates by impersonating his clients in order to eliminate threats to his principal.

Chance was created by Len Wein and Carmine Infantino and made his early appearances in back-up stories in Action Comics in 1972. Later, the feature appeared in Batman titles such as The Brave and the Bold and Detective Comics.  In 1999 a Human Target mini-series, written by Peter Milligan and published under DC's Vertigo imprint, lead to a 21 issue ongoing series published from 2003-2005.  The vertigo series was a lot more mature and dealt with espionage-related stories and the psychological effects of constantly becoming someone else had on Chance.

A new Human Target television series will premiere on FOX on Sunday, January 17, 2010 and will air on Wednesday nights after American Idol at 9:00PM Eastern/8:00PM Central. The first season is expected to be 13 episodes.
