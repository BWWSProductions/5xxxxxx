

The Ultimate Guide to Graphic Design By Nik Rawlinson
Publisher: Dennis Publishing 2010 | 196 Pages | ISBN: 1907232478 | PDF 

Design is a deeply ingrained part of the human psyche. Since the earliest days when we were painting cave walls, we have been interested in creating objects that that tell a story or simply brighten up our surroundings. The advent of the computer has brought our interest in design to a whole new level. We can now manipulate our creations as often as we like, producing a definitive copy to share with friends, family and the world at large. Covering Photoshop, InDesign, QuarkXPress and more, this comprehensive guide provides step-by-step guides to harnessing the power of your computer and using this to create professional, appealing and engaging projects.
