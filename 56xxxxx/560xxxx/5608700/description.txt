SSI Web is a powerful survey software platform. It can do general interviewing as well as conjoint analysis. The following modules are available within SSI Web:

    * General Survey Software (CiW)
    * Choice-Based Conjoint (CBC)
    * Adaptive Choice (ACBC)
    * Adaptive Conjoint Analysis (ACA)
    * Traditional Full-Profile Conjoint (CVA)
    * Item Scaling (MaxDiff)
