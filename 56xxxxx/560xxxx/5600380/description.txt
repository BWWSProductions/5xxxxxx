BBC R4 Documentary - 'Looking For Lawrence' [T E Lawrence]
Readings by Sam West
Presented by John Simpson
Produced by Christine Ocapsue
Broadcast October 15, 2005
Coded from tape at 128/44.1

An exploration of the life of T E Lawrence
'Lawrence of Arabia'  based on interviews
and recollections of family, friends and
military colleagues.

Includes recordings of Siegfried Sassoon,
EM Forster, and Lawrence's brother.
