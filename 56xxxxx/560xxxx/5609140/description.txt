Arion is an immortal sorcerer from the nobility of ancient Atlantis who was born in 45,000 BC.  Arion began as a back-up feature in the Warlord v1 #55 and was created by Paul Kupperberg and Jan Duursema.  The Arion feature continued its run until issue #62.  Arion then gained his own series Arion Lord of Atlantis beginning with #1, (November 1982).  The series lasted for 35 issues, running from November 1982 to September 1985. The double-sized special that followed the series showed the destruction of Atlantis. 

Also included is a collection of Arion's appearances.  Recently Arion was the main antagonist in the Superman Camelot Falls storyline.
