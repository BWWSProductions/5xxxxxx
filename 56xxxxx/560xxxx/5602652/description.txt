GAME NOTES

On November 9th, 1989 the Cold War was supposed to end,
it didn't. World in Conflict is the action strategy game
where players defend their country, their hometown, and
their families in the face of Soviet-led World War III,
delivering an epic struggle of courage and retribution.
You are a field commander leading the era's most powerful
military machines in the heroic effort to turn back the
invasion. One city and suburb at a time. This war isn't on
television. It's on our soil and in our backyards. Prepare
yourself for the most intense strategy game ever created.
