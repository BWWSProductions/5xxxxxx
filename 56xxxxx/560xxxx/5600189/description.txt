Akiko Tsuruga - 3 Albums - Jazz Organ

Akiko Tsuruga is a jazz organist from Osaka, Japan. A graduate of the Osaka College Of Music, she has resided in New York since 2001.

Akiko began organ lessons at age three, and started to performing professionally throughout Japan after graduating from Osaka College Of Music. While performing in Japan, she encountered and played with many noted American musicians as Roy Hargrove, Jeff 'Tain' Watts and Grady Tate, to name a few. Grady Tate has been most influential in her career. He played on her debut CD, 'Harlem Dreams' and encouraged her to move to New York.

After arriving in New York Akiko established herself as one of area's top organists. Her trio has been working many jazz nightclubs, private parties and summer outdoor concerts. 

List of included albums:
2003 Harlem Dreams
2006 Sweet And Funky 
2007 St. Louis Blues
