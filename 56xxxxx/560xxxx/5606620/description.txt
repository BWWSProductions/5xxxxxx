Rolling Stones - Old Grey Whistle Test 
October 2, 1973

The Old Grey Whistle Test (usually abbreviated to Whistle Test or OGWT) 
was an influential BBC2 television music show that ran from 1971 to 1987. 
The program was presented by DJ Bob Harris.
