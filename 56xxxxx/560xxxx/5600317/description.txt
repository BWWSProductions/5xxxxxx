
When life gets out of hand, when you need a moment to reflect, to "take five" and to be alone with your thoughts, this book will help you rediscover your inner compass. The Point in the Heart is a unique collection of excerpts from a man whose wisdom has earned him devoted students in North America and worldwide. Dr. Michael Laitman is a scientist, a Kabbalist, and a great thinker who presents an ancient wisdom in a compelling style. In these stormy days, his popular site, www.kabbalah.info, offers a silver lining for those who seek true and lasting comfort.
