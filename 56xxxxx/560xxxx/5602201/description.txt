Matt Sharp is a self titled debut from former Weezer bassist and former The Rentals frontman Matt Sharp. The album is notable for featuring no electric guitar, synths, or percussion. Instead lap steel, acoustic guitar, piano, and organ are the only accompaniment to Sharp's vocals. The album was recorded in Tennessee.
