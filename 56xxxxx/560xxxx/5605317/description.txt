Superman: War of the Supermen serves as the culmination to the Superman line-wide event New Krypton.

Following the events of New Krypton, World of New Krypton and Last Stand of New Krypton, the people of the new planet Krypton and Earth wage war after General Zod issues a formal declaration of war after discovering a human (Lex Luthor) helped destroy their planet.  Superman  himself is caught in the crossfire and must keep peace between Earth and the people that represent his heritage.

Written by James Robinson Sterling Gates, with art by Eddy Barrows, Jamal Igle, Eduardo Pansica, Wayne Faucher, CAFU and Bit.
