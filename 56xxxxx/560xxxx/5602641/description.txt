Plot:

Tells the story of operation Market Garden. A failed
attempt by the allies in the latter stages of WWII to
end the war quickly by securing three bridges in
Holland allowing access over the Rhine into Germany.
A combination of poor allied intelligence and the presence
of two crack German panzer divisions meant that the final
part of this operation (the bridge in Arnhem over the Rhine)
was doomed to failure.
