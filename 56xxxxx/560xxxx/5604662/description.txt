The Liberation Of Europe (Release 2010)(Retail)


PLOT


This special documentary series follows the same route as The Band of Brothers.
From Normandy beaches to Bastogne and the Ardennes, the Rhine and then on to Berlin.
This was an excellent presentation of the battles and strategies in Europe. These documentaries are made at the end of World War II.
There are no changes.
