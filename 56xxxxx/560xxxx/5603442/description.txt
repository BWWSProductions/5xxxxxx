Genre: Southern Hard Rock
Origin: Raleigh, North Carolina, United States

Albums:
1997 Bring it On 
1999 Take a Look The 
2003 We Drink For Free 

Members 
Karl Agell - Vocals; Scott Little - Guitar and backing vocals; Graham Fry - Guitar and backing vocals; TR Gwynne - Bass and backing vocals; John Flowers - drums

Leadfoot is a stoner rock band founded in 1995 at Raleigh, North Carolina by vocalist Karl Agell and bassist Phil Swisher, both former members of the sludge metal band Corrosion of Conformity.
