Revealing the dynamic bond between human and tree, this inspired yoga handbook offers a detailed review of the ancient wisdom of Kundalini yoga and unveils the inner power of trees, as well as their unique characteristics and energies. Yoga exercises based on this wisdom are provided, each of which operates by fostering a connection with the inner essence of a different tree, from Birch and Lime to Elm and Rowan. Full illustrations and step-by-step instructions ensure that beginners and expertsalike will enjoy positive results from each meditation and strengthen their connection with nature as a whole.
