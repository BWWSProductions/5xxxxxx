BBC R4 Documentary - 'Triumph Of The Will'
Broadcast in the 'Reel Histories' Series
Produced by John Burn
Presented by Gerry Northam
Broadcast February 12, 2005
Coded from tape at 128/44.1

'Reel Histories' is an intermittent series that compares
the 'history' shown in films with the 'real thing'.

This one is about the 1934 Nazi Party Congress and Leni
Riefenstahl's propaganda film, 'Triumph Of The Will' .
.
