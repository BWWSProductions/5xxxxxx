 Release Notes

 This high-quality compilation, created by DJ Paolo with 10 years of
 experience in Goa...

 ...collected the best Goa artists and the best psychedelic Goa sounds.

 All the tracks are created from artist with big experience in the biggest
 dance-floor of festival around the world. Like in all of the previous 6
 compilation of dj Paolo Goanmantra, Goavibration is studied to create the
 state of trance that people try to catch up from million years with mantra,
 percussions, rhythms or uninterrupted and repetitive melodies to create a
 state of trance! All the tracks in this compilation of magnificent musical
 quality with innovative acoustic effects, don't have discontinuance of rhythm
 and melody and also no breaks. From the names of the artists in this
 compilation is evident that is perfect to make explode every dance-floor,
 especially in forest parties, Goa parties and every psychedelic festival.

 Creating new dimension with this digital sound, Goanmantra guarantees to all
 the DJ and collectors of psytrance, the magical power of Goavibration!
