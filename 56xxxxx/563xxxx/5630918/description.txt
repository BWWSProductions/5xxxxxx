Laadla (1994)

Director:Raj Kanwar
Writer:Anees Bazmee 
Release Date:17 March 1994  
Genre:Drama/Family  

Laadla (The Beloved Son) is the story of Raju (Anil Kapoor) who lives with his disabled 
mom (Farida Jalal). One day Raju on his way to a job interview saves the life of a rich 
textile mill owner (Anupam Kher) who offers him a job in his factory which is run by his 
ambitious daughter Sheetal (SriDevi). Sheetal's competitive attitude and open insults 
result in her making several enemies in her industry. Raju works as a mechanic in the 
mill and confronts Sheetal several times to get the workers proper treatment.
