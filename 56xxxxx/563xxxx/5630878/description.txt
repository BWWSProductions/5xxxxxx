Save lives from every side of an emergency response with this game that gives you six different ways to play the hero. Trauma Team puts you on the frantic frontlines of emergency response and challenges you to keep cool in the face of intense, life-or-death circumstances. Every second counts whether you're playing as a paramedic, orthopedic surgeon, general surgeon, diagnostician, endoscope tech, or medical examiner. Each scenario plays out in an engaging hand-drawn style inspired by Japanese animation and comics and accompanied by dynamic voice acting. Feel the pressure of high-stakes decision-making as you explore the most intense side of medicine.
