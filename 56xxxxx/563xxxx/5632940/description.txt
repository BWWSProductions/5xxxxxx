BBC R4 Documentary - 'Laurie Lee's Long Sleep'
Readings by Laurie Lee
Presented by Christopher Cooke
Produced by Chris Eldon Lee
Broadcast September 24, 1997
Coded from tape at 128/44.1

This started off as a simple documentary about
Laurie Lee, but he died while it was being made,
and so it turned into an obituary-eulogy hybrid.

Revealing contribution from John Mortimer.

Musicians
John Law - Harmonium
Ian Watson - Organ
