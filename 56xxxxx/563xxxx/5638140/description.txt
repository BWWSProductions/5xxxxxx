Product Description
This blend of reminiscences and photographs presents a personal impression of the Korean War and aims to show the reality of the conflict, as opposed to that portrayed on the television at the time.
About the Author
Lt. Col. George Forty CBE FMA is a leading authority on land warfare in the twentieth century. A former tank officer - he had command of a tank in the Korean War, and was wounded in battle - he established his reputation as Curator of the Tank Museum at Bovington, which he brought to be one of the world's leading military museums. He is a prolific author
