== Alpha Centauri: Power of the Mindworms ==

Power of the Mindworms is a graphic novel based on the characters 
and creatures of the Alpha Centauri universe. The story focuses 
on the struggles between the Gaians and the Morganites, and a young 
Gaian who learns to control Planet's native Mindworms...

The novel was written by Steve Darnell and illustrated by Rafael Kayanan.
