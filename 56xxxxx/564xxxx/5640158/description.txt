When his best friend, a championship runner, is killed in a car accident while running home from a party, high school football star Cam Stiles suffers a nervous breakdown from the guilt and pain. It also tears apart his relationship with his father, who also happens to be his football coach.

When Cam decides to quit football and join the track team to honor his friend, his former teammates turn against him, too. Eventually, Cam goes for a sub-four that seems impossible and manages to win and lose the race at the same time...
