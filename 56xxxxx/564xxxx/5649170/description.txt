"Your Love" is the second single from American rapper and singer Nicki Minaj's upcoming untitled debut album. The leaked demo of the song was released on Barbie World mixtape in January and the full version was released as a single from her debut album on iTunes on June 1.

Many critics have criticized the over usage of auto tune in the song and it sounding like a ballad. The song currently stands at #26 on the Billboard Hot 100, becoming Minaj's highest charting solo single and third highest charting single over-all.
