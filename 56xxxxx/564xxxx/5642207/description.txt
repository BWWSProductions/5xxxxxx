Meeting The Master(s) - vol.1   Nine Inch Nails compilation

Multi-cam and single-cam videos from the NIN soundcheck series in North America during the Wave Goodbye tour 2009.
Mixed with backstage shots before the shows.
Some songs are incomplete!

05. Clarkston  (Metal)
06. Toronto  (Home/ The Good Soldier)
07. Mansfield  (Trent Reznor Q&A)
08. Camden  (Dead Souls)
09. Holmdel  (I'm Afraid of A./ In This Twilight)
10. Wantagh  (Suck)
11. Columbia  (Piggy/ Discipline/ Metal/ TDTWWA)
