Alexis Breeze - Hair Undresser
Added: June 21, 2010

"Alexis is an unhappy housewife. Her 

husband keeps a tight leash on what she 

wears, buys, and does. Tired of being 

controlled, Alexis attempts to make 

herself feel more appreciated by getting 

some attention from her hair dresser."
