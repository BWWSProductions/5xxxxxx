BBC R4 Documentary - 'The Hinge of Fate - The Battle Of Alamein'
Research by Imperial War Museum & The Royal British Legion
Written by Eric De Mornay
Presented by Eric De Mornay
Produced by Harry Schneider
Broadcast October 25, 1992
Coded from tape at 128/44.1

Broadcast for the fiftieth anniversary of the destruction
of Rommel's Afrika Corps at El Alamein, Egypt, October 1942.

Contemporary voices, sounds and music, together with
reflections of Allied & Axis' personnel who were there.

This was the event that led to Churchill's: 'Now this is not
the end. It is not even the beginning of the end. But it is,
perhaps, the end of the beginning.' (November 25, 1942)

He later remarked: "Before Alamein we never had a victory.
After Alamein we never had a defeat."
