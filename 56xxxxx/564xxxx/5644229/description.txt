As one of the most important representatives for the "Rock Progressivo Italiano" genre, Le Orme was deeply influenced by ELP-like keyboard sounds. The Italian band is still active today, but its best albums are from the classic period between 1972 and 1974.


Studio albums (The Classic Trilogy):

1972 - Uomo Di Pezza
1973 - Felona E Sorona
1974 - Contrappunti
