Burn Notice is an American television action drama series created by Matt Nix. The show stars Jeffrey Donovan, Gabrielle Anwar, Bruce Campbell, Sharon Gless, and, beginning in the fourth season, Coby Bell.[1] The series premiered on June 28, 2007, on USA Network. On July 28, 2009, Burn Notice was renewed for a fourth season, which premiered on June 3, 2010.[2][3] On April 15, 2010, the show was renewed for two more seasons (seasons 5 and 6).[4]
