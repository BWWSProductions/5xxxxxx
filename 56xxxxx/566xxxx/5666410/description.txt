Following the apparent deaths of long-standing Justice League members Batman and Martian Manhunter in Final Crisis Green Lantern Hal Jordan and Green Arrow lead a spin-off Justice League composed of Starman Mikaal Tomas, Congorilla, Freddy Freeman, Batwoman, Atom Ray Palmer and Supergirl seeking a more pro-active stand for seeking justice.

Written by James Robinson (Starman) and drawn by Mauro Cascioli (The Trials of Shazam!).
