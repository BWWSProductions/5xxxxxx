Rugrats: Decade in Diapers

2001NR161 minutes

Celebrating 10 years of the Nickelodeon hit animated series "The Rugrats," the show's producers took a fan poll. The result of the poll is this video that collects viewers' top ten episode choices, including "Chuckie vs. the Potty," "Vacation," "No Bones About It," "Doctor Suzie" and more. Expect to see the best of Chuckie Finster, Tommy Pickles, Angelina Pickles, Phil and Lil Deville and the whole gang for some good laughs.
