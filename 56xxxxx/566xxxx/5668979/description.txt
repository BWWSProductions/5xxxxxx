Welcome to By Pilots for Pilots

Note: By Pilots for Pilots is for TRAINING PURPOSES ONLY. Use current manuals and publications for the most current and accurate data. 


Welcome to By Pilots for Pilots. This tool is designed as a study aid for the Airbus A319/320/321 systems. All material is subject to change, and you should consult your Airbus manuals for accuracy.

By Pilots for Pilots includes:


1.	Systems and Panels program

2.	MCDU Simulation (Multipurpose Control and Display Unit)

3.	Basic ECAM System Pages Simulation (Electronic Centralized Aircraft Monitoring)

4.	PFD (Primary Flight Display) and ND (Navigation Display) screens with item descriptions

Installation Requirements

Minimum requirements for installing and using By Pilots for Pilots are:

Windows 95/98 or Windows NT 4/2000; Pentium class CPU; GREATER than 256 color-capable video set to a screen resolution of 800x600; and mouse or similar pointing device.
