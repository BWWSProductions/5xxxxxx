When It's Time is a song by the American  rock band Green Day, the single was released in the UK through Reprise Records on June 11, 2010.

The song was recorded by lead singer Billie Joe Armstrong with the other members of Green Day, it was originally recorded for their seventh album American Idiot in 2004, though it appeared on American Idiot: The Original Broadway Cast Recording in 2010. It was orginally written many years before for Armstrong's wife, Adrienne Armstrong.
