BBC R4 - Michael Bywater's 'Lost Worlds'
Abridged by Jill Waters 
Read by Stephen Fry
Produced by Jill Waters 
Broadcast 19-23 December, 2005
Coded from tape at 128/44.1

Michael Bywater's compendium of things that have gone.

Entries include Absurdity, Angels and Bakelite, Banks, Proper;
Bottles, Water, Hot; Call Centres and Common Prayer, the Book
of; Fishing rods, Built Cane; Gloves, LPs and Noddy, Patchouli,
Poofs; Dunwich and other submerged towns, Death and Cartography,
plus Tonic, My; and Weasel, Pop, Goes the.
