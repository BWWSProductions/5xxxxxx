The Rise and Fall of Thuggy Stardust and the Hustlers from Mars is MAN-CAT's mashup album in which all the tracks from David Bowie's classic Ziggy Stardust album are mixed with a wide-ranging variety of rap, including Blackalicious, Nate Dogg, 2Pac, Mase, G-Unit / 50 Cent, Missy Elliot, Jurassic 5, Nas, Jay-Z, Wu-Tang Clan, and Notorious B.I.G.
