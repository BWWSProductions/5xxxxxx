Garmin MapSource Special version 6.16.2

This modified executable will let you upload (Transfer>Send To Device) your
mapset (gmapsupp.img) to your local and network drives too, not only to or your GPS.

New unlocking mechanism modified too:
This version doesn't need .gma unlock files but accepts unlock keys also for
FIDs above 2200, e.g.: CN Europe 2011.10 and CN North America 2011.10.

Instructions for use:
- Install MapSource 6.16.2
  (get it from Garmin website: (see link in .nfo)

- Replace MapSource.exe
