June (jessica drake) works as a bartender at Fuego, a club frequented by vacationing college students looking for a little fun away from home. Young Cody (Dane Cross) is your run-of-the-mill frat boy, poser friends and all. When their paths cross it sparks an intense and passionate fling. Three days later, he wants more but she thinks he should move on. Can June overcome what has become an all too familiar cycle of three night stands? And will Cody go home and get over June? Come to paradise and find out...
