Corel has been collecting pictures by photographers from around the world to create the Corel Stock Photo Library CD series. This collection covers an amazing array of topics - Wildlife, Architecture, Landscapes, Vehicles, Plants, Textures, People, ... Corel offers high quality images. These images are in the Kodak Photo CD format and can be easily exported as TIFF, BMP, EPS or PCX for both Mac and Windows.

CD's also include the following utilities:


    * PhotoCD Lab

    * CorelMOSAIC

    * Corel CD-Audio

    * Artview Screensaver

    * Wallpaper Flipper
