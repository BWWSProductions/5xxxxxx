 -   - -- -----------------------------------------------:-- - - -+- -   -

   Take command of five Dwarf adventurers as they dive into long-abandoned
   mines to search for incredible wealth,  lost treasures, dangerous monsters
   and bragging rights at the drinking hall. Be wary though, up to four teams
   set forth at once, and your greatest obstacle may be a fellow Dwarf with a
   different colored hat!

   Delve Deeper is a Adventure/Strategy game which pits you against monsters
   as you and up to three rival teams build an ever-changing dungeon map and
   compete to pilfer its loot as quickly as possible.
