Discovering covert and faulty intelligence causes a U.S. Army officer to go rogue as he hunts for Weapons of Mass Destruction in an unstable region.

Chief Warrant Officer Roy Miller travels across war-torn Iraq, pursuing the intelligence he's been given, but every site indicated comes up empty of WMDs.

Investigating the source of the intelligence, he finds himself caught between CIA agent Martin Brown and politician Clark Poundston over the identity of "Magellan", the supposed source.

As Miller tracks down an Iraqi general, he ends up further and further afield, facing danger from all sides...
