Product Description
From mountain bikes and road racers to hybrid and leisure bikes, find out how to keep yours in peak condition. This title is fully updated 'encyclopedic insight into how a bicycle works and how to maintain it'. It is suitable for beginners and dedicated cyclists. Clear, jargon-free advice in this title steers you through a complete maintenance programme. Get up to speed with the hottest developments and pick up the latest in gear-shifter technology, new maintenance techniques for disc brakes and hints & tips for mountain-bike suspension. This is a pocket-sized manual with flaps to easily mark your page - perfect for when you're on the road.
