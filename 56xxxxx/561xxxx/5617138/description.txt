A group of criminals try to rob the circus by having a beautiful woman seduce the circus owner.They did not count on the code of the freaks, "You Hurt One of Us; There's going to Be Trouble."

The best part of this movie is that it features "real" sideshow folks, including Larry Gomez "the Mexican Wolf Boy"; VeeKay, Sideshow Performer and Barker; Jimmy GoldMan, Armless Wonder; Bill Quinn, Legless Wonder, and several other oddities.



Actors: Christopher Adamson, Rebekah Kochan
Language: English
Run Time: 85 minutes
