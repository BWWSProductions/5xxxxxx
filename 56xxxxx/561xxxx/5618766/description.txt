Uncovered is a solo studio album by Australian singer John Farnham,[1] it was produced by Little River Band's Graeham Goble,[2][3] and released on 3 July 1980, which peaked at #20 on the Australian Kent Music Report Album Charts.[4] Farnham's first single from the album was a reworking of The Beatles' song "Help!",[5] it peaked at #8 on the Kent Music Report Singles Chart.[4] In 2000, to coincide with its 20th Anniversary the album was re-mastered by Goble and re-released.
