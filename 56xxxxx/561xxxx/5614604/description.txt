MeeGo will provide expanded features, improved performance, and richer user experience for netbooks. With its advanced support for touch and connectivity, MeeGo is a compelling platform for netbook and tablet creation and application development. MeeGo is a light-weight, scalable, fast-booting and brand-able operating system.

The MeeGo software image that you are about to download contains Google Chrome. Google Chrome is subject to an end-user license agreement (EULA), which you need to agree to before downloading the software image. If you do not want to accept this agreement, you can download a MeeGo software image with the Chromium browser from http://meego.com/downloads.
