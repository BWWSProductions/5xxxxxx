Junkyard is a hard rock/sleaze rock band formed in 1987 in Los Angeles, with members formerly in Minor Threat, The Necros, and Dag Nasty.The band has often drawn comparisons with Guns N' Roses.

The original line-up of the band was David Roach (vocals), Chris Gates (guitar, ex-Big Boys), Brian Baker (guitar, ex-Dag Nasty/Minor Threat),Clay Anthony (bass) and Patrick Muzingo(drums).
