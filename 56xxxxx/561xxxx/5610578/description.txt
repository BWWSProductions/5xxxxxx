BBC WS - Richard Norton Taylor's 'Nuremberg'
Directed by David Hitchenson
Broadcast November 19, 2005
Coded from tape at 128/44.1

Another take on the Nuremberg War Crimes' Trial. This time a
dramatised reconstruction of key statements, cross examinations
and judgements. Concludes with a discussion about the court we
now have to try cases of 'Crimes Against Humanity'.

Originally performed on stage at the Tricycle Theatre.

Cast Includes
Michael Cochrane
William Hoyland
Jeremy Clyde
