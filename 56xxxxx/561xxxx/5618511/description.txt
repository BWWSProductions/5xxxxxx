Preacher's Kid is a 2010  Christian film directed by Stan Foster, loosely based on the Parable of the Prodigal Son. Original songs and score are composed by recording artist and music producer Tim Miner. The film was written and directed by Stan Foster, and stars LeToya Luckett, Gregory Alan Williams,Rae'Ven Larrymore Kelly, Kiki Sheard, Durrell Babbs and Clifton Powell. The film was released to theaters on January 29, 2010.

Angie King is a twentysomething who is tired of being a preacher's daughter and joins a traveling gospel show. She finds the experience to be challenging but worries about going home without anything to prove and with the fear that her father would not love her anymore
