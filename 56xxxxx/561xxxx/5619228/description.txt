Plot outline:
Once upon a time, in a country not so far away, our beloved world was one big super-continent, but in a matter of few million years, the Earth was split into several continents, and the different mysterious creatures evolved from each other and became larger and more bizarre than ever before.
The age of the truly bizarre dinosaurs has arrived!
