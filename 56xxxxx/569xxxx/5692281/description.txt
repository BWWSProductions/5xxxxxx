Dear Galileo

Genre: Romance, Drama, Comedy

Runtime: 93 min
Director: Nithiwat Tharatorn
Cast: Chutima Teepanart, Jarinporn Joonkiet, Ray McDonald

.: Plot :.
Two Thai girls decide to go to Europe to travel around and work. One has split up with her boyfriend and the other is in trouble at her university for forging her teacher's signature. In Europe they get jobs illegally without work permits and have to deal with the European way of life which they are not used to. They travel to England, France and Italy and meet many other Thais there. One finds love, one gets a job but things don't turn out as they planned.
