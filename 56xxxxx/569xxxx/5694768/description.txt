Molestation (USA): 'We Came to Molest 2 EP' (2009) [320k/MP3]
-------------------------------------------------------------

MOLESTATION is a 'Horror-Grind' act out of Salinas, CA - USA.

'We Came to Molest 2' is a re-mastered version of
MOLESTATION's 1st DEMO, adding a cover of Death's
'Zombie Ritual' to the mess in the process.


Molestation is currently inactive (2010),
but cite a return, 'sooner than later'.
