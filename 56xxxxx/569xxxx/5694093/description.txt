Boris Karloff Tales of Mystery was a comic book series published by Gold Key between 1962 and 1980.  The series featured host Boris Karloff introducing a mix of stories involving intrigue, suspense, and macabre drama.  Originally titled Boris Karloff Thriller after the anthology television series of the same name the comic changed it's name after the show was cancelled in 1962.
