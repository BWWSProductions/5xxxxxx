When you finish installing Medieval 2 total war on your mac with crossover

1: open crossover games.
2: go to the top of the computer and click "Configure" "Manage Bottles"
3: click on your medieval 2 total war bottle and then click "Open C:Drive in Folder"
4: "Program files" / "SEGA"  / "Medieval II total war"
5: the replace the current Medieval2.exe. with the new one and the same for kingdoms.exe
6: now you are ready to play
