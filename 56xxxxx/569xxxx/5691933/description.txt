As a prelude to the movie Inception, Warner Bros has released comic film entitled "The Cobol Job." The comic, which is written by Jordan Goldber and arted by Long Vo, Joe Ng and Crystal Reid of Udon, serves as a prequel to the film and tells a story of one of the jobs pulled by Leo DiCaprio and Joseph Gordon-Levitt's characters.

For those of you who somehow live in some kind of box, Inception is the latest film from The Dark Knight director Christopher Nolan. Written and directed by Nolan, the film's synopsis reads: "In a world where technology exists to enter the human mind through dream invasion, a single idea within one's mind can be the most dangerous weapon or the most valuable asset." The movie is out this Friday
