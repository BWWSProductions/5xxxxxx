Metallica, have been top of their game since the early 80's and as the recent sold out gigs at 02 Arena prove, interest is certainly not waning!! The sevnth biggest selling American artist of all time, Metallica are a household name, quite a feat for a band whose blistering guitars and pounding drums were originally known as thrash metal. With 9 studio albums, 44 singles, and countless Anthems under their belts, Metallica have been an influence on hundreds of bands, bringing metal to wider audience with-out ever compromising their sound. We have complied the best of those who have chosen to cover Metallica on this awesome Tribute album.
