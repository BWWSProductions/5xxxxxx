A teenager is imprisoned by a man claiming to protect her from a vicious beast in this dark thriller set in the Welsh countryside.

Sophie is a girl who lives to explore the unexplained. She's heard the stories about the mysterious animal, and can't resist the opportunity to find out for herself if it really exists. Venturing out into the fields with her friends, Sophie becomes the victim of a savage attack, and awakens in a locked room.

On the other side of the door is Gavin, a man who wages an unrelenting struggle to hold the beast at bay...
