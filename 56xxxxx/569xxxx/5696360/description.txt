A compilation of hit songs from mostly the last 90's. I tried to avoid too much grunge or techno, and kept it to songs that you use to hear on the radio all the time, for that extra nostalgic feel.

PROTIP: For extra 90's awesomeness add more Backstreet Boys.

Enjoy.
