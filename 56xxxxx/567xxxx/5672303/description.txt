Sonny Munroe (who is from Green Bay, Wisconsin) becomes a cast member on her favorite TV show, So Random!. She's an eager beaver to join the show, but Tawni Hart isn't so thrilled. Sonny seems to get along with the other cast members, who are Zora Lancaster, Nico Harris, and Grady Mitchell. Will Sonny be a good addition to So Random!, or will she and Tawni drive each other crazy?
