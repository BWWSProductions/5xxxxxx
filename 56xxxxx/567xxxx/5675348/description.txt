Forced Entry were a three piece technical thrash band from Seattle, USA. Formed in 1982 by Tony Benjamin and Brad Hull. They recorded two demos "All Fucked Up" (1987) and "Thrashing Helpess Down" (1988) prior to releasing this; their 1989 debut album "Uncertain Future".
