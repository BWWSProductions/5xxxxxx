Collection of two graphic novels adapting the first two novels in the Artemis Fowl series written by Eoin Colfer.  The graphic novels are adapted by Eoin Colfer and Andrew Donkin with art by Giovanni Rigano and colour by Paolo Lamanna.

The series follows the adventures of Artemis Fowl II, a twelve-year-old ruthless master criminal who takes charge of rebuilding his family fortune following the disappearance of his father.  In the first book after significant research, Artemis believes that he has confirmed the existence of fairies and decides to kidnap one, with the objective of obtaining the gold which the fairy government has set aside to ransom captured fairy folk.
