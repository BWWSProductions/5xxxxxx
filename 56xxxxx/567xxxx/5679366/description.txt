Dennis Miller Show

2010-07-09 (Fri)
---------------- 

Hour 1: John Wordin: Executive Director of Ride 2 Recovery [Ride2Recovery.com]
Hour 2: M. Zuhdi Jasser: President and Founder of the American Islamic Forum for Democracy; Kelly LeBrock: Actress
Hour 3: Dennis Ex Machina: Listeners call in to ask Dennis anything about anything

In our first hour, Dennis welcomed John Wordin, the Executive Director of Ride 2 Recovery.com, to the show. Wordin talked about a number of upcoming events which will raise money for injured veterans. Then, in Hour 2, M. Zuhdi Jasser, President 
and Founder of the American Islamic Forum for Democracy, was on the show, along with actress Kelly LeBrock, who talked about her comeback. Finally, Hour 3 was a Dennis Ex Machina, which means that listeners called in to ask Dennis anything about anything!
