Plot
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Based on the short Stephen King horror story of the same name, 1408 is about 
supernatural writer Mike Enslin, an author of two successful books on 
supernatural phenomena. As research for his latest book, Enslin is determined to 
check out the notorious room 1408 in a New York City hotel by personally staying 
as a guest in the fabled room. He believes that 1408 is just a myth perpetuated 
by stories and rumors that Enslin has collected for his past works. However, 
hotel manager Mr. Olin has strong objections to Enslin's stay and only warns him 
of possible danger to come. Enslin is determined to go anyway. But what Mike 
Enslin is about to experience is no myth, as 1408 truly is a room where the 
guests don't check out by noon.
