SYNOPSIS

Arnaud Desjardins met the Dalai Lama for the first time in Mussoorie in 1963 and 
obtained his support for the filming of this movie.

All the most revered masters agreed to participate in the making of this film: 
The Dalai Lama, Dilgo Khyentse Rinpoche, Dudjom Rinpoche, the 16th Karmapa, Drukpa 
Tupse Rinpoche, Chatral Rinpoche, Situ Rinpoche, the yogis Lopon Sonam Zangpo and 
Apo Rinpoche and many other eminent masters. The original sixties commentary has 
been retained, even though it may sound a bit dated in places.

This was filmed in India, Sikkim and Bhutan by Arnaud Desjardins in consultation 
with Sonam Topgey Kazi, the senior interpreter to the Dalai Lama at that time.
