Plot
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Forty-year-old priest, Pitkanen, considers himself a rebel. In fact, the only 
signs of his rebellion are his ponytail and the boots he wears under his priest 
vestments. He is stuck on the intermediate level in the church hierarchy, a rank 
which is too low for his wife's family, but too high for his best friends. One 
Sunday, after an overdone rebellious sermon, the bishop wants to meet Pitkanen. 
He thinks he's in deep trouble, but the bishop wants him to take on the bishop's 
office. Pitkanen grabs the oppurtunity, but first he has to sell all his ideals 
in order to realize them. And the worst thing is that he has no idea that his 
wife is plotting with the bishop behind his back for his promotion.
