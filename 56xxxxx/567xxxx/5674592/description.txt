Once upon a time some kind soul uploaded a torrent of the first 12.5 seasons of Good Eats, plus several extras.

Unfortunately, (a) the torrent stopped being seeded, and (b) many of the files had the wrong episode numbers, and a handful had incorrect names (not sure what happened - perhaps The Food Network changed the names and numbers
at some point?) I was able to download 99% of what was uploaded, but the torrent has been without seed for over a year.  So, I found the missing pieces, and added the missing episodes.

This Torrent contains all of the files of the first 8 seasons, plus the special episodes.  A second torrent contains seasons 9 to 13.

Hopefully this one can keep seeding for a while and Alton fans everywhere can get more than their fill of Good Eats!
