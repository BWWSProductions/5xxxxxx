Mishaps. Mayhem. Misadventures. Oh joy.

A wickedly entertaining movie about the misadventures of the three Baudelaire orphans who must use their wits and resources to escape the clutches of villainous Count Olaf (Carrey), a distant relative who plots to steal their family fortune...

Based on the Lemony Snicket books 'The Bad Beginning', 'The Reptile Room' and 'The Wide Window'.
