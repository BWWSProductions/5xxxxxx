Once upon a time some kind soul uploaded a torrent of the first 12.5 seasons of Good Eats, plus several extras.

Unfortunately, (a) the torrent stopped being seeded, and (b) many of the files had the wrong episode numbers, and a handful had incorrect names (not sure what happened - perhaps The Food Network changed the names and numbers
at some point?) I was able to download 99% of what was uploaded, but the torrent has been without seed for over a year.  So, I found the missing pieces, and added (most) of the missing episodes from the 13th season.

This Torrent contains all of the files from Seasons 9 to 13, except exposides "S13E15 - It's A Pan, It's A Dish, It's Paella" and "S13E19 - The Catfish Will Rise Again", plus "Ask Alton" episodes.
A second torrent contains Seasons 1 to 8 plus special episodes.

Hopefully this one can keep seeding for a while and Alton fans everywhere can get more than their fill of Good Eats!
