Perfect Ace: Pro Tournament Tennis provides an easy to understand but challenging
gaming arena and features an eclectic mix of 32 Characters with specially Digi-masked
lifelike faces to provide the facial expressions and animations known only to the world's
top players. Extremely complex 3D scanning technologies have been implemented to give
players realistic heights, builds, races, and weights, and motion capture studio time has
provided Perfect Ace with realistic baseline-to-net movements and the dives. Form a duo
and represent your National Team through the Pro Tournament Mode, or face challenging
and gripping doubles and singles matches flying the National Flag as you go. Only
available for the European market.
