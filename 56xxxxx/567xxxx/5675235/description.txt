Cinema Bizarre is a synthpop band from Berlin, Germany formed in 2005. They started as Glam rock, Goth rock with some New Wave influence. Later the New Wave presence was increased with Electronic music elements, now merging into synthpop. They released their debut album in 2007, and do all of their vocals in English.


The albums included are Final Attraction, ToyZ, and BANG!.
