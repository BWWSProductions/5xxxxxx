A third-person action title from Acclaim, Gladiator: Sword of Vengeance tells the story of Rome in the year 106 A.D. It follows the exploits of an evil emperor named Arruntius as he gains aid from the demon gods Phobos and Deimos in an effort to destroy and rebuild the Roman Empire in his own image -- with only the reborn gladiator Invictus Thrax around to stop him. Spread out across many different lands, Sword of Vengeance includes dissimilar kinds of authentic weaponry, a unique experience and upgrade system, and bloody fatality sequences. Also available for Microsoft's Xbox.
