BBC R4 Documentary - 'At War With Wellington - Battles'
Presented by Dan and Peter Snow
Produced by Alan Shipton
Broadcast August 4 & 8, 2008
Coded from a digital source at 128/44.1

Dan and Peter Snow visit some of the key
sites of the Peninsular War and, with the
help of dramatised readings, describe some
of the battles, in particular:

Part 1 - Vimeiro and Talavera

Part 2 - Badajoz and Vitoria
