All-Ireland Songs & Heroes (1997)           

This Is A Wonderful Musical Tribute To All The 32 Counties Of Ireland
With A Live Version Of The West's Awake & The Irish National Anthem 
Amhran Na BhFiann Its An Absolutely Perfect Collection For All GAA Supporters
And Irish Abroad

Seed & Enjoy!
