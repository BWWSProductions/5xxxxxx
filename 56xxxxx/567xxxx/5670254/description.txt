This band from Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada has a pop rock feel to there music. It makes you want to get up and dance along. 

The band states this about there album:

"It's only 5 songs, but I think that what we might lack in quantity we make up for in the quality department. These songs are going to blow your minds! 
You think you know what's coming? You have no idea."

Track Listing:

1. Call The Cops
2. Tonight
3. Green Eyes
4. I Was Alive For 15 Days In The 80's
5. My Rags, My Riches
