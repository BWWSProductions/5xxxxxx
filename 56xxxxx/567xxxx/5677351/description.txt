BBC R4 Documentary - 'At War With Wellington - Diaries And Letters'
Prepared by Lizzie and Alan Shipton
Presented by Peter and Dan Snow
Produced by Alan Shipton
Broadcast August 4 - 8, 2008
Coded from a digital source at 128/44.1

Peter and Dan Snow present readings from the diaries
and letters of some of Wellington's soldiers and camp
followers from the Peninsular War [1808-14].

Part 1 - Transport and marching.

Part 2 - Rations and entertainments.

Part 3 - Loot and pillage.

Part 4 - Camp followers.

Part 5 - Wounds, and doctoring in the field. 

Cast
Neil Dudgeon
David Holt
Frank Sterling
David Westhead
