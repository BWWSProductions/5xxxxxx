BBC R4 - Yevgeny Zamyatin's 'We'
Adapted by Sean O'Brien
Directed by Jim Poyser
Broadcast in two parts April 18 & 25, 2004
Coded from tape at 128/44.1

Maths and logic rule One State. Liquidation is the penalty for dissent.

Adapted by Sean O'Brien from Yevgeny Zamyatin's prophetic
dystopian Russian novel written immediately after the revolution.

'Brave New World' and '1984' are indebted to this work.
