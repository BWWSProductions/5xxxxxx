BBC R4 - Tobias Smollett's 'Travels Through France And Italy'
Broadcast in the 'Book At Bedtime' slot
Abridged and read by Roger Allam
Broadcast August 17-21, 2009

Readings for Smollett's memoir of a tour
he made with his wife in 1766.

worse, Versailles and Paris aren't up to much,
horses can't be controlled, there's snow in the
Midi, Rome isn't worth the bother, and Florence ...
