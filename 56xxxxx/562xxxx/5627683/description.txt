Live is Canadian alternative rock band Our Lady Peace's first live album. It was recorded on January 27, 2003 and January 28, 2003 in Calgary and Edmonton, Canada; and on February 5, 2003 in Montreal, Quebec, Canada. It was released on June 24, 2003 by Columbia Records.

This is the full version which had been released on DVD9, hence the size.
