The Night Has Eyes 1942 B/W 78 Min

From Wikipedia;

The Night Has Eyes is a 1942 British thriller film directed by Leslie Arliss and starring James Mason, Wilfrid Lawson and Mary Clare.[1] Two young teachers travel to the Yorkshire Moors where their friend disapeared a year before. Before long they have encountered the man they believe to her murderer. [2]
