BBC R4 Documentary - 'In A Secret State'
Presented by Peter Hennessey
Produced by Zaria Massani
Broadcast in three parts, March, 3, 10, & 17, 2002
Coded from tape at 128/44.1

An assessment of 'intelligence' files related
to nuclear weapons declassified in 2002.

How accurately did British intelligence
assess the Cold War threat from the USSR?

Part 2 - 'Preparing For The Worst'	

What are the preparations for dealing
with the results of nuclear war?

Part 3 - 'Red And Amber'	

How close did we come to nuclear war?
