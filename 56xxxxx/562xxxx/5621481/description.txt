Plot outline:
How could a small Tyrannosaurus-baby grow up to be a 6 ton heavy predator ?
Follow loading scientists under guidance by the famous paleontologist Jason Brougham, while they uncover the truth about this fascinating animal and create an accurate, anatomically correct, mechanical version of a young, walking T-Rex.
