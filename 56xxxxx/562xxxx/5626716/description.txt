Here are the original soundtracks for Mega Man 1 to 9, all lyricless.

The tracks are organized in sub-folders by game, and the ones under 30 seconds are in their own section, organized similarly.

Composers:

Mega Man 1: Manami Matsumae, Yoshihiro Sakaguchi

Mega Man 2: Takashi Tateishi, Manami Matsumae, Yoshihiro Sakaguchi

Mega Man 3: Yasuaki Fujita

Mega Man 4: Minae Fujii, Yasuaki Fujita

Mega Man 5: Mari Yamaguchi

Mega Man 6: Yuko Takehara

Mega Man 7: Yuko Takehara, Toshihiko Horiyama, Makoto Tomozawa

Mega Man 8: Shusaku Uchiyama

Mega Man 9: Ippo Yamada, Yu Shimoda, Ryo Kawakami, Hiroki Isogai
