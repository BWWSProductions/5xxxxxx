The story of the first major battle of the american phase of the vietnam war and the soldiers on both sides that fought it.

Based on the book by Lt. Col. Harold Moore (ret.) and journalist Joseph Galloway, We Were Soldiers offers a dignified reminder that the Vietnam War yielded its own crop of American heroes.
