Late For The Meeting, Be Ready For The Punishment

Missy Stone

November 04, 2009

Pornstars Punishment

Scott is patiently waiting on his colleague Missy to show up for an important business meeting. He loses out on the multi-million dollar deal because she comes 37 minutes late! Furious, Scott knows that he will probably lose his job for this, but he decides to give Missy what she secretly loves and deserves for her incompetence, an insane punishment!
