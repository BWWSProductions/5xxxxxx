BBC R2 Documentary - 'Soundscape Of 1969'
Newspaper extracts read by Bill Bingham
Presented by Johnnie Walker
Broadcast August 22, 2009
Coded from a digital source at 128/44.1

Radio 2's annual soundscape from 40 years ago. The audio collage of the
news, music and media of the time includes Monty Python, Lennon's Bed-in
Woodstock, moon landing, Londonderry, Colour TV, Jumbo jets, & Concorde.

Clips taken from the BBC's Archives.
