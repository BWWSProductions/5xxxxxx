Devasuram (1993) is an Indian film by I. V. Sasi, one of the most successful Malayalam movies starring Mohanlal. The rivalry between Mangalassery Neelakantan (Mohanlal) and Mundakkal Shekaran (Napoleon) is the main theme of the movie. The screenplay was by Renjith
