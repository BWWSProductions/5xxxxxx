This computer-animated kids' series follows the adventures of fun-loving farm animals led by Otis the cow, a real party animal, and his friends Bessy the bovine, Pip the mouse, Freddy the ferret, Peck the accident-prone rooster, and the dim but loyal Pig. Based on the 2006 feature film BARNYARD.
