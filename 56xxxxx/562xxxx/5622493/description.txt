Collection of comics featuring Robert E. Howard's (Conan) Puritan adventurer Solomon Kane.  Solomon Kane was a late 16th / early 17th century Puritan, a somber-looking man who wanders the world vanquishing evil in all its forms.

Solomon Kane featured in several comics  published by Marvel Comics in the 1970s and 1980s and Dark Horse Comics began publishing a new series of Kane stories in 2008.
