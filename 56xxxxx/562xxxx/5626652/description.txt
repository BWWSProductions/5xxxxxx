Audrey Bitoni and Alexis Texas - The Driver

Added: June 14, 2010

Johnny may not have the most exciting job as a driver; he may not make a lot of money; but sometimes he catches the eye of a female passenger. On this day, his passenger is Alexis Texas and she introduces him to Audrey Bitoni. They seduce him while their husbands are busy talking about lucrative real estate property.
