"Commander" is a song by American singer Kelly Rowland from her upcoming third self-titled studio album as well as her debut single with Universal Motown following a change in record labels and management. It is a female empowerment record written by Rico Love accompanied with an electro-house  production from French disc jockey, David Guetta. "Commander" was released in May 2010 as an American promotional single and as the international lead single from the project.
