Drovers Run is about to host the first Gungellan Fresh Cross Country Challenge and Regan's horse-riding lessons have paid off making her confident in mustering cattle for Jodi which are part of a contract for Worthingtons. However, Regan leaves them in the wrong paddock and they stray through a broken fence onto Killarney land, where Sandra finds them and plots revenge on Jodi who stole a beef contract from her. Jodi decides they must do everything to win the Cross Country Challenge and dumps Regan from the team and Stevie is installed in her place.
