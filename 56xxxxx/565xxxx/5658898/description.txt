"Te Amo" (English: "I Love You") is a song performed by R&B Barbadian singer Rihanna. It is written by Mikkel S. Eriksen, Tor Erik Hermansen, James Fauntleroy II and Rihanna and produced by Stargate. The song tells the story of a girl who is trying to let another woman know that she loves her as a friend but she is not a lesbian. It serves as the third international and fifth overall single from her fourth album Rated R.
