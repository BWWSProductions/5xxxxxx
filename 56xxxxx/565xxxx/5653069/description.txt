VA - Influential Rock I

Disk I (Hard Rock) + Disk II (Alt. Rock)

A Compilation Of Some Of The Most Influential Bands Of This Decade. This 
Album Is Divided Into 2 Disks Disk I, Which Consists Of Hard Rock & Rock 
Tracks And Disk II Which Consists Of Alt. Rock. Most Bands Contribute 1 
Track To Each Disk.
There Are Lots Of Other Influential Bands That Have Not Featured In This 
Album And Will Feature In Other 'Influential Rock' Albums In The Future.
This Might Bring Back Nostalgic Memories For Some And Makes Up A Great 
Collection Of Tracks.

Album Art Is Included For Each Track.
