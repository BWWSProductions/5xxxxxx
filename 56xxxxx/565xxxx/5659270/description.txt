Rugrats: Tales from the Crib: Three Jacks & a Beanstalk

2006NR121 minutes

The Rugrats are back! Part of the Tales from the Crib series, this feature-length film is a Rugrats-style retelling of the beloved fairy tale "Jack and the Beanstalk." In this inventive version, ornery Angelica is the menacing giant who threatens Tommy and the gang as they climb the beanstalk to see what they can see. Sure to entertain, this lively update of the classic story features the vocal talents of Amanda Bynes, Mo'Nique and Kathy Najimy.
