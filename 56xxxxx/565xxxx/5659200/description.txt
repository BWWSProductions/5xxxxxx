Report from Footballer Support on the problems concerning The England Team in the World Cup 2010 in South Africa.

Report details the problems within UK Football, how long this has been going on, and how it effects players not only on the international level, but within UK Clubs, and young players coming into the game.

Solutions cover the required changes to improve overall footballer performance, esepcially at international level.

Additional advice for players at all levels is also included.

Includes links for Questions, Enquiries and Requests for additional support for players.
