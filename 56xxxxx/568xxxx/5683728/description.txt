I Am Comic (2010) ShowTime Documentary XviD [WDM]
-------------------------------------------------
Filmmaker Jordan Brady takes a behind-the-microphones look at what it takes to stay relevant in the competitive world of standup comedy, one that is dependent on timely observations about daily life and current events. Featuring comments from an all-star roster of popular comics that includes Sarah Silverman, Louis C.K., Dave Attell, Lewis Black, Kathy Griffin, Jim Gaffigan, Roseanne, Jeff Foxworthy, Carlos Mencia, Janeane Garofalo, Tommy Davidson, Dana Gould and Margaret Cho.
