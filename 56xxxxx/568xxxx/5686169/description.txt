HOW TO READ -
************
(i) All Triptaka e-books are in PDF format, use a PDF reader to read them.
(ii) If you do not have a PDF reader, simply install Adobe Acrobat Reader 
from - http://get.adobe.com/reader/

(Pls note you will need Adobe reader version 7.0 or higher to read these
 .PDF files)


USEFUL TIPS FOR READER -
***********************

The table of contents of each book have been linked with associated pages, 
for your convenience. You can easily click on the page number or content 
description in the "Table of Contents" and you will be directed to the 
relevant page. Bookmarks located in the left pane of reading area can be 
used to navigate back to the "Table of Contents" page. If you would like 
to view the Pali page on your left and the relevant Sinhala translated page 
on your right side, select the menu items as follows. VIEW menu, PAGE DISPLAY 
option and select TWO-UP CONTINUOUS option.
