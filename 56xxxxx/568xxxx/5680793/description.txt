Yeti are an English rock band founded in 2004 by John Hassall, formerly of The Libertines. Hassall met Brendan Kersey, Andrew Deian and Mark Underwood (formerly Harmony Williams) through mutual friends, and the line-up was completed when drummer Graham Blacow responded to a classified ad. The band is based in North London. Matthew Jeffery was approached by band manager in February 2009 to join.

The Legend of Yeti Gonzales is the debut studio album by the band Yeti.
