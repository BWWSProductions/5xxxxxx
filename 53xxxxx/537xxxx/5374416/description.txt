Description: An easy to use tool to preform brute force attacks on hashes. MD4, MD5, RIPEMD160, SHA1, and SHA2 family hashes are supported. Ideal for password recovery, and demonstrating to people why they should not use weak passwords. Hashes are text containing data encrypted in an irreversible algorithm, and are most used for storing passwords.
