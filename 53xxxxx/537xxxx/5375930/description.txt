BBC R4 - Rudyard Kipling's 'Barrack-room Ballads'
Performed by Kenneth Cranham
Produced by Tim Dee
Broadcast November 19, 2006
Coded at 128/44.1 from listen again

Kenneth Cranham performs (or rather, chants)
several of the Barrack-room Ballads. Along the
way he gives a potted biography of Kipling, and
provides details of the ballads.

Ballads:
Tommy Atkins
Danny Deever
Cells
Gunga Din
Boots
The Ladies
Chant Pagan
.
