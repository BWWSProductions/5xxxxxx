Title: GeNext United
Publisher: Marvel
Issue and Volume Number: Complete Series (issues 1-5)
Year: 2009

Having rescued their friend, No-Name, GeNext (Oli Raven, Rico Richards, Pavel Rasputin, Bekka Munroe, No-Name, and Megan Summers, with the aid of Dr. Hank McCoy) are off to India after saving Sati, an Indian Super-Hero. There Rico finds an old flame and No-Name gets the chance to re-invent herself, while Oli is reuinted with his father and sister.
