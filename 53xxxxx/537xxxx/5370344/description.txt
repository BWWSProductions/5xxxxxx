Englisch Information

Learning Curve - SG-1 visits a planet named Orban where knowledge is harvested through its children (called "Urrone"). When a Urrone reaches a certain age, their nanites are harvested and they regress to an infantile state and remain that way as the Orbanians have no concept of traditional education.  

Point of View - Using the mirror from "There But For the Grace of God", an alternate Carter and Charles Kawalsky come to the SGC from an Earth that has been invaded by the Goa'uld.

Dead Man's Switch - SG-1 is captured by a bounty hunter who uses them to help catch a Tok'ra. The Goa'uld has his race addicted to a drug that can be supplied only by them.

Demons - SG-1 visits a planet ruled by a Goa'uld-infested Unas, who uses the persona of the Devil to keep the residents ruled by fear.
