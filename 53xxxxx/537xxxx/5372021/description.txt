From the Box:

From John Carpenter, the master of horror behiknd 1998's hit John Carpenter's Vampires and classics like The Thing and Halloween, comes a sci-fi thriller full of explosive action and bone-chilling suspense.

Natasha Henstridge (Species) is Melanie Ballard, a headstrong police Lieutenant on Mars in the year 2025. Humans have been colonizing and mining the on the red planet for some time, but when Ballard and her squad are sent to a remote region to apprehend the dangerous criminal "James Desolation Williams", played by Ice Cube (Three Kings), they discover that he's the least of their worries. The mining operations have unleashed a deadly army of Martian Spirits who take over the bodies of humands and won't stop until they destroy all invaders of their planet.
