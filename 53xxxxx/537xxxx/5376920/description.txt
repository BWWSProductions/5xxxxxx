                              Broken Heels

                       Single by Alexandra Burke 

                        from the album Overcome 
   
"Broken Heels" is a pop song performed by British pop/R&B singer Alexandra Burke, to be released as the second single from her debut album, Overcome.It is written by RedOne, Bilal Hajji, Savan Kotecha and produced by RedOne,the single was released on digital download in the United Kingdom on 17 January 2010 and physically on the following day, on 18 January 2010.

       
"Broken Heels" entered the UK Singles Chart at #36 on 26 December 26, 2009 and over a period of five weeks climbed to its current peak of #8. The single spent two weeks in the top 10, then fell to no. 11 on 31st January 2010. It also entered the Irish Singles Chart at #25 in December 2009 and has so far peaked at #5, becoming her third top five hit there. it entered the Bulgaria Singles Chart at 33 in its 2nd week it moved up 3 places to 30, it's so far peaked at 24.

Its sold over 200,000 copies in the UK
