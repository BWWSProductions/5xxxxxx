HD Suisse: Swiss View

-----------------------------------------------------

Year: 2007-2009
Country: Switzerland
Genre: Documental, Virtual Trip, Shooting from a helicopter
Duration: ~ 655 min (12 parts)

Director: Marco Fumasoli

Description: Satellite channel HD suisse invites you to a fascinating journey through Switzerland. A stunning beauty of the Swiss TV documentary, filmed specifically for the promotion of the beauties of the country to potential tourists.

07 - Saane, Fribourg, Schiffenensee
