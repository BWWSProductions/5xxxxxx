Release notes:

The Recording Academy's GRAMMY Recordings has partnered with EMI Music to
release the 2010 GRAMMY Nominees CD, the 16th edition of the best-selling series
highlighting artists and songs nominated for the 52nd Annual GRAMMY Awards.
Nominations for the 52nd Annual GRAMMY Awards reflect an eclectic mix of the
best and brightest over the past year in music, as determined by the voting
members of The Academy.

The disc contains many of the most widely recognized GRAMMY categories with the
20 tracks representing nominees for Record Of The Year, Album Of The Year, Best
Female Pop Vocal Performance, Best Pop Vocal Album, Best Country Performance By
A Duo Or Group With Vocals, and Best Rock Performance By A Duo Or Group With
Vocals.
