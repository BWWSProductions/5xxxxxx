Bible Hangman 

The object of the game is to guess a word from the Bible before the stick-man is hung. 

From the Game menu select the category (or categories) of words you desire then click on new game to start.

You can also change the skill by selecting multiple levels to see how many words you can guess before you hang the stick-man.

Language: English
