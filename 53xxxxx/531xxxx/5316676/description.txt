Year: 2005
Genre: Pop



Review:

This is a unique collection of the visual works of Elvis Costello captured for the first time on DVD. From his first video for Radar Records through to his last with Warner Brothers this collection showcases 27 videos ;
featuring material rarely seen since the release of the singles they supported. Costello has recorded a witty 
and lively commentary full of incredible insights for the fans ; there is also a host of other special features.
