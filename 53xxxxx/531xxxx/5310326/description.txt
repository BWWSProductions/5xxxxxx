Eyes Down was a short-lived comedy starring Paul O'Grady as 
Ray Temple, the manager of a Bingo Hall in Liverpool, England called 
The Rio, although the series was filmed in Rayners Lane in London.[1]. 
Although it had moderate ratings, the programme only lasted for two seasons 
until it was cancelled by the BBC in 2004. The show was written by Angela 
Clarke 
and directed by Christine Gernon.
