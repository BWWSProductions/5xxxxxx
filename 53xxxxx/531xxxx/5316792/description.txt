Product Description
MenuMinder... Create quick, simple alert, email and SMS 
Text Message (mobile phone) reminders straight from 
your menu bar. Quickly create a reminder using the 
system-wide menu that can be accessed from any 
application. Never forget anything ever again.
Attach tasks to your reminders. Schedule your computer 
to play a song, open a URL, run an application, or even 
run an AppleScript.
Enter an email server or use your default email account 
in Apple Mail.
