Harley-Davidson: Race Around the World
Infogrames Entertainment has released a new group of screenshots from its upcoming motorcycle racing game, Harley-Davidson: Race Around the World. The new screens show off some of the different locations that will be featured in the game, including several recognizable sites in Asia and Europe such as Paris and Stonehenge. Players will be able to customize their rider and choose from a variety of different Harley-Davidson motorcycles.

Harley-Davidson: Race Around the World is nearing completion at Nitro Games, and it is scheduled for release in September.
