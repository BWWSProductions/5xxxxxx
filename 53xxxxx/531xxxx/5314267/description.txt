The Monoxides were formed in 1988 by members Steve and Ken at the ages of 13 and 12 respectively. PJ joined the band in 1990, followed by Derek in 1991. In February 1995, the band signed a contract with Handsome Boy / BMG Canada. That July saw the release of their debut EP Out of the Marsh which included the video / single I've Got An Idea.
