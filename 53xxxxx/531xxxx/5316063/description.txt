T.O.K. is a dancehall group hailing from Kingston, Jamaica. The group consists of Alistaire "Alex" McCalla, Roshaun "Bay-C" Clarke, Craig "Craigy T" Thompson, and Xavier "Flexx" Davidson. They are one of the most Internationally successful and well-known Dancehall groups of all time and are best known for such hits as "Footprints", "Gal You Ah Lead", "Chi Chi Man" and "Eagles Cry". "Chi Chi Man" is controversial for its lyrics advocating homophobic violence.
