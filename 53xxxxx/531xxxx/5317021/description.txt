INFO: 

Binaural Beats with river sounds for meditation / relaxation. 

DURATION:

30 minutes.

NOTE:

Binaural Beats take some time to show effect. Do not expect any dramatic results within the first few listens. For some people results may begin to appear in about 7 days of listening. For others it may take 4-6 weeks. Listen for 30 minutes on a daily basis for 4-6 weeks for optimum effect. Listening with headphones essential.
