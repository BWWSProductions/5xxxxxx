Barber in Berlin

Recorded 23rd May, 1959 at the Deutschland Halle, Berlin

Originally released on the Columbia label as part of the "Lansdowne Jazz Series".

These tracks ripped from remastered CD released by Lake Records 2005

Band members:

Chris Barber - Trombone
Pat Halcox - Trumpet, vocal track 7
Monty Sunshine - Clarinet
Eddie Smith - Banjo
Dick Smith - Bass
Graham Burbidge - Drums
Ottilie Patterson - vocal

If, like me, you've lost your original vinyl copy, I hope this gives you great joy and brings back many fond memories.
