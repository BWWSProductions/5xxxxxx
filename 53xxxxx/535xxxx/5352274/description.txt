Texas rockers Sunset Heights formed in 1991,taken their names from the Houston area

suburb which the band called home. Debuting a year later with Eclipse of the Sun,

in 1993 the band was named "Best New Act" in Houston Press Music Award; in 1994

the Sunset Heights launched their first national release, Texas Tea, returning the

following year with Born In Houston--Live. After resurfacing in 1996 with S.N.A.F.U

the group, now consisting of singers/guitarist Jackie Hibbard and Jorge Castillo,

bassist Jason "Big Daddy" Youngblood and drummer Rick Hall issued 1998's Medicine 

Hat; a self-titled LP appeared a year later...
