In Boogie SuperStar, you don't just play along, you are the star of the game! Players will have a blast belting their favourite tunes into the microphone, and performing real dance moves that are captured on screen using the Wii's motion-sensing technology. The journey to stardom doesn't have to be travelled solo - sing and dance with or against friends in fun competitions that show off your singing and dancing talent. Featuring nearly 40 of today's hottest tracks, Boogie SuperStar will have you bursting with excitement as you discover you can both sing and dance to your favorite songs in one game!
