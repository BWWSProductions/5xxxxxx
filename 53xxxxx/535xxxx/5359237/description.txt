Jindo Intangible Art Culture Series 5 (2007)


This CD contains 18 funeral songs from Jindo, an island off the southwest coast of Korea (click on my username for more music from Jindo). The first 10 tracks are performed by holders of the Jeollanamdo Provincial Intangible Cultural Property 19 (Jindo Manga) based in Inji village, Jisan-myeon. The remaining tracks are from Changyoo village, Jodo island (a small island about 45 minutes from Jindo).

I didn't write the track names here, but the tracks are labelled.
