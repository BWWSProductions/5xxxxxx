Number Nine, Number Nine... we worked out a new label sampler. We tried to bring a batch of newcomers into the Dead Bees family. New faces, new songwriters... this ninth compilation gathers a lot young blood, but also brings along some well established bands such as Anton's BJM.

We meant this music to be shared under the terms of a Creative Commons license. So please spread this record. Make copies of it, and give them away.
We will also post it onto various websites, file sharing portals, and non-profit free music sites, such as Archive.org or FreeMusicArchive.org.
