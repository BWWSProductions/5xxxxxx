BBC R4 Archive Hour - 'Plucked From The Fire'
Presented by Tim Whewell
Edited by Maria Oblinska
Produced by Tim Whewell
Broadcast May 28, 2005
Coded from tape at 128/44.1

The story of The Jewish Scientific Institute - YIVO. An archive
of the history of East European Jewry, how it was assembled by
volunteer collectors before the Second World War, and how it
was then rescued from the Nazis.

Includes archive recordings of vibrant music.
