Pinball FX: combines the arcade thrills of traditional pinball with enhanced gameplay and innovative features. With the power of the Xbox 360 behind it, Pinball FX sets a new standard for realistic ball physics and graphical detail in pinball video games. The three vibrant 3-D tables provide dozens of hours of play. The tables start out simple, but new challenges open up as your skill grows. Control the flipper arms with hand/arm motions or chat live via the Xbox 360 Live Vision camera. Pinball FX is pinball at its best!
