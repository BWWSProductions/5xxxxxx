Ivanhoe

Sir Walter Scott 1771-1832

----

A century has passed since the Norman Conquest, and England is 
still a colony of foreign warlords. Prince John is plotting to 
seize the throne from his brother, Richard the Lion-Hearted, and 
Robin Hood and his merry band are making fools out of the Sheriff 
of Nottingham.

Wilfred, knight of Ivanhoe, the son of Cedric the Saxon, is in love 
with his father's ward, Rowena. Cedric, however, wishes her to 
marry Athelstane, a descendant of the royal Saxon succession.

With a colorful cast of chivalric knights and fair ladies, this 
action-filled novel comes complete with feats of derring-do, the 
pageantry of a tournament, and a great flame-engulfed castle--all 
of which makes it the most enthralling of Scott's creations.
