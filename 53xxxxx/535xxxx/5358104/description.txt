THAWNKHAM- HAIBANG HONG NGAK


tuan pupate, laitual leng
khuambang luahna zogam lei
Khawhsa tuksa, khawi leh vulh ching
mimza tangza aai uh ee..

tong in lam in zunung sanung
nundang gamdang ngailo ee
ngaihhuai mah ee maw pu leh pa lenlai ni

khuahunte hong piandang in,
nuntak khuasak haksa ee
namdang gamdang te lak a
zogam tate va bang leng
inn hoih louhoih hongsiam unla,
alim a al hong tut un
sung gilkial tunnu Zogam' haibang hong ngak

chorus 
Hanlung chiamni, u leh nau aw
itna masuan in sem ni
sinthu khat in tuibang luang
luangkhawm leng
sian hawm sinthu paibang pom
zaitha san machiang suankhawm ni
zogam nun in nopna ding
zomi gual tungtuanna ding

neihleh lamte tawh huh inla
pilna siamnate hawm in
tunnu zogam tung sianmang lung awi hi ee


mangvom mangkang te lak ah
nungta khuasa i teng zong
zomi zota mah hi hang
nasuan nakhak tu leh ta
zolai zopau te theilo in
zomi hihna mansuah ding
lung ka phallo ee laizom u leh nau aw..

sesum minthang ngaihman in
khattang sawlbang na heina
namdang gamdang te lak ah,
haksat lawhchin na tuah chiang

 
tun leh zua, chiin leh tuai ci aw
angkawi von nih thum chi aw
zogam zomi khan kumsawt naa..hen ci aw

neihleh lam te tawh huh in la,
pilna siamna hawm in
sunggilkial tunnu zogam hai bang hong ngak
