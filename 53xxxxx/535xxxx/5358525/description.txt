Come aboard a beautiful 1600 mile journey along the spectacular Pacific coast of North America. Enjoy the sights and sounds of the California coast, the Siskyou Mountains, and the Cascade Range of Oregon and Washington as you travel north, from San Diego to Seattle. In addition to the Amtrak journey, enjoy side trips made to the Yosemite Mountain Sugar Pine Railroad, the California Western Railroad, the Sacramento Southern Railroad, the Spirit of Oregon Dinner Train, and the Mt. Hood Railroad. Also included are visits to the San Diego Trolley system, the California State Railroad Museum, the Seattle Monorail, and Yosemite National Park. Full DVD
