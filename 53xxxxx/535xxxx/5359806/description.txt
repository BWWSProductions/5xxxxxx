BBC R4 Archive Hour - 'Refuge From Tyrany'
Presented by Tony Kushner
Produced by Bob Dickinson
Broadcast January 24, 2004
Coded from tape at 128/44.1

Tony Kushner looks back at the experiences of Jewish
refugees fleeing from Nazi persecution who escaped to
Britain in the 1930s. Refugees who then faced further
difficulties when they arrived. Such as the lack of 
work, and internment as enemy aliens.

This programme had access to, and plays some of the
interviews surviving refugees gave to an archive project.

Also included is a short BBC World Service programme:
'Always It Haunts Me'. Survivors of the holocaust
resident in Australia talking about their experience.
.
