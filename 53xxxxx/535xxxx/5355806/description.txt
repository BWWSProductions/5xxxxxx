Dio-Holy Diver
==============
Released on May 25, 1983, the album has been hailed by critics as Dio's best work and a classic staple in the heavy metal genre.[1][2]  The album was eventually certified Gold in the US on September 12, 1984 and Platinum on March 21, 1989.
