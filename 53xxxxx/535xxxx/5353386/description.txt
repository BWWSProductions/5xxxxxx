A classic american band, succesfully blending trash with heavy and power metal. Melodic, good technically and catchy, they were always old school and faithfull to their roots, still remaining fresh. Never gaining mainstream importance, Metal Church was a very respected and influental band in the field of '80 and '90 metal.

Albums included:

(1984) Metal Church
(1986) The Dark
(1989) Blessing In Disguise
(1991) The Human Factor
(1994) Hanging In The Balance
(1998) Live
(1999) Masterpeace
(2004) The Weight Of The World
(2006) A Light In The Dark
(2008) This Present Wasteland
