BBC R3 Documentary - 'Mendelssohn, The Nazis, And Me'
Presented by Sheila Hayman
Produced by Jessica Isaacs
Broadcast May 9, 2009
Coded from a digital source at 128/44.1

Felix Mendelssohn, one of Germany's revered composers,
was born a Jew, but became a Christian. This programme
describes what happened to his music and his descendants
when, a hundred years on, the nazis noticed that he had
Jewish roots.
