Six books relating to evolutionary transitions.

Cradle of Life-- The Discovery of Earth's Earliest Fossils
(Ediacaran/Vendian to Cambrian)

Gaining Ground-- The Origin and Evolution of Tetrapods
(fish to amphibian)

At the Water's Edge-- Macroevolution and the Transformation of Life
(whales, pinnipeds, and other mammalian returns to the sea)

Taking Wing-- Archaeopteryx and the Evolution of Bird Flight
(archaeopteryx and early birds)

Unearthing the Dragon-- the Great Feathered Dinosaur Discovery
(feathered dinosaurs and early birds)

The Ghosts of Evolution-- Nonsensical Fruit, Missing Partners, and Other Ecological Anachronisms
(modern plants with possible missing animal partners)
