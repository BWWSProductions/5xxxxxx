                                 Morning After Dark (Live)

                     Single by Timbaland featuring Nelly Furtado & SoShy
 
                      from the album Timbaland Presents Shock Value II 

  "Morning After Dark" is a song by American hip-hop record producer and rapper, Timbaland taken from his third studio album, Timbaland Presents Shock Value II. The song features French singer SoShy and was released as the first single from the album on October 26, 2009.

The international version of the song features an additional rap verse from Canadian singer Nelly Furtado and it is this version which features on the album.
  
  
   
The song has so far debuted at 83 on the Billboard Hot 100 Airplay in the U.S on the week of November 9. It debuted at number 52 in Canada. It debuted at number 16 on the Swedish Singles Top 60 on the week of November 5. It also debuted at number 62 on the ARIA Charts in Australia on the week of November 9.On the week ending December 12, 2009, it also debuted at #76 on the Billboard Hot 100 and has since moved up to #61. On December 6, 2009, it entered at #9 on the UK Singles Chart and #5 on the UK R&B Chart, it has since climbed to #6 and #3 respectively

   Timbaland performed part of the lead single "Morning After Dark" live appeared with Nelly Furtado and SoShy for the first fully featured live performance of the song at the American Music Awards (2009) on November 22, 2009..
