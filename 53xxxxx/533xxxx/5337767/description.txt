 Monster Diesel Bike

Senior and the guys at OCC work to create the first ever diesel-powered chopper for the Monster Diesel Inc. Junior attends the grand opening of the new dog park he's designed for his hometown of Newburgh, NY. Mikey sets his fears aside to perform his stand up routine in front of a packed house at a NYC comedy club. Senior invites his wife Beth and her two sons to the shop to help him work on a personal project.
