                                 Aldiko Premium Version 1.2.4


                                                            
Aldiko is an ebook reading application which enables you to easily download and read thousands of books right on your Android phone.  Browse extensive catalogs of ebooks (most of them FREE)and download them directly from the app. Comfortable and highly customizable reading experience (font type, color margin, night mode, etc.), bookmarks, full text search.



                                         Requirements:


                                             None
