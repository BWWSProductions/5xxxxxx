BBC R4 - Christopher Trumbo's 'Trumbo'
Directed by Roger Mitchell
Broadcast August 15, 2009
Coded from a digital source at 128/44.1

Author Dalton Trumbo's experiences at the hands of
the House Un-American Activities Committee during
its investigation of the entertainment business.

Trumbo was one of the 'Hollywood Ten', the first
group of film professionals to testify before the
committee in 1947.

The play, prepared by Trumbo's son, is based on
transcripts of HUAC hearings and letters Dalton
Trumbo wrote during his ordeal.

Cast
Narrator - Nick Waring
Dalton Trumbo - Corin Redgrave
.
