As detailed in this week s Famitsu, Pop N Music Portable retains the simple rhythm gameplay of past entries in the series. The game lets you select between three, five, seven and nine button play. Modes of play include an adventure mode that s set in Pop N Land, a Free Play Mode, which lets you select a song of your choice for play, and a Music Player Mode, which lets you select a song of your choice just for listening. At least 80 songs will be included.
