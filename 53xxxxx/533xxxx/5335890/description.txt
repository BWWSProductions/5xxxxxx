After tracking down a threat to The Man of Steel and all of Metropolis Red Robin must team up with the new Kryptonian Nightwing to end this mysterious threat and rescue Flamebird.  But is all this just a red herring to distract the heroes from an even bigger threat to Gotham City and New Krypton? 

Written by Sterling Gates (Supergirl) and illustrated by Julian Lopez.
