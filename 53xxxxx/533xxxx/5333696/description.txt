Description
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Luminous Creatures is an ambient experimental electronic music project bringing forth introspective soundscapes in the ritual tradition of Coil and the early Industrialists. Taking inspiration from the visual arts, nature, mundane life, contemplation, mystery and all wonderful things, Luminous Creatures spins a web of novel sound.
