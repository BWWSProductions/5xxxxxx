6-issue miniseries based on the Electronic Arts video game developed by EA Digital Illusions CE (DICE) and published by Electronic Arts. 

Faith is a Runner in the city - a courier who delivers sensitive cargo by traversing the rooftops of the city's skyscrapers. But how did she come by this unique trade...and what secrets from her past may affect her future?

Written by Rhianna Pratchett, with art by Matthew Dow Smith and covers by Niko Henrichon.
