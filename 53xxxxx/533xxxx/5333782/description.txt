                                   iHome Version 1.5.0


                                                             
easy to use, fast and efficient home screen replacement for android, supports all major screen resolutions



                                        Features:



* Support OS 1.5,1.6 & 2.0

* provide a whole new experience on Droid perfectly

* support aHome,openHome&pandaHome's themes effectively.

* support QVGA,WQVGA400,WQVAG432,HVAG,WVGA800,WVGA854


                                        Requirements:

                           Requires Android OS version 1.5 & above
