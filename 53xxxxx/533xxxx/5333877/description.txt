                              TasKiller Full Version 2.6.1


                                                              
Taskiller is a tool allowing you to close your android aplications with one click. Many programs continue to run in the background while we believe them closed. These programs cluttering up memory and slow your android. With its widget "kill-all", with one click all your applications are closed simultaneously. With its ignore list, keep essentials applications unkilled.



                                      Requirements:

                                           None
