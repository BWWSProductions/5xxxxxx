BBC R3 - Alan Bennett's 'Kafka's Dick'
Music by Nicholas Kok!
Produced by Gordon House
Broadcast February 21, 1991
Coded from tape at 128/44.1

Franz Kafka fans Syd and Linda are visited at home in
Yorkshire by the shades of Franz Kafka and Max Brod. Franz
is still unhappy that Brod did not destroy his MSS.

Another point of departure is reached when Franz's dad shows
up. Franz now has something else to worry about, will his dad
reveal his big secret?
