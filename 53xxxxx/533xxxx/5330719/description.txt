This is a book of sweater patterns from the 50s or 60s which contains almost any sort of sweater you might want to make.  Some of the patterns are dated, but the majority are classics: cardigans, pullovers, v-necks, crew necks. . you name it.

There's also a section with blank patterns for all sorts of sweaters so that you can do your own designs.

Over 50 pages.  Well worth using.
