First time in history the EWTO publish an official DVD of the 1st and the 2nd section of the wooden dummy and its applications. The Leader of the EWTO, Grandmaster Prof. Dr. Keith R. Kernspecht and his Team of high ranked WingTsun-Masters present the first two sets of the wooden dummy form, the classic applications of the first two sets (Wooden Dummy Chi-Sao), and Fighting Drills and applications for real fighting. You will also find some shots from Langenzell Castle, where Grandmaster Kernspecht shows how to mix up the techniqes totally freely without prearranged order, which never before was shown on Video.
