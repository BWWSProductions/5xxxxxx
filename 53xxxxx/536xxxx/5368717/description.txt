BBC R4 Documentary - 'Walking A Stick Back Home'
Presented by James Crowden
Produced by Matt Thompson
Broadcast May 23, 2008
Coded from a digital source at 128/44.1 

James Crowden inherited a walking stick once
owned by Thomas De Quincey. He takes it home
to Wordsworth's Dove Cottage.

Along the way he talks about De Quincey and
the Wordsworths.
