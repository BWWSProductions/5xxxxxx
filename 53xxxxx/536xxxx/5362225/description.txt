Locul de intalnire nu poate fi schimbat [Era caritatii]/Meeting place cannot be changed

Seria 1-5 Complet

A young officer Volodya Sharapov returning to Moscow to work as a police officer following the end of WWII, meets Gleb Zheglov, who is the chief of the squad that fights organized crime. Their main task is to track down the Black Cat (Chernaya Koshka) gang that has been terrorizing the city, but in order to accomplish this, Sharapoy must infiltrate the gang by going undercover.

Subtitles: RO and EN
