BBC R3 Documentary - 'Chaplin, Celebrity, And Modernism'
Presented by Mark Kermode
Produced by Fiona Dye
Broadcast July 17, 2007
Coded from tape at 128/44.1

BBC Blurb
Mark Kermode investigates Chaplin's celebrity role
and influence on world culture from the modernists
and Dadaists, through to the Russian avant-garde
to imitators in Bombay.

Chaplin's private archive reveals remarkable letters
written to Charlie Chaplin by, for example, Truman
Capote, Winston Churchill and James Agee.
End quote
