Our kids frequently ask us to read something aloud. However, the adults often need somebody to read for them too. That is why 1st Read It Aloud! was created. Since now, you can listen to the text you want being read by the voice you prefer with the pitch and speed you'd like to. Just select some text from any application, press hot key and enjoy listening to the live voice reading your documents, readmes, notes, e-mails, web sites and a lot of other useful stuff. You even don't need to copy desired text into clipboard! What can be easier? Just select and listen!

1st Read It Aloud! support numerous voices in more than twenty different languages, It also supports both old SAPI4 and new SAPI5 speech synthesis technologies. Give a rest to you yes. Let computer talk with you and read you aloud as your parents did in your childhood.
