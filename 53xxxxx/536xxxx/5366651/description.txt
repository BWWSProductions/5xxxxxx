Title: Upstream Pre-Intermediate B1
Description: DVD Video PAL
ISBN: 978-1-84679-260-1
Level: Pre-Intermediate
CEF Level: B1
Authors: Virginia Evans, Jenny Dooley


Upstream Beginner A1+ to Level B1+ DVDs combine factual reports, in the style of TV documentaries, with 2-D or 3-D animation of stories. The language syllabus is based on the relevant coursebooks. The DVDs can be used for practice at home or in the classroom, as a component of each course or as a supplement to any course at the same level.

Upstream Beginner A1+ to Level B1+ DVDs are accompanied by Activity Books to be used with the DVDs.
