BBC R3 Documentary - 'School Of Illumination'
Presented by Shusha Guppy
Produced by Phil Pagan
Broadcast March 19, 2006
Coded from tape at 128/44.1

The story of how Islamic philosophers brought 
classical Greek thought back to the West.

By the 9th Century, the works of the great Greek
philosophers, kept alive in the East, had largely
been lost to West. As the Islamic empire spread
to Europe, this knowledge travelled back with it.
