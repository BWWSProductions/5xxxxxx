Super strength, the ability to fly and extra-speedy reflexes should make a girl's life easier, but they only seem to complicate things for Supergirl.  Life gets a lot crazier, though, when a new junior high adversary, Belinda Zee shows up.  And with girls like Belinda around, facing down Super-Villains doesn't seem so bad.

Written by Landry Q. Walker, with art by Eric Jones.
