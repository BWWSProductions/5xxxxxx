Radwimps is a Japanese rock band formed in 2001 while the members were in high school. Their name is a mix of the English words 'rad' and 'wimp'. It is a term they coined themselves meaning "excellent weakling" or "superlative coward". Sometimes the band goes by just the shortened version of their name, Rad.
