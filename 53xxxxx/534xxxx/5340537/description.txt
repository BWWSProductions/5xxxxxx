Tearing its way through the events of Day of Vengence and Infinite Crisis, the mystical Blue Beetle Scarab has chosen its new guardian: teenager Jaime Reyes!  But supernatural powers can be a blessing or a curse, and when it comes to the powers of the Scarab, you don't get one without the other.

written Keith Giffen, John Rogers and Matt Sturges, with art by Cully Hamner and Rafael Albuquerque.
