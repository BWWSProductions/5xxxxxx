Developer...............: Monolith Productions.
Publisher...............: Sierra Entertainment.
Platform................: Microsoft Windows.
Release date............: 2001.
Genre...................: First-person shooter.
Mode....................: Single player, multiplayer.
Media...................: CD.

==========================================================================|

Aliens versus Predator 2 allows the player to choose one of three
characters: an Alien, Predator or human Colonial Marine. Each character
has different objectives, abilities and weapons at their disposal. The
single-player campaigns present the player with conventional series of
levels that are designed around the abilities of each character.

Aliens versus Predator 2 Primal Hunt The player is once again able to
play as either a human, Predator, or Alien character, each with its own
campaign and abilities. The plot lines of the three characters intersect
and set up the events of the original game.
