Garmin MapSource Special version 6.15.7

This modified executable will let you upload your mapset (gmapsupp.img)
to your PC drives too, not only to or your GPS.

This is the last version of MapSource, which uses old type unlocking (doesn't create gmapsupp.gma) and can be used with CN Europe 2010.30 and CN North America 2010.40 and newer.

Instructions for use:
- Install MapSource 6.15.7 (get it from Garmin website:)
- Replace MapSource.exe
