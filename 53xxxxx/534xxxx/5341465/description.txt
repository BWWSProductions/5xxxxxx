Genre...................: Tank Combat, Racing
Mode....................: Single player, multiplayer.
Media...................: CD

==========================================================================|

You are in control of an artificially intelligent tank, that along with
its fellow AI tanks, has decided to abandon its human masters and just
go have some fun.

The tanks come in two varieties: steel and liquid. The steel tanks
resemble real-life tanks, while the liquid types are whimsical fantasy
tanks. Weapons and power-ups are scattered around the maps, and range
in destructive power from light machine guns to tactical nuclear missiles.
