Garmin MapSource Special version 6.15.11

This modified executable will let you upload your mapset (gmapsupp.img)
to your local and network drives too, not only to or your GPS.

This version uses new unlocking mechanism (creates gmapsupp.gma) for the latest maps e.g.: CN Europe 2010.30 and CN North America 2010.40.

Instructions for use:
- Install MapSource 6.15.11
  (get it from Garmin website:)
- Replace MapSource.exe
