Etta James(Born Jamesetta Hawkins on jan 25 1938)is an american blues, soul, R&B, 

rock&roll and jazz singer and songwriter. James is the winner of four grammys and

seventeen Blues Music Awards. She was inducted to the Rock & Roll Hall Of Fame in

1993, the Blues Hall Of Fame 2001 and the Grammy Hall Of Fame both in 1999 and 2008.
