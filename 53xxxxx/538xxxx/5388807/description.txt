- Description
This is a wide collection, a videography if you will, of Front 242.  Included
are official music videos as well as live recordings of concerts,
interviews, and miscellany.  Because the band members have been involved in a
lot of side projects, I have included what I could dig up, too.  Some rare
Cobalt 60, LaTchak, Modern Cubism, 32 Crash, and CodeR23 are also here.

- Purpose
I am uploading this media, because Front 242 is an old band and one of my
favorites.  Primarily because a lot of this media is irreplaceable and comes
from dated analog sources, distributing it on the Internet freely as such
should help liberate it and keep it alive forever.

- Housekeeping
I have spotty Internet access right now, so I will seed this over the next
few weeks.  It's a lot of data to distribute while travelling.

- Requests
If you have a full copy of Front 242's so-called "Tape One" that is a
live recording of them in Paris, please seed or upload it.  See 
http://thepiratebay.org/torrent/5338645/ for details.

If you have any recording of Red Team live, this is exceptionally rare and
should be uploaded.

If you feel that you have something you can add to this, please reply
in-comment form and seed your own torrent.  Lossless format is best, but
we take what we can get.

There are other videos that should be digitized and uploaded:
Integration 8x10, Front by Front Live 1989, Official Version Live,
Tyranny Live, etc.  Plenty of others remain.

Further side-projects should be digitized and uploaded.

I encourage all other fans to distribute what they have.  Let's make it a
good team effort to digitize what we can.  This is especially good niche
music that should not be lost on future generations.
