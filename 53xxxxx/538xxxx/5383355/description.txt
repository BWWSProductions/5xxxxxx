.                                M A N E I G E
			 - First Three Studio Albums -


=====================================================================================

Widely overlooked Jazz Rock Group from Quebec. Their music is a blend of classical and folk elements together with jazz and chamber rock creating a very progressive and unique style.

Highly  recommended!

=====================================================================================
