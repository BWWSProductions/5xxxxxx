PLOT

Syfy Stargate Universe's newest series follows a group of sailors, scientists and citizens who must defend against an attack when their secret base is detected.
They are forced to evacuren through the Star Gate. The survivors are desperate to board an old vessel that follows a preset rate and the survivors can not return to Earth.

The danger, the adventure and the hope that they find on board the Destiny, will reveal who the heroes and who the enemies are.
What begins as a simple reconnaissance mission turns into a mission without end,
when the Universe crew discovers the ship no longer can return to Earth and that they will have to maintain themselves aboard the Destiny.

and the crew has no control over the navigation scheme of the ship, if anyone is left behind,
there is no way back for them, there are new species discovered, they encounter new enemies and experience new adventures.
