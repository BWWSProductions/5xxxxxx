CD1 features the first studio performances of one of the worlds greatest performers, Marilyn Manson with his band the Spooky kids. Recorded in 1990 by record producer Rat Bastard Falestra and Marilyn Manson's then girlfriend Missie Romero.

CD2 was recorded live in 1990 in Fort Lauderdale, Florida, USA.
This recording is the first recorded live album by America's indisputable king of glam and goth  rock.
Featuring unknown tracks that have never been heard on any of his later albums.
