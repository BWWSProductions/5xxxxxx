Valkyrie Profile 2: Silmera, developed by the creators of the Star Ocean series and Radiata Stories, tells the story of another valkyrie sister

that takes place hundreds of years before the events of Valkyrie Profile: Lenneth. In this latest installment of the legendary RPG series from

fan-favorite developer tri-Ace, players can delve into an advanced battle system with complex combo attack strategies and intricate puzzle-solving adventures. An epic score and gorgeous visuals serve as a backdrop against

which a gripping story of Norse deities, revenge, and divine warfare unfolds.
