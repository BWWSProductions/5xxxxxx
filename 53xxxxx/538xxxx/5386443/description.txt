third film of the very talented british award winning director lynne ramsay, known by her debut feature film "ratcatcher". "gasman" tells the story of two children who react with naive simple emotion to a situation imposed upon them by their father's secret. "gasman" won the jury prize at the cannes festival in 1998. this release was riped from the criterion collection dvd of "ratcatcher", which include her three first short movies.
