The Singing Detective (2003):

A film noir, The Singing Detective is based on the BBC miniseries of the same name, a work by Dennis Potter. Starring Robert Downey Jr. and Mel Gibson, we are allowed access into the life of novelist Dan Dark who is suffering from the skin disease psoriasis and a crippling arthritis. As Dark continues to deal with his pain, he delves deeper into fantasy, resulting in several stroy lines being told simultaneously. 

Also starring: Robin Wright Penn, Jeremy Northam, Katie Holmes, Adrien Brody and Alfre Woodard.
