        the evil witch Hydia watches the event via her cauldron and, 
        disgusted by the frivolity, tells her daughters that they must 
        ruin it. Hydia's daughters, Draggle and Reeka, are inexperienced
        at causing mischief and fail utterly at ruining the festival.
        They return to the Volcano of Gloom in disgrace, where Witch 
        Hydia presents a better plan - they'll make Smooze. The sisters
        collect a series of disgusting ingredients, but neglect to obtain the                 Phlume, the prospect of which terrifies them. The spell seems 
        to work regardless, creating a pool of sentient purple lava 
        that gleefully buries Ponyland.
