Wildfire - Mando Diao Bootleg Collection Vol.1 2009 LDG Release by Tooken

This is a bootleg from the swedish rock band Mando Diao. One of the best bands around in my opinion. This is a collection of live songs played between 2003 and 2009 in Europe and the U.S.A. The quality of some songs is not as good as it should but mind that this is a bootleg so it's for Diao fans. Hope you all enjoy and maybe a Vol.2 will come soon. Track listing is as it follows.
