When a mysterious denizen of Gotham City gives Bruce Wayne an old box, it sets in motion a series of events that shake Batman's world.  Suddenly, dark villains including Ra's al Ghul, the Penguin and Catwoman, as well as dozens of mysterious mercenaries are battling to possess the simple drinking bowl in Batman's hands.  Could this really be the Holy Grail of legend?

Written by Chuck Dixon and illustrated by John Van Fleet.
