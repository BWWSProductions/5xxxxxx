Adapted by John Harvey
Directed by Sally Avens
Broadcast April 13, 1998

Post WWII reflections on a brief doomed wartime
affair and a struggle with orthodox faith.

Loosely based on Greene's wartime thing with a
Liberal peer's wife, Lady Catherine Walston.
