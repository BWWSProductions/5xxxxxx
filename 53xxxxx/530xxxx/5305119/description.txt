"Baby" is a song by Canadian recording artist Justin Bieber. It was released as the lead single from the latter half of Bieber's debut album, My World (Part II). The track was written by Bieber with Christopher "Tricky" Stewart and Terius "The-Dream" Nash" who worked with Bieber on "One Time" and also by R&B singer Christina Milian and rapper Ludacris.It was available for digital download on on January 18, 2010. The song received airplay directly after release, but will officially impact mainstream and rhythmic radio on January 26, 2010.

Bieber first premiered the song performing on MuchMusic on December 28, 2009..
