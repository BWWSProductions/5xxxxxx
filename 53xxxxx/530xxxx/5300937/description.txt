BBC R3 - Yasmina Reza's 'Art'
Translated by Christopher Hampton
Music by Steven Warbeck
Production Manager - Sarah Scott
Directed by Christopher Morahern
Broadcast May 7, 2000
Coded from tape at 128/44.1

A three way meditation on taste, style and
fraternity erupts when Serge pays 100,000
Euros for a canvas painted white.

In the late 1990s' this play was a big success
in London and New York. But the stage never
managed to assemble a cast like Radio 3's ...
