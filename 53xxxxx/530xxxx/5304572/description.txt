BBC R4 - Mark Lawson's 'St Graham & St Evelyn - Pray For Us'
Directed by Robyn Read
Broadcast March 7, 2003
Coded from tape at 128/44.1

The Vatican's modernising branch suggests canonising a prominent
Catholic writer. Evelyn Waugh & Graham Greene are the front-runners.

Deliberations showing that the two differed on key moral issues are
interspersed with illustrative scenes from the writers' lives.

Cast
Evelyn Waugh - Simon Day
Graham Greene - John Sessions
Cleric one - Peter Wickham 
Cleric two - Daniel Evans
