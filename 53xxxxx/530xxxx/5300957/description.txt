Return to 221 Baker St. and play as the famous Sherlock Holmes to solve 16 mysterious crimes in Victorian England. Dust off your magnifying glass and use your astute observational skills to 
gather evidence in 60 brilliantly rendered locations around Victorian England including 
Stonehenge, Marlsbury Castle, London's Music Hall and more! Unlock vital clues by playing over 50 
mind-bending puzzles and mini-games in each case, including anagrams, cryptograms, and more. 
Featuring over 40 levels of hidden object and find the difference gameplay with more than 100

to catching the culprit!
