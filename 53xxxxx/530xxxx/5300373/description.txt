'Saturday' is Brit Pop band Ocean Colour Scene's ninth studio album 

and marks the 21st anniversary of their formation in 1990. After 

touring with Oasis in 1995 and chart success with 'Moseley Shoals', 

Ocean Colour Scene continued to release new material as well as a stem 

of successful live albums. 'Saturday' continues with the band's brand 

of retro orientated rock and was recorded at the iconic Rockfield 

studios.
