BBC R4 Documentary - 'Vive L'espagne!'
Presented by Huw Edwards
Directed by Michael Sircan
Broadcast March 22, 2005
Coded from tape at 128/44.1

In 2005 Huw Edwards presented a series called:
'One Country to the Tune Of Another', in which
he examined musical influences countries had
on one another.

This one looks at Spanish influences in the
music of Ravel, Bizet, and Chabrier.
