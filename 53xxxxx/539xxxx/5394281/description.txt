Necrodeath is an Italian thrash metal band from Liguria. It is one of the first thrash metal bands born in Italy, along with Schizo and Bulldozer. They take inspiration from Slayer, Possessed, Kreator, Celtic Frost and Bathory[1]. The band is also renowned for its great live performances, and notably for their mighty "wall of sound".
