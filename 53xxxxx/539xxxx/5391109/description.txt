What's more fun than 10 in the tank and four on the floor?
Tom Wopat, John Schneider, Catherine Bach and more of your
Dukes of Hazzard favorites together again!

In Reunion!, folks come home to Hazzard and discover a plot
to turn Hazzard Swamp into a theme park...leaving the swamp
critters high and dry. Before you can say wait a durn minute,
the cousins are back in the General Lee, tearing up the roads
to stop the nefarious scheme.
