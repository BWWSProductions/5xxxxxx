Dance with Me is the fifth studio album by Canadian country music artist Johnny Reid. It was released on March 10, 2009 by Open Road Recordings. The first single, "A Woman Like You," reached the top 10 of the Radio & Records Canadian country singles chart. The album was certified Gold within 48 hours of release.
