Disc 1 is ram packed with dance acts that have been taking the charts by storm and tracks that are set to light things up in the coming year. Sidney Samson kicks things off with the track that's been jostling for the number 1 spot and getting everyone doing that dance - 'Riverside (Let's Go!)'. Hot on its heels from Radio 1's A-list are Example with 'Won't Go Quietly' and Drum & Bass golden boy Sub Focus with his surprise Electro smash 'Could This Be Real'. There are massive number 1s from Calvin Harris and Dizzee Rascal, and the future hits come thick and fast from the likes of Simian Mobile Disco, Zinc and Deadmau5.

CD 2 picks up the pace with a pumping mix of Trance anthems. Sash!, Angel City, Chicane, Agnelli & Nelson, Ian Van Dahl, Fragma, DJ Sammy; a who's who of the most uplifting names dance music. This euphoric disc ends on a high with a banging version of the massive 'Tik Tok'. Finally CD 3 delivers the finest selection of house classics, featuring Snap!, Nightcrawlers, Basement Jaxx, Moloko, Bob Sinclar, Freemasons, Armand Van Helden and loads more.

With The Very Best Of upfront, Trance, and classics, it can only be Euphoric Dance Breakdown 2010.
