When Geri Halliwell (Ginger Spice) departed from the immensely popular pop quintet The Spice Girls, the group was reduced in number but not in popularity. The remaining four, (Baby, Sporty, Scary & Posh) remained a well-polished singing and dancing sensation. In 1998, the foursome set off on a whirlwind 35 city tour of the United States, entertaining millions across the huge country.

SPICE GIRLS IN AMERICA is a candid documentary which details the behind-the-scenes action of this tour. Get to know the four distinct personalities who make up the Spice Girls as they move through hotel rooms, television studios, malls, and their tour bus.
