Directed by Jose Antonio Dorado, this fictionalized biographical drama stars Fernando Solorzano as Jaime Caicedo, aka El Rey -- Colombia's first cocaine kingpin. When El Rey meets an American Peace Corps volunteer (Olivier Pages) in the 1970s, the twosome hatches a lucrative scheme to export massive quantities of blow to the United States. The film's supporting cast includes Cristina Umana, Vanessa Simon and Marlon Moreno.
