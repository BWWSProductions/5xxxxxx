Plot

Liberty Wallace (Linda Fiorentino) is a key player for one of the largest gun manufacturers in the United 

States, McCloud Industries: she's the Vice President of Marketing, the daughter of the company's founder, and 

married to the company's ruthless CEO, Victor Wallace (Oliver Platt).

While former war hero Victor is busy trafficking in international armaments, Liberty takes business breaks to 

conduct an affair with actor Russell Williams (Martin Cummins).

The balance of their marriage of convenience shifts when a sniper named Joe (Wesley Snipes) aims his rifle at 

Liberty as she walks through a busy Los Angeles park on her way to meet Russell.
