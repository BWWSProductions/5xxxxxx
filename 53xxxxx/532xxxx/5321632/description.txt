LEONARD COHEN: I'M YOUR MAN (2006)

Appeared at the 2006 Sundance Film Festival 
Poet, songwriter and legendary ladies' man, Leonard Cohen comes into intimate focus in documentary filmmaker Lian Lunson's celebratory profile. Centering around a January 2005 tribute concert at the Sydney Opera House, I'm Your Man offers a captivating look into the life and art of the legendary Canadian troubadour. Featuring a rare interview and performances of many of Cohen's best-loved songs by Bono, Nick Cave, Kate and Anna McGarrigle, Rufus Wainwright and others. A "wonderful documentary portrait"
