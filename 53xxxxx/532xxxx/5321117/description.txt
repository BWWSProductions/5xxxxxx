        designed specially to fulfill the needs of large enterprises in

        hardware RAID and Windows 2000, 2003, 2008, XP, Vista Operating

       simplify their jobs while using their resources more efficiently.

       * Disk Copy Wizard, Partition Copy Wizard to protect data.
       * Supports Hardware RAID.
