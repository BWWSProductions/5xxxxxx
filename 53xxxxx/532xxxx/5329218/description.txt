This is Chris Chameleon's first solo album in Afrikaans. Chris Chameleon composed the music and the lyrics are poems of the South African poet Ingrid Jonker.

Hierdie album is Chris Chameleon se eerste solo album in Afrikaans. Die liedjies is gedigte van Ingrid Jonker wat Chris Chameleon getoonset het.
