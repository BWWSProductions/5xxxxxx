The Wonder Years was aired on ABC from January 31, 1988 until May 12, 1993. Neal Marlens and Carol Black created the series. Kevin Arnold deals with social issues and historical events by narrating what he have learned from his experience. Each episode features the experiences of Kevin during his adulthood. He encountered full range of trials and traumas together with his best friend Paul and sometimes with his girlfriend Winnie. The Wonder years has achieved to be on the top spot of the Nielsen awards. It also won Emmy for best comedy series. It was awarded a George Foster Peabody Award and at the same time it was really a show that was loved by its audience. Major characters were Fred Savage, Danica Mc Kellar, Josh Saviano, Dan Lauria, Alley Mills Olivia d'Abo and Jason Hervey.
