Nero 7 is a comprehensive multimedia package that allows for much more than just
recording discs.

Nero is one of the most advanced programs in its class,
allows many types of data recording to CD / DVD / Blu-ray Disc (BD-AV).
Its additional advantage is the accompanying application, designed to test
recorders, audio editing, create album covers, etc.



Operating System: MS Windows 98/Me/2000/XP/Vista/Windows 7
