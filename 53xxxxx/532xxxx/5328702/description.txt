Comic book series based on the based on Blizzard's smash-hit game, which focuses on the War Pigs, a team of outlaws reunited by their former captain for one last job: the assassination of Jim Raynor! 

In December 2009, WildStorm stated that the the publication of Starcraft would end with #7 and they would change the format to original graphic novels set to be released in 2010.
