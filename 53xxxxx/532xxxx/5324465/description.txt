Batman: The Dark Knight Strikes Again is the sequel to Frank Miller's 1986 miniseries, Batman: The Dark Knight Returns and was written and illustrated by Miller with colours by Lynn Varley.

Some years after the events of "The Dark Knight Returns", America has become an even worse place to live under the dictatorship of Lex Luthor.  Batman comes out of self-imposed retirement, bringing an army of other forgotten heroes to bear in the war against the crime and corruption of Luthor's police state.
