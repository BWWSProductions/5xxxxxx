                              Can't Fight the Moonlight

                                Single by LeAnn Rimes 

                              from the album I Need You 

    "Can't Fight the Moonlight" is a song written by Diane Warren, recorded by LeAnn Rimes and featured on the soundtrack of the film Coyote Ugly, and in the film itself. Released first in 1999, then in 2000 and 2001, the song had huge success, reaching number-one in eight countries.

    
The song sold 565,000 copies in the UK as stated by the Official UK Charts Company. The song was the fifth best seller of 2000 in the UK and the 55th of the 2000 decade.it enjoyed two chart runs on the Billboard Hot 100. On its original chart run in 1999/2000, "Can't Fight the Moonlight" stalled at number 71. It regained renewed interest in late 2001 and 2002, and rose up the charts to number eleven. In the U.S..
