PwnageTool 3.1.4 is a Mac based iPhone firmware creation tool for creating custom ipsw/firmware for the iPod Touch (1st, 2nd and 3rd gen), iPhone, iPhone 3G and 3GS  This version supports the 3.1.2 firmware from Apple. For full details (which are often updated) visit the iphone DevTeam blog. http://blog.iphone-dev.org
