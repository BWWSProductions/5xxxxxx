Lighthouse is the sophomore EP from Swedish alternative quartet Orange Earth. The record was released without label in October 2009.


Tracklisting:


01. Airless.
02. Dissonance
03. From Noah
04. LXXVIII (Lighthouse)
05. Saturday Dawn
