========================================================================|

Essentially, there are two stories in the game that intertwine, the
first involving the major events of the film (with the character
making central contributions) and a personal story arc. Gameplay
consists of undertaking and completing eighteen story missions 
while in the meantime performing various tasks such as taking over
rival family holdings and carrying out contract hits.
