"Don't Stop Believin'" is a song recorded by the cast of American television series, Glee. It is the first single released from the upcoming soundtrack of the series, Glee: The Music, Volume 1 and was performed on the first episode of the season, Pilot.

It is a cover of "Don't Stop Believin'", a 1981 power ballad by American rock band, Journey. Released as a digital download on June 2, 2009, the song managed to perform well in Australia and the United States where it charted within the top five on both of their national charts.
