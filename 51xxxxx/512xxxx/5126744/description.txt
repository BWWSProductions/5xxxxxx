Host Terry Schappert, a former Green Beret, invites viewers to travel the globe to discover exactly what it takes to be a warrior. Terry takes viewers inside the culture, the weaponry, the tactics, and ultimately inside the psyches of the most famous warriors of all time. From the Spartans to the Samurai, Zulus to Medieval Knights, Terry digs deep into the mindset of a warrior, revealing the timeless qualities that resonate with History viewers; bravery, loyalty and courage. It's all part of the amazing journey to discover the heart and mind of a warrior
