The Band - Cahoots - 1971

Cahoots is the fourth LP by Canadian-American rock group The Band, and their last all-original studio album for four years. It received only mixed reviews when it first appeared. Robbie Robertson wrote or cowrote all but one of the album tracks: Dylan's When I Paint My Masterpiece. Robertson's occasional co-writers include Danko and Helm on Life is a Carnival, and Van Morrison on 4% Pantomime.
