Police: Destruction Street is very a fast arcade game. City streets are overrun by criminals - there are escaped convicts on every corner and every driver on the road is crazy. Catch them all and bring back the order.

Features:
-4 large cities to explore
-highly destructible environments
-4 types of missions
-cartoon graphics
-extremely fast paced gameplay

System Requirements:
System: Windows XP,Vista
CPU: 2 GHz
RAM: 512 MB
Graphics card: Pixel Shader 1.2 compatible (GeForce 4, Radeon 8500 or newer)
