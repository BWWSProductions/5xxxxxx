DJ Manian (born Manuel Reuter) is a German producer, trance DJ, label owner (Zooland Records) and a member of the award-winning music dance trio Cascada, alongside fellow DJ Yanou (who he has collaborated with many times) and Natalie Horler(who has had guest vocal with some of his songs). He has produced a number of singles under various pseudonyms, and maintains a number of different musical projects, including Spencer and Hill (under the pseudonym Josh Hill), Tune Up! (with Yanou, now defunct), Bulldozzer, M.Y.C., Ampire, Liz Kay
