"Three Words" is the upcoming debut album by British pop singer Cheryl Cole.

The album is Cole's first as a solo artist, following five studio albums with pop group Girls Aloud. Cole's first foray into a solo music career occurred when she featured on will.i.am's "Heartbreaker".
