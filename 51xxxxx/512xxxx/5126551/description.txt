Science: So what? So everything!

London science teacher Alom Shaha investigates a question that is very close to his heart: Why is science important? In addition to lots of teachers and education professionals, contributors to the film include:

- A.C. Grayling, philosopher
- Susan Blackmore, psychologist
- Marcus du Sautoy, mathematician
- Adam Hart-Davis, broadcaster
- Robin Weiss, oncologist
- Francisco Diego, astronomer
