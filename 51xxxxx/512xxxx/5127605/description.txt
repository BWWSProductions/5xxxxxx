  in a distant future. As the player, you command

  sports arena. Core game play centers on intense
  tank-on-tank multiplayer team combat seen through
  an immersive, first person cockpit. Layered on
  this is a goal-oriented game type that allows for
  high level strategy and team dynamics. Players
  can level up to unlock new ranks, weapons, and
  upgrades.
