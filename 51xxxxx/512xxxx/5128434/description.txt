  apples? Perform a somersault?

  bake delicious homemade apple pies for her
  piglets. So she's gone out to the forest to
  gather some apples.

  But those apples aren't coming in without a fight!

  path (not to mention the forest's many hungry
  inhabitants), ready to snatch her apples from
  right under her nose!

  Only with your help can Mrs. Piggly overcome
  rain, sleet and snow and coax those apples
  safely into her home.
