Features 7 1954 recordings by Elvis and the Blue Moon Boys such as Maybellene, That's Alright Mama, Blue Moon of Kentucky, expressing the raw emotion packed atmosphere of his early live performances.

During his time in the Army and Germany, Elvis gave few interviews to the media without the consent and control of his manager, Colonel Tom Parker. This CD contains a number of such rare interviews where Elvis, in unguarded moments, revealing his vulnerability and his true, uncomplicated nature that his world-wide fans understood and loved.
