Starring disabled actor Mat Fraser, along with gangster stalwart Frank Harper and Faye Tozer (formerly of Steps), Kung Fu Flid centres around the story of Jimmy Loveit (Fraser), who has to try and rescue his wife and daughter from the clutches of a people trafficking gang led by underworld boss Barry Morgan (Harper) after a bungled revenge attack.
