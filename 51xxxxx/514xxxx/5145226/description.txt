The complete soundtracks for the original Star Wars films (A New Hope, The Empire Strikes Back, and Return of the Jedi), performed by the London Symphony Orchestra with John Williams conducting.  Also includes the soundtrack for the first Indiana Jones film, Raiders of the Lost Ark, and the Boston Pops Orchestra recording "Summon the Heroes," containing all three themes composed by Williams for the Olympic Games.  Most of the tracks are numbered rather than named but a list is included for each set.
