-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                                 Release Notes
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PASS ON TRADITIONS started out back in late 2008, with influences from bands 
like Millencolin, NOFX, Pennywise, Rise Against and many more. We went through 
some names, recruited members, played live a few times and spent a lot of hours 
in the rehearsal room during the first year.

In 2009 we recorded our first demo. The demo is produced by Gustav Brunn, 
guitarist in the swedish melodic punk rock band Atlas Losing Grip.
