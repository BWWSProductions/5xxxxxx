Brothers Anton and Lewis Patzner started off playing metal violin and cello duets on the streets in Berkeley, California in 2002. They quickly released a seven-song acoustic EP and began playing rock shows as Judgement Day. 
http://www.stringmetal.com/judgementday/

As the title suggests, this is an official collection of excerpts!
The first three songs are from "Peacocks/Pink Monsters", the fourth is a bonus track and the following six are from "Opus 3 Acoustic"
