-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                                Release Notes
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Took the BD Region A release of Akira, encoded with 2-pass x264, trimmed the
black edges, applied a small degree of Warpsharp to clean the noise from the
original.
  Worked the original 192Kbps stereo audio track from the R4 Madman DVD with
Audacity & eac3to. The goal was to get the hidef video and the original audio
dub to fit into 4G. It does, but I've also interleaved the 5.1 Pioneer audio
dub (recoded to 448 Kbps AC3-5.1 from the BD's trueHD track) as well, so the
file, as is, fits on a DVD-5 blank. The video and first audio stream will fit
on a 4G flash drive if rebuilt using mkvmerge.
