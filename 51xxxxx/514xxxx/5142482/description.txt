The "PPL Confuser" includes the JAR PPL Questions, Answers & Explanations for:

# Aviation Law & Procedures
# Meteorology
# Navigation
# Aircraft General (Aeroplanes)
# Flight Planning & Performance
# Human Performance
# Radio Telephony


In the PPL Confuser the answers are explained and illustrated to fully enlighten you as to why a particular answer is the correct choice.
Highly informative but also very concise and easy to read and digest, they are excellent publications to consolidate knowledge and understand what the examiner is asking.
