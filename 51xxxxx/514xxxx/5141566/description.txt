    between the two classic Star Wars movie trilogies.
  * Additional content includes two levels set on Coruscant and Tatooine and

    package.

    becoming the most powerful Sith Lord in the universe.

    Sith. During their hunt for the reclusive Jedi, players will also explore
    the depths of Jabba the Hutt's palace and encounter notorious bounty

    planet Hoth on a crusade to crush the Rebellion once and for all. While
