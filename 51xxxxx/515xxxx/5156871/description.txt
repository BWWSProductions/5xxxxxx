ZEARLE DISCOGRAPHY

Three albums by Zearle
revolutionary Marxist and hip-hop musician:

Youthful Folly
Class War
Vanguard

These albums are all available from zearle.com

Zearle is a revolutionary who makes what he describes
as 'music for the vanguard proletariat' - political
hip-hop that is progressive as well as conscious
party music for international youth

If you listen only to one track, check out track 7
from 'Class War'  - Hackers & Crackers
the anthem to piracy
