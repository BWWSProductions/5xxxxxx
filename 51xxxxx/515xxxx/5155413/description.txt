This MCTS Windows 7 Exam Study Guide has excellent coverage of all the exam objectives in a systematic approach. So you can be confident that you're getting the instruction you need to take Microsoft's new MCTS exam (70-680). This book is packed with practical guidance and hands-on exercises to reinforce critical skills. Over 350 screenshots and figures, as well as an oustanding author who brings real-world scenarios, comprehensive self assessments tests, and step-by-step instructions to put what you've learned in the context of actual job roles. Also includes a challenging Self Assessment test at the end of the book to make sure you are prepared for the exam day.
