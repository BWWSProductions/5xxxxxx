Harry's game Part 1 of 3 


Harry's Game is a British television miniseries made by Yorkshire Television for ITV in 1982.

The three-part serial starred Ray Lonnen as Capt. Harry Brown, a British soldier, sent undercover to Northern Ireland to find and kill Billy Downes (Derek Thompson), a Provisional Irish Republican Army (IRA) assassin. It was based on the Gerald Seymour novel. The series' theme tune was performed by Clannad.
