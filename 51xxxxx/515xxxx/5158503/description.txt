Description: Ingmar Bergman puts his indelible stamp on Mozart's
opera in this rendering of one of the composer's best-loved works.
In an unusual structure, the film moves between a stage production
of the opera and the backstage intrigues of the actors. This other-
worldly fantasy, sung in Swedish, involves a kidnapped princess
(Irma Urrila), a mysterious and powerful Queen of the Night
(Birgit Nordin), and a carefree, flute-playing wanderer.
