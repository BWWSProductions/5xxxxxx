Main card

    * Heavyweight bout: Russia Fedor Emelianenko vs. United States Brett Rogers

    Emelianenko defeated Rogers via KO (Punch) at 1:48 of round 2.

    * Middleweight Championship bout: United States Jake Shields vs. United States Jason Miller

    Shields defeated Miller via unanimous decision to become the new Strikeforce Middleweight Champion.

    * Light Heavyweight bout: Armenia Gegard Mousasi vs. Cameroon Rameau Thierry Sokoudjou

    Mousasi defeated Sokoudjou via TKO (punches) at 3:43 of round 2.

    * Heavyweight bout: Brazil Fabricio Werdum vs. Brazil Antonio Silva

    Werdum defeated Silva via unanimous decision (29-28, 29-28, 29-28).
