Street Warriors are an Australian hip hop group. The group is made up of brothers Abie and Warwick Wright from Local Knowledge.They perform in both English and the Awabakal language

Street Warriors released their debut EP, Real Knows Real, in 2007. Which was followed, in 2009, by their debut album, Unstoppable Force and a single, "Solid Rock" with Shannon Noll (a re-working of the ARIA Award winning song by Goanna).

Street Warriors were nominated for a Deadly in 2008 and again in 2009.
