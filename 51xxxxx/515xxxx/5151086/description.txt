Travel to the beautiful city of Istanbul, Turkey, and help Alexia stop an ancient family curse 

that has started to affect her friend, Ogan. A mysterious symbol has appeared on his hand, and he 

needs your help to prevent it from spreading! Explore timeless temples to find clues and a cure 

for the Curse of Babylon! Journey through gorgeous Hidden Object scenes, and play magical 

minigames, as you piece together the past and save Ogan!
