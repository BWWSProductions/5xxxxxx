The Revolution is the second studio album from Christian rock band Inhabited.
It was released on July 12, 2005 on Fervent Records. Four songs were released as radio singles: "Open My Eyes", "One More Night", "Revolution" and "Rescue Me", which charted in the top 10 on Christian rock radio and top 20 on Christian music charts.
