Closed Beta of Zodiac Online on Nov. 1, 2009

Zodiac Online is a colorful, free to play MMO based on the Chinese Zodiac. Its 12 characters are set in a fantasy land borne of an amazing legend of allegiance, valiance, and guardianship. In the game, players will act as Zodiac Envoys on a mandate from heaven, which creates an unforeseen and mysterious set of circumstances.

Zodiac Online will enter Closed-Beta testing on Nov 1, and the publisher Ingle Games is sending out their warm invitations to all the gamers. Now you can get a CB key to start your Zodiac journey and get involved in the wondrous world. Many good items will be granted along your journey with the CB code and lots of events have been prepared for you. Don't miss a good chance like this!
