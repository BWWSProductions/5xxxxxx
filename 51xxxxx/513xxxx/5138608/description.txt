This final installment of Battlestar Galactica comes in the form of a Syfy Channel television movie. The special event will follow the stories of the human-form Cylons on the Galactica and on Caprica during the preparation for the massive Cylon attack on the Colonial homeworlds. Edward James Olmos (Admiral Adama) directed. Co-executive producer Jane Espenson wrote the screenplay.
