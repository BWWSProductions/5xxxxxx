Strange Adventures (DC Comics) (2008-2009) (8 Issues)


This series continues the "Aberrant Six" storyline, as well as those advanced in "Rann/Thanagar Holy War" as well as "Countdown to Adventure".  It follows the adventures of Adam Strange and Captain Comet as their past catches up to them in to form of Synnar the Demurge as he plots to control all of existence.  Guest starring Bizarro, Hawkman, The Weird, Eye, Prince Gayvn.

I have collected all 8 issues here.  Got both covers on the first issue.  All issues are the highest quality minutemen scans.  Big props to minutemen in general for awesome quality scans.
