Young and naive Amy has been the biggest fan of
world famous sex therapist Dr. Samantha Byer for years.
After a chance sexual encounter in Dr. Byer's office
Amy gets caught up in a lust filled love triangle with
Dr. Byer's trophy wife, Bettie. Will Amy end up gaining
the love she so desires from Dr. Byer or will she end up
way over her head in a twisted web of technology,
betrayal and extortion? Join Amy on her persuit of love
as she follows her sexuality down the rabbit hole!
