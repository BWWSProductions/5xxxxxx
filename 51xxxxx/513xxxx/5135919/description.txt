Image United Prelude is a companion peice to upcoming crossover event, Image United. This short story concentrates on the new Image character, Fortress, created by Whilce Portacio. It was divided into four parts that were published in Savage Dragon #153, Witchblade #131, Invincible #67 and Spawn #195.
Art by Whilce Portacio, Erik Larsen and Ryan Ottley.
