Based on the acclaimed Australian novel, Closed for Winter
written by Georgia Blain

The emotional and poetic story of a beautiful young woman
haunted by a tragic event in her youth. As Elise attempts to
piece together the mystery of her sister s disappearance at
the beach, twenty years before when both were children, she
must face dark family secrets that have remained unspoken. As
the past is revealed, Elise at last finds the courage to begin
to live

Based on the Georgia Blain s critically acclaimed novel of the
same name, Closed for Winter, features a stunning performance
by international star Natalie Imbruglia in her first leading
role. Closed for Winter is about the devastating consequences
of unresolved grief and finding the courage to face dark
family secrets and learning to live again.
