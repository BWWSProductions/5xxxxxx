PACIFIC GUNNER

Company: Atari, Running Dog 
Category: Rail Shooting 
Release: Dec 24, 2002

Pacific Gunner is a WWII stationary gunner game where you man up to 10 stations to defend waves of Japanese attacks.
You will be able to lead assaults at historical sites, such as Pearl Harbor and Okinawa as you sink incoming aircraft with high-powered howitzers. Switch quickly between strategically placed gun turrets to blow away Japanese aircraft including Kates, Vals, and Bettys.
