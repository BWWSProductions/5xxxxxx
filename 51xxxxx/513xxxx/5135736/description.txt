Colbie Marie Caillat (born May 28, 1985 in Newbury Park, California) is an American pop singer-songwriter and guitarist.

"Breakthrough" is the second studio album by Colbie Caillat. The album was released on August 25th 2009 in the United States and debuted at the top of the Billboard 200, selling 106,000 copies in its first week and giving Caillat her first No.1 album. She also topped the Billboard Top Digital Albums chart and, surprisingly, the Top Rock Albums chart. 'Fallin' For You' was released as the album's lead single on June 30th 2009.
