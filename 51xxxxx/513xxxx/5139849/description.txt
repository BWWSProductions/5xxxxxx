After your ship washes up on the shore of a seemingly deserted island, 
you discover that the island is not only inhabited, 
but also home to a powerful volcano! 
Your presence has awoken and angered the volcano and put the entire island in jeopardy! 
Fortunately, the islanders are masters at SKIP-BO and want to teach you their favorite game. 
Only you can play SKIP-BO with the friendly inhabitants of the island, calm the active volcano, 
and restore the quiet peace of this tropical paradise!


System Requirements
-------------------

* Windows XP/Vista
* 800MHz or faster Processor
* 256MB RAM

Language: English
Genre: Card-game
