Hosts : Tyler Harcott & Karyn Bryant.

Challenge : build a golf-playing vehicle. 

As the teams aren't allowed to touch the over-sized golf balls everything must be done mechanically. 

Once built they must play a 3-hole par-11 round of golf on the Junkyard Wars off-road golf course. 

The team to score the least amount of strokes wins the Junkyard Wars trophy.

Contestants - 

Green Team : The Jet Doctors.
Team captain Kirk, Tama & Jeff.
Expert : Kimric smith.

Blue Team : The High Flyers.
Team captain Ed, Bob & Rolf.
Expert : Sean Dines.


NOTE : This is the final episode of the US series. There are no more.
