Care of Wounds is a successful text which addresses all aspects of wound management. The third edition continues to reflect current research and evidence based practice, incorporating the many developments in practice since the publication of the second edition. New topics include managing wounds in the community; the development of nurse-led ulcer clinics and specialist wound management centres, and the use of pressure redistributing equipment.
