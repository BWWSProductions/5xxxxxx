The Dracula Files 

Michael and Quinn Harker grew up thinking Dracula was nothing more than a fairy tale. But now, 
after over 100 years, the undead count has risen from his grave as Vladimir Draco, thirsting for 
blood and revenge.

Only the descendants of the Van Helsing and Harker families can stop him & save their friend 
before he is lost to the darkness forever. Search for cursed artifacts and holy relics, facing 
werewolves, vampires, and dark spirits in a globe-spanning Gothic hidden object adventure.
