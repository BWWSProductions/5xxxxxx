                          Vito Sound Explorer Version 1.83



SoundExplorer is an advanced player and audio recorder that offers the most comprehensive and modern set of tools for professional and hobby use. SoundExplorer turns your device into a feature rich digital dictaphone and audio player providing comfortable use and reliable mechanisms for interviewing, lecture recording, language learning and listening to music.


                                                  

                                      Features:


* Records and plays: MP3, WAV (including GSM and ADPCM)
* Bookmarks
* Equalizer
* 4 different playback modes
* One-button recording
* VAS
* Scheduling
* Integrated file manager
* WAV > MP3 converting

                                                           
                                       Benefits:


* records and plays the most popular formats
* full editing of audio notes
* reliable recording in any situation
* Easy playlist creation
