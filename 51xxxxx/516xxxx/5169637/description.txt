Kairo  is a 2001 Japanese horror film directed by Kiyoshi Kurosawa. The film was based on his novel of the same name, and was released in the US in 2005 as Pulse. The movie is the subject of a 2006 English language remake Pulse. The movie has a cult following.

The film was screened in the Un Certain Regard section at the 2001 Cannes Film Festival.
