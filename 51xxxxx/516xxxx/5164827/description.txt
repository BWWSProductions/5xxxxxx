The Fugitive
S03E01
Wings of an Angel

The Fugitive Audio Restoration Team (F.A.R.T.) is back with the third season of The Fugitive in excellent quality video with the original soundtrack restored! As you probably know, the DVD's of season three of The Fugitive were released with a some of the original music replaced by a new, synthesized music score which destroyed much of the show's drama. Now, thanks to the Fugitive Audio Restoration Team, you can enjoy the show in top quality as it was originally intended to be experienced.

Kimble, wounded while rescuing a young girl, is taken to a prison hospital---where he's recognized by a blackmailing trustee.
Demarest: Greg Morris. Willis: Harold Gould. Janet: Lane Bradbury. Maddox: Lin McCarthy. Kulik: Val Avery.
