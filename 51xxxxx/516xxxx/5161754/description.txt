A human machine forged from the bodies, powers, and weapons of six individuals from the DCU: Professor Emil Hamilton, Automan, Rosie the Riveter, Ford, Brainstorm and Doctor Cyber.  Built by its creator as a tool to further a noble plan, the six desperate heroes sacrifice their lives to fuse into one being with the potential to change the entire DC Universe.  

Written by Joe Kelly (Action Comics) with art by the critically acclaimed Ted McKeever (Eddie Current, Metropol).
