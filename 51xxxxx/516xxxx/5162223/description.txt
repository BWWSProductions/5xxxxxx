Nederlands:
De studente Wendy Christensen voorspelt een achtbaancrash.

"De Dood" is echter onverbiddelijk en komt de levens opeisen van de mensen die eigenlijk

When Wendy Christensen has a vision of an accident on the roller coaster, 
resulting in her and her friends' deaths, she instantly begins to panic, 
causing more of her friends to be left of the ride. The remaining friends, 
including Wendy's boyfriend, are stuck on the roller coaster and find themselves involved in the accident. 
With death waiting around the corner, Wendy and Kevin Fischer must try and work out death's plan, 
before they and the remaining survivors end up dead.
