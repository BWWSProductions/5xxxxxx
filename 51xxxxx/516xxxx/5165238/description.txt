This torrent contains 123 000 files ? logos season 09/10, kits, faces and backgrounds.
How to install:

1. Unpack the archive in My Documents-Sports Interactive-Football Manager 2010

2. Start the game, Go to Preferences -> Display & Sound
- uncheck Use Skin Cache
- check Always Reload Skin On Confirm
- check Show Badges/Logos
- check Show Backgrounds Pictures (we have to wait for the next SI Update to show the backgrounds)

3. Confirm, wait for the skin to reload and play!

Seed and enjoy!!!
