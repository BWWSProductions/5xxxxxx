Our four heroes and the Grand Master of the Knights Templar travel to Malta to return the lost treasure 

of the Snake Crown to its rightful owners, the Maltese Knights. Mathias is attracted to Elena, a 

beautiful and mysterious Maltese girl, and before they can bat an eye he has said too much, the crown 

is stolen and Mathias is kidnapped by an ancient order of priests. Our youthful protagonists now have 

to use all their guile and cunning to get the crown back and save their friend.
