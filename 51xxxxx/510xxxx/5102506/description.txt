Navigon 1.2 EUROPE no maps - Just the application

Download your chosen maps from Navigon 1.2 EUROPE only maps and insert them in Navigon 1.2 EUROPE-no maps.

Mac OSX procedure:

rename navigon.ipa to navigon.zip
expand navigon.zip
open /payload/navigon.pkg
drop your chosen maps
compress
rename to navigon.ipa
