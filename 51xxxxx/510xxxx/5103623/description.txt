D'Artagnan et les Trois Mousquetaires (D'Artagnan and the Three Musketeers) is a new telling of the classic novel by Alexandre Dumas.
Intrigue and revenge take on a new, diabolical edge in this cloak-and-dagger adventure based on Dumas' unrivalled tale of the Three Musketeers and their reckless, romantic friend, D'Artagnan. Exile and possibly death are in the cards for the Queen of France if Cardinal Richlieu has his way. Aiding him in his sinister campaign is Milady de Winter, a beautiful and mysterious young woman who possesses supernatural powers. But are her powers strong enough against D'Artagnan and his friends? Swashbuckling duels, wild gallops, fiery showdowns and, for D'Artagnan, passionate lovemaking dot the pulsating adventures of the musketeers as they fight for justice, love and the queen's honour.
