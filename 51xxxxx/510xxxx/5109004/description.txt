Kensuke's parents have become overloaded with debt, and to ease the debt, have indentured him to his super-rich former childhood friend, Karen.
However, she's become a selfish princess who tortures him mentally and sexually, making him her pet.
Included in the mix is Achiki, Karen's futanari maid, who just loves Kensuke's semen! 	
Koku, a bullied girl has a crush on Kensuke after the group saves her.	
And Tsukishiro, the student council president wants to steal Kensuke away from Karen!
