***Release notes...

Private CD transferred to digital with a stunning quality by Martin Bartosik                         
from the original US "Airborn" double vinyl VA 13143 edited by Virgin on February 1980.              
Project finished on September, 1999 with revision on June, 2000. 
Various "cleaning" processes were used, including the subsonic filtration and denoising. 
To preserve all those tiny sounds of bell, vibrapone or glockenspiele there was 
NO automatic decliking or descratching. 
The CD contains the full "Platinum" album but replacing "Woodhenge" with "Guilty" 
as bonus track which is a special version only avaliable on this US edition whose only 
difference is that runs faster. All four parts of Platinum suite appears as only one track. 
The live versions of "Tubular Bells" and "Incantations" are not the same recordings as on "Exposed".
