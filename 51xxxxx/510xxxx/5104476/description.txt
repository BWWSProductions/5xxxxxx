Three teenage boys share the common bond of witnessing a brutal murder, and the aftermath of the event creates a loyal friendship that's inextricably linked to violence. Shortly after their disturbing experience, one of the boys is savagely attacked by a group of thugs and his date is raped, leading the three to plot bloody revenge in this intense and unnerving shocker, the first part of a Japanese horror trilogy directed by Katsuya Matsumura.
