Aboard the starship Seeker 3000 lies the last vestiges of mankind.  Sent off into space in search of a new home after the Sun went supernova and destroyed the Earth, the Seeker contains the genetic legacy of our world.  But the sinister group known as 'The Six' have their own agenda for Seeker 3000.

Written by Doug Moench and illustrated by Tom Sutton.
