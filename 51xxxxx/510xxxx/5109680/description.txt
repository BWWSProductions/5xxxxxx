M.I.K - I Declare War (War Reports)

M.I.K (Music Is Knowledge) is an established artist from South East London, having collaborated with well-known artists and producers such as Deverlish, Ratchet, Swizze Star, Crayzee Banditt, Beat Creatures, J Beatz, Jeeday Jawz, Doller Da Dustman, & Jendor to name just a few. M.I.K has also had many radio appearances along side some of the scenes top Djs which include ScoreFive, Scope, Spooky, Limit & not to mention appearing on Logan Sama's Kiss FM & Tim Westwood's shows on 1Xtra.
