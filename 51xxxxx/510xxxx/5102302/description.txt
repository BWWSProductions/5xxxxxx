The Uncommon Reader - Alan Bennett  (2007)




Alan Bennett reads from his book about a monarch for whom literature is an all consuming interest.



1/5. One morning, the Queen discovers a travelling library parked next to the Buckingham Palace kitchens.

2/5. Having borrowed books from the travelling library, the Queen encounters some memorable plots
and characters.

3/5. Not content with reading the books, the Queen now wishes to meet the writers.

4/5. The Queen's devotion to books causes problems for everyone from her courtiers to her Prime Minister.

5/5. Much to the consternation of her Prime Minister, the Queen makes a life changing decision.
