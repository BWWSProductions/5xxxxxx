"This Is Who I Am" will be the first single from Vanessa Amorosi's forthcoming album. The song, "This Is Who I Am", made its radio debut on the Kyle & Jackie O show on 28 August 2009 on the 2Day FM radio station in Sydney. The single will be released in Australia on 9 October 2009.

The video clip for "This Is Who I Am" was shot in Los Angeles and directed by Christopher R. Watson. The video was shot on green screen and was launched on 14 September 2009. 
The video premiered on the 'Take 40' and 'The Hot Hits' web pages a few days before its official release.
