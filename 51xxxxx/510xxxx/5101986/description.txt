A documentary (in Italian) about problems of making live music in Italy, made by the singer-composer Ilaria Scattina (www.ilamusic.it) together with many musicians and people in the show business. Converted from original WMV format (downloadable from www.ilahand.com/dvd/scarica.htm) to compressed AVI. A document not to be missed for anybody who breathes music, from listeners to professionals.
