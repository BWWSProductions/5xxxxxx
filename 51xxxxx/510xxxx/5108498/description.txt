Aria is a mystical fantasy comic book published by Image Comics, written by Brian Holguin and drawn by Jay Anacleto.  There have been several Aria miniseries written over the course of 1999-2003.

The series follows the adventures of Lady Kildare, usually just called Kildare, of the house of Dannan who is a princess of Faerie living on our side of the metaphysical fence.  She has lived in the human world for several centuries and is currently living in New York City where she owns the bookstore/collectibles shop "Otherworld".  In the orginal series Kildare investigates a group of shadowy creatures that are killing off Faerie folk.
