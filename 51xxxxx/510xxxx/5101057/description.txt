"Battle Cry" is the third and final single released by Bajan singer Shontelle from her album Shontelligence. It was the third single taken from the album following "T-Shirt" and "Stuck with Each Other". The song was released as the third single in the US on June 9, 2009 and will be released in the UK as a digital single bundle on 10 August 2009.

In October 2008 Shontelle released a "Battle Cry" tribute video for Barack Obama onas it was featured on the compilation album Yes We Can: Voices of Grass Roots Movement.

In May 2009, she shot the official music video for the single release in Los Angeles, California with video director Jessy Terrero. On June 26, Shontelle's official music video for Battle Cry premiered on her official fansite.
