Insider Tales 2 the Secret of Casanova game is a breathtaking mix of hidden object and puzzle 

games that takes you across the most beautiful European cities. Walk through gothic streets of 

Prague, speak to charming Monsieur in Paris and more. Search for prompting messages, collect the 

clues, prepare a magic potion, solve tricky puzzles in Insider Tales 2 game! Featuring authentic 

locations, challenging puzzles, lots of cleverly hidden objects and cute story Insider Tales 2 

the Secret of Casanova is a great game for the whole family.
