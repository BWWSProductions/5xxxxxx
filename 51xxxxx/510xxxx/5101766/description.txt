"Welcome to the World" is the second single from Kevin Rudolf's debut album In the City. The album version and radio single version features rapper Rick Ross whereas, the music video features Kevin Rudolf solo, who sings a third verse instead. There is also an alternative version that features Kid Cudi, which was chosen to be the second single in the UK. The melody of the chorus is taken from the 1994 Oasis song "Supersonic".

The music video was release to MTV on May 1, 2009, and Kevin Rudolf sings a 3rd verse in the song instead of the Rick Ross verse. The video shows Rudolf singing in the streets with his band. Lil Wayne and Birdman also make a brief appearance in the video.
