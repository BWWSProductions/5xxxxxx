Lt. Cho is a cop on the edge who's put in charge of a seemingly impenetrable mystery. Dismembered corpses are being found in garbage bags around Seoul, and an investigation reveals that the body parts once belonged to men romantically involved with the same woman, Suyeon Chae. Cho soon realizes that the secret to the horrifying murders lies in the carefully guarded memories of Suyeon's past.
