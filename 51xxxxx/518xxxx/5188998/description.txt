This is a collection of all the Fallout 3 DLC in one folder.  Simply drop them into your Data folder and enable them like any other mod in fallout.  Doesn't require Window's Live, an installer, or anything fancy.  Just a little bit of know-how.

This Torrent Includes:
Operation Anchorage
The Pitt
Broken Steel
Point Lookout
Mothership Zeta

Installation Instructions

1. Copy all files to your Bethesda SoftworksFallout 3Data folder.
2. Enable the .esm files with Fallout or something like Fallout Mod Manager.
3. Enjoy!
