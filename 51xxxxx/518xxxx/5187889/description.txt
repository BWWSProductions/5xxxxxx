This is a live recording from 1970 by Chuck Mangione featuring the Rochester Philharmonic Orchestra, the LP was released on the Mercury label, personnel is Chuck Mangione on flugelhorn, Don Potter on guitar & voice, Bat McGrath on guitarron & voice, Gap Mangione on electric piano, Stanley Watson on guitar, Marvin Stamm on trumpet and Gerry Niewood on sax, plus the Rochester Philharmonic Orchestra, an additional horn section and other players (among them Steve Gadd and Tony Levin).
