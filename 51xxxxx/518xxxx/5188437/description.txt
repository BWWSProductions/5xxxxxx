CD Bibliologia 1.0 (2003) Christian resources library with BibleQuote 5 - Bibliologia Edition software program 
http://jesuschrist.ru/news/2003/4/14/3630

"Here you will find a lot of useful Christian Biblical based information on various subjects. The information is presented in different forms including books and articles in PDF and HTML formats, Biblical and theological courses, Christian web-sites and Christian resources. Our hope is that the materials of this library will help you in your ministry and your Christian life. We tried to make these resources affordable, understandable and easy to use for a broad audience of users. We express our deepest gratitude to all the Christians publishers and missions who responded to our request for materials and provided us with their published works free of charge for placement on the Bibliologia 1.0 CD."

The BibleQuote 5 - Bibliologia Edition program offers a convenient system of navigation and search in all the integrated materials. The program also allows comparison of different Bible texts, commentaries, cross-references, dictionary entries, Strong's definitions for Greek and Hebrew words, etc. One of the important features of the program is that experienced users can easily integrate in it their own texts. More information on this is in the User's Guide found in the pulldown Help window of the program.

BibleQuote 5 - Bibliologia Edition contains modules:

- King James Version with Strong's numbers
- Russian Synodal Translation  with Strong's numbers
- Biblia Hebraica Stuttgartensia Michigan-Claremont text
- Septuagint BIBLIA GRAECA IVXTA LXX INTERPRETES
- Greek New Testament with accents and morphological analysis (Novum Testamentum Graece, Nestle-Aland 26th edition)
- Modern Russian and Ukraininan translations
and many others.
