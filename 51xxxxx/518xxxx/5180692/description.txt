Collection of comics based on animated television series and toyline from the mid 80's.

M.A.S.K. (Mobile Armored Strike Kommand), was a hybrid of popular era cartoons G.I. Joe and The Transformers.  It featured a special task force featuring an array of characters, led by Matt Trakker, with transforming vehicles engaged in an ongoing battle against the criminal organization V.E.N.O.M. (Vicious Evil Network of Mayhem).
