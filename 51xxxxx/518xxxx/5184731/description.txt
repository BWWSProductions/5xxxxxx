The peers of the new electronic movement have teamed up for the hottest
BERLIN/LONDON connection on the dancefloor: EROL ALKAN & BOYS NOIZE present
their first tracks on AUGUST 31st 2009, the forthcoming twelve inch and digital
release "WAVES / DEATH SUITE".

"Waves" is a top of the night Balearic/disco storm; "Death Suite" is a more
abstract, cut "n" paste techno/acid rollercoaster. Both tracks were initially
envisaged purely as battle weapons for Erol and Boys Noizes`respective DJ sets,
but the response has been such that a full release became inevitable.
