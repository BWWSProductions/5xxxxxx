Annotation:

"The sun goes down behind the line of forests after a day of work and concern. Only the red clouds of heaven slide over horizon. The peace comes into nature... However night is full of a wide range of voices and sounds in the peaty wetland of Novaraistis ornithological conservation area (Lithuania). These nature sounds weave into one wonderful music of the wildlife with melodies identified only by attentive listener. Hoot of the bittern, bleat of the snipe, grunt of Water Rail and croak of frogs mixes with Lapwing 'geevee geevee', gaggle of gray geese and wings rustle of ducks. Hearing and realize, that all this could only be created by a greatest of composers - Nature itself".
