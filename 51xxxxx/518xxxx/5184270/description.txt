                                   "Beat It"   

                                  Fall Out Boy

                       Background, recording and release


   In early 2008 it was announced that pop punk band Fall Out Boy were to cover "Beat It" for their **** Live in Phoenix album.It was also announced that John Mayer was to add the guitar solo previously played by Eddie Van Halen.

   The music video featured numerous cameos, including a karate class/dance session being taught by Tony Hale, and Donald Faison dressed up like Michael Jackson. The short film later received a MTV Video Music Award nomination for Best Rock Video.
