  Release Description:
  ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

   Presto Transfer Ultimate is an application designed to help automate the
   transferral of your data from various common programs to another. Once
   installed Presto Transfer Ultimate's easy to use wizard interface
   creates a self-extracting transfer archive of your data which can be
   restored by simply running it on the destination computer. The transfer
   archive can be automatically burned to CD/DVD, written to a USB MP3
   player, a removable drive, or simply shared across a network.
