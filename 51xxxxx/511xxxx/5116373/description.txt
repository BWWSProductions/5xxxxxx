Onimusha 2: Samurai's Destiny

Plataform:  PS2 NTSC US

Language:   English

Format:     ISO

Onimusha 2: Samurai's Destiny, released in Japan as Onimusha 2 is the second     
installment in the Onimusha series of video games. The plot revolves around a    
new protagonist, Jubei Yagyu, as he battles a demonic Genma army which is        
invading 1571 Japan. Nobunaga Oda, a sinister warlord who was slain in the       
previous game, has been resurrected and placed at the head of the Genma forces.  
Apart from the main character, the game features four playable sub-characters,   
each of whom shares a part in the story. The player's actions determine which    
characters will decide to help Jubei in his quest.
