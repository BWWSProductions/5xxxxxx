Rumpole of the Bailey by John Mortimer.



The Splendours and Miseries of an Old Bailey Hack.

All thirteen half-hour episodes of John Mortimer's hilarious comic take on the British legal system.

Starring Maurice Denham as Horace Rumpole and Margot Boyd as Hilda Rumpole.
