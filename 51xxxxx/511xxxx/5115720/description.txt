A talk by E. Michael Jones, editor of Culture Wars magazine (http://www.culturewars.com/) and author of The Jewish Revolutionary Spirit: And Its Impact on World History (http://www.amazon.com/Jewish-Revolutionary-Spirit-Impact-History/dp/0929891074/), about why Catholicism has a problem with Judaism, and why Nostra Aetate (the Vatican II pronouncement relating to relations with the Jews) doesn't change that. 1 hr. 50 min.
