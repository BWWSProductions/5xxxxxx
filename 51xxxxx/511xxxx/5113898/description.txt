TV adaptation of Bruce Jay Friedman's off-Broadway play. Tandy, Merideth and assorted others unexpectedly wake up in a Steambath with no easy exit. After spending some time there, it becomes clear that the steambath is not quite what it seems...and the indifferent souls telling their stories to the attendant picking up the towels come to an astonishing Realization....
