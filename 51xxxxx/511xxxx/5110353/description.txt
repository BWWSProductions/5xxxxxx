Supernatural: Origins is written by series producer Peter Johnson and drawn by Matthew Dow Smith.  The first issue features a backup story by famed DC writer Geoff Johns, with art by Phil Hester. 

Supernatural: Rising Son is written by Peter Johnson and Rebecca Dessertine, with art by Diego Olmos.
