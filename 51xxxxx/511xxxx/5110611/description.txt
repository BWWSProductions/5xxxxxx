Freifunk Halle Interview Loop

Freifunk.net is a non commercial open initiative to support free radio networks in the German region. Freifunk.net is part of the international movement for free and wireless radio networks. (from Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia)

Activists from Halle (Saale) and Leipzig tell their view on Freifunk and give reasons why they are involved. They explain the ideas behind the movement and give an overview about the development of Freifunk in Halle.

Sourcepost: http://blog.freifunk.net/2009/freifunk-halle-interview-loop
