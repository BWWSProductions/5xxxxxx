Artist:  Sergio Mendes & Brasil 66

Title:  Look Around

Date:  1968

Label:  A & M Records   SP 4137

Art Director:  Tom Wilkes

Produced by Herb Alpert and Jerry Moss, Sergio et. al. play through the songs of the Beatles, Bacharach/David, Gilberto Gil and a few originals.  The sound is the Tijuana Brass with a little more of a South American feel to the beat.
