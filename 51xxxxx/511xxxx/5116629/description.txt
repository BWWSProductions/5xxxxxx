Description of FIFA 99

Edit by Brothersoft: Graphically, it is a major improvement over FIFA '98, with the inclusion of basic facial animations and different players' heights as well as certain other cosmetic features such as improved kits and emblems, although they are unlicensed. Users may also create their own custom cups and leagues and select the teams they wish to participate.
