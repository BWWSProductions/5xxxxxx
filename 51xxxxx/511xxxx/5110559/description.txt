Kymatica is the sequel to Esoteric Agenda by Ben and Daniel Stewart from Talismanic Idols. Kymatica ventures into the realm of Cymatics and Shamanistic practices. It offers insight into the human psyche and discusses matters of spirituality, altered states of consciousness and much more! Featuring Interviews with Bruce Lipton and Henrik Palmgren.
