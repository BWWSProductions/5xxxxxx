Novembre is a progressive death metal/doom metal band from Rome, Italy.

  Started in 1990,(then called Catacomb),by the brothers Carmelo(lead vocals & guitar) and Giuseppe(drums) Orlando, Novembre grew to develop their unique atmospheric sound and textures combining death metal hardness with surprisingly poetical melodies and great rhythmic variety into rich,complex song structures.

  Albums:

    * Wish I Could Dream it Again (1994)
    * Arte Novecento (1996)
    * Classica (1999)
    * Novembrine Waltz (2001)
    * Dreams d'Azur (2002)
    * Materia (2006)
    * Memoria Stoica (2006)
    * The Blue (2007)
