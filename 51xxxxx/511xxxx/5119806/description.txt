Features the world of insects and arachnids that is hidden in the undergrowth. Also features a world populated by the undergrowth invertebrates that have migrated into houses and inhabit the dark corners and spaces in the living room, larder or kitchen. 

Includes everyday bugs, beetles, centipedes, mites, spiders, dragonflies and moths, as well as scorpions, mantids and locusts.
