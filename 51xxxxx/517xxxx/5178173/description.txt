This is my complete Skream Discography

Skream is a Dubstep prducer from Croydon, London, and is hailed as being one of the pioneers of the dubstep sound. 
He works closely with fellow producer Benga and has many releases on the Tempa Record lablel, including his popular "Skreamizm" EP series.


This discography contains all of his vinyl releases, remixes & digital releases.

All tracks come complete with artwork, front and back if possible for vinyl/cd's, and all contain full release format data and catalougue number.

All major Skream releases - Skreamizm 1-5, self titled albums & CD released mixes are all 320kbps, most other releases such as remixes 
on vinyl and other 12" singles are of equally high quality with only a few not in 320. Myspace rips and set rips are of a lower quality.
