                            
                             One Love (David Guetta album)

                             Studio album by David Guetta

        
One Love is the title of the fourth studio album by French DJ, David Guetta. It was released onto the official iTunes Store on 21 August 2009 and was released on CD format on 24 August 2009 throughout Europe and 25 August 2009 in the United States. A double disc deluxe edition will also be released, the second disc contains extended/club mixes of album tracks plus 2 new tracks.


   "When Love Takes Over" was the first single released and featured Kelly Rowland. The song was a worldwide hit and peaked at #1 in the UK.

   "Sexy Bitch" was the second single from the album and featured Akon. The song peaked at #1 in the UK, Australia and New Zealand.

                 "One Love"(03m28s)is the third single from the album.

                               Morning After Dark

              Single by Timbaland featuring Nelly Furtado & SoShy

    "Morning After Dark" is a song by American hip-hop record producer and rapper, Timbaland taken from his upcoming third studio album, Timbaland Presents Shock Value II. The song features SoShy[3] and was released as the first single from the album on October 26, 2009. The album version of the song also features an additional rap verse from Canadian singer Nelly Furtado, and the video features just SoShy.

    The song has so far debuted at 83 on the Billboard Hot 100 Airplay in the U.S on the week of November 9. It debuted at number 52 in Canada. It debuted at number 16 on the Swedish Singles Top 60 on the week of November 5. It also debuted at number 62 on the ARIA Charts in Australia on the week of November 9.
