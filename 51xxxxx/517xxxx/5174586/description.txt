Featuring the voices of John Travolta and Miley 
Cyrus, this animated adventure (nominated for a 
Best Animated Feature Oscar and Golden Globe) 
centers on a dashing German shepherd named Bolt, 
a canine actor forced into making an action-filled 
cross-country trek when he's accidentally shipped 
from Hollywood to New York. Believing that he and 
his fictional character are one and the same, Bolt 
gets a glimpse of what it takes to be a hero in 
the real world. 

Starring: John Travolta, Miley Cyrus

Director: Byron Howard
