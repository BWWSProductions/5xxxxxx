"Ca se dispute" is a French talk show hosted by Laurent Bazin with 2 permanent guests: Nicolas Domenach, left wing journalist from Marianne and Eric Zemmour, right wing journalist and author from Le Figaro.

This week instance is covering the following:
* political buzz about Ire/France football match
* Sarkozy and the local administration reform
* Segolene Royal last gaffe
* Belgian president for EU

Note: not sure this show can be interesting for anyone outside France, except maybe if a) you are engaged in the long and painful process of learning French language b) you love bashing the French
