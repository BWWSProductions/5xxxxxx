LEARN TO DJ WITH ABLETON LIVE

Ableton Certified Trainer Craig McCullough takes you through the fundamentals of DJ'ing with Ableton Live. Completely shot in Live 8, this title demystifies many of the commonly misunderstood and overlooked skills and techniques needed to create a solid foundation for a DJ set.

Learn tempo matching, filtering, warping, mapping controllers and much more. It's time to get the club moving...
