Demis Roussos, born June 15, 1946. Roussos began a solo career with the song "We Shall Dance". Initially unsuccessful, he toured southern Europe and soon became a leading vocalist. His solo career peaked in the 1970s with several hit albums. His single, "Forever and Ever", topped the charts in several countries in 1973 (1976 in U.K.). Other hits were "My Friend the Wind",
"My Reason", "Goodbye, My Love, Goodbye", "Someday" and "Lovely Lady of Arcadia". 
This compilation was released 1992 but the songs take you back to the 70's
