Glee - (Glee Cast Version OST) Ep12.Mattress [MusicRoutes.Blogspot] [caprio4us]

Recurring cast members who appear in this episode are Heather Morris, Naya Rivera, Harry Shum, Jr. and Dijon Talton as glee club members Brittany, Santana Lopez, Mike Chang and Matt Rutherford, Iqbal Theba as Principal Figgins, Patrick Gallagher as football coach Ken Tanaka, Bill A. Jones and Earlene Davis as local news anchors Rod Remington and Andrea Carmichael, Max Adler as hockey player Dave Karofsky and James Earl, III as football player Azimo. The episode features covers of "Jump" by Van Halen, "Smile" by Charlie Chaplin and "Smile" by Lily Allen. Studio recordings of the songs were released as singles, available for digital download and also feature on the album Glee: The Music, Volume 2. Jenna Ushkowitz named "Jump" one of her favorite songs on the album, explaining that it was fun to perform on mattresses and comparing it to "Proud Mary", performed in the episode "Wheels", using wheelchairs as props
