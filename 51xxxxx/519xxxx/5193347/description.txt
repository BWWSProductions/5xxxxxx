Graffiti is the forthcoming third studio album from American R&B/pop singer Chris Brown. It is currently set for release on December 8, 2009.
Released as a digital download on September 29, 2009, the album's lead single "I Can Transform Ya", features Lil Wayne and Swizz Beatz who also produced it. So far it has reached #22 and #11 on the U.S. Billboard Hot 100 and U.S. Billboard Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs charts respectively.. The Music Video aired on MTV at 6am on 27th October 2009
