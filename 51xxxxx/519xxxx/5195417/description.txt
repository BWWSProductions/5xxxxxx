http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/William_byrd

The Byrd Edition is a 13 CD set of the complete Latin church music of William Byrd (including his unpublished works), as sung by The Cardinall's Musick, on the ASV/Gaudeamus and Hyperion labels.  

Cardinall's Musick is a superb choir, with a marvellous tone and separation of voices.  Personally, I rate them more highly than the better-known Tallis Scholars, and far above The Sixteen (who, to my tastes at least, have a dull tone with no edge whatsoever).  Deservedly, these recordings have picked up numerous awards.

This torrent contains CDs 01 to 11 (12 has only just been released, and 13 is due next year).  I will provide the two missing CDs as a separate torrent when I come to possess them.
