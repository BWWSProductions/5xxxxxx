Documentary that proposes a bold new interpretation of the factors that have shaped history by looking at the relationship between empires and technology. Starting with the bronze weapons that created the first Semitic empire, and ending with the LHC collider at CERN, Rage Against the Machine gives a detailed account of why human beings have been subject to the violence of men and their devastating weapons for the past 5000 years. This is a must see documentary if you care about the fate of humanity.
