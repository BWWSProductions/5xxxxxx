In the far distant future humankind has been wiped out by the warlike Jekkarans.  Another alien race, the Makers, find the fabled home world of mankind, Earth.  There they hope to unlock the secrets of man's heritage and make them live again, but the Jekkarans have heard of the plan and will stop at nothing to prevent it.

Written by Stuart Moore, with art by Martin Montiel Luna.
