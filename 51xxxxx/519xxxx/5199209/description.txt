"Christmas in Hollis" is a single by Run-D.M.C. from the Christmas compilation album, A Very Special Christmas. It was originally released in 1987 by A&M Records and was produced by the group along with Rick Rubin. The song has become very popular during the holiday season and reached number 78 on the Billboard Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs chart in 2000, 13 years after it was released.

The song samples Clarence Carter's "Back Door Santa".
