
    Game Notes:
    ~~~~~~~~~~~
    Experience the evolution of lego racing and enter the futuristic Drome
    for multi-terrain racing at break-neck speeds. 

    You are Max Axel, pacing your pit area, tensions high as you await the
    first stage of the Drome Championship. You've trained for the intensity
    of Multi-Challenge Racing -- each race a seamless set of stages, mixing
    tracks from realistically stunning City, Mountain, and Canyon
    environments. Now you must prove you're up to that grueling challenge.
    With high-tech racing machines based on new cars from the 2002 LEGO
    Racers construction toy range, Drome Racers is a racing experience
    like no other.

    Game Features:
    ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
    - Fast-paced, anything-goes racing in sleek, high-tech LEGO racing
      machines. 

    - Multi-Challenge Racing takes you through a series of stages on 
      all-terrain tracks set in incredibly detailed environments, including
      City, Mountain, and Canyon. 

    - Arcade mode for instant play options and Career mode for the full 
      story-based, Multi-Challenge Racing league. 

    - Place well to earn credits for upgrading your engine, tires, turbo, 
      aerodynamics, and more. 

    - Compete in three of the top forms of motor racing: high-speed road 
      racing, intense dragster battles, or tough-nut off-road courses. 

    - Multiple pick-ups including a guided missile which can hone in on the
      leaders or a sneaky mine to catch your pursuers off guard. 

    - Take on your friends in the head-to-head, split-screen, two-player mode. 

    - Eighteen playable cars based on the 2002 LEGO Racers construction toy
      range.
