Sitcom pilot following master illusionist and stage hypnotist Dermot Flint (played by Flight Of The Conchords star Rhys Darby) as he attempts to get his career back on track after an unfortunate incident "involving two brothers and some french kissing under hypnosis".

In the space of one week Dermot's wife leaves him, his TV show is cancelled, and he moves into rehab. However his attempts to get his life back on track are ruined by his ego, a body double and a deaf nurse.
