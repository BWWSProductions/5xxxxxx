Falco - Der Kommissar [alternate promo].VOB



HQ very rare, uncensored promo music video. "Der Kommissar" is a song first 
recorded by Falco in German in 1981. Originally written by Robert Ponger and 
Falco, the Falco version reached the top of the charts in many countries. 

In his first official music video for "Der Kommissar", Falco flees from the 
police, with several police cars in the background. This is the lesser-known, 
rare second official music video for the Falco single.
(video does not have any logos)
