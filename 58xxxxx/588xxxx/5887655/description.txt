14 Shades of Grey is the fourth studio album by American rock band Staind, released on May 20, 2003.The album continues the developed sound from the bands last album Break the Cycle some songs on the album are lighter in tone and have a less agressive feel to them while songs like Price to Play and Fray and Falling down have the trademarks of the classic Staind sound the song Layne is a tribute to the late lead singer of the band Alice in chains Layne Staley.
