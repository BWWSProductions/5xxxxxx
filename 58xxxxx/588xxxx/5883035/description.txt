
Sale el Sol (English: The Sun Comes Out) is the upcoming third bilingual album and seventh studio album by Colombian Latin-pop singer-songwriter Shakira. The album is set to be released on October 19, 2010.The album cover was released on August 31, 2010.In a promotion strategy, Los 40 Principales in Spain premiered the album on streaming on October 12, one week before the album release.
