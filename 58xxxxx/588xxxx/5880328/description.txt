What Do You Got? is a mid-tempo rock ballad by American hard rock band Bon Jovi. It is one of four songs written for the band\'s Greatest Hits album, set to be released in November, 2010. The song will be the first single from the compilation album. The song was released on the band\'s official website on August 27. It was officially released on September 21, 2010. Physical single will be released in Germany on October 15, 2010.

The music video to What Do You Got was released on Monday, September 20, 2010. Directed by Wayne Isham, it is the first Bon Jovi video to be filmed in 3-D.
