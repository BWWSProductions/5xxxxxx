Vandae Maatharam is a 2010 multilingual action film with Mammootty, Arjun and Sneha in the lead roles. The movie, originally made in Malayalam and Tamil was also dubbed and released in Telugu and Kannada. The film is directed by D. Aravind after a long time. He turned Tamil film industry to technology with well made films like Oomai Vizhigal and Senthoora Poove in the eighties. Even the producer, Henry is known for quality social films like Marumalarchi, Bharathi Kannamma and Pudhumaipithan before. It was slated as a Ramzan release on 10 September 2010 but delayed to 17th september. The movie was released overseas on 10 September itself.

Mammootty and Arjun are tough police officers. They analyse farmers struggle and related social violence to put forward an amicable solution to have lasting harmony. They are highly disturbed by the terrorists' infiltrations. Sneha, an Aviator, is Mammootty's wife and they are sad that they don't have a child. The rest of the story deals with how they counter act against the terrorists and save the land.
