Joomla Knowledgebase is a Joomla Extension for managing your knowledgebase.

Use Joomla Knowledgebase to manage your all FAQ articles, categories, comments and acronyms from right within the Joomla admin console. With full support for attachments and multiple categories JoomlaKB is a great solution for any website.

Joomla Knowledgebase is developed by the team at Blue Flame IT (Jersey) Ltd. who have been active Mambo and Joomla developers for many years.
