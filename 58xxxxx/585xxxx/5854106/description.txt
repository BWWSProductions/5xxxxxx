U N I V E R S   Z E R O
- Last Four Studio Albums -

After the demise of Univers Zero in 1986 a long hiatus followed until Daniel Denis in 1997 decided to resurrect the band, both as a live- and a studio project. Besides an ever changing line-up but in close collaboration with Michel Berckmans he released four studio albums: 

1999 - The Hard Quest
2002 - Rhythmix
2004 - Implosion
2010 - Clivages

(all albums with cover and cue-sheet)
