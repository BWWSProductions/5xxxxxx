Synopsis:

Happy Gilmore (Sandler) is a frustrated wannabe hockey player who discovers he has an untapped talent for golf. When his grandmother's house is repossessed he goes professional to earn the money he'll need to buy it back. But his quick temper and dislike of golfers threaten to derail his plan before he even gets started.
