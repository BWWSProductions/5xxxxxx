Essence to Essence is the eleventh studio album, and thirteenth album overall, from Scottish singer-songwriter Donovan. It was originally released in both the UK and the U.S. in December 1973. On January 26, 1998, Epic/Rewind Records re-released Essence to Essence in the UK, marking the first CD release for the album.
