Gary Larson - The Far Side Gallery - Volumes 1 & 2


== INFO ==

The Far Side Gallery is an anthology of Gary Larson The Far Side comic strips. Cartoons from previous books The Far Side, Beyond the Far Side, and In Search of the Far Side are featured, all of which were printed from 1982-1984.

The Far Side Gallery 2 is the second anthology of Gary Larson The Far Side comic strips. Cartoons from previous collections Bride of The Far Side, It Came from the Far Side, and Hound of the Far Side are featured, all of which were printed from 1985-1987. The foreword was written by Stephen King.
