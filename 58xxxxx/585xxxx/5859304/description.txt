
                                      "Like a G6"

                 Single by Far East Movement featuring The Cataracs and Dev 

                               from the album Free Wired 

 "Like a G6" is a 2009 electro house single by Far East Movement featuring The Cataracs and Dev.

The name of the song is open to listener interpretation.However, many consider the name to refer to theGulfstream G650, a twin-engine aircraft under development by Gulfstream Aerospace.

The song's chorus samples lines from "Booty Bounce" by Dev.

At the end, Far East Movement board a Gulfstream G650.

Colette Carr, a fellow Cherrytree Records artist, who makes a guest appearance in the music video, released a remix of "Like A G6" with her own lyrics, featuring Cherry Cherry Boom Boom.
