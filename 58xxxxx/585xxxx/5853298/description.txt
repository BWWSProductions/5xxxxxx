Synopsis: 	

Dr. Bruce Banner has a unique condition - when he is under stress, he transforms into a humongous green monster. He is left scouring the planet for a cure, pursued by The Abomination, a horrible beast who wants to come between him and the cure to his condition. He must call upon the hero within in order to remain safe from the evil that wants to use him.
