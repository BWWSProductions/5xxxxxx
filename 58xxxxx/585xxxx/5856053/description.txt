Rating: 	PG
  
Synopsis: 	

When Bella Swan chooses to live with her father in Forks, Washington, she doesn't expect much of anything to change. Then she meets the mysterious and dazzlingly beautiful Edward Cullen, who is a vampire, and loves Bella more than anything. Their risky romance is intoxicating and thrilling. But when unexpected visitors come to town and realize that there is a human among them, Edward must fight to save Bella from certain death.
