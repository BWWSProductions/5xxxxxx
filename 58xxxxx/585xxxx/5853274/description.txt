Synopsis:

Marine biologist Henry Roth finds the perfect woman, Lucy Whitmore and falls head over heels for her. When he sees her the following day she hasn't a clue as to who he is. Lucy suffers from a rare brain disorder that wipes her memory clean every night. With the help of his friend Ula, Henry has to concoct new and increasingly clever ways to meet Lucy and get her to fall in love every day.
