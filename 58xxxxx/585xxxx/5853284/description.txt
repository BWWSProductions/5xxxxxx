Synopsis:

Adam Sandler stars as Bobby Boucher, a 31 year-old loser who manages to channel a lifetime of abuse and humiliation into aggression on the football field, becoming a quarterback-destroying machine in the process. Sandler shines in another example of the physical comedy he specializes in.
