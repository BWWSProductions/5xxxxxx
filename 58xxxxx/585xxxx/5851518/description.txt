Swarm Intelligence (The Morgan Kaufmann Series in Evolutionary Computation)

Publisher: Morgan Kaufmann; 1st edition | ISBN : 1558605959 | April 9, 2001 | 512 pages | PDF | 6.5MB

[img]http://img820.imageshack.us/img820/4916/book318.jpg[/img]

This book contains: an overview of evolutionary programming techniques; an exposition of the argument that intelligent behavior has a large social component in addition to a genetically determined component; the presentation of an optimisation technique whereby a swarm of possible solutions fly through a problem space and base their search trajectories not only on personal experience but also on the experiences of the group. ie- There is a social component to the search of the problem space.
