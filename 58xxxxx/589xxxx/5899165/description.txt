The Hollies - Long Cool Woman (in a Black Dress) [promo].VOB



HQ very rare promo music video. "Long Cool Woman in a Black Dress" is a 
song written by Allan Clarke, Roger Cook, and Roger Greenaway and performed 
by British pop rock group The Hollies. According to Clarke, the song was 
written "in about five minutes" and when the song made its mark in America, 
Clarke had already left the band, but Clarke feels that 
"it wasn't unfortunate" on account of he wrote the song.
