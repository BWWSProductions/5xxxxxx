Manfred Mann - Do Wah Diddy Diddy [promo].VOB 



HQ very rare promo music video. "Do Wah Diddy Diddy" is a song written by 
Jeff Barry and Ellie Greenwich and originally recorded in 1963 by the 
American vocal group The Exciters. In 1964, it was covered by British 
R&B, Beat and pop band Manfred Mann. Manfred Mann's version, which was 
more commercially successful, was recorded on June 11, 1964, released 
on July 10, and spent two weeks in August at number one in the UK 
Singles Chart, and in October of that year it spent two weeks at the 
number one spot in the U.S. Billboard Hot 100 chart.
