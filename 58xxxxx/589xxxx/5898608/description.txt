Red is a three-issue comic book mini-series published in 2003 and 2004 by WildStorm imprint Homage Comics. It was created by writer Warren Ellis and artist Cully Hamner and tells the story of Paul Moses, a retired CIA agent, who is targeted for assassination by his former employers.

The mini-series was developed into a movie by screenwriters Erich and Jon Hoeber which was recently released and stars Bruce Willis as the main character, although his name was changed to Frank Moses.  Included in this pack are the four movie prequel comics.
