THE TIME SHIFTERS

Description  
Tom Merrick (Casper Van Dien) is a recently fired 
reporter who is assigned the grunt work of researching 
great disasters of the past. He realises that in 
many photos of the disasters, he can see the same 
unusual man lurking around. Not long after, 
he sees the same man on a flight to Washington. 
Tom has to team up with another reporter to find out 
about this mysterious man and other people apparently 
travelling through time and lurking around terrible disasters.
