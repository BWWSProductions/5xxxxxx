The KeredCast is a free downloadable dance music podcast hosted, mixed and produced by Kered. The show also features guest DJ mixes and interviews from the world's best DJs such as John Digweed, Axwell, Hernan Cattaneo, Benny Benassi, Sharam, Mark Knight, Darren Emerson, Satoshi Tomiie, Nic Fanciulli, Moby, Chris Lake and many more!
