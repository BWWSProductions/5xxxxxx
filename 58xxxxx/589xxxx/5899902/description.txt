An American adventurer & the spoiled daughter of his wealthy financier are shipwrecked on an uncharted island, inhabited by whip-cracking Nazi war criminals, who like to perform ghastly experiments on the local jungle maidens. Will Jerry Turner (Irish Mc Calla), ever get off her high horse and stop worrying about her powder-blue cashmere shortie? And what IS a moth-ball monstrosity? See this drive-in classic & find out!
