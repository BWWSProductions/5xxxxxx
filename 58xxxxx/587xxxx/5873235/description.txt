This is the film version of the 2007 West End version of Shakespeare's Macbeth, which had been reinterpreted for a setting in the 20th century.  Starring in the title role is Patrick Stewart.

The 10-minute "A Conversation with Sir Patrick Stewart" featurette was aired immediately following the film, and it is included in this release.
