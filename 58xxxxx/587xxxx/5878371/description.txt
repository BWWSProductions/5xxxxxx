Everyone's favourite Hero Next Door is back and more action-packed than ever before!

Between Mandy and her video camera, Norman's new super-hero identity and Charlie mistaking Sarah for a damsel in distress, life in Pontypandy is just as adventure-filled as ever and now Fireman Sam has even more friends and exciting rescue vehicles to help with the job.

Episodes Comprise:

1. Paper Plane Down
2. Hot & Cold Running
3. Sniffer Dog
4. Hearts on Fire
5. Perilous Path
6. New Hero Next Door
