Plot -
The story of the film revolves around the tragic happenings in the lives of four friends who come from a poor background and are always together during their good and bad times. Like any other human being of their type, they too dream of winning a jackpot. All of a sudden, their dream comes true and their life takes a dramatic turn. Soon thereafter, a series of unexpected things also occur and within six months time, each one of them die one after the other, under strange circumstances. What is the real cause of such happenings ? Who is behind such deaths ? All these questions may strike your mind. But, hold on; till the release of ?Lonavala By Pass?.
