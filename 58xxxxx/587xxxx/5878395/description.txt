Georgia Rule (2007)


Directed by Garry Marshall. 
With Jane Fonda, Lindsay Lohan, Felicity Huffman. 
Genre: Comedy | Drama | Romance

Plot: The rebel, reckless and spoiled teenager Rachel Wilcox travels from San Francisco to the conservative Mormon Hull, Idaho, with her alcoholic mother Lily to spend the summer with her grandmother Georgia. Rachel and Lily have a troubled relationship and Georgia follows severe rules at her home. While with Georgia, Rachel reveals a traumatic secret from her past that explains her rebelliousness and brings her mother back to Idaho to check whether her daughter is telling the truth. 

SUBTITLES: external, English for hearing impaired, French, Arabic, Portuguese, Finnish.
