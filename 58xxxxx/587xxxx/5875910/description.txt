The Complete Series from Award-Winning producer/director, Ken Burns entitled "The War"

Contains 7 parts detailing the followings of folks in 4 American towns, that each had those fighting & those staying home to keep the "War Machine" going. First-hand accounts largely litter the landscape for us to follow & is surely what makes this series one of Ken Burns' best.


Pt 1) "A Necessary War

Pt 2) "When Things Get Tough"

Pt 3) "A Deadly Calling"

Pt 4) "Pride of Our Nation"

Pt 5) "FUBAR"

Pt 6) "The Ghost Front"

Pt 7) "A World Without War"
