Sublime - What I Got [promo].VOB



HQ rare completely uncensored promo music video. The video for "What I Got", 
shot after Nowell's death, mainly contains a collage of archive videos and 
photos of Bradley, as a tribute to the singer. The video includes images of 
Long Beach, CA, where the band met their success, as well as live footage 
from a Sublime show at The Capitol Ballroom in Washington, DC.
(video does not have any logos)
