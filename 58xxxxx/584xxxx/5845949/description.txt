Plot:
The romantic comedy follows two best friends Soontri and Ter (Ramita Mahapreukpong and Rattanrat Eetaweekul) on their trip across Thailand, Laos, and Vietnam. The girls socially awkward, one hides behind thick glasses while the other is hearing impaired.

They eventually meet with Somchun (Montrol Jira a.k.a. J Monton), the adventurous male lead, in Vietnam. As the story goes on, and both girls develop a crush on Somchun they struggle with the complexities of their relationships.
