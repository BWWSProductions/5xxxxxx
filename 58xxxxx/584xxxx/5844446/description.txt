Shaun the Sheep - Series 2, Part 2 (2010) Episodes 31 to 39

started on May 17th, 2010, ended with only one final episode left yet to be captured.



Ep#71: "The Magpie" 
A magpie has nested in the big tree and is causing chaos by stealing everything around the farm. The magpie's nest soon begins to fill up with loot and Shaun has to come up with a brilliant plan to outwit the burgling bird.


Ep#72: "Operation Pidsley" 
While the farmer is away on a night out, the sheep sneak into the farmhouse for a party.



Ep#73: "Pig Swill Fly" 
The farm is looking spotless and everyone is on their best behaviour when the dreaded farm Inspector pays a visit.
 


Ep#74: "Shirley Whirley" 
In a bid to help move Shirley around the farm Shaun has the idea of fitting up Shirley with wheels.



Ep#75: "Foxy Laddie" 
A fox arrives at the farm, disguising himself as a handsome ram to infiltrate the flock.  



Ep#76: "Shaun goes Potty" 
The sheep have fun with the Farmer's new pool table. 



Ep#77: "An Ill Wind"
The Farmer receives a huge electricity bill and decides to build his own wind turbine to power the farmhouse



Ep#78: "Fireside Favourite"
Bitzer has caught a cold and the Farmer decides to tuck him up in the house to recover.  



Ep#79: "Snowed In"
Shaun and his chums awake to find the farm covered in snow!
