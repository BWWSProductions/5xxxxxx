A glimpse into life in the Yemen as rarely seen by Western audiences: images of the country's landscape and insight into the characters of the colourful Yemeni people and their rich customs. The film's journey across the Yemen is seen from the points of view of both expert and novice - The Sheikh and The Gentleman.
