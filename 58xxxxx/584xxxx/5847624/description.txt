A Beginner's Guide To The World Economy.


Seventy-Seven Basic Economic Concepts That Will Change the Way You See the World



Randy Charles Epping, an American citizen currently
living in Zurich, Switzerland, has worked in international
finance for several years, holding management
positions in European and American investment
banks in Geneva, London, and Zurich. He holds a
master's degree in international finance from Yale
University. In addition, he has received a master's degree
from the University of Paris-Sorbonne, and a
bachelor's degree from the University of Notre Dame.
He is the managing director of IFS Project Management
A.G., a Swiss-based international consulting
company. Mr. Epping is fluent in six languages:
English, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, and
Spanish.
