Synopsis: 	

In the fourth movie of the series, Dr. Henry "Indiana" Jones is called back into action when he becomes entangled in a Soviet plot to uncover the secret behind a mysterious artefact known as the Crystal Skull. He must restore it to its rightful place or the Soviets will gain power to overthrow the West.
