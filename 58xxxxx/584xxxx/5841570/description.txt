                            Papa Stacker Version. 1.3.1

                                                             
Stack cute shapes all up in a stable way, and last for 10 seconds. When you touch the screen, a stack will follow your finger. Move the stack to the position desired. Then release your finger to let it go. Level editor enables you to edit 
your own creative levels.
