Brooke Gabrielle Fraser (born 1983) is an award-winning New Zealand, Contemporary Christian music and pop singer-songwriter. The daughter of Bernie Fraser, a former All Black, Fraser was musically active from a young age.

Born on 15 December 1983 in Wellington, Fraser is the eldest of the three children born to former All Black, Bernie Fraser and his wife Lynda Fraser. She grew up in Naenae, Lower Hutt and attended Dyer Street School, Naenae Intermediate School and Naenae College.

Fraser took piano lessons between the ages of seven and seventeen. She started writing songs at age twelve and taught herself the acoustic guitar at fifteen, although despite her singing success she has never taken singing lessons.[3]
