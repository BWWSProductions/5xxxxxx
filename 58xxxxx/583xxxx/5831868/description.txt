Kylie Minogue, Australia's very own goddess of love, celebrates her huge dancefloor following with Aphrodite. Premier British producer Stuart Price lends his magical skills to Kylie's eleventh studio album, and the first since 2007's X, which looks set to take the club scene by the scruff of the neck with some delectable electro dance-pop - no-one does it better than Ms. Minogue.
