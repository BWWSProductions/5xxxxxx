About the game:
===============

Embrace the past, present, and future in Star Crossed Love, a romantic search across time to find your soul mate. Julia is a smart and successful woman moving up the ladder at her job, but getting nowhere in her love life. Shes tried bars, clubs, and friends to no avail. She never feels a connection to any of the people she dates, and realizes something is missing; it just doesnt feel right. On a strange whim one night, Julia posts an ad to an online dating site and gets a cryptic reply, one that sends her down a path to meet the man shes yearned for all her life, and surprisingly, known throughout the ages.

FEATURES:
========
*Take a journey of selfdiscovery in this Hidden Object romance.
*Search around the world and across time for your soul mate.
*Overcome the karmic forces that have kept you apart in this lifetime.
*Solve puzzles and locate time capsules to reawaken your memory.
*Can you reconnect with your one true love?
