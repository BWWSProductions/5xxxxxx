Summary:
In the 1880's, Chon Wang's father and keeper of the Imperial Seal has been murdered
by Parliament and royal family member Rathbone, (who steals the Imperial Seal) with
Chon Wang's sister, Chon Lin, witnessing the murder. Chon Lin follows Rathbone to
London to kill him, while sending Chon a letter telling him of the murder. Chon
then travels to New York for Roy O'Bannon. Together they travel to England and
meet up with Chon Lin to kill Rathbone and get the Imperial Seal back.
