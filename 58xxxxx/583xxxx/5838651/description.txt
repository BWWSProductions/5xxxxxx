Le Noise is the title of an upcoming album by Neil Young. It is scheduled to be released on September 28, 2010. The album was recorded in Los Angeles and produced by Daniel Lanois. Lanois stated in an interview with Rolling Stone Magazine that "We cut a couple of solo acoustic songs, but the rest is very electric" and "There's no band, but I got in there with my sonics." On August 20th, Young posted to Facebook the release date for different formats (including an app for iPhone and iPad) and revealed that it would be a solo album. A music video for the track "Angry World" was premiered on September 14, 2010.[4] A music video for "Hitchhiker" was also premiered on September 17, 2010.
