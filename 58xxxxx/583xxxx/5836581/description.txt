 - Description - 

The Prodigy's fourth studio album, Always Outnumbered, Never Outgunned was released on 23 August 2004, and 14 September 2004 in the USA. A precursory and experimental single, "Memphis Bells", was released in very limited numbers, followed by the traditional release of the single "Girls". The U.S. version of the studio album contained a bonus track; a remix of "Girls", entitled "More Girls". The album was promoted by a tour, which lasted over 2 years.
