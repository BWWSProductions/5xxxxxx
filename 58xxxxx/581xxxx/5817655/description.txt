A Thousand Suns is the fourth studio album by American rock band Linkin Park, due for release on September 10th (Europe), 13th (UK) and 14th (US), 2010, through Warner Bros. Records. The album was co-produced by Linkin Park vocalist Mike Shinoda and Rick Rubin, who previously worked together to produce Minutes to Midnight (2007). The lead single for the album, "The Catalyst", was released to radio and digital music retailers on August 2, 2010.
