Destination Hitchcock: The Making of 'North by Northwest' (2000) 
 
http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0293087/

Destination Hitchcock:The Making of North by Northwest is a 2000 documentary film about the making of Alfred Hitchcock's classic thriller film North by Northwest. It is hosted and narrated by the film's female lead, Eva Marie Saint and features interviews with Hitchcock's daughter, several of the technical and behind-the-scenes people involved with the production of the film, and the main surviving actor, Martin Landau.
