Modern live performances, loyal to the authentic Rembetiko style. Antonis Ainitis plays bouzouki and sings. Live recordings that took place between 2005 and 2010.
Antonis Ainitis is a bouzouki player, singer and composer. A worthy upholder of the Rembetiko tradition, and of the Greek folk urban tradition. With many years of experience on folk music stages, concerts and participation in many musical events in Athens and the entire Greece.
He has chosen not to move to discography yet. He is a truly folk musician and probably the most important contemporary representative of Rembetiko. He has endless friends in Greece and abroad!
--------------------

Nouvelles seances, fideles au style authentique de Rebetiko. Antonis Ainitis joue de bouzouki et chante. Ces enregistrements sont sur scene, et ont eu lieu entre 2005 et 2010.
Antonis Ainitis est un joueur de bouzouki,chanteur et compositeur. Noble continuateur de la tradition de Rebetiko et de la tradition urbaine populaire grecque. Il a beacoup d'ans d'experience aux scenes folkloriques, paricipations aux concerts at evenements musicals a Athenes et en Grece.
Il a choisi de ne pas enregistrer d'albums. Il est un vrai folk musicien et probablement il reste le plus important representeur de Rebetiko de nos jours. Il a tant d'amis en Grece et a l'etranger!
