PAPA ROACH - ABOUT THE BAND

Papa Roach is an American rock band from Vacaville, California. They realised 7 studio albums: Old Friends From Young Years (1997), Infest (2000), Lovehatetragedy (2002), Getting Away With Murder (2004), The Paramour Sessions (2006), Metamorphosis (2009) and Time For Annihilation (2010). The current band members are Jacoby Shaddix, Jerry Horton, Tobin Esperance and Tony Palermo.
