 Photoshop CS5 One-on-One: Advanced, the second part
of the popular and comprehensive series, updated for
CS5, follows internationally renowned Photoshop guru
Deke McClelland as he dives into the workings of
Photoshop. He explores such digital-age wonders as
the Levels and Curves commands, edge-detection
filters, advanced compositing techniques, vector-
based text, the Liquify filter, and Camera Raw. Deke
also teaches tried-and-true methods for sharpening
details, smoothing over wrinkles and imperfections,
and enhancing colors without harming the original image.
Exercise files accompany the course.
