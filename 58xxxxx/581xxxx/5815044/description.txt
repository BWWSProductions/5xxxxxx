Guest Stars (in supporting roles) :
John Marley and Tom Skerritt

The main character is played by buck-toothed 
Kevin Coughlin, a man who died in a hit-and-run 
car accident at the age of 30 in 1976. 
Kevin appeared in several TV westerns in the 
1960s and 70s, particularly GUNSMOKE towards the end. 
He started in television as a child actor in the 1950s.
