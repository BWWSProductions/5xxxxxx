Ankahee aka The Unspoken (1985) is a rare film from the Indian parallel cinema era of the 70s & 80s which produced a lot of cinematic gems. It stars two of the most significant- & also my favorite- actors of that era - Amol Palekar & Deepti Naval. The film is directed by Palekar himself.

Pandit Satyanarayan Chaturvedi is a renowned astrologer who lives with his wife, Devki, and his only son, Nandu. Nandu is in  love with Sushma & wants to marry her but then his father tells him that his wife will die within an year of the marriage. Nandu is now in a dilemma & must decide to believe his father and let go of Sushma, or defy his astrological beliefs and marry her.
