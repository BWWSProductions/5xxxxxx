The Best of KC and the Sunshine Band was a compilation album by KC and the Sunshine Band, first released in 1990. The album contained every track included in their 1980 Greatest Hits compilation (which had gone out of print), along with their top 20 1983 hit "Give It Up" and other moderately-successful singles.
