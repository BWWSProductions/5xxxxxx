Republic of Doyle: The Hidden Agenda

This episode appears to be the "unaired" pilot for the CBC series, Republic of Doyle. The episode is titled "The Hidden Agenda" and is half the length of the regular series episodes at about 22 minutes. One of the main characters, Malachy Doyle, is played by Peter MacNeill in this episode but was replaced by Sean McGinley for the series.

The resolution is 608x352 and the quality is quite good.

This episode aired on CBC on August 19, 2010.
