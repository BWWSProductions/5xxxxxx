Pro Evolution Soccer 2011 (officially abbreviated as PES 2011 and known as World Soccer: Winning Eleven 2011 in Asia) is a football video game in the Pro Evolution Soccer series being developed and published by Konami with production assistance from the Blue Sky Team.The game was announced on 9 February 2010 and is set to be released on 8 October 2010 on PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360. With Nintendo Wii, PC, PlayStation 2 and PSP being released soon after, in October to November. The UEFA Champions League and UEFA Europa League will feature within the game

The game is russian but an english commentary and english menu is included. Just copy them to the img folder and set them in the game.
