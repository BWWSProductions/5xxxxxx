Description
From Nia Vardalos, writer and star of My Big Fat Greek Wedding, 
comes the uplifting comedy My Life In Ruins. Georgia has lost 
her kefi (mojo). Discouraged by her lack of direction in life, she 
works for a travel guide, leading a rag-tag group of tourists as 
she tries to show them the beauty of her native Greece while 
waiting to land her dream job. Opening their eyes to an exotic 
new land, she, too, begins to see things in new ways - finding 
her kefi and possibly love in the process.
