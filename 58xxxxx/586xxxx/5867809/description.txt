This new series, presented by Rory McGrath, looks at life in one of Britain's favourite holiday destinations during a busy summer and the characters who inhabit it.

The landscape of Lake District was millions of years in the making, and it's not hard to see why people come from all over the world to experience over 800 square miles of rugged landscape, towering mountains and breathtaking views, all nestled in the North West of England. From Windermere to Derwentwater, Coniston to Ullswater, sixteen lakes give this district its name. This new series, presented by Rory McGrath, looks at life in one of Britain's favourite holiday destinations during a busy summer and the characters who inhabit it.
