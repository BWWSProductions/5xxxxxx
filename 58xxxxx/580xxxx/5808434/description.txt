Titolo originale: The Viking World
Anno: 2004
Regia: Bo Landin
Produttori: Scandinature,Discovery USA, ZDF Germany, France 5,Swedish Television
Distribuzione: IMP
Uscita in Italia: 2008
Lingue Audio: Italiano,English,Deutsch,Suomi,Svenska


The Viking ships,symbol of terror and pillaging,set sail from Scandinavia shores towards rivers and seas of all Europe.The Vikings were not only bloody conquerors,but also brave sailors who reached unknown lands.
How could their fast and light ships resist to oceanic travel? How could they rule the seas without navigational instruments? And how could they survive in a new land?
You will know the secrets of these proud sailors and the legendary saga of Erik the Red,who traveled from Iceland to Greenland,and of his son Leif Eriksson,who reached North America.
