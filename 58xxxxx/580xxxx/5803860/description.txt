'Barking' is the eighth studio album by British dance act Underworld. This effort sees band members Karl Hyde and Rick Smith expand their signature deep electronic sound by working with a diverse selection of guest producers. The likes of High Contrast, Paul Van Dyk and Dubfire all contribute to what is the duo's most intriguing and rewarding album to date.It includes the single 'Scribble'.
