Horton Hears a Who! (2008) 

Directors: Jimmy Hayward & Steve Martino
Writers: Ken Daurio & Cinco Paul  
Release Date: 19 March 2008  
Genre: Animation/Adventure/Comedy/Family
Plot
One day, Horton the elephant hears a cry from help coming from a speck of dust. 
Even though he can't see anyone on the speck, he decides to help it. 
As it turns out, the speck of dust is home to the Whos, who live in their city of Whoville. 
Horton agrees to help protect the Whos and their home, but this gives him nothing but torment from his neighbors,..
