In Photoshop CS5: Selections in Depth, author Jan Kabili offers a comprehensive tour of Photoshop CS5's selection features.     * Using the Marquee and Lasso tools
    * Working with the Colour Range command
    * Selecting with the Pen tool and paths
    * Making easy selections with the Quick Selection tool

    * Sharing selections between images
