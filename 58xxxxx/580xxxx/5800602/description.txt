
Obsessed with Vertigo (1997) (TV) 
 
http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0192387/

A documentary about the making and restoration of Alfred Hitchcocks masterpiece Vertigo. Narrated by Roddy McDowall, with behind-the-scenes talk from Barbara Bel Geddes, Henry Bumstead, Robert A. Harris, Patricia Hitchcock, James C. Katz, Kim Novak, Peggy Robertson and Martin Scorsese. Brings fresh perspective, not just to the film and the director, but to the Fifties Hollywood as well.
