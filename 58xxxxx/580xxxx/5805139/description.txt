Vertigo 2005: Live from Chicago is a concert film by rock band U2 from the first American  leg of their Vertigo Tour. Recorded on May 9 and May 10, 2005 at the United Center on the band's featured stop in Chicago, Illinois, in front of 25,000 people, it was released November 14, 2005 on DVD. The concert was filmed by Hamish Hamilton, who has directed other U2 concert DVDs. The video is the band's best-selling DVD, having sold 285,000 copies as of January 2007, according to Nielsen Soundscan.
