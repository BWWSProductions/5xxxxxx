Titolo originale: Ancient Warfare
Anno: 2005
Regia: Ali McGrath, Stuart Clarke
Durata: 50 min.
Produttori: Wild Dream Productions
Distribuzione: IMP
Uscita: 2008
Audio: Italian,English,Deutsch,Suomi,Svenska

In the first century A.D. the powerful Roman Empire ruled over the cities of all Mediterranean Sea coasts.How did it became so large? Professional soldiers and skilled generals were the basis of this success.
The roman army,organized and disciplined close to perfection, won against much larger armies. Romans adopted greeks brilliant war inventions and they improved siege technique in order to strike and terrify the enemies.
Siege towers,rams and catapults were decisive instruments against the enemies of Rome: those complex machines were fundamental for roman victory in the dramatic sieges of Iotapata,Jerusalem and Masada.
