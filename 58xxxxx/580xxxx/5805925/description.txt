Great news for all you mafia fans out there!
This mod unlocks the Free Ride Mode in Mafia II.

Copy the pc directory into the Mafia II directory 
Make sure to back up the original files and your savegames!!
Savegame location: "%UserProfile%\AppData\Local\2K Games\Mafia II"

Run Mafia II, at the Main Menu click on The Story and click Free Ride.
You can choose from six modes (Summer Day, Summer Day Rain, Summer Night, Winter Day, Winter Day Snow, Winter Night).


Updates:

* All houses are open (mom's house, uptown, vito's house, marty's apt)
* Fixed clothes changing from follow/wait
* All blips appear on the map
* All cars are in the garage
* Added all weapons with full ammo
* Added all suits to the wardrobe
* Plenty of money
