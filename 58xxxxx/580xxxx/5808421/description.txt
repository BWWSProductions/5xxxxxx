Titolo originale: Stonehenge:Secrets of an Ancient Monument
Anno: 2003
Regia: Carl Hall
Durata: 50 min.
Produttori: Parthenon Entertainment
Distribuzione: IMP
Uscita: 2008
Lingue Audio: Italiano,English,Deutsch,Suomi,Svenska

About 5000 years ago, a group of primitive farmers started building one of the most terrific megalithic structure of neolithic Europe.It took 1500 years to finish construction.
Why have they started such an impressive project,without the help of the whell and with primitive tools? How could they carry the stones? How could they erect them,in order to form the famous circle of Stonehenge? What does the accurate alignment with solar and lunar movements mean?
This fascinating documentary inspects the misteries of this building,on which archeologysts and researchers have formulated many different theories.
