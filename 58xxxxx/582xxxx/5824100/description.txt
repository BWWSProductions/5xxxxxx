Information on what to do if you have been impacted, kicked, punched or squeezed between the 

legs.

To be on the safe side you should never ignore this type of injury as it can lead to problems in your 

sex life, having children, and even your sport effecting Groin and Core Stability.

Advice is included on what to do initially after a impact, and whenever possible you should have a 

check-up as soon as possible after the incident.

Swollen and Painful Testicles is covered by Footballer Support's 24 Hour Emergency Service for 

players in the West London area (U..K.) and this service is also available to non-clients and non-

athletes. Full details given in the file.
