Genre: Sludge/Doom Metal
Origin: United States of America (Kenner, Louisiana) 

When The Kite String Pops is the debut album of the sludge metal band Acid Bath.
Released on August 8, 1994, it is considered an underground classic.
The album's artwork is a painting made by notorious American serial killer John Wayne Gacy while in prison awaiting execution.
