
"Stay The Night" is the lead single from British singer/songwriter James Blunt's third studio album, Some Kind of Trouble. The single is set to be released on October 25, 2010.[The single was released in Austria on September 10, 2010, and has since peaked at #4 there. Blunt will perform the song live for the first time at the Help For Heroes Charity Concert in Twickenham on September 12, 2010.
