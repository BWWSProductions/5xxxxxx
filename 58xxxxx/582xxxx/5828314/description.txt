Genre: Comedy

Country:USA

Languages:English

After tailing his dream girl into a sex addict support group meeting, Andy (Josh Cooke) concocts a fake identity and tries to fit in. But as he woos the object of his desire, the fake sexaholic struggles to keep his real life from falling apart. Directed by Lawrence Trilling, this outrageous romantic comedy co-stars Greg Grunberg (who co-wrote the screenplay) and features Tom Arnold, Lisa Lampanelli and Henry Winkler.

Cast: Greg Grunberg, Josh Cooke

Director: Lawrence Trilling
