Big Fish (2003) PG-13

In this Tim Burton fantasy based on the novel by Daniel Wallace, William Bloom (Billy Crudup) 
tries to learn more about his dying father, Edward, by piecing together disparate facts from a lifetime
of fantastical tales and legends of epic proportions. Ewan McGregor co-stars as the young Edward, a 
traveling salesman, with Albert Finney playing him as an older man. Helena Bonham Carter and Steve
Buscemi also star.
