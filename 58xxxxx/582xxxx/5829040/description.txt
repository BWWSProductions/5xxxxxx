Here is a program that will change the way you teach about cesarean birth. With first of its kind, 3D cesarean animation intertwined with one's couple's unplanned cesarean birth story, viewers gain a better understanding of the procedure than ever before, without the distress that showing actual surgery can pose. Learn reasons for planned and unplanned cesarean births, strategies for reducing the risk of needing a cesarean, steps of the surgical procedure, benefits and risk.
