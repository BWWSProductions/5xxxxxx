Voluntary Madness -- My Year Lost and Found in the Loony Bin 

by Norah Vincent 



Investigative journalist Norah Vincent spent two years disguised as a man. As a result of her gender experiment, she developed and battled chronic depression. Heeding medical advice, she decided to check herself into a mental facility. Here, she details her experience, scrutinizing the use of drugs as treatment, the dysfunction among patients and caregivers, and the effects of institutionalization.
