Synopsis:
Through the eyes and imagination of a young girl, Architecture of Reassurance  explores life in a suburban wonderland where objects and design create an illusion of identity, belonging and comfort. Mike Mills blurs the lines of narrative film with documentary technique by exploring the ideas and lives behind suburbia's architecture.
