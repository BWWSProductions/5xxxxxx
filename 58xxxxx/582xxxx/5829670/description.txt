The 1996 album Now I Got Worry was recorded on the heels of the unexpected success of the Orange album. JSBX responded by delving even deeper into black American music, recording in Memphis and Mississippi, and working with guest artists R.L. Burnside, Rufus Thomas and Money Mark (Beastie Boys). Expanded with 12 b-sides and unreleased tracks and 4 radio spots.
