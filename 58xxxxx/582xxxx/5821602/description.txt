RESTA IN ASCOLTO is Laura's 7th commercial Italian release and all I have to say is WOW!!!!!!!! This time, Laura experimented more on a contemporary rock sound that will definitely captivate listeners from song to song....It is full of emotion and power packed vocals and lyrics. The most touching and inspiring song is DOVE L'ARIA E' POLVERE, which is a tribute Ali Ismaeel Abbas (the 12 year old boy from Iraq who lost his family and his arms when his house was attacked by a rocket. www.aliabbas.net)
