The Virtual Music School introduces WYSIWYH technology for music education:[i] What You See Is What You Hear[/i]. In these movies you see melody, harmony and explanation simultaniously with hearing the audio. This way, music theory that looks complicated in a book is easily understood.

This file contains a Quicktime movie about notation, explaining how to write proper rhythms and accidentals (flats and sharps), and explains how to deal with sixteenth notes. The package also contains two PDFs, one page with assignments, and one with answers.
