mini movies off Assassin's Creed.

Assassin's Creed: Lineage is a series of three short films based on the universe of the video game Assassin's Creed. The films are made by Ubisoft, the game company itself, and the first episode was released on October 26, 2009 onThe films have a promotional goal for the game as well as an attempt for Ubisoft to make its first step in the film industry..
