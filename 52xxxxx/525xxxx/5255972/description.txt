The Gloomiest Celebration Of The Year / Vuoden synkin juhla

Info:
The Gloomiest Celebration Of The Year (2008) is the first full lenght film about gothic culture in Finland. The main focus in the film is the Lumous Gothic Festival in the year 2007.

Live acts from Bands such as Suruaika, Machine Park, Sinmasters, Knifeladder,
Das Ich and All Gone Dead are seen. On the soundtrack you hear The Mimic, Huuto49, Uhreja Kuten Me Kaikki and Two Witches.

The rockumentary is directed by Mikko Martikainen.
