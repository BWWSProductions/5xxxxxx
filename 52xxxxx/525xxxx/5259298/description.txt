God Is in the House is the fifth album in the live praise and worship series of Christian Contemporary music by Hillsong Church.

Making of the album

God Is in the House was recorded live at the Hills Entertainment Centre by Darlene Zschech, Russell Fragar and the Hillsong team.
[edit] Album Design

God Is in the House is the first album to feature Darlene Zschech or the Hillsong team on the front cover. The cover artwork (designed by Chris Perry Graphic Design) uses the font Optima manually condensed to around 84%. The cover artwork for this album was changed when the new Hillsong logo was created.

Songwriters on this album include: Darlene Zschech, Reuben Morgan, Russell Fragar, Steve McPherson, Lucy Fisher, Dennis Jernigan & Tanya Riches.
