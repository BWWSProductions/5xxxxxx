The Laughter of God, By John Roland Stahl: An Outline of Metaphysics explaining the Origin and Nature of the Cosmos, the Process of Change, and the Patterns of Illusion in the Dance of Life, with reference to the I Ching, the Kabbalah, Lao Tzu, Pythagoras, and Hermetic Alchemy.  Published with a collection of additional essays on a wide range of topics.
