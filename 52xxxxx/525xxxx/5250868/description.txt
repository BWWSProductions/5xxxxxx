After your trip to Amsterdam, the time has come for a relaxing holiday in Youda Legend: The 

Golden Bird of Paradise! It all seems peaceful, but again the journey will be nothing like you 

expected it to be. The tropical environments will bring you more than a relaxing time and 

cocktails at the beach as your Hidden Object skills are tested. Questions rise and irreversible 

decisions have to be made to finally reveal the mystery of The Golden Bird of Paradise!
