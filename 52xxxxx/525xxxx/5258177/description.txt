  People have been gobbling up puzzles of all sorts in newspapers and books
  for years, but with Puzzler Collection they get as many as they could ask
  for on one disc. Puzzler Collection contains over 2,000 puzzles across the
  categories of Crosswords, Sudoku, Wordsearch and Fitword.

  There are a number of game modes for your money: 'Puzzler Tournament'
  invites you to challenge the Puzzle Master and climb a ladder by completing
  several different puzzle types. Each successfully completed rung will see
  your rank rise. 'Quick Blast' invites you to create a daily puzzle
  challenge that fits into your routine. You can tell the game how much time
  you have to play, and you will be set a number of challenges especially
  picked to make the most of the available time. 'Fast And Fun' invites you
  to complete a series of partially completed puzzles against the clock
  without making an error. 'Simply Puzzle' is for when all you want to do is
  play at your leisure. Pick any type of puzzle and any level of difficulty.
  That's all there is to it.

  On completion of the Crossword and Fitword puzzles, you are given a chance
  to try and solve a 'Spellout' for additional points. The 'Spellout' is
  basically an anagram for the solving. A small bonus, but a welcome one.

  Features:
  ~~~~~~~~~
  - Featuring Fitword, Crossword, Sudoku and Wordseach!
  - 5 game modes
  - Puzzler Tournament - Climb the tournament ladder as you attempt 20
    different puzzles, each with a target time
  - Quick Blast - create a daily puzzle challenge that fits into your routine
  - You get to name your puzzle, choose your difficulty level, your time
    limit and which puzzles you want to include
  - Simply Puzzle - when all you want to do is play any puzzle at your
    leisure. No scores, no timers â€“ just puzzles
  - Fast and Fun - complete 15 or more mini puzzles against the clock without
    making any mistakes
  - Play solo or challenge a friend in 'Head-To-Head'
  - Three difficulty levels: beginner, intermediate and pro.
