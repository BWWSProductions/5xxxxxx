The Burtons have just moved into their beautiful new dream home, which unfortunately for them, happens to be haunted! Now the Burtons need your help to clear their house of a group of particularly unfortunate ghosts. Discover details of the departed houseguests by solving enigmatic riddles left behind for the living. Use household objects to assemble a custom ghostcatchers for each pesky poltergeist, and then challenge them in unique minigames where they make the rules! Unravel the mysteries of the ghosts' unfortunate fates and help the Burtons take back their home in Mishap: An Accidental Haunting.
