This new edition of "Colloquial Estonian" is easy to use and completely up-to-date. Specially written by an experienced teacher for self-study or class use, the course offers you a step-by-step approach to written and spoken Estonian. No prior knowledge of the language is required. What makes "Colloquial Estonian" your best choice in personal language learning? It is interactive - lots of dialogues and exercises for regular practice; clear - concise grammar notes; practical - useful vocabulary and pronunciation guide; and, complete - including answer key and special reference section. By the end of this rewarding course you will be able to communicate confidently and effectively in Estonian in a broad range of everyday situations.
