Powers is an American creator-owned comic book series by Brian Michael Bendis (writer) and Michael Avon Oeming (artist). 
The series' first volume was published by Image Comics (2000 to 2004). 
Subsequent volumes (beginning in 2004 along with David Mack's Kabuki) have been published by Marvel Comics as a part of the Icon imprint.
