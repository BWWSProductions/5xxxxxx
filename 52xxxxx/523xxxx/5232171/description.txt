Ray Columbus & the Invaders were a pop group from New Zealand active from 1964 to 1966.

The group was influenced by the early 1960s work of Cliff Richard and The Beatles. They scored a #1 hit in Australia and New Zealand with "She's a Mod" in 1964, a cover version of a song by The Senators. They had several more hits in New Zealand before disbanding in 1966. Leader Ray Columbus later joined California psychedelic band The Art Collection. Billy Kristian played bass for the invaders, He later joined Headband and Night.
