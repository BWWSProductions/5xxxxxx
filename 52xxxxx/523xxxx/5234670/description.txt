English:
A futuristic twist on Robert Louis Stevenson's Treasure Island, 
Treasure Planet follows restless teen Jim Hawkins on a fantastic journey across the universe 
as cabin boy aboard a majestic space galleon. Befriended by the ship's charismatic cyborg cook, 
John Silver, Jim blossoms under his guidance and shows the makings of a fine shipmate
 as he and the alien crew battle a supernova, a black hole, and a ferocious space storm. 
But even greater dangers lie ahead when Jim discovers that his trusted friend Silver 
is actually a scheming pirate with mutiny on his mind.
