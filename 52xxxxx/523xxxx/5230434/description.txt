'Introducing... Stan Walker' is the debut Album of Australian Idol 2009 winner Stan Walker. The album consists of songs he performed on Australian Idol 2009, such as Prince's "Purple Rain", Jeff Buckley's "Hallelujah", and the smash hit "Black Box". It also includes a track produced by the original Australian Idol Guy Sebastian.
