Santa's image is almost universally recognised as one of our most enduring and popular legends. Yet the holly old gentlemen with his bag full of toys, steering his reindeer and sliding down the chimneys is a relatively modern one. This festive documentary shows how today's Santa is a fusion of cultures and traditions associated with the Christmas season.
