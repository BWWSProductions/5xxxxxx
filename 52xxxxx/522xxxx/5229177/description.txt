Armand Van Helden & A-TRAK Present Duck Sauce

Duck Sauce is the new collaboration between A-Trak and Armand Van Helden, who teamed up to craft brand new bangers in the spirit of their favorite vintage disco house tunes, keeping the good vibes intact while adding that 2009 club knock.

2. You're Nasty
