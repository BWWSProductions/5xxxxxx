Du animaciaj filmoj lau versaj fabeloj de Aleksandr Pushkin, faritaj en studio Sojuzmultfilm en la 1940-aj jaroj. Pli detalan informon vd. lau adresoj http://kinejo.blogsome.com/2009/12/18/fabelo-pri-popo-kaj-lia-dungito-balda-1940/ kaj http://kinejo.blogsome.com/2009/12/19/fabelo-pri-caro-saltano-1943/ Kun subtitoloj en Esperanto.

Two animated versions of Aleksander Pushkin^s verse tales, made by Soyuzmultfilm studio in the 1940s: The Tale of the Priest and of His Workman Balda (1940) and The Tale of Tsar Saltan (1943). The films are in Russian, with inner Esperanto subtitles. About the literary sources, which are classical children^s reading in Russia, see at http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Tale_of_the_Priest_and_of_His_Workman_Balda and http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tsar_Saltan. The Tale of Tsar Saltan is readalbe in English at http://home.freeuk.com/russica4/books/salt/saltan.html Also you can watch online 1984 animated version by Ivan Ivanov-Vano at(in Russian) and http://www.overstream.net/view.php?oid=mse5abiks0ts (with Esperanto subtitles).
