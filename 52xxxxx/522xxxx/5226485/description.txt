Andreas Scholl (1967 - ): 
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Andreas_Scholl

A CD with some truly beautiful tracks, sung by Andreas Scholl (arguably the world's best countertenor for generations).  This CD is, imo, not quite a collection of his "Best" - as with most compilations thus named, it's more a collection of famous bits from divers styles - but most of it is good, especially Track 6 which is simply sublime even when compared to versions by other artists (Chance, Blaze, Jaroussky, etc).

I did not rip this CD myself; it is one of the few I possess from an internet source.  I'm not sure who to credit, though!  This CD does not appear in the main torrent indices, so I think its worthy to upload as a torrent here.
