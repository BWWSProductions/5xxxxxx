          ---Boy Sopranos from Selected Bach Cantatas---

All selections and track references are from the volumes in the Harnoncourt edition of Bach Cantatas
 (e.g., 38b-17 is Volume 38, CD 2, track 17).  

There are a few duets, a few alto solos, and one tenor aria, in addition to the sopranos.

     (These selections are not taken from a complete collection.)
