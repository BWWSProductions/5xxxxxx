How to install/use
====================================================

Firefox.

3) Open it with Firefox.

4) When it ask you if you want to install the addon, 

select Install.

5) Restart Mozilla Firefox.

6) Go onto your Facebook account and search for 

Bejeweled Blitz.

7) Add the game and start it.

8) When the game starts, so will the addon.
====================================================
List of commands
====================================================

Press 1-7 while your cursor is over a gem to change 

it.

Press F to turn the gem under your cursor into a 

flaming gem.

Press S to turn the gem under your cursor into a 

star gem.

Press R to turn the gem under your cursor into a 

rainbow gem.

Press M to turn the gem under your cursor into a 

score multiplier gem.

Press N to turn the gem under your cursor into a 

normal gem.

Press Backspace to delete the gem under your cursor.

Press Delete to delete the gem under your cursor but 

leave the sprite (causes glitches)

Press B to activate Blazing Mode (all matches 

trigger explosions)

Press F9 to pause the timer.

Press F9 while paused to advance one frame.

Press Shift+F9 while paused to unpause.

Press F1 to show FPS and FPS history (debug 

information)
