Synopsis:
Ping Lumprapleng, the director of Kon Hew Hua and Loveaholic (Koht Rak Eng Loei) comes back with his new project in Dreamaholic(Fan Koht Koht), a romantic drama about an unlikely couple's different points of view on love, life and dreams.
