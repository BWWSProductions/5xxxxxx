so start it with the loader.(Some people may have not this problem)

Instructions:

1.Install sjsro_client_setup.
2.Paste the pk2 files over.
3.Install sjsro_patch_001
4.Put the loader in the sjsro map, change in Data the Path to your location.
5.Play the game! and enjoy.
