Debut album of this blackened death metal band from Phoenix, Arizona.

For fans of: Belphegor, 1349, Lord Belial, Vader, Vital Remains, Urgehal, Marduk, Order of The Ebon Hand, etc.

Recording Line-up:

Rob Reyes - All Vocals
Fern Portillo - Guitar
Jose Gutierrez - Guitar (Bass on Track 4)
Dewitt Jose - Bass (on Tracks 1, 2, 3, 5, 6, 7, & 8 )
Bert Trejo - Drums

Recorded, Mixed and Mastered
at Arcane Digital by Ryan Butler
between April and May of 2009

Cover Art by Jumali

Additional artwork by Adriana Aranda

Layout by Minerva Gutierrez

All Music written by H.Trejo, J. Gutierrez,
A. Portillo, R. Reyes

All lyrics by R. Reyes
