Photoshop Plugin for Windows  32 & 64 bit Photoshop 

It can turn low-contrast photos into vivid and detailed images and transform ordinary photos into interesting and dynamic shots.
 ContrastMaster works with dozens of graphics applications including Photoshop, Paint shop Pro, Photoshop Elements, Photo-Paint, IrfanView and PhotoImpact under Windows and MacOS X. 
It also runs natively in Photoshop CS3 and CS4 on Intel-Macs. 
For accelerated rendering it supports hyperthreading as well as multiple processors and cores. ContrastMaster works with 8bit and 16bit RGB images.
