Dark Void is an adventure game that combines science fiction full of adrenaline mixture of air and ground fighting in a parallel universe called The Void ".
Play the role of Will, a pilot who goes through incredible circumstances after encountering the mysterious Void. In a desperate struggle to survive, Will joins a rebel group called the Survivors humans fighting against a dangerous alien race that plans to threaten Earth.
The unique combination of air combat through the backpack propulsion and third-person combat Dark Void create exciting gameplay that encompasses a seamless transition between ground combat and air.
