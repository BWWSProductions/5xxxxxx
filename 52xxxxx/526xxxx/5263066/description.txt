SYNTHEWAY STRINGS is a "rich-sounding" sample based string VST instrument. 
Its Multisampled Ensemble Strings: Violin, Viola, Cello & Double Bass, has the ability to produce beautiful and lyrical lines, as well as powerful and forceful articulations.  You'll find string orchestra sustained, Espressivo, Staccato, Marcato, Crescendo, Decrescendo and more.  Now in the version 2.5 includes synthetic, hybrid and pizzicato modes.
