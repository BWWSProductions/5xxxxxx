Psychedelic Sexfunk Live from Heaven is a video album filmed in concert at Long Beach Arena (30 December 1989), California released in 1990 on VHS. The video contains footage of the Red Hot Chili Peppers with the lineup of Anthony Kiedis, Flea, Chad Smith, John Frusciante and Keith "The Tree" Barry following the success of their 1989 album Mother's Milk.
