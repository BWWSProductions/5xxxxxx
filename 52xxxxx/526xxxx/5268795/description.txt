Genre...................: Action-adventure
Mode....................: Single player

The game's core gameplay is simple. Taking control of Harry Potter, the
player explores Hogwarts castle and grounds. Throughout the course of
the game, the player will encounter events that tie into the storyline
of the second Harry Potter book.
