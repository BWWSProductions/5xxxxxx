The Final Destination

The movie series that taps into your deepest fears returns with a new and imaginative
Chapter that delivers the expected- in so many keep-you-guessing, unexpected ways!
A strange premonition causes friends to abandon their day at the speedway, just before a crushing pileup hurtles cars into the bleachers with fiery consequences. They have cheated Death. But Death is only getting started. From the director of Snakes on a Plane comes the eerie innovative tale of the friends fierce fight to keep themselves and others alive.
