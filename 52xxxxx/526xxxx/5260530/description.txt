3CD set featuring some of the greatest hits of the 80s. Including a wide array of artists who made their name in the great decade. Discs 1 and 2 feature tracks such as 'Fade To Grey' by Visage; 'Chant No.1 (I Don't Need This Pressure On) by the recently re-formed Spandau Ballet, and 'Don't You Want Me' by The Human League. Other artists featured include Ultravox, David Bowie, Duran Duran, Talk Talk, Dead Or Alive and Soft Cell.
Disc 3 moves into slightly darker territory featuring some of the more unknown classics from the decade from the likes of Brian Eno & David Byrne; Telex; Iggy Pop; Japan and The Psychedelic Furs.
