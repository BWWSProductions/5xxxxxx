Wiz Khalifa "This Plane" (CDS) includes two edited versions, the advanced LP version, proper album retail version and instrumental version. Also included are two of the best songs off Wiz Khalifa's "Deal or No Deal" 2009 album, Lose Control and Chewy. Chewy was originally released on a mixtape mislabeled as Chevy Remix.
