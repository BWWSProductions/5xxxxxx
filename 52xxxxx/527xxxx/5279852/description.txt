 

Star Wars  #  : Invasion is an American comic book  #   series set in the Star Wars  #   universe. The series, published by Dark Horse Comics  #  , is written by Tom Taylor and illustrated by Colin Wilson with color  #   by Wes Dzioba. Just like another ongoing series by Dark Horse - Star Wars  #  : Legacy - it is confirmed to include various ties to the novels by Del Rey, while still remaining accessible to new readers. It will launch with two online-only preview issues on StarWars.com in May and June 2009. The first printed issue is scheduled to be published on July 1, 2009.

Published by Dark Horse Comics  #  , this new ongoing series will be set in the New Jedi Order era and depict the events of the Yuuzhan Vong War.
