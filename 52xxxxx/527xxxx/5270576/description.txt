When the mysterious masked superhero-killer Onomatopoeia arrives in Gotham City, it seems his sights are set against the Caped Crusader.  But how does the Joker feature in his nefarious plan?  With the bullets flying, the Dark Knight will be forced to make a terrible choice...stop the assassin or save the life of his greatest foe!

Written by Kevin Smith, with art by Walt Flanagan and Phil Hester.
