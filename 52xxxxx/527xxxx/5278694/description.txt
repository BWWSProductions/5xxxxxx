Broken Sword 2.5: The Return of The Templars is an unofficial Broken Sword game created by mindFactory, a team of fans of the series. The story takes place between The Smoking Mirror and The Sleeping Dragon. The game is designed in 2D to follow the steps of the first two games. The game was released free of charge on September 28, 2008. Voices are in German, while everything else (including subtitles) is in English.
