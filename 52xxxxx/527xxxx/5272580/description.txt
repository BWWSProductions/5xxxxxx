Cash, cars, boats, houses - Tony Stark has got it all.  The only thing that could ruin his day?  If every single one of his Iron Man armors were stolen, and then turned against him.  Tony has to battles his greatest creations while he tries to discover who could have stolen them.

Writer Joe Caramagna and artist Craig Rousseau produce a modernization of the classic Armor Wars storyline from the late 80's in an all ages friendly, continuity free mini-series.
