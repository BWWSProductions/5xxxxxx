-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                                 Release Notes
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Tsering Shakya talks about Tibet's contested history, particularly focusing on 
the 17th and 18th century.

Tsering Shakya teaches in the Contemporary Tibetan Studies Program at the 
University of British Columbia. His primary research interests are the political, 
cultural, and literary histories of twentieth-century Tibet. His publications 
include Fire Under the Snow: The Testimony of a Tibetan Prisoner (1997) and The 
Dragon in the Land of Snows: A History of Modern Tibet Since 1947 (1999).

Lecture was delivered on May 5, 2006 at the University of California, Berkeley.
