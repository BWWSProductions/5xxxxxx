Synopsis
Disney's PRINCESS STORIES tells heartwarming stories starring everyone's favourite animated princesses. This collection includes volumes 1 to 3.


VOL.1: A GIFT FROM THE HEART features Jasmine from ALADDIN, Belle from BEAUTY AND THE BEAST, and Ariel from THE LITTLE MERMAID, who discover that when you give a gift from the heart, magical things can happen!

VOL.2: TALES OF FRIENDSHIP compiles a new bunch of stories featuring familiar faces like Ariel and Jasmine as well as Snow White from SNOW WHITE AND THE SEVEN DWARVES. Each of the three stories here demonstrates that the most important part of being a princess is realising the importance of friendship.

VOL.3: BEAUTY SHINES FROM WITHIN features Ariel, Snow White and Cinderella as they tell the stories of their transformation from awkward, shy girls to confident, full-fledged princesses but teach viewers the most important lesson of all--that exterior beauty only comes from internal goodness.
