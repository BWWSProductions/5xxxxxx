On September 28, 1993, MCA released a 4-CD audio collection titled "Bing Crosby-His Legendary Years 1931-1957". To date, this is the most comprehensive collection from his most prolific recording period. Other collections are available covering his recordings from 1926-1930 and from 1958-1976.
