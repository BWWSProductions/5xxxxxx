Lead guitarrist for heavy metal bands Dr. Sin and Almah (as a guest), acclaimed producer and ingenious songwriter, Edu Ardanuy now lends his shredding hands to his long-awaited all-instrumental debut - Electric Nightmares. Fulfilled with fast and melodic guitars, Edu shows why he is considered one of the most respected musicians of South America.
