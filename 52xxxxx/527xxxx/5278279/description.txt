Jolin Tsai: The Acquired Talent


Detailing the true story of a dancing diva's path to success!

Jolin Tsai held her Hong Kong and Taiwan concert tour in 2006, stunning fans with her sexy dances and dynamic songs, winning great applause from the audiences. Her previous album Dancing Diva topped the sales chart by selling more than 250 thousand copies. These were results of hard work and diligent effort, and she thus named her new collection The Acquired Talent. The album consists of  100 minutes of non-stop musical fascination.
