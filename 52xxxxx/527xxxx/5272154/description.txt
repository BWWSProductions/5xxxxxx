Batman: Turning Points is a five-issue Batman limited series, exploring the turning points of the relationship between Batman and Commissioner James Gordon. 

Issue 1 is set after the events of Batman: Year One, issue 2 after the events of Batman: Dark Victory and before Robin: Year One, issue 3 is set after the events of Batman: The Killing Joke and Batman: A Death in the Family, issue 4 is set during the events of the Knightfall saga and issue 5 after the events of No Man's Land.

Written by Greg Rucka, Ed Brubaker, and Chuck Dixon, with art by Steve Lieber, Dick Giordano, Bob Smith, Brent Anderson, Paul Pope, and Claude St. Aubin.
