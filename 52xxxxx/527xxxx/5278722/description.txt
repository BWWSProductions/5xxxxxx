Artist: Arbre Noir
Genre: Electronic 
Style: Illbient, Experimental, Ambient, Tribal/Dark Ambient, Industrial
Country: Germany
Quality: lossless



ARBRE NOIR uses different didgeridoos in combination with voices, percussions, sounds of nature and synthesizer. The so-created soundscapes, floating between spheric didgeridoo-improvisations, original rhythms and modern drum sequences, allow the listener to escape into imaginary, film-like worlds of sound.



Albums:
Beyond (2000)
Serpent (2001)
Arbre Noir meets Polygon - Traveller (2003)
Roam (2004)
Madurai 2006)
