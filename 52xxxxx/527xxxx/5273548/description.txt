600 years old, Ra's Al Ghul has spent his existence trying to wipe out humanity.  But now, with his life draining away, Ra's turns to Batman, to help save him, whether to aid his ancient namesis, Batman's decision may be wrested from him when another party enters the fray with their own terrible agenda! 

Written by Greg Rucka, with artwork by Klaus Janson.
