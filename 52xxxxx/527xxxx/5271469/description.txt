The Terror Titans are a supervillain group that mirror the superhero group, Teen Titans, and are composed of "legacy" villains.

The Clock King hatches a plan for the Terror Titans, as well as their deadly new member - the Teen Titans' own Ravager!  But if he's to succeed, Clock King must first stabilize the underground metahuman gladiator arena known as the Dark Side Club!

Written by Sean McKeever, with art by Joe Bennett .
