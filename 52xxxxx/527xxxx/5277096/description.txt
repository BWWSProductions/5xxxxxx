Shadow of the Batman was a five-issue limited series published under DC's deluxe format. The series reprinted classic Steve Englehart/Marshall Rogers stories originally printed in Detective Comics #469-479.  This includes the retroactively named Strange Apparitions storyline which deals with a number of elements coming to a head at one point in Batman's life.  

The series also reprinted the issues from Weird War Tales, Mystery in Space and House of Mystery.
