Cherry Tree is a 2004 EP by the indie-rock band The National. "Murder Me Rachael" is a live version taken from a Black Sessions recording, while "Reasonable Man (I Don't Mind)" is a song featuring Padma Newsome.

Cherry Tree was also released on limited edition red-colored 10" vinyl.
