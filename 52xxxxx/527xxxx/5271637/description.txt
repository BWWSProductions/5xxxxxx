2009 crossover between DC Comics Titans, Teen Titans and Vigilante titles.

When Cyborg is attacked in the Teen Titans' base, they realise one of their number is a traitor... but nobody knows who the traitor is!  When finally revealed, the tables are turned on the aggressor, as the psychotic Vigilante is aiming to deliver final justice!  Will the Teen Titans stand together to defend one of their own, if one of their own has already turned on them?

Written by Marv Wolfman and Sean McKeever.
