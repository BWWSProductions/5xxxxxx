Released in 1994 to arcades everywhere, Virtua Cop was eventually ported over to the PC in 1997 under the name Virtua Squad. Set in Virtua City, Virtua Squad has the player playing as members of the Virtua City Police Department as they uncover an illegal gun-running scheme headed by the mysterious EVL Inc.
