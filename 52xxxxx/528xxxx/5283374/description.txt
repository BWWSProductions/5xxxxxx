"Starstrukk" is a song recorded by American electronic music group, 3OH!3. It is the second single to be released from their second studio album, Want. A radio-only, and the deluxe album version has been released featuring pop artist Katy Perry. The single has had considerable success in the United Kingdom, Ireland, Finland and Australia, reaching the top 10 in all of these countries.

A music video for the Katy Perry version of the song has also been confirmed. The new music video for "Starstrukk" including Katy Perry was shot at the Los Angeles County Natural History Museum's Rose Garden in Exposition Park on Monday September 21st. It features 3OH!3 getting assaulted by a group of girls after stealing their coins from a wishing fountain. Scenes with Katy Perry singing are also intercut. The video was directed by both Marc Klasfeld and Sum 41 drummer Steve Jocz. It appears to also be directly related to the plot of the new film When In Rome as the video appears on the official site and the song appears in the trailer.

"Starstrukk" debuted on the Billboard Hot 100 at E95 and peaked at #66 six weeks later. On the Canadian Hot 100, the song has peaked at #34.

The song debuted at #5 on the UK Singles Chart on the week ending 20th December 2009, the week of the Christmas chart. It stayed at #5 the following week before falling to #6. On January 10, 2009 it rose to #3, not only marking it's highest UK Chart peak, but it's overall Chart peak.

It has peaked at #4 on the ARIA Chart becoming the groups second Top 5 hit in Australia. In New Zealand, the song has peaked at #16.

Want is the second studio album by Colorado electronic duo 3OH!3. It is their first album with record label Photo Finish. The album was produced by award-winning producer Matt Squire. Songs from the album were played at the annual Bamboozle music festival in 2008. The track "Punkbitch" was included on the Warped Tour 2008 Tour Compilation. It's the first 3OH!3 album with a "Parental Advisory" label.

The first single from the album was "Don't Trust Me". The second single was "Starstrukk" which was released on June 8, 2009. A remix version featuring Katy Perry was released as a single on September 8, 2009.
