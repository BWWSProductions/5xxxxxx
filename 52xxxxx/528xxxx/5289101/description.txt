Lizzie Johansson, born 1932, is an independent swedish musician, poet and painter. 
All songs were recorded november 2009 with simple equipment and is very authentic. Earphones or good speakers is recomended to get good enough sound due to the simple
recording equipment. The songs can be seen as a cultural heratige of swedish folk and christian songs. Some of the songs Lizzie has written her self, but most of them are covers. The full copyright list of songs can be found at her hompeage.

Please visit her homepage for more information: http://drop.io/lizzie_johansson

Online albums with covers can be bought online via Itunes and other online stores linked on Lizzies site.
