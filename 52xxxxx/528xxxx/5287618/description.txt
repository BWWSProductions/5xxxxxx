The Dark Knight Detective enters a world of secret agents and government sanctioned murders in order to stop the return of a psychopathic pyrokinetic.  It has been over ten years since the black ops soldier Deathblow was murdered while on a mission to take down a rogue agent.  Now Batman must solve the mystery of his death before the killer strikes again.  But before he can take down the firebug killer, Batman must weave his way through the webs of secrecy and lies that made up Deathblow's life.
