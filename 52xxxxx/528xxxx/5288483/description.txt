BBC R4 Documentary - 'Mozart's Legacy'
Presented by John Suchet
Produced by Rosie Bolton
Broadcast December 26,27, & 28, 2005
Coded from tape at 128/44.1

Part 1 - The Jupiter Symphony, Broadcast December 26, 2005
Part 2 - The Magic Flute, Broadcast December 27, 2005
Part 3 - The Requiem, Broadcast December 28, 2005

Each programme focuses on the last work that
Mozart wrote in a particular musical genre.

John Suchet discusses with experts the way
these works are great pieces of music, and
how they influenced music that came after them.

NB Part 2's sound quality is impaired by some
low level interference, but is quite audible.
