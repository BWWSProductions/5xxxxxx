BBC R4 Documentary - 'Mozart 40'
Presented by Huw Edwards
Produced by Martin Smith
Broadcast December 25, 2005
Coded from tape at 128/44.1

On the eve of Mozart's 250th anniversary, Huw Edwards
visited Vienna to explore current knowledge, and to
lay some myths, about Mozart. 

A couple of the musicians provide real insight.

A marvelous programme.

Speakers
Nicholas Kenyon
David Cairns
Mitsuko Uchida
Rainer Lefevre
