The tenth disc issued to go with Great Danes 9 disc "Complete BBC Sessions" from a tape of an FM broadcast of the March 12,1963 show "Here We Go".

Only 3 tracks are Beatles tracks.

The recording session took place at the Playhouse Theatre in Manchester on Mar 6th, 1963.

The complete recording session included 4 tracks by The Beatles but I SAW HER STANDING THERE was never broadcast.
