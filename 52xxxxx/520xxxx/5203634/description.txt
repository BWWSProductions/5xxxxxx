Son of a Lion is a 2007 Australian-Pakistani drama film set in Darra Adam Khel, in Pakistan's North West Frontier Province.[2] The film tells the story of Niaz Afridi (Niaz Khan Shinwari), a Pashtun boy who wants to go to school instead of carrying on the family business of manufacturing firearms.[1] The director, Benjamin Gilmour, is an Australian former paramedic; Son of a Lion is his first film. It was filmed in the area around Darra with the cooperation of local residents; the actors are local non-professionals, and much of the dialogue is improvised.[3][4] It has received generally positive reviews.[1][5][6][7][8] The film is in Pashto with English subtitles.
