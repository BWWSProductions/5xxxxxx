Two CD set that celebrates the classic 'West Coast sound' that has inspired and enchanted millions of people around the world. From The 12-string jangle of The Byrds to the growl of Warren Zevon, from the rallying cry of Buffalo Springfield to the unstoppable Fleetwood Mac, this is the true sound of California. Whether you roamed Sunset Strip and the streets of San Francisco or just laid back in Laurel Canyon, the music was never far away. This double disc set features 40 tracks from the cream of the California crop including The Eagles, James Taylor, The Grateful Dead, Tim Buckley, Rickie Lee Jones, Linda Ronstadt, Arlo Guthrie, Gram Parsons, Bonnie Riatt, Love, Randy Newman, Little Feat, The Byrds, Warren Zevon, Buffalo Springfield, Fleetwood Mac, Bread, The Doobie Brothers, America and many more...... Great music, great times.
