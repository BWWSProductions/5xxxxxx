Snowbunnies follows the trail of a mother and stepson who must abandoned the leafy suburbs for the inner city.  It follows the single mother in her attempt to make a new home for the two of them and the son who must fit in to the rigors of a new all boys charter school in the unknown environment of a mostly black big city.  

This is NOT a movie.  It is a "user experience" with images, narration and video,  It is a flash-based program and only requires an internet browser (IE is best) to view.

More of the same can be found at the sissytrainer blog.
