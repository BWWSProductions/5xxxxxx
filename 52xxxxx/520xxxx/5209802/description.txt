Homeworld is a real-time strategy computer game best known for its full
three-dimensional movement, engrossing storyline and unique soundtrack.

Beneath the scorching sands of Kharak, the Kushan people have discovered
the remains of a long-forgotten titanic spaceship. Buried within the
ancient remains, the secret of their lost homeworld.
