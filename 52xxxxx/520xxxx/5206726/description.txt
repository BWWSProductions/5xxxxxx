Glumpus Van Der Hloed's Tales From The Crypt, starred Andrew Sachs, Mel Smith and Griff Rhys Jones.

Glumpus Van Der Hloed, ex-paranormal investigator, now London cab driver, must investigate a spate of bizarre deaths, leading him to the Bishop of Darkness and his nefarious  plot, while being aided by Sandy Cheesburger (an Anthropology Student from UCLA) and Creephole.
