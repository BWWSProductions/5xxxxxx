Plot Summary:
Story follows a teenaged girl named Lucy Heartfilia who aims to join the world's most
notorious mage guild, Fairy Tail. During a daring rescue, Lucy encounters Natsu "Salamander" Dragneel,
a quirky Fairy Tail member and a teenaged practitioner of the ancient Dragon Slayer magic.
Lucy is eventually welcomed into the Fairy Tail guild as she, Natsu, and other members go
on various quests together.
