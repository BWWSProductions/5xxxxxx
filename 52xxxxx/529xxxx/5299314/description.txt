Year: 2006

Desctiption: This is the official French pressing of "Schrei (so laut du kannst)" by Tokio Hotel. I managed to get this on eBay :D win.
Anyway, this is the edition including the re-recorded "Schrei" and "Der Letzte Tag", which have been named accordingly.
Also on the album are the acoustic version of "Schrei" and the unplugged version of "Durch Den Monsun" (also available on the Durch Den Monsun single).

This is just a taster of what's to come. I have got my hands on almost every single Tokio Hotel single and album version possible, with the exception of one single.

Let me know if you want me to upload more ;]
