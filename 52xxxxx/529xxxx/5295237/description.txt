The demon known as Belasco is burning a path around the globe searching for the legendary Bloodstone.  With it, he can rip open a dimensional portal, allowing all of Limbo to pour into our world!  Enter Storm, Banshee, Sunfire, Colossus, Nightcrawler, Wolverine and the new Thunderbird!  But can even they stop the fiery fiend from stealing the souls of their most cherished loved ones? 

Written by Chris Claremont and penciled by Thomas Derenick, with painted covers by Vince Evans.
