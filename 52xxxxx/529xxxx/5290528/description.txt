              1945 U.S. Tube Data
              ===================

This .pdf was created from the 1945 edition of the 
Radio Amateur's Handbook tube data section. (pages 472-512)
 
It contains "...essential characteristics and typical 
operating data for all U.S. made standard receiving, 
transmitting and special purpose vacuum tubes for which 
data is available, classified by use."

The 41 pages of tube data were scanned at 200dpi (Jan 2010)
