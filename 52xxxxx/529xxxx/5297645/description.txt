On June 22nd, 1941, five million axis soldiers crossed the borders into the Soviet Union, beginning the largest, most ferocious and bloodiest theatre of war in history. This is the Eastern Front.

War is fun to play in a game, but in reality, it is death. We should not forget that. Today, many people are spared by the horrors of war, but are suffering nonetheless. A lot of people wanted to donate money to our mod, and we decided to give it to a charity organisation. We think that the national multiple sclerosis society is a good choice. Multiple sclerosis is a painful disease that affects the nerve center, and there is no cure yet.
