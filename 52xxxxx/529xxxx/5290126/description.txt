BBC R4 Documentary - 'Vienna And The Turk'
Presented by Huw Edwards
Directed by Michael Sircan
Broadcast March 15, 2005
Coded from tape at 128/44.1

In 2005 Huw Edwards presented a series called:
'One Country to the Tune Of Another', in which
he examined musical influences countries had
on one another.

This one is called 'Vienna And The Turk', and
looks at Turkish influences in the music of Haydn,
Mozart & Beethoven. 

Edwards also has some interesting things to say
about the music used to support silent movies.
