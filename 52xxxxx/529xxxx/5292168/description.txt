This is a collection of scripts (mostly python, thus platform independent).
They help in removing DRM from ebooks (rightfully purchased) and converting them in different formats for a reading experience outside a proprietary software or hardware reader (think MobiReader, Kindle, RocketBook).

This collection has been assembled from various sources; their urls are included.

As is the nature of these sites, some of them suddenly vanish. This collection preserves the stunning intellectual achievements of motivated individuals and groups.

As is the nature of ongoing developments, this archive (Jan.2010) is just a snapshot of current activities. For updates check the sites themselves and read the comments...
