Greatest Hits is a compilation album by American punk rock band
The Offspring released on June 20, 2005 internationally and 
June 21, 2005 in North America. 

It includes hit singles from five of the band's studio albums as 
well as the previously unreleased songs "Can't Repeat" and 
"Next to You", the latter being a cover of The Police song and 
appearing as a hidden track at the end of the album. Greatest Hits 
peaked at #8 on The Billboard 200 with 70,000 copies sold in its 
first week of release.
