Play with time! Gross Beat is a time manipulation effect designed for repetition and scratching effects. Gross beat is perfect for real-time or rendered gating, glitch, repeat, scratching and stutter performances. Gross Beat stores audio in a 2-bar rolling buffer under the control of 36 user definable time and volume envelopes, giving you unlimited creative control.Key features:











-Powerful MIDI controlled envelopes
-2 bar audio buffer
-Real time effect, perfect for live performance
-36 volume/gating envelopes with user-controlled smoothing
-Linked or independent volume & gating slot operation
-A large number of creative presets ready to go
