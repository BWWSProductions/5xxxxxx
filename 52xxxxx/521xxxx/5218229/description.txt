nDs-mPeG, usually abbreviated DPG, is a special format of MPEG-1 video specifically for playback using the homebrew Moonshell program for the Nintendo DS. The video is encoded on a computer and then transferred, along with Moonshell, to the DS for playback.


Directors: Phil Lord + Chris Miller

Writers: Judi Barrett + Ron Barrett

Release Date: 18 September 2009 (UK) 

Genre:Animation | Family 

Tagline:Prepare to get served

Plot:The most delicious event since macaroni met cheese. Inspired by the beloved children's book, the film focuses on a town where food falls from the sky like rain.
