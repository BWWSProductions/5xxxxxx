                                Release Notes
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Ripped video, French audio and subtitles from R2 blu ray. Ripped English
audio from the Columbia Tristar R2 DVD soundtrack, worked it over with eac3to
and Audacity to sync with the blu ray release.
  Normally I hardsub English into the video stream, but the subtitles for this
movie are noted for being utterly inconsistent with the English dub. It's
pretty funny to watch the English dub with the English subs on, if you ever
want to re-watch with a bit more humour than the flick has to offer. At least
Lambert does his own English dub...
  Directed by Luc Besson, starring Christopher Lambert.
