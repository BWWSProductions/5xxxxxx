Yndi Halda (pronounced YIN-dee hal-DAR) are a post-rock band from Canterbury, United Kingdom. 'Yndi Halda' is Old Norse for "Enjoy Eternal Bliss", also the name of their self-titled debut. The band's music is characterized by expansive, cinematic pieces that have seen the group compared to post rock and modern classical genres.
