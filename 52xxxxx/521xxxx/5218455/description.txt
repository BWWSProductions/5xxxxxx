Documentary looking at the Spitfire designer R.J. Mitchell, his background, and the aircraft designs that he did in the 1920s and 1930s, culminating in the Spitfire, which successfully replaced the main Royal Air Force planes in Jun 1936, one year before Mitchell died of cancer. Also looks at the important and invaluable role played by the Spitfire planes during World War II. 

Director:
    MARTIN, Jonathan
