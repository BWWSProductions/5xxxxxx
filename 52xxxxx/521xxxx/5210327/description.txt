This is the First album by "The Hiatus", another one of the Ellegarden Splinter Groups. This one contains the lead singer from Ellegarden.

The Hiatus is a Japanese rock band formed by Takeshi Hosomi; the former lead vocalist of Ellegarden. Other members include guitarist Masasucks from Fullscratch, bassist Ueno Koji from Thee Michelle Gun Elephant, drummer Kashikura Takashi from Toe, and pianist Hirohisa Horie from Neil & Iraiza. It was newly announced that keyboardist Ichiyou Izawa from Tokyo Jihen and drummer Masakazu Ichinose from Asparagus joined as tour artists on May 25, 2009.
