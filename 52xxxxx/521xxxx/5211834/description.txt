Full Descriptionstars of today relive the stories of lesbians from long ago. Annabelle Lee is set to marry Melissa Monet's son -- but it's the older brunette Melissa that cute redhead Annabelle truly wants. Will love blossom over afternoon tea? Or will her letter to Melissa go unanswered? Meanwhile, Nina Hartley is writing letters to the lovestruck Nica Noelle, but Nica is too intimidated to make the first move. When Nina shows up unexpectedly, will passions finally flare between the two 'proper ladies'? Luscious brunette Zoe Britton is in love with a mysterious woman, and Julia Ann is tired of it. When she demands to see the letter Zoe is writing, will she get an unexpected surprise? And finally, Magdalene St. Michaels has had it with teenaged Nicole Ray's exploits. Will a good old fashioned spanking lead to something more erotic? Featuring authentic Victorian dresses and undergarments, shot and filmed in an real Victorian mansion.
