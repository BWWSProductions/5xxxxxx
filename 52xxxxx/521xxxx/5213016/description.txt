         Easy Plan Pro is designed to quickly and easily draw high   
         quality floor  plans,   site   plans,  and  architectural   
         designs.                                                    
                                                                     
         Easy  Plan  Pro provides a  full   array of graphic  tool   
         buttons to let you easily pick a tool or action. Once you   
         choose a tool, Easy Plan Pro tells you how to use it. You   
         may  use the mouse or  keyboard, or both, to  create your   
         plan.                                                       
                                                                     
         Easy Plan Pro  gives you many  choices for printing  your   
         plans. You may select a  specific scale, or elect to have   
         your plan printed at the largest  size  that  will fit on   
         the paper you select.                                       
                                                                     
         While there are  a number of impressive and powerful  CAD   
         programs available, they are more  difficult to learn and   
         use than Easy Plan Pro. To preserve its intuitive ease of   
         use and  low cost, Easy Plan  Pro  is  not   designed  to   
         compete with full-fledged CAD programs.
