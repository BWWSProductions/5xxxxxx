Homeworld: Cataclysm was originally developed in 2000 as an expansion of
Homeworld, but was released as a stand-alone game.

The story focuses on the struggles of Kiith Somtaaw, a minor clan amongst
the Kushan, as they unwittingly release a deadly menace known as The
Beast on the galaxy and seek to contain it. The Turanic Raiders, Bentusi
and Imperialist and Republican Taiidan also appear.
