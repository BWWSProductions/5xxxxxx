      pictures. The left side of the screen contains a set of buttons and a
      scrolling list of joints. The joints are arranged in seven groups,   
      according to usage:                                                  
                                                                           
      chairs                                                               
      tables                                                               
      table tops                                                           
      doors and panels                                                     
      boxes                                                                
      drawers                                                              
      cabinets
