RTE documents the Ireland Rugby Union team's journey from the despair of defeat in 2005 to their historic grand slam of the 2009 Six Nations.  Interviews include  players Ronan O'Gara, Brian O'Driscoll, Tommy Bowe, Paul O'Connell, Paddy Wallace, John Hayes, Rob Kearney, Luke Fitzgerald, Peter Stringer, and Gordon D'Arcy; coaches Gert Smal, Les Kiss and Alan Gafney and the modest mastermind himself: head coach Declan Kidney.
