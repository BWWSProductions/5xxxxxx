The re-release of Relapse, titled Relapse: Refill, includes seven bonus tracks, including the single "Forever", also featuring Drake, Kanye West and Lil Wayne (previously found on the More Than a Game soundtrack) and "Taking My Ball" (also released with the DJ Hero video game), and five new tracks. Relapse: Refill was released on December 21, 2009.
