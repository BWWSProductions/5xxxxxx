In 2007 Kristian Mattson released a self-titled EP. On March 5, 2008 he released his full-length debut in Sweden titled Shallow Grave. 
Pitchfork Media rating the full lengt debut album 8.3 out of 10.[1] Shallow Grave was also rated the sixth best album of 2008 by Obscure Sound.[2] Matsson toured the United States with John Vanderslice in 2007-2008 and Bon Iver late in 2008.
