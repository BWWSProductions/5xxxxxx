Brad Paisley announced on January 26, 2009 his new tour named "American Saturday Night." Dierks Bentley and Jimmy Wayne will be opening in the majority of the shows. Brad Paisley's newest album, American Saturday Night was released on June 30, 2009.

American Saturday Night is the seventh studio album by American country music artist Brad Paisley.

The album has been lauded by critics. According to Metacritic, the album holds a score of 86 out of 100, indicating "universal acclaim".
