This step-by-step DVD guide shows all aspects of starting and maintaining a Tropical Aquarium, chapters include: Fish Selection, Substrate, Lighting & Temperature, Introducing Fish, Plants, Feeding, Filtration, Water Quality, Fish Health/Diseases, and Breeding.

This DVD is the creation of Paul Talbot, who owns the largest commercial aquatic maintenance service in Sydney, Australia. Approximately 68 minute runtime. 2005 Copyright. Encoded to play in all regions of the world.
