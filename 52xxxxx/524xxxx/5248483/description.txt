Tony Robinson examines societies that have flourished as a result of climate change. In Peru, the Hauri people responded to a savage drought by developing advanced techniques of water management, while in Europe, a mini ice age triggered the Black Death, which resulted in a period of unprecedented progression. The Industrial Revolution was hastened by the benefits of a stable weather pattern, but this appears to be ending, bringing a new threat to mankind. Last in the series
