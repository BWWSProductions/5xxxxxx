The Fall of Great Empires brings ancient civilizations to life through its in-depth investigations of the rise and fall of the Egyptian, Roman, Persian, Holy Roman, Byzantine and Carthaginian empires. Along the shores of the Mediterranean to the deserts of the Middle East, powerful rulers struggled for domination, political and religious ideals clashed, and victories and defeats changed the course of history. Through lavish imagery, dramatic reenactments and expert interviews, the people and events that embodied each mighty imperial power unfold.
