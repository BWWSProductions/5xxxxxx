From the twisted mind of Doug TenNapel, one of the creators of Earthworm Jim comes this Claymation point-and-click adventure (the prequel to the PlayStation platformer, Skullmonkeys). Immerse yourself in the strange clay-animated world of the Neverhood, a challenging adventure chock-full of mind-wreching mysteries and an onslaught of odd-ball humor.

Release Date: September 22, 1997
