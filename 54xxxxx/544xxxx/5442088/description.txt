This is archived webcomic 1.000.000 Miles Away, telling a story of a group of space bounty hunters with shady past and many secrets.

Before author ended creating the comic, he managed to do 180 episodes, of which 65 were translated to English by fans.

This torrent is an archive of those 65 English episodes.

1.000.000 Miles Away can still be found at http://www.mma.rut.pl
