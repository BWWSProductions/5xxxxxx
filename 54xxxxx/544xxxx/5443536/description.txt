This is the complete discography of Aural Planet, a polish group composing electronic music simmilar to trance or goa genres. 
Aural Planet formed in the 90's among the polish demoscene community.
They've stopped existing after leaving of skorpik who decided to make solo career and started composing music and to games (in example the soundtrack to The Wither).
The rest of the group still exists as Sundial Aeon, they've published one album, "Apotheosis".

Albums:

    * Lightflow (1997)
    * Part: Second (2000)
    * 5EX Engine (2002)
    * Power Liquids (2003)
    * Acoustic Plantation Releases (2004)
    * Reworked (2004)
