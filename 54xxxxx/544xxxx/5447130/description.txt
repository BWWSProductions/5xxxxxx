Stardust Awards 2010 took place at Bandra Kurla Complex in Mumbai. Actor, actress and various other artists from Bollywood were awarded on the occasion of Max Stardust Award Ceremony in 2010. The glittering awards ceremony night witnessed host of Bollywood stars performing, giving and receiving awards.
 
Best Film of Stardust Awards 2010 was awarded to 'Chintuji', wheras Preity Zinta bagged the award for the Best Actress for film 'Videsh'. Star of the Year Male award went to Amitabh Bachchan for his outstanding performance in 'Paa' while Kareena Kapoor grabbed the Star of the Year Female for 'Kurbaan' and '3 Idiots.
