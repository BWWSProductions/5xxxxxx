Haruka is an only child who has lost her mother to sickness when she was little. 

When her mother was still alive, she received a hand mirror from her as a present and she treasured it a lot.

One day, she decides to go to a local shrine and pray for the return of her lost mirror. It is there that she accidentally encounters a small masked creature which she secretly follows to the land of forgotten items.
