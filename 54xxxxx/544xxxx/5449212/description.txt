[b]TVO Allan Gregg in Conversation, March 12, 2010: Wade Davis author of "The Wayfinders"[/b]

Many of us are concerned about how many plants and animals are becoming extinct, but what about human cultures? Renowned anthropologist [url=http://www.nationalgeographic.com/field/explorers/wade-davis.html]Wade Davis[/url] warns that half of the seven-thousand languages spoken around the world are set to disappear within a generation. He calls this a danger to humanity's survival. Davis delivered this year's [url=http://www.anansi.ca/titles.cfm?series_id=4]Massey Lectures[/url] which are collected in a new book. It's called [url=http://www.anansi.ca/titles.cfm?pub_id=1359]The Wayfinders: Why Ancient Wisdom Matters in the Modern World.[/url]
