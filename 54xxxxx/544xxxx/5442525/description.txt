The explosive breakout, a worthy rival Ricochet Infinity in the nomination for the best title. There is almost everything: innovative management, exciting gameplay, chic design, atmospheric soundtrack (90 minutes) in a retro-style, 6 modes, 10 different worlds, and even the possibility of playing together.

System requirements: 
OS: Windows XP/Vista/7 
CPU: 2.0 GHz 
RAM: 512 MB 
Video: 128 MB with shader model 2 support 
DirectX: 9.0c or higher
