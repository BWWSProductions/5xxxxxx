This folder contains the required image files for flashing and rooting your Droid Eris. Not included is the Android SDK and Java.

Files Included:
PB00IMG.zip (Original Root ROM)
Eris_03.zip (Updated ROM)
recovery.img (Amon's recovery image)
flash_image (Amon's Recovery Image)
