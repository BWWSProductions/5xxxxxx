Speed Video Splitter is a small video splitter tool. It also has very fast speed like Speed Video Converter. You can split all supported video files by setting the startting and stopping time.
Speed Video Splitter supports various video formats, such as AVI(Divx,xDiv), MPEG-4, mpeg(vcd,svcd,dvd compatible) QuickTime , wmv, VOB, FLV, iPod, iPhone, 3GP, PSP, RM.
