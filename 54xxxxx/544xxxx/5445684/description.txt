One of the most talked about pop stars of the year, million-selling teen sensation Justin Bieber releases his brilliant first full-length album. Glistening with catchy hooks and big choruses, My Worlds finds the 16-year-old in remarkable voice, aided by a plethora of special guests including Ludacris, Jessica Jarell and Sean Kingston.
