Do you have what it takes to challenge fifth graders? Can you handle the pressure of art, English, math, music, reading, science, social studies, geography, and history? Find out, in this trivia game based on the wildly popular game show. Every question - and there are 3000 of them in 28 different categories - comes straight out of a fifth-grade textbook. Choose from one of five virtual fifth graders to help you on your quest. Answer 10 questions in a row correctly, and your player will win one million dollars! And of course, you can always "peek" or "copy" to help you get the answer.
