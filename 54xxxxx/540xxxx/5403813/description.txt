Story:

Tantai (Chatchai Plengpanich) , a smart hit man is hired to silence some of powerful godfathers that link to a business case. One of the major targets is Lheemeng (Nirut Sirijunya), a big drug agent who's opposite to village chief Baum (Thanit Jintanukul). The conflict is also linked to a misleaded soldier Sompong, Tantai's old foe. While detective Chat (Santisuk Promsiri) is secretly watching all the event, waiting for his time to clean up the outlaws.
