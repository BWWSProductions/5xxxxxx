Issue 8 of Sex and Murder Magazine brings you face to face with horror like you've never seen before. There are tales of backstabbing gangsters, murdered hookers, and convenience store robbers; plus a cannibal love story you won't want to miss. You'll also find a host of poems that explore the unsavory side of the human condition. So come on in to Sex and Murder Magazine, and see just how good it can feel to be scared.
