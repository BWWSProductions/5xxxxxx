2009 compilation ,   a musical introduction to the work of the great Alejandro Escovedo. This collection contains material taken from his albums Gravity,   13 Years and The Hand Of My Fathers. Alejandro first came to prominence as a member of Rank And File and True Believers before embarking on a solo career. This collection highlights some of his best recorded work and features material that's still part of his live set today. Stand out tracks include a stunning live version of The Velvet Underground's 'Pale Blue Eyes'
