Based on the Disney movie created by Tim Burton and inspired by the classic Lewis Carroll novel, Alice in Wonderland allows players to guide, protect and aid Alice as she journeys through the world of Underland while unraveling the game's many twisted mysteries. Playing as the Mad Hatter, White Rabbit, Cheshire Cat, and other memorable characters from the film, players get to explore a world filled with whimsical settings, optical illusions, curiously challenging puzzles, and formidable adversaries.
