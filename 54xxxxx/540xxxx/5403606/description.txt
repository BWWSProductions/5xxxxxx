THE DISTINGUISHED RACING HERITAGE OF PORSCHE

the triple freatures of this DVD take a close look at the PORSCHEs that have made the name an icon in automobile history.  Rare racing footage, interviews
and close-up looks at one-of-a-kind cars make this collectors set a must see and own.

The first program features the 917 PORSCHE race cars in their 12 cylinder form.

The second; PORSCHE 930 Turbo.  A detailed look at the 76 thru 79 version of the most sought after PORSCHE.

The third program is "PORSCHE A LeMANS Retrospect". This is a gathering of every factory PORSCHE race car ever produced at the Laguna Sece Raceway in 
California.
