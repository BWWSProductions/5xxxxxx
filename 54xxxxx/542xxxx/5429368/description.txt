Resume:

Since its original publication in 1990, this work has been translated in 9 languages and has become one the key works of contemporary feminist theory. It offers a powerful critique of heteronormativity and of the function of gender in the modern world. Material is in sections on subjects of sex/gender/desire; prohibition, psychoanalysis, and the production of the heterosexual matrix; and subversive bodily acts.
