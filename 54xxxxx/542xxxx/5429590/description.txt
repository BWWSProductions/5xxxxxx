Standard Catalog of World Paper Money: General Issues 9th Ed, Volume 2

PDF | 1177 pages | scanned copy

This catalog compiles all government paper money, bank notes and emergency money (substitute currency) that has circulated worldwide between the years 1650 and 1960. This edition has been updated to include 50,000 prices, 19,000 listings in three grades of condition and a new identification guide.
