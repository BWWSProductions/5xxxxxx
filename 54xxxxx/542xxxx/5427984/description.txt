Jindo Folk Songs
Jindo Intangible Arts Culture Series 6 (2008)

This Cd contains 10 Namdo (Southwest region) folk songs (tracks 1-10) and 6 Local Jindo folk songs (tracks 11-16). The former are performed by professionally trained singers and instrumentalists at the Korean Centre for Namdo Traditional Performing Arts (in Jindo-gun), the remaining by local Jindo performers lead by Bak Dong Mae, holder of Intangible Cultural Property 51 (Namdo Field Songs) and Seol Jae Rim, holder of Intangible Cultural Property 19 (Jindo Funeral Songs). I have previously uploaded CDs of both Namdo Field Songs and Jindo Funeral Songs (click on my username). The six regional songs were transmitted in Inji-ri, Jisan-myeon.
