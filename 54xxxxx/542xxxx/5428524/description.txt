When Zeke Kirby and his team of lunar waste disposal technicians make a routine maintenance call to the Intercorp lunar sci-station they find it disturbingly empty.  Zeke is about to discover just what happens to someone suffering from lycanthropy when they're not under the moon but on it.

Written by Joe Casey and Caleb Gerard, with art by Damian Couceiro.
