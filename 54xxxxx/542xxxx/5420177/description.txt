** About **
This video is a short documentary about Hatsune Miku, her creators and users.
It includes Slovenian subtitles. Everything is soft-subbed though so if you're
only looking for a raw, go ahead and grab this. It's a remux of the superior
video from Asahi's official YT channel and audio from the raw that's
circulating around on Share. No postprocessing done on the sources.

** Specs **
Video: h.264 1280*720
Audio: AAC 128kbps
Subs: Fancy ASS (vobsub/libass enabled player required) + Plain SRT (for VLC etc.)

** Playback **
Windows: CCCP + MediaPlayerClassic recommended (http://www.cccp-project.net)
Linux: mplayer + SMPlayer front-end is probably the simplest
