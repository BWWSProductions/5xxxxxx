The Lionshare (+ Legacy). The latest free-to-share release from the VODO. This fiction feature from Josh Bernard centers around the lives and times of some kids from Brooklyn, a fictional BitTorrent tracker, The Lionshare, and the attentions of a flighty girl. Introduced by a stunning short sci-fi animation, Legacy, by Grzegorz Jonkajtys.

More information about The Lionshare can be found on the official homepage - http://www.lionsharemovie.com
