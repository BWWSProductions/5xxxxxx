ISSUE 285 - MARCH 2010 - WHITE
Code: 285W.

Chart Breakers - This chart topping mix features new and forthcoming releases from lady GaGa, Black Eyed Peas, Sugababes, Honorebel feat. Pitbull and Three 6 Mafia feat. Sean Kingston.
Glee: The Mix - Unless you have been hiding away in a cave for the last few months then you cannot have helped but notice the hype surrounding the hit TV show 'Glee' . It has become a global phenomenon and spawned several hit recordings. This mix features a selection of the biggest tracks from the show, including the top 3 smash hit 'Don't Stop Believin'.
Smash Hits - This mix features a selection of latest hits from Timbaland, Lady GaGa, The Saturdays, Sugababes & Chipmunk.
Total Ska Party - This one is guaranteed to have them on their feet! This uplifting mix features classic tracks from Specials, Jools Holland and Maytals as well as the current hit from Paolo Nutini -'10/10'.
Noughties ... But Nice! - As we are now in a brand new decade, we thought it appropriate to look back at the last 10 years and produce a selection of mixes featuring some of the biggest tracks. This mix features songs from Sophie Ellis-Bextor, Rachel Stevens, Kylie, Sugababes & Girls Aloud.
Extended Floorfiller: 'The Irish Rover' - This is the perfect mix for the forthcoming St. Patrick's Day celebrations and for any party in the future. It's always been a party anthem and now it's even longer! The big question is...can your audience keep up?
Extended Floorfiller: 'I Don't Feel Like Dancin' - The 2006 number one is given the extended treatment to ensure this party classic keeps them on the dancefloor for longer.
*The Creeps vs. The Inbetweeners - The hit comedy show meets dance anthem in this cheeky little mix that features the backing of The Creeps with samples from The lnbetweeners over the top.
*The Inbetweeners Toolkit - This is one of, if not the, best TV comedy show ... ever! It's like a cross between Grange Hill & Shameless and is very, very funny! This toolkit features some of the best one-liners and quotes from the hit TV show... but be warned ... they are not for the prudish!

ISSUE 285 - MARCH 2010 -BLACK
Code: 285B.

Commercial Dance - An incredible dance mix featuring current hits from Whiley feat. Chew Fu, Chuckie & LMFAO, Sidney Samson feat. Wizard Sleeve, SBS Goes Dutch and the brilliant new release from Steve Aoki feat. Zuper Blahq. Mixed by Danny Holland.
Battle Of The Babes: Beyonce vs. Rihanna -This mix features 10 tracks .... 5 from each artist. The tracks are alternated throughout the mix so it appears that each artist is battling against the other! It's pure urban magic and you will love it!
David Guetta Club Anthems - He is one of the world's leading OJ/Producers and everything he touches seems to turn to gold! This mix features a selection of his biggest hits, including 'Sexy Chick' & 'When Love Takes Over'.
Chipmunk Triple Tracker -The urban superstar is immortalised in this 3-track mix that features the hits 'Oopsy Daisy', 'Look For Me' & 'Diamond RIngs'.
Mash Ups -This months mash ups are Disco Cell vs. Pitbull (Popcorn vs. Hotel Room Service),
Dave Dorell vs Ke$ha (Children vs. Tik Tok). Good Things Come In 3's -Lady GaGa vs. Britney vs. Kells (Paparazzi vs. 3 vs. Get Along With You) & Timbaland vs. Snap (The Way I Are vs. Rhythm Is A Dancer)
