 Yo! Bum Rush the Show is the debut album of American hip hop group Public Enemy, released January 26, 1987 on Def Jam Recordings in the United States. The group's logo, a silhouette of a black man in a rifle's crosshairs, is debuted on the album's cover.

 Yo! Bum Rush the Show features a sample-heavy sound by production team The Bomb Squad.
