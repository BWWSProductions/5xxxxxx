Commandos, Beyond the Call of Duty - Direct Play - Mod edition

Installation:
-Unzip the folder "Commandos, Beyond the Call of Duty" to the location of your choice.
-Go to the folder "Commandos, Beyond the Call of DutyManuals", read and follow the instructions in "Commandos BTCOD registry.txt".
-When it's done, launch "coman_mp.exe".


"Mod edition" note:
-The game provided is EXACTLY the same as the original, no gameplay alterations whatsoever.
-The WAR_MP.DIR file is already unpacked and deleted for your convenience, should you want to create or add mods.
-JSGME mod enabler included, with MODS folder to put your mods in (read the documentation for JSGME in the Manuals folder, it's simple and practical). You don't have to manually backup files anymore.

Nothing was ripped, all the in game sounds, music and videos are present.
