Tear the Signs Down is the third album by Wales-based band The Automatic. It was released on 8 March 2010 and will be the first album released by the band on their own Armoured Records after being dropped by B-Unique. The first single "Interstate" was released on 6 December, with second single "Run & Hide" released a week prior to the album itself on 1 March 2010.
