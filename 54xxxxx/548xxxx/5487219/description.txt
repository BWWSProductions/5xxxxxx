Stronger with Each Tear (often styled as Stronger withEach Tear) is the ninth studio album from American R&B and soul  singer Mary J. Blige. The album was released in the U.S. on December 21, 2009 under Blige's own imprint Matriarch Records, making it her third consecutive album to be released during the Christmas period following The Breakthrough (2005) and Growing Pains (2007).

Internationally it was released December 18, 2009 in Australia and Germany, December 21 in France, December 23 in Japan with further international releases (in some cases re-releases) in February and March 2010.
