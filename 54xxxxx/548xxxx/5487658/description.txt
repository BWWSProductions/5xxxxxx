SUMMARY
Linna is a SWAT officer who is faced with a series of bizarre murders. The victims are often found eviscerated and slashed, as if by huge claws, their flesh torn asunder. Then, a new string of crimes begin - the murders are similar but the targets are all men, seemingly seduced, then ambushed and brutally butchered. At about the same time, Linna's best friend, a beautiful stripper named Kata, has begun acting very strangely. Linna races to crack the case before her boyfriend becomes the kill's next target.
