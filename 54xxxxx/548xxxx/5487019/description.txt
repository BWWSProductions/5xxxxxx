Warlands is a fantasy comic book published by the now-defunct production house, Dreamwave Productions, which was founded by Pat Lee and his brother Roger Lee in 1996.

In the first volume of Warlands the Vampire hordes of Datara, led by Lord Malagen, have defeated the dwarves, elves and humans living in the eastern part of Warlands and a party of heroes emerges to save the world. In order to do so they must find the ancient artifact, the mysterious Darklyte Armor.

Written by Adrian Tsang with Pat Lee provided plot and pencils.
