Marvel Universe was a seven-issue series written by Roger Stern that covered stories before the Fantastic Four gained their powers.  

The first three issues featured The Invaders attempting to stop Baron Strucker from building an atomic bomb and was illustrated by Steve Epting.  #4-7 featured The Monster Hunters fighting Gorgilla and was illustrated by Bret Blevins and Jason Armstrong.
