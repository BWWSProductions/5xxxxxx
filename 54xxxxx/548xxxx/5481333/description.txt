(RELEASE  NOTES)   
   
DTL Prokyon is a special sans-serif due to the shapes of the   
letters have been brought back to their nature. Erhard Kaiser  
has cut away parts of the letters with surgical precision 
until a powerfull skeleton has been left, i.e. the bends of    
the lowercase letters a, d, m, n, p, q, r and u are attached   
to the straights and therefore no parts of them are sticking   
out. However, This did not take away the details. The view has 
become rounder and softer and gives an elegant image. Besides, 
Kaiser has put in various substantial details, i.e. the bends  
in k, v, w, x, y and z. Also the capital letters have gotten   
these particular details. The most impressive part is the 
lowercase letter 'g'. This letter itself makes DTL Prokyon
very recognizable without becoming a gimmick.   
   
DTL Prokyon is named after the main star in the constellation  
Canis Minor, also known as "The Smaller Dog". DTL Prokyon is a 
true star among the sans-serifs.
