Most of Wukimon's songs are rather mellow electronica songs with influences from indiepop and triphop as well as folk. The music is likely to appeal to fans of artists such as Bjork, The Knife, Mum, Depeche Mode, IAMX, MGMT, Tunng, Psapp, Portishead and Massive Attack.
