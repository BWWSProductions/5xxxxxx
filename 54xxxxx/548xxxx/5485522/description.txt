Dave Dee, Dozy, Beaky, Mick & Tich, aka "DDDBMT", were a chart topping British pop/rock group of the 1960s.
Two of their single releases secured global sales in excess of one million copies each, and they reached Number One in the UK with the second of them, "The Legend of Xanadu". (Wiki)
