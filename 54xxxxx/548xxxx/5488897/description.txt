"Would You Raise Your Hands" is a dance-pop song written by Jamie Appleby, Melinda Appleby, Melinda Jackson and Elliot Gleave, and produced by Ash for the Rogue Traders' upcoming fourth studio album Night of the Living Drums. It was as released as the album's second single in Australia as a digital download on March 12, 2010 and as a CD single on April 9, 2010. This is the second single with new lead singer Mindi Jackson



This release was 2010 not 2009.
