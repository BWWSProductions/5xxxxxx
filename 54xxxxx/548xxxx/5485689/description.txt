Victorious is an American  television sitcom, starring Victoria Justice and created by Dan Schneider (creator of the Nickelodeon series The Amanda Show, iCarly, Drake and Josh and Zoey 101, the latter of which Justice was previously a series regular). The pilot episode, advertised as a "sneak preview" of the series, aired on Nickelodeon on March 27, 2010

The show will revolve around Justice's character Tori, an aspiring singer who attends a performing arts high school while getting into wacky screwball situations on a daily basis.
