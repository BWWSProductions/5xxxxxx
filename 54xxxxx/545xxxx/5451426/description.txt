Command Conquer 4: Tiberian Twilight is a real-time strategy video game
and the latest installment of the popular Command Conquer franchise
released March 16 2010. It constitutes the final chapter in the Tiberium saga.
Command Conquer 4 Is the first game in the series to implement a form of DRM
that requires constant Internet access. Command Conquer 4 will also
use EA s own servers for online play rather than Gamespy Servers which
EA has relied on for Command Conquer games in the past. A closed beta of
the game was officially released to contest winners by EA on November 21 2009.
