Peter Hammill's	exaggerated screaming, craving or whimpering is not everyone's taste, mine included. Apart from judging his singing, V.D.G.G. are a compulsory lesson for those claiming to have a clue of what prog is all about.

These are the 2005 remastered versions (with bonus tracks) of their most important and appreciated albums:

1970: The Least We Can Do Is Wave To Each Other
1970: H To He, Who Am The Only One
1971: Pawn Hearts
1975: Godbluff
1976: Still Life

covers and booklets included
