Remember all those quarters you spent down at the arcade? Now is when all that work pays off! Data East Arcade Classics brings together 15 of the company's all-time greatest games. Play BurgerTime, Burnin' Rubber, Side Pocket, Caveman Ninja, Peter Pepper's Ice Cream Factory, Bad Dudes vs. Dragon Ninja, and other unforgettable favorites. Each game has been adapted for play on the Wii. Reach 75 goals across all the games to unlock some sweet bonus content, or show that you haven't lost your touch by linking your high score to your Mii. You can also play solo, head-to-head, or co-op multiplayer with your choice of Wii controller configurations.
