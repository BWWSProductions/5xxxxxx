These are all The Silent Hill Soundtracks up to Shattered Memories, and since Akira
Yamaoka left Konami, I thought it's a good moment to upload them, includes:

Silent Hill Original Soundtracks (1999)
Silent Hill 2 Original Soundtracks (2002)
Silent Hill 3 Original Soundtracks (2003)
Silent Hill 4 - The Room - Original Soundtracks - Disc 1 (2004)
Silent Hill 4 - The Room - Original Soundtracks - Disc 2 (2004)
Silent Hill Zero Original Soundtracks (2008)
Silent Hill Homecoming Soundtrack (2008)
Silent Hill Shattered Memories Soundtrack (2009)

These are MP3 encoded with LAME at V0 quality from the FLACs (except Shattered
Memories encoded from Lossless WAV) tagged and with embedded (and external) covers
included.
