Beachcomber By the Way was a short-lived radio programme that aired from March 1989 to December 1994. 
There were 18 half-hour episodes and it was broadcast on BBC Radio 4. 

Whimsical series based on JB Morton;s Daily Express column;By The Way;.
Appearing between the wars, for which he adopted the guise of Beachcomber.  
Bringing to life such characters as Prodnose, Mr Justice Cocklecarrot and Dr Strabismus of Utrecht
