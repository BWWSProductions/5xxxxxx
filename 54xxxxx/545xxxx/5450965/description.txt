Addicted To Bass 2010 is the latest floor-filling compilation from Ministry Of Sound. Packed with over 60 exhilarating, pumping tracks, this triple CD collection features unbeatable bass-heavy mixes from the likes of The Prodigy, Dizzee Rascal and Sidney Samson. The ultimate blend of electro, drum 'n' bass and funk to get your party started!
