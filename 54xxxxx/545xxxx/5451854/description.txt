Wikipedia - Buzz is the third studio album by British pop group Steps, released on October 25, 2000. It reached number four on the UK Albums Chart. The album saw the group edging away from the PWL sound to a more mature sound, working with top producers from Sweden. Each of the five group members co-wrote a track on the album. "Better the Devil You Know", "Summer of Love", "Stomp", "It's the Way You Make Me Feel", "Here And Now" and "You'll Be Sorry" were released as singles.
