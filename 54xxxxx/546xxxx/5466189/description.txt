Wiki:

Now That's What I Call Music! 36 or Now 36 was released on 24 February 1997 on audio cassette and Compact Disc. The album is the 36th edition of the (UK) Now! series. Eight tracks in this volume reached number one on the UK Singles Chart. They are "Mama", "Don't Speak", "Your Woman", "A Different Beat", "Discotheque", "Breathe" and "Beetlebum".
