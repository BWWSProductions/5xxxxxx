Koi Mere Dil Mein Hai (2005)

Director:Deepak Ramsay
Writer:Humayun Mirza  
Release Date:3 June 2005  
Genre:Drama 

The wealthy Malhotra family consists of dad, Vikram, his wife, and son, Raj. 
They would like Raj to get married to their friend, Ishwarchand Mangatram 
Gore's only U.S. based daughter, Simran. But Raj is intent on finding his 
very own soul-mate - which he does in his sister's tutor, Asha, who comes 
from a poor family, and is refusing to accept any advances from him. 
On the other hand, Simran too refuses to marry Raj,..
