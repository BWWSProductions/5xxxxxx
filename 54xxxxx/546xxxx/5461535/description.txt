Comic book adaptation of the movie starring Dominic Monaghan (The Lord of the Rings, Lost, FlashForward) and Ron Perlman (Hellboy, Sons of Anarchy).

When 19th Century justice catches up with the infamous grave robbers Grimes and Blake, the condemned Blake reveals to the peculiar clergyman, Father Duffy, who you can never... ever... trust a corpse.

Written by Glenn McQuaid, the writer and director of the movie I Sell The Dead, with art by Braham Revel (Guerillas).  Braham Revel is currently selling the original art pages of I Sell The Dead.  You can check out them out at;
