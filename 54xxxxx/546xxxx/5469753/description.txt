Following the events of the Codename: Patriot story-arc humanity turns against the Kryptonians and General Lane is declared a national hero.

Supergirl, Flamebird and Nightwing try and prove their innocence when the world thinks that they murdered Mon-El.

Written by Greg Rucka, Sterling Gates, James Robinson and Eric Trautmann, with art by Pere Perez, Bernard Chang, Fernando Dagnino and Raul Fernandez.
