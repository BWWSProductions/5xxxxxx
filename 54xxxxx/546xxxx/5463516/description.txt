Leroy Browne, who had defected to China during the 1964 Olympics in Japan, seems to be dissatisfied during a series of appearances in Hong Kong. Kelly Robinson and Alexander Scott are assigned to approach Browne to see if he really wants to come back to the U.S. If Browne is interested, his life may be in danger from Chinese agents who won't want to the star athlete to un-defect. Written by Bill Koenig
