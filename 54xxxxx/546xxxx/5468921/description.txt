Timeless Wisdoms - DSS John-Roger
Publisher: Mandeville Press | 2008-09-01 | ISBN: 1893020479 | PDF | 218 pages | 5.77 MB

Following from the premise that each person is a unique, divine being, this collection of short essays serves as a constant guide to the spirit within. Each essay provides a few moments of repose and reflection to balance the fast pace and multitasking of daily living. Concise and compassionate, the wisdom in this volume is pertinent in any given time or situation.
