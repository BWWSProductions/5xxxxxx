Crud is a Detroit industrial band featuring Vinnie Dombroski (Sponge, Spys4Darwin, The Orbitsuns, Sons of Perdition), Danielle Arsenault (Stun Gun), David Black (Seduce), Dana Forrester (60 Second Crush) and Eric Hoegemeyer. Crud's music has been featured in many TV shows and movies. Quality is 320 VBR.
