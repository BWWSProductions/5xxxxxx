T A L K   T A L K  - Spirit Of Eden -

After leaving the 'New Romantics' terrain by their transitional album "The Colour Of Spring" they ultimately shifted into a unique and inimitable avantgarde-rock style.
"Spirit Of Eden" from 1988 is the first part of a twin set (together with 1991s "Laughing Stock") that was praised as an influential item to the post-rock* movement of the 90s. *(http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Post-rock)

Musical fast food consumers are wrong here because this album refuses conventional rock or pop formulas in a very categorical manner.
