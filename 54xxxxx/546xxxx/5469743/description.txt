"Eenie Meenie" is a song performed by Jamaican-American singer Sean Kingston and Canadian singer Justin Bieber. It was released as the first single from Kingston's untitled third studio album on March 23, 2010.  It is also included on Bieber's My World 2.0 album. The song will also impact mainstream radio on March 16, 2010.  The track was written by Bieber, Kingston, and Carlos & Steven Battey and was produced by Benny Blanco.


"Eenie Meenie" is the first of two songs that Bieber and Kingston have collaborated on to appear on their respective albums.
