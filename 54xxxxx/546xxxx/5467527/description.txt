Driver Installation (Diskette 1)

1. Double Click on Setup.exe FROM DISKETTE 1
2. Choose language -> [Next]
3. Click on [Next] 4 times
4. Choose Destination Location -> [Next]
5. 'Information' window appear -> [OK]
6. 'Information' window "Please calibrate..." appear -> [OK]
7. 'Game Controllers' window appear -> [Add]
8. From 'Game Controllers' list choose "InterAct ProPad6" and then -> [OK]
9. Click on [Properties] to Test or Calibrate GamePad
