The Muppet Movie is the first of a series of live-action  musical feature films starring Jim Henson's Muppets. Released in 1979, the film was produced by Henson Associates and ITC Entertainment. It is a fictional account of how the Muppets came to be. The film is famous for breaking the fourth wall, as well as its many running gags and inside jokes.
