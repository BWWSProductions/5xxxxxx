The long awaited second edition of Principles and Practice of Pharmaceutical Medicine provides an invaluable guide to all areas of drug development and medical aspects of marketing. The title has been extensively revised and expanded to include the latest regulatory and scientific developments. 

New chapters include:
* European Regulations
* Ethics of Pharmaceutical Medicine
* Licensing and Due Diligence
* Pharmacogenomics
* Encompassing the entire spectrum of pharmaceutical medicine, it is the most up-to-date international guide currently available.
