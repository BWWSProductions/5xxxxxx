Collection of comic books based on the American hip hop duo from Detroit, Michigan.  The members of the duo are Violent J and Shaggy 2 Dope.  Insane Clown Posse performs a style of hardcore hip hop known as horrorcore  and is known for its elaborate live performances. 

The main series, Insane Clown Posse: The Pendulum, follows Insane Clown Posse and the Dark Carnival's attempts to stop a demon named Killnor from destroying a priest named Father Jesus, who possesses miraculous healing powers.

Due to scenes of graphic violence and swearing this pack comes with an ADULT rating.
