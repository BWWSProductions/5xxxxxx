"Not Myself Tonight" is a song by American pop singer Christina Aguilera and is the lead single from her forthcoming fourth studio album, Bionic. The track is written by Ester Dean and Polow da Don and was produced by Polow da Don.


On March 23 2010, after a 24-hour countdown on her relaunched official website, Aguilera revealed that "Not Myself Tonight" would be the name of the first single from the album.

The video for Not Myself Tonight premiered on March 30.
