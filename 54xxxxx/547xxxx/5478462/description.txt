Ben and Holly's Little Kingdom is an animated pre-school childrens show shown on Nick Jr. Ben is a loveable little elf while his best friend Holly is a bossy but loveable fairy princess. The show is set in an enchanted magical kingdom with fairies, elves and insects.

The episodes included are:

1. A Trip To The Seaside
