Bob Dylan With Tom Petty and The Heartbreakers 1986
Hard to Handle - Live in Australia

Filmed live during their Australian tour, this 1986 concert performance features Bob Dylan, accompanied by Tom Petty and the Heartbreakers, and includes a very memorable vocal duet of Dylan's "Knockin' on Heaven's Door." The electrifying show also features the timeless tracks "In the Garden," "Just Like a Woman," "Like a Rolling Stone," "It's Alright Ma (I'm Only Bleeding)," "Girl from the North Country," "Lenny Bruce" and many more.
