
VITO Metronome is the easiest way to master your musical skills. Real musicians will be pleased with the stylish & neat interface and different skins. Learn to play evenly and tune your instruments using this inspiring program with stunning looks.

Main features of the program include:

    * Easily switch between preset tempos: Largo, Larghetto, Adagio, Andante, Andantino, Moderato, Allegretto, Allegro, Vivace, Presto, Prestissimo,
    * Manually switch between preset tempos or tap the tempo with joystick button
    * Switch between most commonly used measures: 2/4, 3/4, 4/4, 5/8, 3+2, 6/8, 7/8
    * Set necessary volume
    * Enjoy different skins
    * Tune the instruments

Languages:

English

    * OS: Symbian OS
