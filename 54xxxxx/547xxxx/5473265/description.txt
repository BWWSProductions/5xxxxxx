It's a MySims MyStery! Enter a world of twisting plots, sinister villains, thrilling adventures, tricky puzzles, and high-tech gadgets as you stay hot on the trail of an evil crime boss. When the ruler of the underworld threatens the city, you're the special agent summoned to stop him. Pick locks, hack computers, track footprints, and more as you follow leads and collect clues. Recruit other MySims to form a crime-solving team, then send them out on missions. Customize your headquarters with treasures you find and tools to help your team. The mystery will send you through the city and beyond and push your skills to the limit!
