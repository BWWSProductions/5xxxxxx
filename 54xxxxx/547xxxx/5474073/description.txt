The Nickelodeon Kids' Choice Awards, also known as the KCA's, is an annual awards show that airs on the Nickelodeon cable channel, which airs live and is usually held and telecast live

voted by Nickelodeon viewers. Winners receive a hollow orange blimp figurine

The show features numerous celebrity guests and musical acts. In recent years, slime stunts have been incorporated into the show. The KCA's also host live entertainment.

Hosted by Kevin James
