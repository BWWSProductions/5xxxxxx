Wiki:

Now That's What I Call Music! 41 or Now 41 was released in 1998 on audio cassette and Compact Disc. The album is the 41st edition of the (UK) Now! series. Now 41 contains seven songs that reached number one on the UK singles chart. They are No Matter What, Millennium, Heartbeat, I Want You Back, Bootie Call, Turn Back Time, Too Much, and Girlfriend.
This album has a double appearance from Robbie Williams.
