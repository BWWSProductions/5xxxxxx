Chad Smiths Bombastic Meatbats is an American 
instrumental funk-rock band from Los Angeles, 
California. It consists of Red Hot Chili Peppers 
drummer Chad Smith, guitarist Jeff Kollman (from 
Cosmosquad), bassist Kevin Chown (Uncle Kracker, 
Ted Nugent), and keyboardist Ed Roth.
