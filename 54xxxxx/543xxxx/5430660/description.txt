Oskar Schindler is een ijdele en hebberige Duitse zakenman die echter tijdens het Nazi-regime

Oskar Schindler is a vain, glorious and greedy German businessman who becomes unlikely humanitarian amid the barbaric 
Nazi reign when he feels compelled to turn his factory into a refuge for Jews. 
Based on the true story of Oskar Schindler who managed to save about 1100 Jews 
from being gassed at the Auschwitz concentration camp. A testament for the good in all of us.
