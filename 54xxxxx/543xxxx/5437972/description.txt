Miley Stewart has a secret. By day, she's a normal teen girl. She goes to school, hangs out with her friends, and does her homework. But by night, she's Hannah Montana, pop diva extraordinaire! When Miley's pop-star persona threatens to take over her life, her father decides she's had enough and hustles her home to Crowley Corners, Tennessee, for a dose of real life. Live both lives as you take control of Miley and get into hijinks and shenanigans in her hometown! Choose wardrobe and accessories from the hit movie and more. Plus, you can unlock secret songs and concert stages that go beyond the movie!
