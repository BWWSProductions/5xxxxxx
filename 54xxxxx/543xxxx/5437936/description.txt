"Rocket" is a synthpop song performed by British duo Goldfrapp. The song was written and produced by Alison Goldfrapp and Will Gregory, with additional production by Pascal Gabriel, for the duo's fifth album Head First (2010). It will be released as the album's first single on 8 March 2010. There was a music video shot to accompany the song. To promote the single release, the duo is going to perform the song on the Friday Night with Jonathan Ross TV show on 26 March 2010. The music video to "Rocket" was released on 5 March 2010.
