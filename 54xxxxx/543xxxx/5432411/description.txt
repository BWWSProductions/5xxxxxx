Rio De Janeiro is breathtaking.Upon sailing into its harbour in the 1920s the English writer Peter Fleming comically observed that Rio seemed set upon "a continent without adequate self-control".Nowhere on earth are so many soectacular natural elements combined in quite such abundance.Ringed a series of impossibly steep peaks,home to tropical forests teeming with wildlife and an explosion of exaggerated colour,cradle of shimmering lakes and meandering canals,and delineated by a string of crescent beaches - Rio De Janeiro is an embarrassment of natural riches.

Review
A collection of softly toned samba and bossa from Brazil, Rio by Night includes many of the country's musical legends, like Joao Gilberto ( doing his first hit "Chega de Saudade ), Marcos Valle ( Samba de Verao ), Elis Regina ( Sai Dessa ), Djavan ( Tem Boi Na Linha ) and Milton Banana ( Garota de Ipanema ). There are a few quibbles -- no Jobim? -- but for the most part, the set is a great Brazilian compilation.
