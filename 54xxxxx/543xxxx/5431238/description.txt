                           Description                            
                                                       
                                                                        
                                                                          
        Business Trip Buddy helps business travelers keep track of        
     flights, hotels, rental cars, confirmation numbers, contacts, and    
     other trip related information using their Windows Mobile device.    
           Works in both connected or disconnected flight mode.           
       Are you tired of printing out confirmation pages for flights,      
         hotels, and rental cars for every business trip you take?        
           Are you tire of shuffling through those pages to find          
        confirmation numbers, flight times, hotel phone numbers, or       
                directions to the rental car dropoff site?                
     Are you tired of trying to enter and dial your cellphone to call     
          a hotel for directions while driving a rental car in an         
                             unfamiliar city?                             
                                                                          
       If you answered yes to any of these questions, Business Trip       
     Buddy can help! With Business Trip Buddy, you can keep track of:     
                                                                          
                             Customer Contacts                            
        Integrates with Pocket Outlook contacts for customer phone,       
                     address, driving directions, etc.                    
      Enter Trip name, customer. notes, start and end dates, and trip     
                                  status                                  
                                                                          
                                  Flights                                 
         Choose from a list of common air carriers or add your own        
        Choose from a list of common airport codes or add your own        
          Automatically defaults flight dates based on trip dates         
       Enter flight numbers, flight times, and seat numbers for each      
                              flight segment                              
        Automatically reverses selected airports for return flight        
                Allows single or multiple segments per trip               
                Allows single or multiple airlines per trip               
                                                                          
                                  Hotels                                  
         Choose from a list of common hotel chains or add your own        
     Integrates with Pocket Outlook contacts for hotel phone, address,    
                         driving directions, etc.                         
           Enter confirmation number, room type, and room number          
               Defaults check in and out dates to trip dates              
                   Allows multiple hotel stays per trip

      Integrates with Pocket Outlook contacts for rental location''s      
                 phone, address, driving directions, etc.
