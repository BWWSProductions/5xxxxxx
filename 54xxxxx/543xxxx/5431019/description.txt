Alice Through the Looking Glass (1998)

Director: John Henderson
Alice: Kate Beckinsale
White Knight: Ian Holm
White Queen: Penelope Wilton
White King: Geoffrey Palmer

A fantastic live action adaptation of Lewis Carroll's masterpiece sequel to Alice in Wonderland! Alice traveled to Wonderland once - but now she takes another journey to a fascinating world with memorable characters... Tweedledum and Tweedledee, Humpty Dumpty, Red King and Queen, Red and White Knights, and Jabberwocky. Join Alice on this enchanted and magical adventure sure to delight and amaze the whole family!
