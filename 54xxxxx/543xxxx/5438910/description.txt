It was in the context of the collapse of the power and authority of the Han Dynasty, which occurred gradually over a 35-year period, that the three kingdoms began to take shape led by the three most prominent heroes of the time: Cao Cao, who established the Kingdom of Wei in the north, Liu Bei, who established the Kingdom of Shu in the west and Sun Quan, who established the Kingdom of Wu in the south.

Enjoy repeating the historic struggles of these great men in a PC
