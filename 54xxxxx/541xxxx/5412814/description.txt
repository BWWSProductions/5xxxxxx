Who I am and who turned me into a killing machine? To find the answers to these difficult questions, the main character Alex Mercer PROTOTYPE must survive in hell brutal battles. You can look at the world through the eyes of ruthless mutant, devoid of memory and living alone, a thirst for revenge against those who turned him into a monster. On your way get up many obstacles, but none of them unable to stop the powerful anti-hero. And destroy, blow up, kill and go to the truth at any cost!

At your disposal are truly unique abilities. Alex does not know anything about his past, but also wholly owns the present. Ruthlessly destroying anyone who becomes a hindrance, he assigns it to the memories and skills, becoming more powerful. He - a prototype of inhumane weapons, created by someone unknown and it is unclear for what purpose. Fighting on two fronts - the professional military and the infected mutants, the hero with your help to unravel the plot and make the guilty pay in full on the account.
