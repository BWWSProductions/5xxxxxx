             Softwarenetz Cash book was developed for                         
             self-employed persons, freelancers and small                     
             companies, who like to keep their own cash book.                 
             Even without knowledge of accounting it is easy to               
             keep a cash book.                                                
                                                                              
             The program consists of more functionality, which                
             enables you to do the complete accounting of your                
             company yourself. You can register all kinds of                  
             revenues, expenses (cash, bank accounts,                         
             PayPal-accounts, credit cards), export this                      
             information to a Datev-file and give this to your                
             accountant.                                                      
                                                                              
             The Cash book supports online banking HBCI 2.2                   
             PIN/TAN and FinTS 3.0 PIN/TAN. With just one mouse               
             click you can retrieve your bank statements via the              
             internet. The program recognises many bookings                   
             automatically and will enter them in your accounting             
             directly.                                                        
                                                                              
             For all Ebay-sellers there is a Import solution for              
             your PayPal account. You can import all PayPal                   
             bookings into the program. These PayPal bookings are             
             also recognised automatically and entered because of             
             rules which were defined in advance.                             
                                                                              
             Through the analysis (reports and statistics) you                
             have an overview about your turnover, VAT, etc. at               
             any time. The booking journal enables you to export              
             all bookings in Datev (KNE) format or normal text                
             format.                                                          
                                                                              
                                                                              
             Softwarenetz Cash book for Windows                               
                                                                              
             * Keep a Cash book with or without chart of accounts             
             * Accounting of many other financial accounts (bank,             
               credit card, PayPal)                                           
             * Online retrieval of bank statements (with HBCI 2.2             
               PIN/TAN and FinTS 3.0 PIN/TAN)                                 
             * Import PayPal-bookings from CSV-files                          
             * Automatic booking of PayPal-turnover and bank                  
               statements                                                     
             * Datev-Export (in new DATEV-KNE-Format)                         
             * Grafical statistics and reports                                
             * You are able to keep a cash book for as many                   
               customers you like
