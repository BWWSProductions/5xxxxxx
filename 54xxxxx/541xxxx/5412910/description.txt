Secret Weapons over Normandy
Year: 2003
Genre: Action / Simulator

Publisher: Lucas Arts Entertainment
Platform: PS2
Region: PAL

Description:
Action in the game takes place during the Second World War. You play as a man of the secret team of pilots, whose main task to disrupt the plans of the Third Reich. You are waiting for 30 first-class missions, closest to the real pilots flying fighter aircraft. But do not relax, in the beginning you can check whether you trust the secrets of the war.
