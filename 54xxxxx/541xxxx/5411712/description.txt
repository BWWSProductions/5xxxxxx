 -- Release Notes ---------------------------------------------------------->

  Sonic Classic Collection gathers together the first four Sonic The 
  Hedgehog adventures -- Sonic The Hedgehog, Sonic The Hedgehog 2, Sonic 
  The Hedgehog 3 and Sonic & Knuckles -- introducing some of the most 
  influential Sonic the Hedgehog titles to a whole new generation of DS 
  gamers.
  
  Sonic Classic Collection allows fans both old and new to speed through 
  the much loved zones of the four original Mega Drive adventures, in a 
  bid to collect the chaos emeralds and save the world. Optimised to take 
  full advantage of the Nintendo DS system, Sonic Classic Collection will 
  feature a new save anywhere feature, a first in this original series, 
  meaning that the games can be enjoyed anywhere, anytime! Gamers will 
  also be given the chance to expand their knowledge with a wealth of 
  extras including video and collectable pictures that are sure to be fan 
  favorites.
