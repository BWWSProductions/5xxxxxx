Okay first things first, any Brit Pop compilation without Blur,Oasis or Radiohead is not "Brit Pop The Story". It's a bad title. However this is a great little mid/late 90s compilation. I was quite into making compilation tapes at the time, and a good half of these songs were on those tapes. They weren't necessarily huge hits, or the songs that you'd immediately name as Brit Pop when on Family Fortunes, but none-the-less if you liked your music at the time (or now) they should hit the spot. Hats off to the compilers for taking the path less trodden.....
