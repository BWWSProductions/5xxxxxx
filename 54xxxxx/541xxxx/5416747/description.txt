Kidnapped as a boy, Cedric Zhang was raised to fight in competitions and formed a bond with Christy, a young nurse.  When she disappears with no explanation, Cedric immerses himself in the violent NYC underworld in an effort to locate her, finding himself back in the s horrible world he spent his life trying to escape. 

Written by Brian Wood, with art by Denys Cowan & Kent Williams.
