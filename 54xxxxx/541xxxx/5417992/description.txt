Bodycall, the long established Polish Dark Electro/EBM/Aggrotech squad strikes again. Hard beats, screaming vocals, weird samples and aggresive synths delivered in a consistent, danceable fashion. 


This release also contains band's T-shirt creation kit. 


Highly recommended for every follower of the dark electronic / dark independent scene.
