

A Curious Thing is the second studio album from Scottish songstress Amy MacDonald, following on from debut This Is The Life, and includes the single "Don't Tell Me That It's Over". A helping hand on the album is provided by long-term fan and friend Paul Weller, whose studio and artistic expertise is showcased on "Love, Love" and "This Pretty Face".
