Edubuntu 7.04 used by MK schoolteachers on their Asus eee Pc 900ha
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1) use unetbootin to make a gparted boot-usb 
2) boot from gparted usb and create a 22 Gb ext3 primary partition
3) use unetbootin to make a clonezilla boot-usb
4) boot from clonezilla and prepare to restore partition
   4.1) during the process, insert another usb which contains the 2010-03-06-15-imgedubuntu image
