Tokyo Disney Resort 25th Anniversary

Disney's Halloween 2008

The special events seasons kickoff with Halloween! The Park is adorned with various banners, ghosts, pumpkins, spider webs, etc. Theming also includes 25th Anniversary theme and photo displays at Plaza Terrace and the Plaza hub!

This is the Soundtrack to the "Let's GO Villains" parade which was part of the 25th Halloween Anniversary Spectacular.

The Parade had 2 parts a Daytime parade in which all Good Disney Characters are in Control and then a night time parade in which all the Disney Villains take control and Good prevails over Evil in the end.
