:---<about>-----------------------------------------:

Metal Slug PC Collection is a compilation of seven 
classic arcade games from the Metal Slug franchise 
including Metal Slug, Metal Slug 2, Metal Slug X, 
Metal Slug 3, Metal Slug 4, Metal Slug 5, 
and Metal Slug 6.
  
Each of the games included in this compilation ran on
the NEOGEO AES hardware with the exception of Metal 
Slug 6, which ran on the Atomiswave arcade hardware.
