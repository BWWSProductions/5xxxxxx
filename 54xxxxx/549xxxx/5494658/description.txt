Wiki:
Now That's What I Call Music! 45 or Now 45 was released in 2000. The album is the 45th edition of the (UK) Now! series. It was released on minidisc, audio cassette & CD formats. This album also has a double appearance from Artful Dodger. Now 45 has nine songs that hit the top of the UK Singles Chart. They are "Rise", "Never Be the Same Again", "Fill Me In", "Born To Make You Happy", "Bag It Up", "Don't Call Me Baby", "Don't Give Up", "Toca's Miracle" and "It's Only Us".
