Young Joey Price goes on the adventure of a lifetime when he discovers that there's more to his stuffed toys than just being cuddly.  When the monsters in his closet, The Beasties, come for him his new stuffed animals, The Night Pride, spring to life and protect him.  But during the battle they are drawn into the stuffed animal kingdom and with the way back to reality uncertain it's up to the Night Pride to protect Joey from what lurks in the dark.

Created and written by Mike Bullock (The Phantom) with artwork by Jack Lawrence (Darkham Vale, Doctor Who Adventures), Paul Gutierrez, Michael Metcalf (Timothy and the Transgalactic Towel) and Bob Pedroza (Gimoles).
