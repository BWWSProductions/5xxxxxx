Big Bad Noise is the second album by Aussie rock band Choirboys which was released in 1987. It featured their number one hit and most popular song "Run To Paradise". It also spawned two other top 30 singles Boys Will Be Boys and Struggletown. This album was produced by Peter Blyton, Bryan MaGee and Choirboys.
