* STORY : *

* The Scourge Project is a First-Person Shooter set on Earth in the near-future. The planet has been overrun by a living plague, consisting of nightmarish creatures that are slowly turning Earth into a barren wasteland. With humanity at the brink of extinction and worldwide communications in disarray, small groups of survivors must struggle to stay alive long enough to find out why these creatures are here and, if possible, figure out a way to defeat this dangerous and mysterious threat. 



PC Minimum System Requirements :
================================

OS: Windows XP SP3 / Windows Vista SP1 / Windows 7
Processor: 1.8GHz Intel Core 2 Duo or equivalent AMD CPU
Memory: 1 GB RAM (Windows XP) / 2 GB RAM (Windows Vista and Windows 7)
Hard Drive: 5 GB
Sound Card: DirectX 9.0c compatible
Input: Keyboard / Mouse
Video Card: 256 MB (with Pixel Shader 3.0)
DirectX: 9.0c March 2009
