Semiconductor Physics And Devices: Basic Principles

Publisher: McGraw-Hill Science | ISBN: 0072321075 | edition 2002 | PDF | 768 pages | 22,1 mb








Neamen's Semiconductor Physics and Devices, Third Edition. deals with the electrical properties and characteristics of semiconductor materials and devices. 


The goal of this book is to bring together quantum mechanics, the quantum theory of solids, semiconductor material physics, and semiconductor device physics in a clear and understandable way.
