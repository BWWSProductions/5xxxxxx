     Plot:
      So How Did You End Up Together?
             
      Every relationship is unique. Take the journey between Christina
      (Kaylani Lei) and Curtis (Rocco Reed) for example. Each of them relives
      the incredible adventure of their crazy relationship, but even the happy
      couple each sees things just a little differently. As we follow along, we're
      reminded that when you blend true chemistry with hot sex you're bound to get
      some crazy, mad love...
