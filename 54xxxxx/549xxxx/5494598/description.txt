SYNOPSIS

A panel discussion on the Tibetan People's Uprising and the future of the Tibetan 
Freedom Struggle at Gangchen Kyishong, Dharamsala. This is an initiative by the 
Tibetan People's Uprising Movement to pioneer more open discussion and debate on 
the the Tibetan Freedom Movement among the Tibetan community.

Panelists for the discussion were Karma Choephel, former Speaker of the Tibetan 
Assembly; Sonam Topgyal, former Prime Minister; Bawa Kalsang Gyaltsen, member of 
the Tibetan Assembly; Narkyi Ngawang Dhondup, former member of the Tibetan Govt. 
in Tibet before 1959 and Lukar Jam, a Tibet researcher. Tashi Wangchuk, senior 
reporter for the Voice of Tibet and President of The Association of Tibetan Journalists 
chaired the discussion.
